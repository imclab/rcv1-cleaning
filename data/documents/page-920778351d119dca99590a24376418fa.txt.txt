 Entertainment 
 
 
    Entertainer  Guidelines 
    for 2013 Applications
   
 Look for an exciting array of Northwest and world sounds in a number of
          venues throughout the Festival. Free music is provided during Festival hours on both stages Friday, Saturday and Sunday.       
 
 
 
 
 Main Stage  is located at 3rd
                Street next to the Beer Garden. Great music until 7 pm Friday and Saturday and 5 pm  Sunday. 
 
 &nbsp; 
 
 
 
 
 The  KPLU Stage  offers a variety of free music and entertainment during Festival hours at 8th Street next to the Wine Garden. 
 
 &nbsp; 
 
 
 
 
 The Street Performance Areas  are located at 5th and 7th Streets. Look for Magic, Jugglers, Peruvian music, Salsa dancing and a great accordian act.
