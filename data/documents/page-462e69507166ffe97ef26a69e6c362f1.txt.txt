Tim Keller on Jerram Barrs&#8217;s Echoes of Eden: Reflections on Christianity, Literature, and the Arts , forthcoming next month from Crossway: Echoes of Eden is the most accessible, readable, and yet theologically robust work on Christianity and the arts that you will be able to find. It is biblical, theologically sound, filled with examples, and edifying. It anticipates and answers well all the most common questions that evangelical people ask about the arts. I highly recommend it.
