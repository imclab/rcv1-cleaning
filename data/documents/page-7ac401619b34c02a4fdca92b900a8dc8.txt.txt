 Welcome to  Nova Southeastern University’s Museum of Art | Fort Lauderdale’s New Director, Bonnie Clearwater! 
   
 We are happy to welcome a new member to the  Riverwalk Arts &amp; Entertainment (A&amp;E) District  family:  Bonnie Clearwater, M.A.  was announced as the new Director of Riverwalk A&amp;E partner, Nova Southeastern University’s  Museum of Art | Fort Lauderdale , on July 17! She will officially take over the position on Sept. 3, 2013.
