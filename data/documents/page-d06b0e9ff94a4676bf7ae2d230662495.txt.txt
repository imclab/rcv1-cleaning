     Art & Entertainment District 
 City Center Hagerstown 
Where creativity and talent thrive. Designated as an Arts and Entertainment District, City Center Hagerstown has impressive historic architecture, urban infrastructure, culture incentives, and places to create, live, and learn.  
 
The district also has impressive performance spaces, art galleries, wonderful restaurants, museums, education centers, retail stores, and more. Anchoring the district is the Historic Maryland Theatre on South Potomac Street.  
 
 Incentives &amp; Benefits 
 Incentives and benefits  await those who locate their arts and entertainment enterprise within the art and entertainment district. Those who just visit the district will find places to enjoy culinary arts, appreciate talented performances, and view and purchase one of a kind art.
