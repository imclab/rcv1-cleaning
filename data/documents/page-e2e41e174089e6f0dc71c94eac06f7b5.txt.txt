Cornett Overview | Details | Customer Reviews | Discussion  Overview The new edition of this best-selling text on arts-based teaching redefines literacy in light of the 21st century need for multiple literacies. "Creating Meaning through Literature and the Arts: An Integration Handbook for Teachers, 4th Edition" provides teachers with an Arts Integration Blueprint, comprised of 10 building blocks for arts-based instruction that includes collaborative unit planning, best arts-based teaching practices, and assessment for learning. The Five Seed Strategy chapters provide a compendium of activities to jumpstart the use of literature, art, music, drama, and dance as integral teaching tools for science, social studies, math, and literacy instruction.
