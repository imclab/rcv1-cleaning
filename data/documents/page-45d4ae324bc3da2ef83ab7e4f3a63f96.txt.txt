 Menu Home Contact Us Privacy Welcome to Junk Yard Dog's Bloodhound Service. JYD Bloodhound Service is geared toward helping you purchase the best parts at the best price. Our staff consists of experts in the automotive field. Some are experts in used auto parts, some in new OEM parts and some in aftermarket parts. When you fill out the part request form, your information will be sent to our staff only. This information will only be used to service your request. If you do not supply enough information for actual application to be determined, you should receive a call or email to determine the correct part. If we are able to determine the actual part number, we will respond with a price. You are not committed to a purchase until you make the decision. You may receive a quote for a used part, an OEM part, a rebuilt part or an aftermarket part. Rest assured our professional staff will make a dilegent effort to find you the best deal around.
