The television was first introduced in the country in the year 1999. Based in Blantyre, Malawi TV is the only television channel of Malawi. The senior producer of this TV station is Gladys Gama Gandali. English is the working language of this television station. Apart from providing information on various important issues, the programs broadcasted on TVM (Malawi TV) helps in spreading awareness among the people regarding detrimental effects of AIDS (AcquiredImmuno Deficiency Syndrome). The other programs underscores on various health related issues and gender bias. The Malawi television is also a source of entertainment to the Malawians.
