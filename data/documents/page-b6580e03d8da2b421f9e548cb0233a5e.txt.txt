FPMR 101-26.501 [60.8KB] requires DOD, federal executive agencies and other eligible users to use GSA as the mandatory source for purchasing vehicles. Benefits of purchasing vehicles through GSA: Purchases vehicles at a savings of over 26 percent from the manufacturer's invoice price; Professional engineering services including technical assistance and vehicle design services; Professional contracting teams; On-line ordering tool, AutoChoice ; Reasonable pricing; Wide variety of vehicle types available; and Program team dedicated to customer care.
