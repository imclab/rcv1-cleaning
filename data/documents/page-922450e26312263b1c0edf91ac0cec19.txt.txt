 Welcome to the WAE Group, Women in Arts and Entertainment. 
 If you are an artist, entertainer, or creative professional we welcome you to network with us, enjoy the good times, and build real friendships with other passionate, like-minded artists! 
 Whether you need motivation, coaching with me, business strategy or connections, this group of powerful, ambitious women will have it for you! 
 Develop your leadership skills, build your business, collaborate with others in true feminine synergy and support! 
 - Emily
