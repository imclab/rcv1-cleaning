The Lindsay Automotive Group has beenan important part of the Washington, DC metro area community fordecades. Beginning with Lindsay Cadillac of Alexandria opening in1949, three generations of Lindsay's have been involved in the dailyoperations of the dealership group that now include Lexus ofAlexandria , Lindsay Ford of Wheaton , Lindsay Chevrolet of Woodbridge , and Lindsay Volkswagen of Dulles . All our Lindsay Automotive Groupdealerships have an excellent selection of used and certified preowned cars . Used cars , preowned and certified preowned , import and domesticall at low prices with free Carfax reports.
