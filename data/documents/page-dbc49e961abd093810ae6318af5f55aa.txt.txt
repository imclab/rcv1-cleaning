 Wednesday, July 31,2013 Arts and Entertainment 
 Stars will shine during &rsquo;13 Night With Legends show 
 
 
 
 
 
 Stuart's Opera House this Saturday hosts the annual A Night With the Legends show.  This
 popular annual show, which begins at 7 p.m., features Athens and
