This past weekend, Dr. Travis Fox ofMindFox Productions took home a win in the Special Events Coverage category ofthe 2013 National Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences Emmy® Awards/Pacific Southwest Chapter. Thefootage and audio of “The Fab Four: The Ultimate Tribute,” for which Fox scoredthe Emmy, was recorded inside Pechanga Resort &amp; Casino’s theater on January6, 2012. The PBS special began airing in June2012 and has been seen by millions of viewers across the country. The show featuredthe Beatles tribute band, which has been noted as the best around due to theirprecise attention to detail and “note for note renditions of Beatles songs,”according to the band’s website. The television show was also nominated in thearea of Director Post Production. Fox and other winners in the PacificSouthwest regional Emmy Awards were presented with their winged, golden statuesJune 15 at an awards ceremony in Las Vegas. A Fab Four concert takes the audiencethrough the history of The Beatle’s journey, complete with an actor playing thepart of Ed Sullivan introducing the four lads from Liverpool on his show andshowcasing music from every era of the musicians’ careers. Haven’t seen the Fab Four yet? They planto return to Pechanga for a round of concerts, Jan. 10-11, 2014. Tickets are$15, $25 and $35 and go on sale in October 2013. For more information,  call toll free (877) 711-2946 or visit  www.Pechanga.com .
