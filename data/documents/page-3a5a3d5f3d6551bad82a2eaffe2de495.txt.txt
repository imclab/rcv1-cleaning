If you're spending precious dollars to modify your turbo-equipped ride, it makes perfect sense that you would want to do everything possible to maximize exhaust flow for increased turbo efficiency. A larger-than-stock Coupe GT downpipe frees up exhaust gases and provides impressive power increases and faster turbo spooling, helping to reduce turbo lag, slightly increase boost pressure, and reduce exhaust gas temperature. &raquo;DID YOU KNOW? Once you increase boost in your Coupe GT turbo system, it's a good time to consider replacing the restrictive factory downpipe. An aftermarket Coupe GT downpipe offers a larger diameter and smoother bends than the original, resulting in what is often a substantial horsepower increase.
