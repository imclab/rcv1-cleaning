 Diverse Ideas and Forms of Expression 
 Indiana University and  Bloomington  squeeze a city's worth of culture into a college town, bringing together a wealth of cultural resources and the perspectives and talents of students from all 50 states and more than 125 countries. 
 IU Bloomington's renowned music school, world-class performance spaces and museums, and outstanding programs in fine arts and theater and drama represent just a few of the opportunities for you to experience diverse ideas and forms of expression&#8212;and to create your own.

 Arts and Entertainment Events Calendar 
 113 Days of Art

 Asian Culture Center 
 Provides student resources, educational support, and community outreach to promote awareness and understanding of Asian cultures 
 First Nations Educational and Cultural Center 
 Provides educational opportunities that further awareness of Native American people 
 Indiana Memorial Union (IMU) 
 A hub of campus activity, featuring bowling, movies, concerts, lectures, adventure trips, creative classes, and a hotel and conference center 
 Latino Cultural Center (La Casa) 
 Offers community, academic, and service programs to promote awareness and understanding of Latino/Latina culture 
 Neal-Marshall Black Culture Center 
 Offers educational, cultural, and social programs that promote awareness about the Black experience
