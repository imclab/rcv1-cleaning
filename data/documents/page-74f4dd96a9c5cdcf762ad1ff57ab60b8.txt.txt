But the poor Monster is misunderstood and too scary looking to make friends and ends up causing terrible trouble. “Dracula” is the tale of a very nasty guy who has been ‘alive’ for centuries, although by all accounts, he is dead! Count Dracula lives by biting people on the neck and drinking their blood.
