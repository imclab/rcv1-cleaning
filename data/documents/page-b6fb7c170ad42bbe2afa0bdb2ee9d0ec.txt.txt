Admission is free, but activities will cost tickets, which will be sold four for $1. Families also can get one free ticket for each non-perishable food item brought. The food will be donated to the Kansas Food Bank. The event is a fundraiser for Camp Healing Hearts, a bereavement camp for kids who have lost a loved one. For more information, call 316-744-0310.
