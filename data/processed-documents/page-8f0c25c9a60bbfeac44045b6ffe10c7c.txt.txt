On Campus About this course The Diploma of Creative Arts (Film and Television) program offered by the JMC Academy through SEEK Learning offers a valuable qualification for future film and TV professionals. If youve ever wanted to produce quality TV, be the editing force behind good films or become the next Tarantino, this comprehensive course gives you the skills to start forging your creative film and TV career. Instructed by industry experts in state-of-the-art facilities, youll develop specialised film and TV technical skills in editing, sound recording and lighting, as well as receive training in the technical and production processes that are standard practice in the industry. Students also have the opportunity to articulate from this course into comprehensive degree programs. Eligibility Applicants must have completed year 12 or equivalent to enrol. Areas of study In this film and television course, the subjects you will study include: Film and television 1: industrial production practices Technical operations and logistics 1 Cinematography and lighting 1: techniques  practices Production project 1 Film and television 2: history and theory Editing 1 Sound recording and post-production Production project 2 Get more information Want more information about this course? Fill out the enquiry form to the right and a study consultant will contact you with the details you need. Financial assistance You may be eligible for FEE-HELP, Youth Allowance or Austudy/Abstudy. You may also be eligible to receive private financing through SEEK Learning. Student support JMC Academy offers a wide range of support options for its students. While accommodation is not available on campus, JMC Academys student services staff is able to assist prospective students in finding suitable accommodation options near the campus. Students also have access to welfare support and guidance from their campus manager. How will this course advance my career? The entertainment industry is screaming out for creative and qualified individuals with original ideas and the skills to deliver them. The Diploma of Creative Arts (Film and Television) offers you the chance to enter the film and TV industries with a solid foundation of skills that will see you eligible for a wide range of roles in television stations, advertising agencies or production companies. Having been trained using state-of-the-art recording studios, digital mixing and sound studios, post-production and editing suites and professional cameras and lighting, youll graduate from this course with the valuable hands-on training that employers are seeking. How else will I benefit from this diploma? Upon successful completion of this course you will receive a nationally recognised, accredited Diploma of Creative Arts (Film and Television) from the JMC Academy. Studying online/by correspondence means you can study where you want, at your own pace. Gain industry-relevant skills and a qualification thats highly valued. Course materials provided. Recognition of Prior Learning may be available for previous work experience, formal training or qualifications in this field. Your qualification articulates into university programs to help you advance in your education levels. Receive training in professional studios with state-of-the-art equipment.
