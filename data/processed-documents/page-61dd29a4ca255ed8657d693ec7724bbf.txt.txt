  Undercover Upholstery is a complete total auto and marine custom upholstery and convertible top shop. We offer automotive interior restoration and custom interior work. We specialize in automotive convertible tops and custom seat reupholstering, rebuilding seat frames, panels, marine canvas fabrication and repair for any size boat. We can also customize any race seat to your personal specifications. Undercover Upholstery offers mail order services where you can ship to us your motorcycle car or boat seat covers and if needed the whole seat. We will customize it to your specifications or ours. It would be shipped back to you ready for installation. Contact us for more information.
