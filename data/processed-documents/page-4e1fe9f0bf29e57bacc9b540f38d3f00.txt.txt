Gallery BMW Gran Coupe Concept unveiled at Beijing Auto Show Tweet Tweet Beijing Auto Show marked the presentation of the BMW Gran Coupe, a four-door coupe that will rival models like Porsche Panamera, Mercedes-Benz CLS and Audi A7. BMW Gran Coupe Concept at Beijing Auto Show Germans from BMW revealed a concept that prefigures the successor of 5 Series at the Beijing Auto Show. The model has a length of five meters and is a four-door coupe that combines the advantages of a GT with the one of a sports model. The ceiling of this concept is 100 mm lower than the current BMW 7 Series F01 and the design was done as a development of a conventional sedan in three volumes. BMW Gran Coupe Concept at Beijing Auto Show BMW Gran Coupe will rival models such as Porsche Panamera, Audi A7 and next generation Mercedes-Benz CLS. Also, people at BMW will keep their design values that have established, this model following to present a number of trends that the future models of the manufacturer in Munich will follow.
