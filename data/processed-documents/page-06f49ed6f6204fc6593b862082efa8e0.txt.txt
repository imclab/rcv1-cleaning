Per page: 20 40 60  1 2 3 Convertible Car Seats Convertible car seats are a necessity for any growing family. These seats are used in a forward-facing or backward-facing position depending on the age, weight and height of your child. It is preferable that you place your child in the backseat of a vehicle with a harness system. Most convertible car seats have a weight limit that starts at 22 pounds and goes to 80 pounds. Most convertible car seats feature a five-point harness system, which is the safest system for your child. Some seats offer patented technology and features that guarantee the highest levels of safety for your little one. Depending on your price point, many seats also feature head and neck rests as well as extra cushioning in the seat; you can even find models with cup holders attached to the sides. Convertible toddler car seats come in a variety of colors and patterns to match the design of your stroller system.
