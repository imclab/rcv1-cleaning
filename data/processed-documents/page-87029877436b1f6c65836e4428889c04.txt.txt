Regular cleaning and protection with quality convertible top care products will maintain the appearance, color, and water repellency of convertible tops over the long haul. We carry convertible top care products by industry leaders RaggTopp and 303. Choose from products for vinyl or fabric convertible tops. To see if your convertible top is fabric or vinyl, wet the top with a few drops of water. Rub the water in with your fingertip. If the top eventually absorbs the water, it's fabric. If it does not soak in, the top is vinyl.
