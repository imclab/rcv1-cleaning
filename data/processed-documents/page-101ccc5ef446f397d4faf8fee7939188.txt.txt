Celebrity gossip, music, movies, TV shows and more. Taylor Swift Fans Hate Tank  Taylor Swift fans won't take anyone shaming their Queen! The Bad Kids Clothing tank features 10 of Taylor's exes Taylor Swift fans, known as "Swifties", have taken to message boards and writing strongly worded letters of complaint to a clothing company that is printing a tank top with all the last names of Taylor's alleged exes (we say alleged since many of them are uncomfirmed romances.) Online retailer Bad Kids Clothing was inspired to make the tank tops when they created one custom version for a friend and posted it to instagram, and were floored by the popularity of it. However the Swifties were swift in their response, feeling that the shirt was not only unfair to Taylor, but untrue!
