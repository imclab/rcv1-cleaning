The G Coupe is Infiniti’s near-luxury sport coupe that debuted in 2002 as a 2003 model. For 2007 an all-new second-generation G sedan arrived with a restyled look, enhanced platform, redesigned suspension and upgraded engine. The all-new second-generation G37 coupe followed as a 2008 model with a more powerful engine, redesigned look and refined interior. In 2011, the Infiniti IPL Coupe debuted as the first model of the Infiniti Performance Line, which is designed to add a new level of performance for Infiniti models.
