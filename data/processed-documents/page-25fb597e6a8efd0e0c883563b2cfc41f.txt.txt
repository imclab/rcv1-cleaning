The three television media of Chad are: EURONEWS, Chad: The head office of EURONEWS is in N' Djamena in Chad. CANAL PLUS HORIZON, Chad: It is classed as Television media. The head office of CANAL PLUS HORIZON is in N'Djamena, Chad. TV5 Chad: TV5 is also classed as Television media based in N' Djamena, Chad. In Chad on line television channels are available for the entertainment of viewers in the country. In 1997 there was 1 television broadcast station. The total numbers of televisions in the country was 10,000 by the year 1997.
