Furia Concept Toyota Cubs Camry Volkswagen VWvortex SEMA Super Beetle Volvo S60 Polestar Hyundai Genesis Coupe  2013 HYUNDAI GENESIS COUPE:Significantly redesigned for 2013, the Hyundai Genesis Coupe was one of the exciting new vehicles displayed at last year's Chicago Auto Show (CAS). Returning for a ten-day engagement during the 2013 CAS, the Genesis Coupe is available in 2.0T, 2.0T R-Spec, 2.0T Premium, 3.8 R-Spec, 3.8 Grand Touring and 3.8 Track trim packages. Both four-cylinder and V-6 engines benefit from significant technology enhancements, with the 348 hp 3.8-liter V-6 engine receiving sophisticated direct-injection technology and the 2.0-liter turbocharged 271 hp engine upgrading to a more precise twin-scroll turbocharger and larger intercooler. Delivering all that performance to the rear wheels is the six-speed manual gearbox or new automatic transmission with eight-speeds and paddle-shift manual-shifting capability. Governed top speed for the Genesis Coupe is a respectable 145 miles per hour. New styling includes a "Z"-shaped bodyside character line, aggressively styled front fascia, grille, headlights, fog lights and hood with heat extractor cues. Hyundai's proprietary telematics platform, Blue Link, is now available, providing Genesis Coupe owners with a variety of infotainment services to meet their individual connectivity needs. Fresh styling cues include a new front fascia, grille, headlights, fog lights, hood designs, LED-enhanced taillights plus unique 18- and 19-inch rims.
