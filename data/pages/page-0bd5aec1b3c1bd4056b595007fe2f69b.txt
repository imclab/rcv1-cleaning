<p>This <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Category" title="Wikipedia:Category" class="mw-redirect">category</a> contains articles relating to essential and/or typical automotive parts and includes some articles that concern parts that are not specifically or only found on automobiles but also on other vehicles. Multi-part technologies are addressed in the parent <a href="/wiki/Category:Automotive_technologies" title="Category:Automotive technologies">Category:Automotive technologies</a>. Non-essential, optional parts are the domain of <a href="/wiki/Category:Automotive_accessories" title="Category:Automotive accessories">Category:Automotive accessories</a>, while retailers and suppliers of essential and non-essential parts are found in <a href="/wiki/Category:Auto_parts_suppliers" title="Category:Auto parts suppliers">Category:Auto parts suppliers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Category:Automotive_part_retailers" title="Category:Automotive part retailers">Category:Automotive part retailers</a>, respectively.</p>
