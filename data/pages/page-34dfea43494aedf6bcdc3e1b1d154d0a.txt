              <h3 align="left"><a name="e"></a>E! Terrell Entertainment Series 
              </h3>
              <p>Introduced by the Terrell Alliance for Education and the Arts 
                (TAEA) in 2003, the E! Terrell Series brings renowned artists 
                to the TISD Performing Arts Center stage. Performances include 
                matinees for Terrell students, with age-appropriate lessons about 
                the culture and heritage of the entertainers. Recent concerts 
                have presented the Glenn Miller Orchestra, Chinese Golden Dragon 
                Acroats, Riders in the Sky, and the legendary St. Petersburg Ballet.</p>
              <p>Season ticket holders enjoy free admission to concerts and performances 
                in many other Metroplex and East Texas communities. </p>
              <p><a href="http://www.eterrell.org/" target="_blank">E! Terrell 
                Entertainment Series</a><br clear=all>
              </p>
              <p align="center"><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></p>
              <h3><a name="artsnight"></a>Night of Fine Arts</h3>
              <p>Terrell ISD's Annual Night of Fine Arts is a celebration of arts 
                education in our public schools. Guests enjoy musical offerings, 
                dance, oratory, and theatrical performances presented by talented 
                TISD students.</p>
              <p> Students' visual artwork is on display in the Performing Arts 
                Center during the celebration.</p>
              <p align="center"><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></p>
              <h3><img src="photos/arts2.jpg" alt="Lake Country Jubilee concert" width="275" height="120" hspace="8" vspace="8" align="left"><a name="lake"></a>Lake 
                Country Jubilee Gospel Concerts</h3>
              <p>Lake Country Jubilee features the greatest groups in Gospel music 
                in concert at Terrell ISD's Performing Arts Theater. </p>
              <p>Check the <a href="http://www.lakecountryjubilee.com" target="_blank">Lake 
                Country Jubilee website</a> for featured artists. </p>
              <p>Listen to Lake Country Jubilee's great gospel music <a href="http://www.lakecountryjubilee.com" target="_blank">online</a> 
                24 hours a day, 7 days a week.</p>
              <p align="center"><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></p>
              <h3><a name="net"></a>North East Texas Fine Arts Association (NETFAA) 
              </h3>
              <p><a href="http://www.clubnetfaa.com" target="_blank">NETFAA</a> 
                promotes interest in Fine Arts with regional arts programs. </p>
              <p>Monthly lectures and demonstrations feature some of Texas&#8217; 
                most celebrated artists. The organization supports local artists 
                with opportunities to market their work in local forums and sponsors 
                arts programs for children. </p>
              <p>Members offer painting lessons for children. &quot;Paint Outs&quot; 
                allow artists to gather in a scenic location, enjoy a sack lunch 
                picnic, and share techniques.</p>
              <p>NETFAA monthly meetings are open to all who are interested in 
                expanding their artistic experience. </p>
              <blockquote> 
                <p>NETFAA<br>
                  818 Griffith Ave. , Terrell, TX 75160<br>
                  972-524-6911<br>
                  Meetings: 7 p.m., 2nd Thursday of the month at the Terrell Heritage 
                  Museum, 207 North Frances Street<br>
                  Contact: Mary Jo Woodruff, President, 972-427-6511</p>
              </blockquote>
              <h3 align="center"><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></h3>
              <h3>                <a name="perf"></a>Performing 
                Arts Center</h3>
              <p><img src="photos/arts5.jpg" alt="TISD Performing Arts Center" width="290" height="113" hspace="8" vspace="8" align="right">The 
                TISD Performing Arts Center provides a venue for both school-related 
                and community events. Musical performances, the E! Terrell Entertainment 
                Series, and Lake Country Jubilee concerts fill the stage with 
                entertainment.</p>
              <blockquote> 
                <p>TISD Performing Arts Center<br>
                  400 Poetry Rd., Terrell, TX 75160</p>
              </blockquote>
              <h3 align="center"><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></h3>
              <h3>                <a name="lib"></a>Hulsey Public Library</h3>
              <p><img src="photos/library.jpg" alt="A patron browses titles at Hulsey Public Library" width="185" height="278" hspace="8" vspace="8" align="left">With 
                more than 60,000 volumes and a variety of programs, Husley Public 
                Library offers resources, education and entertainment. </p>
              <p>Little Listeners Pre-school story time introduces youngsters 
                to the wonders of reading. Terrific Tuesdays, Summer Reading Club, 
                Blue Crew, and TeenZone Reading Club offer activities for kids 
                of all ages. </p>
              <p>Literacy programs, exhibits, Readers&#8217; Advisory Services, 
                and community accesss computers appeal to adults.</p>
              <p>Experienced genealogists in the History and Genealogy Department 
                assist patrons in climbing their family trees. The department 
                has also produced documentaries about local history and video 
                interviews of community leaders.</p>
              <p>Historical and artistic exhibits and the annual craft and hobby 
                fair offer more reasons to visit the library.</p>
              <blockquote> 
                <p><a href="http://www.cityofterrell.org/library.htm" target="_blank">Hulsey 
                  Public Library</a><br>
                  301 N. Rockwall<br>
                  Terrell, TX 75160<br>
                  972-551-6663</p>
                <p><img src="graphics/divider2.gif" width="400" height="24"></p>
              </blockquote>
              <h3><a name="movie"></a>Nova Cinema</h3>
              <p>Nova Cinema at the Tanger Center shows first run movies in 5 
                theaters. The theater offers a Summer Kids Series and is available 
                for birthday parties and special/group showings.</p>
              <blockquote>
                <p><a href="http://www.novacinemas.com/" target="_blank">Nova 
                  Cinema</a><br>
                  301 Tanger Dr., Suite 206<br>
                  Terrell, TX 75160<br>
                  972-563-7779<br>
                  movie line: 972-563-8606<br>
                </p>
              </blockquote>
