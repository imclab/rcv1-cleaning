		<h1>Mission</h1>
			<p style="margin-top: 0px;">The Department of Fine Arts educates students in a variety of fine arts fields, 
			as well as preparing students to teach art. Our NASAD accredited programs offer a variety of degree options 
			for students interested in becoming productive artists and craft persons as well as art teachers and art historians. 
			Classes are held in well-equipped studios in small groups with individualized attention. Our students form a diverse community that centers around learning the concepts, skills and techniques needed for successful careers in the arts.</p>
