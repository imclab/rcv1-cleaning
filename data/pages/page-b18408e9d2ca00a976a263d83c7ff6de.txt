      <h2><a name="arts" id="arts"></a>Arts, films, and music</h2>
          <p><a href="http://events.berkeley.edu/">Events calendar</a>:
            Comprehensive, searchable calendar of campus events, organized
            by date and type.            </p>
          <p><a href="http://www.calperfs.berkeley.edu/">Cal Performances</a>:
            Showcase for performing artists and cultural luminaries &mdash;
            from cellist Yo-Yo Ma to jazz vocalist Dianne Reeves and economist
            Paul Krugman. </p>
          <p><a href="http://www.bampfa.berkeley.edu/">Berkeley
            Art Museum</a>: Permanent and visiting art exhibitions,
            featuring established and emerging artists from around the
            world.   </p>
          <p><a href="http://www.bampfa.berkeley.edu/filmseries/">Pacific
            Film Archive</a>: Daily screenings of cinema from every film-producing
            nation; more than 600 distinct
            programs a year.</p>
          <p><a href="http://music.berkeley.edu/">Department of Music</a>: Excellent and affordable performances by student, faculty, and guest musicians on the Hertz Hall stage feature many types of music from a variety of cultures and time periods: University Symphony Orchestra and Choruses, Gamelan, Baroque, African Drumming &amp; Dance, Gospel, Band, Jazz &amp; Improvised, and New Music ensembles. Many performances are free, including the popular Wednesday noon concerts.</p>
          <p><a href="http://tdps.berkeley.edu/">Department
            of Theater, Dance &amp; Performance Studies</a>:  Student
            theater and dance productions, events, and master
            classes, and a &quot;Making Theater&quot; series.</p>
          <p><a href="http://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~superb/">SUPERB Productions</a>:
            Rock-band concerts, first-run films, comedy nights, and other
            entertainment organized by student organization. </p>
          <p><a href="http://facilities.calperfs.berkeley.edu/greek/">The
              Greek Theatre</a>: The traditional venue for Commencement,
              the Bonfire Rally, and other campus events &mdash; and a great
              place to hear <a href="http://www.apeconcerts.com/venue_greekTheatre.cfm">live
              music</a>. </p>
          <p><a href="http://artstudio.berkeley.edu/">ASUC
            Art Studio</a>: Classes in art, crafts, photography, and
            writing, with ceramics, darkroom, and digital-lab facilities.</p>
          <p><a href="http://lunchpoems.berkeley.edu/">Lunch Poems</a>:
            Series of free monthly poetry readings, held in Doe Library's
            venerable Morrison Library. </p>
          <p><a href="http://music.berkeley.edu/performance">Music performance opportunities</a>: Rich performance opportunities include participating in the university orchestra; university, chamber, and gospel choruses; Indonesian gamelan; jazz &amp; improvised music; baroque; wind; and African ensembles. Other opportunities include performing in undergraduate composers club concerts, and auditioning for the opportunity to perform a concerto with the orchestra or a noon concert in Hertz Hall. <a href="http://music.berkeley.edu/performance/audition.php">Auditions</a>, if required, often take place during the first week of each semester.</p>
          <hr />
      <h2><a name="media" id="media"></a>Media</h2>
          <p><a href="/multimedia/index.php">Podcasts, Web video/audio</a></p>
          <p><a href="http://www.dailycal.org">Daily Cal</a>: Award-winning
            independent, student-run newspaper, established in 1871.</p>
          <p><a href="http://newscenter.berkeley.edu/">UC Berkeley NewsCenter</a>:
            Campus news portal, with comprehensive coverage of Berkeley
            news and events </p>
          <p><a href="/news/berkeleyan/">The Berkeleyan</a>:
            Weekly faculty and staff newspaper. </p>
          <p><a href="http://kalx.berkeley.edu/">KALX</a>: Student-run
            campus radio station, at 90.7 FM. </p>
          <hr />
          <h2><a name="dining" id="dining"></a>On-campus dining</h2>
              <p><a href="http://asucstudentaffairs.berkeley.edu/asinside.aspx?uid=361">The
                  Bear's Lair</a>: Food court and bar, located on the ground
                  floor of the Martin King, Jr. Student Union.</p>
              <p><a href="http://www.lib.berkeley.edu/AboutLibrary/fsmcafe.html">Free
                  Speech Movement Caf&eacute;</a>:  A casual caf&eacute; and
                  study spot, located at the entrance to Moffitt Library.
                  Wall exhibits pay tribute to the FSM; events
                  and exhibits at the caf&eacute; offer a forum on current-day
                  political and social issues. </p>
              <p><a href="http://caldining.berkeley.edu/campus_restaurants.html">Cal
                Dining restaurants</a>: <a href="http://caldining.berkeley.edu/golden_bear.html">The Golden Bear</a>, <a href="http://caldining.berkeley.edu/ramonas.html">Ramona's Cafe</a>, <a href="http://caldining.berkeley.edu/pat_browns.html">Pat Brown's Grill</a> and <a href="http://caldining.berkeley.edu/terrace_cafe.html">The Terrace Cafe</a></p>
