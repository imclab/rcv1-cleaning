<!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/27/george-clooney-and-sandra-bullock-enjoy-boat-ride-in-venice/george-clooney-and-sandra-bullock-in-venice/" rel="attachment wp-att-221097"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221097" alt="George Clooney and Sandra Bullock in Venice" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/George-Clooney-and-Sandra-Bullock-in-Venice.jpg" width="500" height="463" /></a></p>
<p>Oscar-winning actress <strong>Sandra Bullock</strong> and her young son Louis enjoyed a water taxi ride in Venice Tuesday, as did her actor pal <strong>George Clooney.</strong></p>
<p><strong></strong>He was accompanied by frequent sidekick Rande Gerber, the husband of model-actress Cindy Crawford.</p>
<p>A-lister co-stars Sandra and George <a title="george venice" href="http://www.justjared.com/2013/08/27/sandra-bullock-george-clooney-venice-for-gravity-film-festival-opening/" target="_blank">are in Italy</a> for the premiere of their film &#8216;Gravity&#8217; at the 2013 Venice Film Festival.</p>
<p>Billed as a techno-thriller, the movie features Bullock and Clooney as a medical engineer and a veteran astronaut stranded in orbit during a Space Shuttle mission.</p>
<p>Sandra won an Academy Award for &#8216;The Blind Side&#8217; in 2010.  Her marriage to former &#8216;Monster Garage&#8217; host Jesse James disintegrated shortly thereafter, leaving the actress to raise her adopted New Orleans baby Louis Bardo as a single parent.  He turned 3 in January.</p>

<!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/27/adam-lambert-and-rolling-stone-defend-miley-cyrus/adam-lambert-gets-miley-cyrus/" rel="attachment wp-att-221080"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221080" alt="Adam Lambert gets Miley Cyrus" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Adam-Lambert-gets-Miley-Cyrus.jpg" width="528" height="322" /></a></p>
<p>While nearly everyone on the planet has been bashing <strong>Miley Cyrus</strong> for her provocative VMA performance Sunday night,  there are many coming forward to sing her praises.</p>
<p><strong>Adam Lambert</strong> <a title="defend" href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/idol-worship/adam-lambert-defends-miley-cyrus-614690?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+thr%2Fcolumns+%28The+Hollywood+Reporter+-+Columns%29" target="_blank">hit Twitter</a> in support of the &#8216;We Can&#8217;t Stop&#8217; singer, &#8220;Listen, if it wasn&#8217;t your cup of tea&#8211;all good but why is everyone spazzing? Hey, she&#8217;s doing something right.  We&#8217;re all talking.  She was having fun-cut her some slack.&#8221;</p>
<p>Lambert made waves of his own by kissing a male bassist on stage and grabbing the crotch of another during the 2009 American Music Awards.  ABC banned the entertainer from appearing on their network for several years &#8211; eventually lifting the ban in 2011.</p>
<p>Another advocate speaking out on behalf of the former Disney star is <em>Rolling Stone</em> magazine.  Their kind words so impressed Cyrus that she tweeted a couple of quotes:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/27/adam-lambert-and-rolling-stone-defend-miley-cyrus/the-world-according-to-rolling-stone/" rel="attachment wp-att-221081"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221081" alt="The world according to Rolling Stone..." src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/The-world-according-to-Rolling-Stone....jpg" width="515" height="212" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Billboard executive Bill Werde totally &#8220;got&#8221; the Miley-Robin act, &#8220;Guys, Miley Cyrus is an all-star hot mess for a hot mess pop moment.  She doesn&#8217;t care what you think and that&#8217;s her brand and she just delivered.&#8221;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Viacom Media Network&#8217;s president Van Toffler, who owns MTV, had this to say, &#8220;Performers turn it up like athletes on game day.  Did Miley do every move in rehearsal that she did the night of the show? No.  We knew her performance was going to be provocative the way her video is.  But on live TV, the performers turn it up a couple of notches.&#8221;  Ya think?</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">A photo of a decidedly shocked Will Smith family received some major traction on Twitter yesterday, with most sources reporting the fam was reacting to the Miley Cyrus bump and grind performance.  We were deeply disappointed to discover that their collective astonishment was captured on audience cam when Lady Gaga revealed her shell bra and thong.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/27/adam-lambert-and-rolling-stone-defend-miley-cyrus/will-smith-family-is-horrified/" rel="attachment wp-att-221086"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221086" alt="Will Smith family is horrified" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Will-Smith-family-is-horrified.jpg" width="500" height="260" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">In any case, we loved the image and felt compelled to add it to our very last bit of discourse on the 2013 VMAs.  Carry on&#8230;</p>

<!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/27/kate-gosselin-files-federal-lawsuit-against-jon/jon-hacked-my-stuff-claims-kate/" rel="attachment wp-att-221068"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221068" alt="Jon hacked my stuff, claims Kate" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Jon-hacked-my-stuff-claims-Kate.jpg" width="500" height="463" /></a></p>
<p>After a period of civility, former reality star <strong>Kate Gosselin</strong> has put the gloves back on and will be dragging her ex-husband into a Philidelphia federal courtroom to face charges of hacking her computer and phone records, wiretapping and invasion of privacy.</p>
<p><strong>Jon Gosselin</strong>, a former IT guy, <a title="stole" href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/people/2013/08/26/kate-gosselin-files-federal-lawsuit-against-ex-husband-jon/2701727/" target="_blank">reportedly stole</a> a hard drive from Kate&#8217;s residence and used other illegal means to supply information to a tabloid reporter who was writing a tell-all.</p>
<p>Robert Hoffman launched  &#8216;Kate Gosselin: How She Fooled the World&#8217; with Amazon and several other distributors last year but the project was quickly shut down after Kate&#8217;s legal team got a court injunction claiming the controversial details within the book was obtained illegally.</p>
<p>Not long ago Jon&#8217;s ex-wife uncovered enough damning evidence to pin the hacking rap on him &#8212; and so she has.</p>
<p>Kate Gosselin is seeking compensatory and punitive damages, along with court fees and actual damages.  This is almost laughable since her ex recently described his living situation as a small cabin in a farm community and noted that their parental relationship is &#8220;transportation and logistics.&#8221;</p>
<p>Two weeks ago PEOPLE wrote a fluff piece titled &#8216;Kate Gosselin: Her Life After Reality TV.&#8217;  In the article she acknowledged that the family is living very carefully these days and added, &#8220;I feel like I&#8217;ve lived two lifetimes already.&#8221;</p>

<!-- Start Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><!-- End Shareaholic LikeButtonSetTop Automatic --><p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/26/media-watchdog-group-blasts-mtv-for-miley-cyrus-sex-demo/miley-channels-jim-carreys-vera-de-milo/" rel="attachment wp-att-221029"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221029" alt="Miley channels Jim Carrey's Vera De Milo" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Miley-channels-Jim-Carreys-Vera-De-Milo.jpg" width="438" height="550" /></a></p>
<p>On the day after the 2013 MTV Video Music Awards, social media emphatically suggests that <strong>Miley Cyrus</strong> didn&#8217;t just blur the line Sunday night&#8211;she blatantly crossed it with her controversial performance.</p>
<p>Watchdog group, the Parents Television Council, <a title="mtv blasted" href="http://music.yahoo.com/blogs/stop-the-presses/miley-cyrus-outrageous-vma-performance-shocks-everyone-165836505.html" target="_blank">blasted MTV</a> Monday for &#8220;marketing sexually charged messages to young children using former child stars.&#8221;</p>
<p>PTC&#8217;s Advisory Board Member Paul Porter contends, &#8220;The Miley Cyrus-Robin Thicke &#8216;Blurred Lines&#8217; performance simply substituted talent with sex.  Heads should roll at MTV.&#8221;</p>
<p>Twitter went crazy the moment the pair ended their public grinding, a fact that wasn&#8217;t lost on the 20-year-old who caused the firestorm.  She seemed particularly pleased when she tweeted:</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.bittenandbound.com/2013/08/26/media-watchdog-group-blasts-mtv-for-miley-cyrus-sex-demo/miley-tweet/" rel="attachment wp-att-221030"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-221030" alt="Miley tweet" src="http://www.bittenandbound.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Miley-tweet.jpg" width="493" height="105" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The former Disney star&#8217;s parents are also in denial.  <strong>Tish Cyrus</strong> jumped to her feet after the performance and applauded like a proud mom.  <strong>Billy Ray Cyrus</strong> tweeted, &#8220;Thanking God for so many blessings tonight.  Continue to pray for world peace. More love &#8230; less hate.&#8221;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Neither Robin Thicke nor his wife Paula Patton have updated their Twitter pages.  We can&#8217;t wait to hear what they have to say on the subject.  His racy &#8216;Blurred Lines&#8217; music video raised eyebrows in July because of the sexual content.  When asked about it, he told reporters, &#8220;I can&#8217;t even dignify that with a response.&#8221;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">One of the common themes we&#8217;ve seen today, suggests that Cyrus was channeling Jim Carrey&#8217;s &#8216;In Living Color&#8217; character Vera De Milo.  We took the opportunity to add his mug as an inset up top for your inspection.</p>
