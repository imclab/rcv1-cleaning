    <div id="content"> <span class="blue_20">Auto Lift Parts For Every Lift...</span><br />
      <span class="blue_15">Repair Parts For Older Lifts, Production Parts for New Lifts</span><br />
      <br />
        If you own an auto lift or you manufacture them, SVI International should be on your speed dial. Because sooner or later, you'll need an automotive lift part for your auto, truck or bus lift.<br />
<br />
        SVI is your answer with over <a href="products/auto_lift_parts/">20,000 auto lift parts</a> in stock for virtually every brand and model ever made &ndash; even the obsolete ones.<br />
<br />
        <span class="blue_15">Auto Lift Parts for Old &amp; Obsolete Lifts</span><br />
<br />
        Some lifts last longer than the companies that made them. This is where SVI shines. Bring us a challenge... a broken or worn out piece from a long-out-of-production lift... and we'll set you up with a replacement auto lift part as good as new.<br />
<br />
        Even if your lift was manufactured 60 years ago, chances are good we'll have the right car lift part in stock or we will make it readily available.<br />
<br />
        Why go to the huge expense of buying a whole new lift when your old lift can be brought back to life with some TLC and some SVI.<br />
<br />
        <span class="blue_15">Automotive Lift Parts for  Current / New Lifts</span><br />
<br />
        SVI doesn't just <em>sell</em> vehicle lift parts; we've been <em>making</em> them since 1977 &ndash; for old lifts and for brand new ones. Distributors and lift owners come to us for custom made parts and many lift manufacturers even come to us for <a href="products/auto_lift_parts/">auto lift parts</a> for their production runs.<br />
<br />
        Three decades in business have taught us a thing or two about car and truck lift parts. Like how to make them just as good as brand name parts but at a much lower price. Our customers like that about us.<br />
<br />
