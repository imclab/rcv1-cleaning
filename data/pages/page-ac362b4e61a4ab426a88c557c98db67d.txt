Every other weekend there will be a car show and in between there will be a few car meets. Although everyone is familiar with the cars, it&#8217;s usually the type of car show and organization behind it that attracts the crowd. So far this year, SPOCOM 2011 and Motion Auto Show 2011 were probably the best all around car shows. Shows like these not only included cars, but also had models, lifestyle and just the general SOCAL car culture all under a convention center roof. It&#8217;s car shows like these that you don&#8217;t want to miss and what makes the socal car scene what it is.
