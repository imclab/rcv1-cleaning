<div style="padding: 15px 19px 0 20px" class="a2"> <b class="a5">Buying and selling cars on eBay</b><br />
<div style="width: 318px; float: left">
<div style="padding: 2px 0 5px 0" class="v1">By Jim Kneiszel (June 17, 2005)<br />
From <a href="http://dw.com.com/redir?oid=4520-10895_7-6233697-1&ontid=10895&siteid=7&edid=3&lop=txt&destcat=edmunds&destUrl=http%3A%2F%2Fwww%2Eedmunds%2Ecom%2F%3Ftid%3Dcnet%2E1%2E1%2E1%2E1%2E2%2E*">Edmunds.com</a></div>
<b>You can save a lot of money buying a car from an eBay auction,</b> but there are pitfalls as well. You often won't have a chance to drive the car before bidding on it, and you may have to travel in order to pick it up. How do you keep from buying a lemon? And what is it like to sell cars on eBay? This article recounts one buyer's story and recounts the practices of a good car seller. We also include a page of tips for buying cars from eBay auctions.<br />
