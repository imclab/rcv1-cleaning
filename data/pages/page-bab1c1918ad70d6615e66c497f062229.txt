					<h1 class="entry-title">Top Five Best Convertible Car Seat Reviews 2013</h1>
					<p class="headline_meta">by <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn">Katie Sanders</span></span> on <abbr class="published" title="2012-11-15">November 15, 2012</abbr></p>
				</div>
				<div class="format_text entry-content">
<a id="dd_start"></a><p>While traveling with your child, it&#8217;s important to have them safely and securely fastened in your vehicle with the proper car seat. Many parents are choosing to purchase a convertible car seat since they adapt and convert as your baby grows older. They save you money since you won&#8217;t have to buy a new car seat once your baby becomes a toddler.</p>
<p>Knowing which one is right for you depends solely on what you&#8217;re looking for in a car seat. Do you want one that is comfortable? Is price an important factor to you? Are you looking for a specific brand or model? If you&#8217;re completely lost or just trying to see which one&#8217;s are out there &#8211; below I have outlined the top 5 best rated and safest convertible car seats of 2013.</p>
<h3>Diono RadianRXT Convertible Car Seat</h3>
<p><a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/10aiGv1"><img alt="Diono RadianRXT Convertible Car Seat" class="thumb-r" src="http://best2013baby.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Diono-RadianRXT-Convertible-Car-Seat.jpg" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li>As my absolute favorite choice for 2013, the <a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/10aiGv1">Diono RadianRXT Convertible Car Seat</a> is probably the highest quality choice currently available with plenty of features that are sure to impress any mom or dad.</li>
<li>Featuring an adjustable head support system with 12 different positions, expandable sides, infant body support cushions, and a longer seat bottom for maximum comfort and leg support &#8211; it&#8217;s not surprising that it&#8217;s rated 4.5 out of 5 stars on Amazon.com by nearly 300 consumers.</li>
<li>Rear Facing: 5-45 lbs. Forward Facing: 20-80 lbs. Booster Mode: up to 120 lbs. or 57 inches tall.</li>
<li>Built with a steel alloy frame and energy absorbing EPS foam to protect your child from injury.</li>
<li>Memory foam padding gives a &#8216;custom-like&#8217; feel that children enjoy &#8211; making those long road trips more bearable.</li>
<li>Backed by a 1 year limited warranty.</li>
<li>Available in 8 different color options.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Britax Marathon 70-G3 Convertible Car Seat</h3>
<p><a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/1058kLE"><img src="http://best2013baby.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Britax-Marathon-70-G3-Convertible-Car-Seat.jpg" alt="britax marathon 70 g3" class="thumb-r" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li>Another great option is the <a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/1058kLE">Britax Marathon 70-G3</a> which utilizes several safety features designed to keep children up to 70 lbs. as safe as possible in your vehicle.</li>
<li>The base of the seat is made using &#8216;Safecell Technology&#8217; that compresses in the event of a crash. This lowers the center of gravity and literally counteracts the forward rotation of the seat that can normally cause the unit to push forward into the front seat.</li>
<li>Built with steel bars for maximum structural integrity that reduces forward flexing motion during impact.</li>
<li>5 point safety harness that will never tangles and provides plenty of cushion for your child. Using the specially designed &#8216;HUGS&#8217; (Harness Ultra Guard System), the Britax Marathon 70-G3 resists forward movement with high impacts.</li>
<li>Protected from side impacts with deep side walls that are lined with EPP energy absorbing foam to help distribute crash forces evenly.</li>
<li>Available in 5 different color options.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Recaro ProRIDE Convertible Car Seat</h3>
<p><a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/16RuW9a"><img alt="Recaro ProRIDE Convertible Car Seat" class="thumb-r" src="http://best2013baby.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Recaro-ProRIDE-Convertible-Car-Seat.jpg" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li>The <a rel="nofollow" target="_blank" href="http://amzn.to/16RuW9a">Recaro ProRIDE Convertible Car Seat</a> is a top rated choice that utilizes side impact protection to protect all 5 vulnerable areas of your child including their torso, pelvis, face, neck, and head.</li>
<li>Use as &#8216;Rear Facing&#8217; for children who weigh between 5 and 35 lbs. and are less than 37.5 inches tall. Use as &#8216;Forward Facing&#8217; for children who weigh between 20 and 70 lbs and are under 50 inches tall.</li>
<li>Very easy to install and adjust as needed. Thanks to the easy-adjust turn dial, parents can get a comfortable and effective fit without any hassle.</li>
<li>Integrated 5 point safety harness with ample padding on the shoulders to keep them cozy on long car rides.</li>
<li>EPS energy absorbing foam is used throughout the constructions to lessen the impact in the event of an accident.</li>
<li>Available in a wide range of 9 different color options to best suit your style.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Graco Nautilus 3-in-1 Car Seat</h3>
<p><a href="http://amzn.to/T2K5Pg" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img class="thumb-r" src="http://best2013baby.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Graco-Nautilus-3-in-1-Car-Seat1.jpg" alt="Graco Nautilus 3-in-1 Car Seat" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li>Features 3 different positions that face forward to allow the safest and most comfortable ride possible and to fit a wide variety of children of different ages.</li>
<li>The <a href="http://amzn.to/T2K5Pg" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Graco Nautilus 3-in-1 Car Seat</a> is available in 3 different color options. Garent, which is of a dark gray color and trimmed in red. Matrix, which has black, light gray, and red accents. Valerie, a perfect choice for girls since it is pink and gray.</li>
<li>Made from a special kind of energy absorbing foam known as EPS which is re-enforced with a steel frame. This helps keep your child safe during their ride.</li>
<li>Tested multiple times in crash scenarios to determine that it&#8217;s a safe product that meets or exceeds US safety standards.</li>
<li>Children are fastened in with a 5 point safety harness that converts to a belt or a backless booster depending on their size and age.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Safety 1st Alpha Omega Elite Convertible Car Seat</h3>
<p><a href="http://amzn.to/ZSpoGP" target="_blank" rel="nofollow"><img class="thumb-r" src="http://best2013baby.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Safety-1st-Alpha-Elite-Convertible-Car-Seat.jpg" alt="Safety 1st Alpha Omega Elite Convertible Car Seat" /></a></p>
<ul>
<li>3 in 1 features 3 different uses for the growing weight of your little one. Rear facing is ideal for 5-35 lbs. Forward facing is ideal for 22-40 lbs. Belt positioning seat is ideal for those from 40 to 100 lbs.</li>
<li>The <a href="http://amzn.to/ZSpoGP" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Safety 1st Alpha Omega Elite Convertible Car Seat</a> reclines to various different positions allowing for optimum comfort by selecting the right angle you desire.</li>
<li>Cup holder can be removed if you don&#8217;t have any use for it.</li>
<li>Built in arm rests pivot so they can be out of the way while not in use.</li>
<li>Head is supported with a large, plush pillow that can be removed if desired.</li>
</ul>
