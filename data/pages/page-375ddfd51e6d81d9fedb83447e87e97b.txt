	<div id="content-left">

				<p><img class="title" title="we-love-cars" src="http://www.rivian.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/we-love-cars.jpg" alt="We love cars" width="143" height="19" /></p>
<p>Rivian’s vehicles, people and culture were born from car enthusiast roots. Company founder RJ Scaringe is an avid car enthusiast and started Rivian with the goal of providing affordable yet highly desirable vehicles. Rivian was created to provide affordable vehicles without compromise.</p>
<p>To make this goal a reality, Rivian has built a <em>dream team</em> of engineers and designers who are among the best in their fields and are passionate about redefining vehicle segments. The team has played key roles in the development of many highly regarded vehicles.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.rivian.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/our-name.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-230" title="our-name" src="http://www.rivian.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/our-name.jpg" alt="" width="110" height="14" /></a></p>
<p>The origin of our name pays homage to the Indian River Lagoon, considered one of the most biologically diverse estuarine systems in the United States. Rivian blends the words Indian and River – taking the first three letters of River and the last three of Indian. The Indian River flows through the length of Brevard County, Florida, where Rivian is currently headquartered.</p>
<p>The continuous motion and the naturally dynamic nature of a river symbolize our flexibility and energy. A river has the ability to reshape its landscape. We are a company filled with vibrant energy working to reshape the way vehicles are developed and produced.</p>
