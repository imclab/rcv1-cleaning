I purchased this car in late 1965 in rough condition,<br>
while I was going to high school and working as a<br>
part time apprentice mechanic at a local garage.<br>
I attended several primitive rod runs with the car and started picking up<br>
friends and acquaintences with similar interests during high school and<br>
shortly after. A couple of these guys and I became fast friends, one,<br>
the late
<a href="../photo/garage/pate/picsp1.html">Jonny Pate</a>
, became my brother-in-law<br>when I married his sister Bonnie,<br>
another
<a href="../fcars/keith.html">Keith Meikle</a>
is still my neighbor today.
</td></tr></table><p>

<table border="5" cellpadding=5><tr>

</tr><tr><td align="center" colspan=2><b><font size=2>
When I was driving it to high school<br>it had a 49 Ford flathead V8 and<br>39
Ford floorshift trans and<br>40 Ford front and rear axles.<br>At the time it was
painted a light turqoise<br>with white trim and seat covers on the stock seat.<br>
This was my only set of wheels,<br>it was driven come hell or high water<br>as can
be seen in the photo above.<p>
</tr></table><p>
