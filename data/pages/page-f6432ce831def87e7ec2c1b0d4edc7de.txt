<p>This week&#8217;s art and literature quiz is all about famous characters from books. We will give you three characters from a piece of literature, old or newer. You need to give us the name of the book as the answer. Ready?</p>
<p><strong>1. Elizabeth Bennet, Charles Bingley and Fitzwilliam Darcy.</strong></p>
<p><em>Pride and Prejudice &#8211; Jane Austen</em></p>
<p><strong>2. Clarice Starling, Jack Crawford and Dr. Hannibal Lecter.</strong></p>
<p><em>The Silence of the Lambs &#8211; Thomas Harris</em></p>
<p><strong>3. Robert Langdon, Jacques Saunière</strong><strong> and Silas.</strong></p>
<p><em>The Da Vinci Code &#8211; Dan Brown</em></p>
<p><strong>4. Anne Shirley, Marilla Cuthbert and Gilbert Blythe.</strong></p>
<p><em>Anne of Green Gables &#8211; Lucy Maud Montgomery</em></p>
<p><strong>5. Wilbur, Charlotte and Templeton.</strong></p>
<p><em>Charlotte&#8217;s Web &#8211; E.B. White</em></p>
<p><strong>6. Atticus Finch, Arthur &#8216;Boo&#8217; Radley and Bob Ewell.</strong></p>
<p><em>To Kill a Mockingbird &#8211; Harper Lee</em></p>
<p><strong>7. Scarlett O&#8217;Hara, Rhett Butler and Ashley Wilkes.</strong></p>
<p><em>Gone With The Wind &#8211; Margaret Mitchell</em></p>
<p><strong>8. Winston Smith, Mr Charrington and Big Brother</strong></p>
<p><em>1984 &#8211; George Orwell</em></p>
<p><strong>9. Arthur Dent, Ford Prefect and Zaphod Beeblebrox.</strong></p>
<p><em>The Hitchhiker&#8217;s Guide to the Galaxy &#8211; Douglas Adams</em></p>
<p><strong>10. Tom Joad, Jim Casey and Rose of Sharon.</strong></p>
<p><em>The Grapes of Wrath &#8211; John Steinbeck</em></p>
