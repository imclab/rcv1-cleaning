				<div class="general_content"><h3>
	About this course</h3>
<p>
	The Diploma of Creative Arts (Film and Television) program offered by the JMC Academy through SEEK Learning offers a valuable qualification for future film and TV professionals.</p>
<p>
	If you&rsquo;ve ever wanted to produce quality TV, be the editing force behind good films or become the next Tarantino, this comprehensive course gives you the skills to start forging your creative film and TV career.</p>
<p>
	Instructed by industry experts in state-of-the-art facilities, you&rsquo;ll develop specialised film and TV technical skills in editing, sound recording and lighting, as well as receive training in the technical and production processes that are standard practice in the industry.</p>
<p>
	Students also have the opportunity to articulate from this course into comprehensive degree programs.</p>
<h3>
	Eligibility</h3>
<p>
	Applicants must have completed year 12 or equivalent to enrol.</p>
<h3>
	Areas of study</h3>
<p>
	In this film and television course, the subjects you will study include:</p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Film and television 1: industrial production practices</li>
	<li>
		Technical operations and logistics 1</li>
	<li>
		Cinematography and lighting 1: techniques &amp; practices</li>
	<li>
		Production project 1</li>
	<li>
		Film and television 2: history and theory</li>
	<li>
		Editing 1</li>
	<li>
		Sound recording and post-production</li>
	<li>
		Production project 2</li>
</ul>
<h3>
	Get more information</h3>
<p>
	Want more information about this course? Fill out the enquiry form to the right and a study consultant will contact you with the details you need.</p></div>

				<div class="general_content"><h3>
	How will this course advance my career?</h3>
<p>
	The entertainment industry is screaming out for creative and qualified individuals with original ideas and the skills to deliver them. The Diploma of Creative Arts (Film and Television) offers you the chance to enter the film and TV industries with a solid foundation of skills that will see you eligible for a wide range of roles in television stations, advertising agencies or production companies.</p>
<p>
	Having been trained using state-of-the-art recording studios, digital mixing and sound studios, post-production and editing suites and professional cameras and lighting, you&rsquo;ll graduate from this course with the valuable hands-on training that employers are seeking.</p>
<h3>
