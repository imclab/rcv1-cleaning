			<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong>48th Annual Downtown Bethlehem 2013 Fine Arts &amp; Craft Show: A Juried Exhibition</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><strong><span style="color: #008000;">Mother&#8217;s Day Weekend</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>Best of Show:</strong> &#8220;Paleozoic Evolution&#8221; &#8211; Booth 68 &#8211; Jocelyn Hoch and Forrest Schaffer</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>2nd in Show:</strong> &#8220;Spotted Maple&#8221; &#8211; Booth 64 &#8211; Kevin Hoining</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>3rd in Show:</strong> &#8221; All Knowing&#8221; Necklace &#8211; Booth 27 &#8211; Staci Smith</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>Purchase Prize:</strong> &#8220;Just Above the Crest&#8221; &#8211; Booth 70 &#8211; Ralph Hlavacek</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>Dick Blick Award</strong> &#8211;  Booth 58 &#8211; Diane Podolsky</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: medium; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; color: #008000;"><strong>Best Display</strong> &#8211; Booth 73 &#8211; Richie Kilinger</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="color: #000000; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;">Saturday, May 11th 10:00am &#8211; 5:00pm and<br />
Sunday, May 12th 11:00am &#8211; 5:00pm</span></p>
<p><a href="http://bfac-lv.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Faces-by-Abbott.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-673 alignnone" alt="Faces by Abbott" src="http://bfac-lv.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/Faces-by-Abbott-240x300.jpg" width="240" height="300" /></a></p>
<p><span style="color: #000099; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;">The piece is entitled &#8220;Faces.&#8221;</span><br />
<span style="color: #000099; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;"> Upcycled Art created by local artist Virginia Abbott and children visitors of the 2012 Mother&#8217;s Day Weekend Art Show in Downtown Bethlehem. </span><br />
<span style="color: #000099; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: medium;"> This piece will be raffled at the 2013 Show to benefit our children&#8217;s art program.</span></p>
