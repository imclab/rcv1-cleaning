<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://www.theartofchildrenspicturebooks.blogspot.com/2013/08/real-science-riddles-talivaldis-stubis.html'>Real Science Riddles, Talivaldis Stubis</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-7002227824652939373' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">Today's vintage children's book, <i>Real Science Riddles</i>, was illustrated by</span><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&nbsp;graphic designer and illustrator</span><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">&nbsp;<a href="http://healthnewsdigest.com/news/Disease_420/Misdiagnosed_Amyloidosis_-_Passing_of_Renowned_Artist_Highlights_Dangers_printer.shtml">Talivaldis Stubis</a>&nbsp;(5/22/26 - 11/08/09). In addition to illustrating almost two dozen children's <a href="http://www.google.com/search?q=talivaldis+stubis&amp;client=safari&amp;rls=en&amp;tbm=isch&amp;tbo=u&amp;source=univ&amp;sa=X&amp;ei=D-ALUu2tC5HlygH6q4GoBw&amp;ved=0CDYQsAQ&amp;biw=1674&amp;bih=843#bav=on.2,or.r_qf.&amp;fp=3892979dadefa13c&amp;q=talivaldis%20stubis&amp;rls=en&amp;tbm=isch">books</a>, Stubis designed hundreds of now famous&nbsp;<a href="http://www.google.com/search?q=talivaldis+stubis&amp;client=safari&amp;rls=en&amp;tbm=isch&amp;tbo=u&amp;source=univ&amp;sa=X&amp;ei=D-ALUu2tC5HlygH6q4GoBw&amp;ved=0CDYQsAQ&amp;biw=1674&amp;bih=843#bav=on.2,or.r_qf.&amp;fp=3892979dadefa13c&amp;q=talivaldis+stubis+poster&amp;rls=en&amp;sa=1&amp;tbm=isch">posters</a> for stage and screen. Stubis won numerous awards for his books, including his 1962 book, <i>A Pocketful of Seasons</i>, that was named one of New York Time's 100 Best Books of the Year.&nbsp;</span><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">Stubis's work was also very popular in Japan.</span></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: left;">
<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Verdana, sans-serif;">to children.</span></div>
