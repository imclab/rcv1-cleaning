			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/11/the-21st-century-muscle-car/" title="Permalink to The 21st Century Muscle Car" rel="bookmark">The 21st Century Muscle Car</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/11/the-21st-century-muscle-car/" title="7:12 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">November 8, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/author/kinl/" title="View all posts by KinL">KinL</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-518" title="Capture" src="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/Capture-300x198.png" alt="" width="300" height="198" /></p>
<p>When the Dodge Challenger got its rebirth back in 2008, many muscle car enthusiasts heralded the era as the 70&#8242;s era reborn. The roads were teeming with new Mustangs, new Camaros, and now the new Challengers, but did they stack up to their 70&#8242;s era grandpas?</p>
<p>With the automotive aftermarket scrambling to provide parts, muscle cars can easily look <em>Gone in Sixty Seconds</em> or just as easily, <em>Tron</em>. Companies like <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Retro-USA-Dodge-Challenger-s/415845.htm">Retro USA</a> make custom body kits and chrome to replicate the classic blue-collar working man cars of old, while <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/t-rex-billet-grilles-t-rex-mesh-grilles-s/280.htm">T-Rex grilles</a> pump up the bling factor. Appearance is not an issue, but what about the guts?</p>
<p>Yesteryear&#8217;s muscle cars were thirsty, pared-down machines with no bells or whistles. The new Challengers and Mustangs are anything but. With the decades of corporate shuffling, environmental concerns, and tech innovation, muscle cars today are more like powerful spaceships. They still drink heavily, but at around 27 mpg, the number is more a trade-off between economy and performance. We might get better mileage with a Civic, but a Mustang kicks like an American. Full of Mercedes chassis parts and other tech, these rides do not buck, they trot comfortably to every tune on your iPhone. More importantly, the price remains blue-collar, leaving more dough for, what else, aftermarket accessories.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/11/aftermarket-parts-and-insurance/" title="Permalink to Aftermarket Parts and Insurance" rel="bookmark">Aftermarket Parts and Insurance</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/11/aftermarket-parts-and-insurance/" title="4:03 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">November 2, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/author/kinl/" title="View all posts by KinL">KinL</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><img class="alignleft" src="http://www.uniqueautodepot.net/v/vspfiles/photos/3dc-691047-2.jpg" alt="" width="216" height="155" />Previously we covered the hows of choosing aftermarket automotive parts; unless you are a car enthusiast, chances are you will not be looking at the parts list closely. It may surprise you to know insurance companies advocate the use of <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/"> aftermarket car parts</a> quite heavily, sometimes to the detriment of the owners.</p>
<p>Depending on who you ask in the automotive market, some parts are as good as factory parts, some are worse, and some perform much better. A<a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Custom-Car-Truck-Billet-Mesh-Grilles-s/467.htm"> billet grille</a>, or any other cosmetic part, would probably increase the value of a vehicle, but any other aftermarket part reduces the resale value immediately. If the vehicle is being fixed, insurance companies may insist on using aftermarket parts to cut their bottom line.</p>
<p>Depending on the state, regulations for insurance companies may ban aftermarket auto parts, allow them only if the owner is informed, or allow them with no restrictions. If the car is planned for resale, an owner can often insist on original parts&#8211;BMW for BMW, Mercedes for Mercedes. Many drivers will decide to opt for less hassle and avoid this decision entirely, but do not fret, aftermarket parts are often just as safe as factory. If the car has been in an accident, often the resale value will be lower no matter what is done to it.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/10/iced-out-car-emblems/" title="Permalink to Iced Out Car Emblems" rel="bookmark">Iced Out Car Emblems</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/10/iced-out-car-emblems/" title="5:52 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">October 26, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/author/tristanl/" title="View all posts by tristanl">tristanl</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com" rel="attachment wp-att-505"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-505" title="iced out emblems" src="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/emblems-300x275.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="275" /></a>If your car or truck is not <em>murdered out</em> then it better be<a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/BMW-7-Series-Swarovski-Iced-Out-Emz-Front-and-Rear-p/bw6566.htm"> iced out</a>! It is true, if you are going to play the auto-customization game, you had best pick a camp. Are you with the flat black, no interior street racers or are you more of <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/BMW-7-Series-Swarovski-Iced-Out-Emz-Front-and-Rear-p/bw6566.htm">a laid back, blinged out kind of driver</a>? Even if you do not fit either of the above categories, aftermarket auto emblems can add a bit of style to your ride without going all the way with new rims or a flashy body kit.</p>
<p>For the uninitiated, iced-out means the item in question is covered in either crystals or diamonds, or both. For the sake of the emblems we sell here at Unique Auto, they are generally crystals. In some cases Swarovski crystals.</p>
<p>We have model-specific<a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/BMW-7-Series-Swarovski-Iced-Out-Emz-Front-and-Rear-p/bw6566.htm"> iced out emblems</a> for many popular models, including the BMW Seven series and the GMC Denali. We also have them for other GMC models. Those who do not own either car will likely find an iced out emblem that fits their needs in our general number and letter emblems.</p>
<p>Be careful when applying emblems, as removal of the old one and installation of new badges can be tricky. It is possible to damage paint while doing this type of work. Hire a professional if you do not feel comfortable.</p>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/10/grill-warz/" title="3:10 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">October 12, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/author/tristanl/" title="View all posts by tristanl">tristanl</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p><a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-491" title="Billet Grille" src="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/grille-300x234.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="234" /></a></p>
<p>Its been happening for a while, since the days of the 90&#8242;s Dodge trucks with the giant grilles, we are stuck in the middle of the auto grille wars. If you do not believe me all you have to do is look around. In the late 80&#8242;s and 90&#8242;s grilles were very out of fashion in automotive design. The sleek, wedge-like shapes of Japanese auto companies reduced grille space and size with sharp angles and low slung front ends. For auto owners that love the look of a big, flashy grille there are plenty of vehicles that now fit the bill. Chrysler and Dodge practically sustain themselves on large, aggressive front facades that no European or Japanese company would ever dare duplicate.</p>
<p>Because of all this, the <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Cadillac-Escalade-EST-ESV-2002-2006-Chrome-Grille-p/cg108.htm">billet grille</a> market is as strong as ever. There are more <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Cadillac-Escalade-EST-ESV-2002-2006-Chrome-Grille-p/cg108.htm">billet grille manufacturers</a> than ever and their products are of increasingly better and better quality. Remember just ten years ago when many car owners had to content themselves with simply buying a wire mesh and zip tie billet &#8220;grille kit&#8221;? Those were the dark ages my friend. Now you can get a nice, shiny, well fitted grille for just about any contemporary make. If that is not a reason to buy American then I don&#8217;t know what is!</p>

<p>A lot of truck owners may not realize it, but taking care of their ride doesn&#8217;t end at maintaining the mechanicals and keeping it clean. Taking care of the actual truck bed its self is equally important.</p>
<p>There are several reasons for this, but the main ones are to enhance the <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Putco-Front-Bed-Protectors-p/51112.htm">resale value of your truck</a> and also prevent rust and damage.  Every time a heavy or oddly shaped load scratches, punctures or abrades your truck bed, you risk damaging rust patches. With enough scratches and damage, even a truck that looks great on the outside will start to take on serious damage from within. This can cause serious structural problems over time, even if it doesn&#8217;t the simple ugliness of a battered bed will turn off some buyers.</p>
<p>A putco truck bed liner is a great way to avert this sort of damage and spare your truck the  value depreciating damage that rough use can cause. Putco truck bed liners are a very popular <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Putco-Front-Bed-Protectors-p/51112.htm">truck bed accessory</a> and offer many stylish features. Including:</p>
<p>- Deburred for no sharp edges.<br />
- Full front protection fits over top of the bed.<br />
- Installs with 3M adhesive tape.<br />
- Long lasting material designed to withstand the rigors of the elements.<br />
- NeveRust Stainless Steel.</p>

An <a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/Xenon-Products-s/49677.htm">aftermarket Xenon body kit</a> or spoiler promises that installation, painting and fitting will be as easy and painless as possible. It&#8217;ll never be a breeze to do body mods on cars, but with Xenon its as easy as ever.</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/08/buy-quality-aftermarket-car-parts/" title="Permalink to Buy Quality Aftermarket Car Parts" rel="bookmark">Buy Quality Aftermarket Car Parts</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/2012/08/buy-quality-aftermarket-car-parts/" title="5:28 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">August 21, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/author/uniqueautodepot/" title="View all posts by uniqueautodepot">uniqueautodepot</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
				<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.uniqueautoparts.com"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-457" title="Aftermarket Car Parts" src="http://www.aftermarketcarpartsinfo.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/mustang-300x182.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="182" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/">Aftermarket car parts</a> are a big business and there are countless aftermarket parts providers out there. So many that it can be difficult to sort through the junk and the good stuff. There are a lot of fly by night operations in the aftermarket auto parts world. You can easily buy Altezza taillights that discolor and fill with water. If you’ve ever seen what a sub $500 urethane body kit looks like, then you probably wouldn’t order one.</p>
<p>Low quality<a href="http://www.uniqueautodepot.com/"> aftermarket parts</a> are made in China, or in other  un qualified factories. When a quality aftermarket part is made, a sample vehicle is used to get the part just right, making sure it will bolt on to the vehicle with a minimum of fuss. Low quality parts may require modification;  fiberglass parts or polyurethane bits may need to be trimmed, shaved , cut and bent. Metal parts may need to be drilled and tapped. All of this will require trips to your increasingly frustrated mechanic just to install an inferior part.</p>
<p>The next time you order aftermarket parts, do yourself a favor and just get a nice piece from a brand people trust. It may cost more up front, but the extra 20% is worth it if ease of installation and good customer service are going to be a factor.</p>
