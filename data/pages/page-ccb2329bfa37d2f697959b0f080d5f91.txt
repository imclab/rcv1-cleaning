<p> After the death of Alan’s father, the wolfpack decides to take Alan to a hospital to be treated for his mental problems. The mob boss ends up taking Doug hostage and sends the other three men to look for Mr. Chow and bring back the gold.</p>
<p>Directed by Todd Phillips, the movie is appealing for adults who enjoy a good laugh and a movie that is good to view on a Friday. The language is not appropriate for some viewers. In comparison to the first Hangover movie, this one has more action and introduces new characters.</p>
<p>Hangover III premiered on May 24. It made $462 million worldwide. The movie is placed a #2 on the must watch movie list for the last weekend in May. This is a movie worth watching movie is worth watching because it’s very humorous and a very entertaining movie for a laid-back weekend.</p>
