</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-413047158812218762' itemprop='description articleBody'>
So after a very boring one week long landscaping job I'm finally free to do whatever I want, which means I can start working on things. <h2 class='date-header'><span>Monday, June 13, 2011</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://sunaminot.blogspot.com/2011/06/new-vid-update-possible-series-coming.html'>New Vid Update, Possible Series Coming</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4109051510981785625' itemprop='description articleBody'>
Yeah, so this is kind of ridiculous, I'm not sure if I should just keep the footage I have (poorly filmed) or try and get people together to re film it.&nbsp; It's taking much longer than it should considering it's really not too good, or long or complicated or anything really.&nbsp; Speaking of all that stuff, I'm thinking of starting a series, it would be sort of a continuation of <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ki0MpHBcbSo">this video</a> which I made for a school project, but I really like.&nbsp; When I made that video, I had to really push myself and try my best to get good results, and I liked that.&nbsp; With this recent video I dunno, I just don't feel as proud of it as Hardly Heroes.&nbsp; So I think I'd like to take the concept of it and turn it into a mini series or something.&nbsp; It would be good for both me and my crew that I make films with.&nbsp; Since it is more complicated it demands much more complex acting, special effects and script writing, something we all need to improve on.&nbsp; Not only that, but it would get us back into the mind set of "ok, we're filming regularly now" as opposed to how we currently are (everyone's really neutral on the idea, not a whole lot of motivation from the team).&nbsp; I discussed this with one of the members earlier today and got mixed feelings from it, but I really feel like pushing for this, it would help us evolve as a group and become slightly more professional.&nbsp; That's all I really expect at this point in time, improvement, while I'd like to make it big as fast as possible, I know that it isn't very likely to happen any time soon.&nbsp; However, I really want to try and make this series something that people will enjoy to watch.&nbsp; That being said, I'd like to get some opinions from you guys.<br />
<br />
In a universe similar to <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ki0MpHBcbSo">this</a> what kind of super powers would you like to see?&nbsp; What kind of heroes and villains spark your interests?&nbsp; And if you have any suggestions, please, please leave them bellow.&nbsp; I want to make these as good as possible, and any input I can get will help tremendously.&nbsp; Thank you.

<h2 class='date-header'><span>Friday, June 10, 2011</span></h2>

          <div class="date-posts">
        
<div class='post-outer'>
<div class='post hentry' itemprop='blogPost' itemscope='itemscope' itemtype='http://schema.org/BlogPosting'>

<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
<a href='http://sunaminot.blogspot.com/2011/06/intuos4.html'>Intuos4</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-4279311663743306105' itemprop='description articleBody'>
So yesterday I finally decided on which tablet to get.&nbsp; I went with the Intuos4, I had to spend a little bit more money but I think it was worth it.&nbsp; I've done a few sketches on my computer with it and I must say it really is like drawing on paper.&nbsp; The buttons on the side are convenient and easy to use, it even has a function to help you zoom in and out while drawing, which is really nice.&nbsp; I got the medium, but I can't imagine me needed anything bigger, it takes up a good amount of space and the active area is big enough for me to comfortably draw about anything on it.<br />
<br />
In other news, I'm supposed to be filming a short today, but due to lack of communication and a storm hitting out of nowhere, that may not happen.&nbsp; This weekend is pretty busy for me, so if I don't film today, the earliest I will be able to film is Monday.&nbsp; Well that's all for now, thanks for stopping by.
