              <h1>Peugeot to introduce new RCZ-R coupe</h1>
            </div>              <a href="/archive/4294533470" class="author">ABR Staff Writer</a>
              <b class="type_news">Published 12 July 2013</b>
              <div class="line"></div>
                <p>French automaker Peugeot will debut its new RCZ R coupe, a super-performance version of its RCZ Sports car, at the 2013 Goodwood Festival of Speed that will be held in the UK.</p> 
              <p><p><img alt="Pegueot_RCZ-R" height="231" src="http://static.progressivemediagroup.com/uploads/imagelibrary/ABR/Pegueot_RCZ-R.jpg" width="470" /></p><p>The car will be powered by Peugeot's new turbo high pressure (THP) 1.6L gasoline direct injection engine that generates an output 270bhp, while offering an acceleration of 0-62mph in about six-seconds and letting out CO2 emissions of 145g/km.</p><p>Peugeot product director Xavier Peugeot said, &quot;All the strength of style of the RCZ is enhanced by the very dynamic performance and the 270bhp of this R version which will delight the enthusiasts of sports cars.&quot;</p><p>According to the company, the engine offers fuel consumption of about 170bhp/L and meets the Euro-VI emission standards that are expected to come into action from 2104.</p><p>The vehicle comes with 19inch alloy wheels, large fixed rear spoiler, rear diffuser, a new fixed rear boot aero-spoiler and two chromed exhaust pipes.</p></p>
