    <h1>Welcome to Samvardhana Motherson Peguform (SMP)</h1>
    <div class="Hd">SMP is a leading specialist for automotive exterior and interior modules in the automotive industry </div>
    <br />
    The company supplies plastic parts to customers across the globe as well as complete systems and modules for the interior and exterior of vehicles, such as door panels, cockpits, bumpers spoilers etc.. The Company has more than 9000 employees who are  dedicated to meeting the ambitious demands of  customers in terms of quality, aesthetics and economic efficiency.
<br />
In the Financial Year 2011-2012, SMP generated sales of more than 1.8 billion Euro.<br />
