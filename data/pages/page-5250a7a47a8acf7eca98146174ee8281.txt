<h2>Events 1930's</h2>
<p>Shantytowns form consisting of wood and cardboard in the United States. They are often referred to in history as Hoovertowns after President Hoover </p>
<br />
<p>The 30's were a time when the depression caused by the wall street crash in late 1929 caused the world to undergo a fundamental change in lifestyles , and as part of the change some new radical politics became popular as seen in the rise of Fascism, Nazism, and Stalinism </p>
<br />
<p>The 30's also provided a strange phenomenon never repeated where bank robbers and murderers were thought of as celebrities ( a sort of modern Robin Hood ) which in truthfulness they did not rob from the rich to give to the poor just to rob and murder any who got in their way.</p>

<a name="sport"></a>
<h2>Sporting Changes In The 30's</h2>
<strong>Baseball </strong><br />
National Baseball Hall of Fame starts with the first players to be chosen Ty Cobb, Honus Wagner, Walter Johnson, Christy Mathewson and Babe Ruth <br />
Joe DiMaggio starts his career at the New York Yankees taking the crown over from Babe Ruth who retires.  <br />
To help with falling attendance due to the depression, night games are started.  <br />
Live Radio broadcasts of baseball games begin to encourage fans to help sell tickets  <br />
<strong>Basketball </strong><br />
National Collegiate Athletic Association NCAA holds first championship tourney in 1939 which Oregon won. <br />
<strong>Association Football (Soccer) </strong><br />
As it's popularity grew, teams in Britain and later the rest of the world bought in managers who instituted a greater degree of Professionalism, tactics and stricter Training regimes taking the game to new levels.<br />
The first world cup is played in Uruguay in 1930 which the home team won.<br />
<strong>American Football </strong><br />
Goalposts were moved from the back of the end zone to the front of the Endzone<br />
NFL Championship game Introduced Between Eastern and Western divisions<br />
The NFL Draft Introduced <br />
<strong>Ice Hockey </strong><br />
Toronto Maple Leafs Win Stanley Cup<br />
Great 30's Depression Hits Ice Hockey Teams<br />
