                                                        <div class="std"><h1>AutoShop 101</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Welcome to the online learning section of AutoEducation.com</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Our AutoShop 101 section provides free online educational learning courses on all the major parts of your car.&nbsp; We have attempted to provide the basics all car owners should know.&nbsp; If you wish to further your education, we offer <a href="../shop/auto-repair-books.html">Automotive Educational Software</a> in our <a href="../shop.html">online store</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&lt;&lt;&lt;&lt;&lt;&nbsp; To get started, use the menu to the left.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>A few tips to get you started...</h3>
<ul>
<li>Take your time and re-read sections which seem confusing.&nbsp;&nbsp; You won't be rebuilding an engine by the end of our courses, but you will have a good understanding of how every system in your car works.</li>
<li>Feel free to print out any pages from the site, and read at your leasure.</li>
<li><a title="Ask a mechanic" href="../ask_the_mechanic/questions.htm">Ask Questions!</a>&nbsp; I always enjoy answering questions and getting your feedback!</li>
</ul>
