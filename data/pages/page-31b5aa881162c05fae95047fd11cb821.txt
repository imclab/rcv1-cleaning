<p>Hear that ladies? Now just play it cool.</p>
<p>Danny is after going and opening his mouth at someone from <a href="http://www.thesun.co.uk/sol/homepage/showbiz/tv/4866296/the-voices-danny-odonoghue-says-bbc-show-gets-him-girls.html"><em>The Sun</em></a> about how his 'newfound fame' from The Voice is getting him all sorts of female attention. 'The girls in the street that would pass you by are the same girls stopping and going fanatical over you. And you get a real kick out of it.' he said. 'Do I kiss the girls? Of course I do. I&rsquo;m a living, breathing man. If I weren&rsquo;t in the public eye I wouldn&rsquo;t get the same action I do now - I&rsquo;ve got a face like a foot.'</p>
<p>Having only been single a year since breaking up with his long-term girlfriend last June, The Script frontman seems to be relishing in his new ladie-magnet status. 'I&rsquo;ll wake up in the morning and there&rsquo;ll be 30 girls outside the bus wanting to take photos with me. But it&rsquo;s not like these women are cockroaches, they&rsquo;re welcome. And half of them will be really good-looking. We&rsquo;ve been blessed with great fans in that respect.' Aw, he's so happy to have pretty fans. That's not superficial at all. The silver lining here though? 'I wouldn&rsquo;t rule out dating a fan or a contestant - ever.'</p>
<p>On your marks ladies.</p>
