Galleries, workshops, theaters and museums are buzzing with creativity that is a <strong>must see</strong> when visiting Canton/Stark County. Our arts community has something for all ages and walks of life. Many of these venues have ever-changing events and rotating exhibits so please check out their websites to find out more.</p>
