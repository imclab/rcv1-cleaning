												<h1><strong><span style="color: #3c6bbc;">Convertible Car Seat Reviews</span></strong></h1>
<p>The following article on convertible car seat ratings is based on a recent review done by Consumer Reports®.</p>
<p>The three criteria focused on:</p>
<ul>
<li>Crash Protection</li>
<li>Ease of Use</li>
<li>Rear Facing fit to vehicle</li>
</ul>
<p>The tests consisted of simulated frontal crash with child crash test dummies and a review of the instructions for clarity.</p>
<p>They also judged the ease of use for clips and buckles and how easy it was to convert a rear facing car seat to a front facing car seat.</p>
<p>The review also rated how easy it was to secure the convertible car seat in the rear position in a variety of vehicles and the<br />
quality of fit for overall for the different vehicles.<span id="more-200"></span><br />
<!-- google_ad_section_start --><br />
<h2><strong><span style="color: #3c6bbc;">The Evenflo Momentum 65 Deluxe Convertible Car Seat</span></strong></h2>
<p><a href="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/65-Deluxe.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-1042" title="65-Deluxe" src="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/65-Deluxe.jpg" alt="Best Convertible Car Seat" width="100" height="98" /></a>The Evenflo Momentum 65 Deluxe was rated a best-buy for convertible car seats with a harness capacity over 40 Pounds.</p>
<p>This convertible car seat has a rear facing capacity from 5 to 40 pounds which is a safety benefit for larger babies.</p>
<p>The forward facing weight range is from 20 to 65 pounds and the height range is under 50 inches.</p>
<p>The seat also has SureLATCH connectors that can help with LATCH belt tension.</p>
<p><strong>The Approximate retail price for the Evenflo Momentum 65 Deluxe convertible car seat is from $130-$200.</strong></p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #3c6bbc;">The Cosco Scenera 40RF Convertible Car Seat</span></strong></h2>
<p><a href="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/cosco-scenera-40rf.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-209" title="cosco scenera 40rf" src="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/cosco-scenera-40rf.jpg" alt="Cosco Scenera 40RF Convertible Car Seat" width="100" height="103" /></a>One of the top rated “Best Buy” convertible cars seat was the Cosco Scenera 40RF.</p>
<p>This convertible car seat didn’t have some of the higher end features found in more<br />
expensive convertible car seats, however it is simple to install and the price is very<br />
reasonable from $44 to $66.</p>
<p>In addition to the reasonable price the Cosco Scenera 40RF has a rear facing capacity<br />
up to forty (40) pounds which is a safety benefit for larger babies.</p>
<p>The car seat is light weight and it’s simple to transfer it to different vehicles.</p>
<p>This convertible car seat is also narrow leaving more room in the back seat for additional car seats or other items.</p>

<h2><strong><span style="color: #3c6bbc;">The Safety 1st OnSide Air Convertible Car Seat</span></strong></h2>
<p><a href="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Safety-1st-OnSide-Air.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-210" title="Safety 1st OnSide Air" src="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Safety-1st-OnSide-Air.jpg" alt="Safety 1st OnSide Air Convertible Car Seat" width="100" height="108" /></a>The Safety 1st OnSide Air convertible car seat was also rated very high and is another Consumer Reports® “Best Buy”.</p>
<p>The benefits of this convertible car set is the rear facing capacity up to forty (40) pounds which is also a safety benefit for larger babies.</p>
<p>The Safety 1st OnSide Air also uses “Air-Protect” technology that potentially improves the damage inflicted in a side-impact crash.</p>
<p>Like the Cosco Scenera it is also light weight and very portable. This car seat is easy to use and has a simple design.</p>
<p>The Safety 1st OnSide Air convertible car seat retails from approximately $75-$95.</p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #3c6bbc;">The Cosco Scenera Convertible Car Seat</span></strong></h2>
<p><!-- google_ad_section_end --><br />
<a href="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Cosco-Scenera.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-211" title="Cosco Scenera" src="http://www.carseatlaws.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/Cosco-Scenera.jpg" alt="Cosco Scenera Convertible Car Seat" width="100" height="113" /></a>This Convertible Car Seat was third (3<sup>rd</sup>) in the ratings as a “Best Buy” and like the Cosco Scenera 40RF doesn’t come with some of the higher end features found in more expensive convertible car seats but it too has a reasonable price and is easy to use.</p>
<p>The approximate retail price for the Cosco Scenera is $60 and is part of the Dorel Juvenile Products group. This car seat was rated excellent in crash protection and forward facing fit to vehicle.</p>
<p>The Cosco Scenera was rated good for ease of use and rear facing fit to vehicle.</p>
<p>Like the 40RF version this car seat is also narrow enough to add more car seats in the back or leave extra space for other items.</p>
