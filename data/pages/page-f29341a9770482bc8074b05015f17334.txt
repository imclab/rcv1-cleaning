<p>Our 1st (and quite possibly our last)</p>
<p><strong>HOLY HUMOR SUNDAY!!!</strong></p>
<p>Holy Humor Sunday is an occasion for people to join in praise, laughter, and good humor in celebrating God&#39;s love for us!  How long has it been since you really had fun inside a church?</p>
<p>Jokes, stories fun songs, outrageous costumes, and a Sunday morning like you&#39;ve never experienced!  </p>
<p>What do you get when you cross the 1960s and the New Testament?</p>
<p>Peter, Paul and Mary!</p>
<p>We&#39;ve got a million of &#39;em so join us on Holy Humor Sunday, January 29, 2010 at 10:30 a.m. and as the sweet corn said to the cabbage, &#34;Lettuce pray!!!&#34;</p></div>
