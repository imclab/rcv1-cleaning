                                <br/>
                                <span class="post-footers"><a href="http://blogs.orlandosentinel.com/entertainment_stage_theat/category/auditions" title="View all posts in Auditions" rel="category tag">Auditions</a>,  <a href="http://blogs.orlandosentinel.com/entertainment_stage_theat/category/comedy" title="View all posts in Comedy" rel="category tag">Comedy</a> &#151; posted by Matthew J.                                 <div class="discussion">Discuss This: <a href="http://blogs.orlandosentinel.com/entertainment_stage_theat/2010/07/update-humor-mill-comedyimprov-auditions-pushed-back-to-july-24.html#respond" title="Comment on Update: Humor Mill comedy/improv auditions pushed back to July 24">Comments(0)</a>

                                <div class="entry-content">
                                    <div class="entry-body">
                                                        <p>In case you missed the comment on the earlier post about the upcoming Humor Mill auditions, a death in the family has meant a postponement in the auditions.</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s the updated information:</p>
<div>
<p>The Humor  Mill Orlando Comedy Troupe, fresh off the successful run of <em>Spare  Change</em> at the 2010 Orlando International Fringe Festival will hold  auditions <strong>Saturday, July 24</strong>, during the morning and early afternoon.  Auditions will be by appointment only.</p>
<p>The Humor Mill is seeking both male and female performers.  The ideal  candidate has had experience performing with an improv or sketch comedy  troupe, with special consideration to those who have experience with  The Second City style of comedy.  If the candidate has no improv or  sketch comedy performance experience, then prior improv training  experience will be considered.</p>
<p>Those interested must be willing to commit to two rehearsals per week  throughout the year.  The group performs throughout Central Florida and  plans to be back at the Fringe Festival in 2011, as well as several  other exciting performance opportunities throughout the current calendar  year including a new venue on I-Drive.  Pay starts at $20 per  performance.</p>
<p>Those interested should submit a headshot and resume listing relevant  experience via e-mail to <a href="mailto:TheHumorMillOrlando@yahoo.com">TheHumorMillOrlando@yahoo.com</a>.   Qualified candidates will be contacted to set up an audition  appointment time.</p>
<p>For more information on The Humor Mill Orlando, including samples of  its  sketch comedy, check out its website: <a href="http://www.thehumormillorlando.com/" target="_blank">TheHumorMillOrlando.com</a></p>
</div>
