	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="entry-body">
			<p><a class="asset-img-link" href="http://www.smailautoblog.com/2012/06/used-kia-certified-pre-owned.html" style="float: left;" target="_self" title="Used Kia Greensburg"><img alt="Kia-certified-vehicles" border="0" class="asset  asset-image at-xid-6a013488a0d73a970c017615ba1c50970c" src="http://smailauto.typepad.com/.a/6a013488a0d73a970c017615ba1c50970c-800wi" style="margin: 0px 5px 5px 0px;" title="Kia-certified-vehicles" /></a>A Kia Certified vehicle has undergone extensive pre-delivery inspections and must pass all required inspections to be accepted as a Certified vehicle.</p>
<p>The Kia Certified symbol signifies that your pre-owned vehicle has been subjected by Smail Kia to a rigorous 150 point inspection, passing the Kia standard for safety, performance and appearance.</p>
<p><a class="asset-img-link" href="http://smailauto.typepad.com/.a/6a013488a0d73a970c017615ba2ade970c-pi" style="float: right;"><img alt="Kia-soul" border="0" class="asset  asset-image at-xid-6a013488a0d73a970c017615ba2ade970c" src="http://smailauto.typepad.com/.a/6a013488a0d73a970c017615ba2ade970c-800wi" style="margin: 0px 0px 5px 5px;" title="Kia-soul" /></a>With a Kia Certified Pre-Owned Vehicle, you will experience the confidence that comes with the value of buying a pre-owned vehicle and you&#39;ll also enjoy many of the same protections of a new vehicle!</p>
<p>Next time you are looking for a pre-owned KIA make sure it is certified  because the pre-owned package includes 10-year/100,000 mile Limited  Warranty program and 10 years/100,000 miles of Roadside assistance, so  you may relax and enjoy the ride.</p>
