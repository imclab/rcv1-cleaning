					<h1>Car Paint Repair &#8211; Blob or Leveling Solvent Technique</h1>
<p>One of my favorite <strong>car paint repair</strong> methods (and the one pro&#8217;s use daily) to do really nice repair and with touch up paint is to use a leveling solvent.  It&#8217;s hands down the fastest way to do a nice paint repair with touch up paint that you can not see from a normal viewing distance of about 2 &#8211; 3 feet.</p>
<div id="attachment_426" class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 310px"><a href="http://www.Car-Scratch-Remover.com/csr-sales-page-2/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','www.Car-Scratch-Remover.com']);"><img class="size-full wp-image-426 " title="car paint repair" src="http://gator1258.hostgator.com/~karlsbad/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/solvent-article-thumb.jpg" alt="car paint repair " width="300" height="147" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Automotive Paint Repair The Right Way</p></div>
<p>You may have read of the term &#8220;Blob Remover&#8221; for doing car paint repair&#8230;I hate that term.  I hate it because you don&#8217;t want a BLOB of touch up paint in the first place when you are doing a paint repair!  When you apply touch up paint correctly you don&#8217;t get an ugly BLOB&#8230;you get a clean surgically applied amount of paint in the scratch, chip or scrape.</p>
<h2>There are several methods by hand to &#8220;surgically&#8221; apply touch up paint when doing a paint repair.</h2>
<div style="width:100%;margin:0px auto;">
<ul class="black_tick_list">
<li>Syringe &#8211; blunt tip</li>
<li>micro tip brush</li>
<li>squeegie</li>
<li>gloved finger</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><a href="http://www.car-scratch-remover.com/csr-sales-page-2" > Get the Ultimate Car Scratch Remover CSR System BUNDLED with The Ultimate Car Touch Up Paint Kit</a></p>
<div id="attachment_707" class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 310px"><a title="CSR &amp; Touch Up Kit Bundle sales page" href="http://www.Car-Scratch-Remover.com/csr-sales-page-2/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','www.Car-Scratch-Remover.com']);"><img class="size-medium wp-image-707" title="CSR car paint repair" src="http://gator1258.hostgator.com/~karlsbad/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/CSR-and-touch-up-kit-300x150.jpg" alt="car paint repair" width="300" height="150" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">The Ultimate Automotive Paint Repair Bundle</p></div>
<p>The way the car paint repair solvent leveling technique works is you &#8220;erode&#8221; the paint with a special touch up paint solvent.  You carefully remove the build up of touch up paint until it is totally flush and level with the surrounding paint.</p>
<p>The paint repair solvent leveling method can take a little practice&#8230;however with the proper kit and formulas you can simply wipe the touch up paint off clean and immediately  start again!</p>
<h3>You can do the solvent leveling method for auto paint very quickly (15 min)&#8230;once the paint dry enough ( 2hrs on a nice warm day)</h3>
<p>Here are the steps for the repair using touch up paint (it may sound like a lot of work &#8211; but it&#8217;s not &#8211; each of the steps will only require a minute or two)</p>
<div style="width:100%;margin:0px auto;">
<ul class="green_tick_1_list">
<li>Clean &amp; degrease the scratch</li>
<li>Polish the area (you want it to be as shiny as possible first &#8211; no glaze or sealant yet)</li>
<li>Apply touch up paint (various methods)</li>
<li>Level the touch up paint with solvent</li>
<li>Polish lightly again if necessary</li>
<li>Apply Glaze to enhance the luster</li>
<li>Apply Sealant to protect the restored paint finish</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p>In this video I added an optional step before applying the car touch up paint.  I decided to wet sand the long key scratch to make it less pronounced and end up with a nicer end result. Since part of the scratch was borderline the wet sanding almost completely removed those parts.  The Safe-T-Sand reusable wet sanding sponge was used.  It uses patented 3000 grit latex &#8220;paper&#8221;.  It is so gentle and effective that pro&#8217;s can use it on 24hr old freshly painted clear coats to remove imperfections&#8230;it&#8217;s that gentle for precision paint repairs.</p>
