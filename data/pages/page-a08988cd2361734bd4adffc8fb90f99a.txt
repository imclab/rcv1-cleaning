    <h2>Pentwater Arts & Crafts Fairs</h2>

    <div class="content">
      <p><strong></strong></p>
      <div id="boxless"><div style="float:left;padding:10px;text-align:center;"><img src="http://amsitemag.com/images/story_images/pent_arts.jpg" name="photo_box" border="0" width="260px" /></div>Pentwater is known for its art galleries, antiques shops and boutiques, so expect some great finds at their summer arts and crafts fairs. With its beautiful location along Lake Michigan, Pentwater attracts thousands of visitors every summer. Special summer events include Farmer's Markets, Pentwater Civic Band Concerts, July 4th fireworks, and Pentwater Homecoming.<br />
<br />
<h2>Below is the 2013 Schedule for Pentwater Arts & Crafts Fairs</h2> <br />
<strong><em>June</strong></em><br />
15 & 16 - Arts, Crafts and Antiques Fair, Village Green. Sponsored by the Pentwater Chamber of Commerce, this two-day event features over 130 vendors. Find handmade crafts, one-of-a-kind antiques--even demonstrations of a special skill or art. The festive weekend includes live music on the Green. <br />
<br />
29 - 4th Annual Pentwater Antique and Appraisal Fair, 9am-6pm, Village Green. Sponsored by Silver Hills Antiques and the Pentwater Chamber of Commerce. West Michigan dealers and a professional appraiser from Muskegon will be on site from 9am-noon. Rain or shine; antique appraisals will be $5 an item.<br />
<br />
<strong><em>July</strong></em><br />
13 - Pentwater Junior Women's Club juried Fine Arts and Crafts Fair, 9am-5pm, Village Green. This is a one-day, juried, high-traffic arts and crafts fair, which has had an excellent reputation for high quality since 1964. Pentwater Junior Women's Club is a nonprofit group dedicated to promoting civic involvement and supporting the Pentwater community. The Annual Fine Arts and Crafts Fair and Silent Auction are the only fundraising events sponsored by the organization. Funds are distributed to local entities and projects for community improvement, education, arts, and other worthwhile causes.<br />
<br />
<strong><em>September</strong></em><br />
28 & 29 - Annual Fall Fest Art & Craft Fair, Village Green, sponsored by the Pentwater Chamber of Commerce. Over 130 vendors provide handmade crafts, antiques, a special skill or art, or a skilled trade. The festive weekend includes live music on the Green.<br />
<br />
<h2>To register for the above Arts & Crafts Fairs, call the Pentwater Chamber of Commerce at (231) 869-4150. For the July 13 show, click <a href="http://www.pentwaterjuniorwomensclub.com">here.</a></h2><br />
