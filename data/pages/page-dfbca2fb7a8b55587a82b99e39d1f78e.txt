The Volkswagen Beetle Convertible R-Line, as well as the coupe, comes with more aggressive styling and an upgraded interior. Both versions of the Beetle R-Line feature new front and rear bumpers, 18-inch Twister aluminum alloy wheels, and various badges. The R-Line Beetle can be quipped, optionally, with a set of 19-inch alloys called Tornado. For the interior of the VW Beetle, the R-Line package offers new seats covered in Kyalami cloth, a leather sports steering wheel, gearshift knob, and handbrake, and stainless-steel kickplates.
