<h2 class="SubHeader-1"><b>Upcoming Events</b></h2>

<p class="DefaultParagraph-1"><b><a name="P23_1144"></a>Aerospace Export Seminars</b></p>

<p class="DefaultParagraph-1"><b><i>When:</i>  </b>May 16, 2013; August 6, 2013; and October 22, 2013</p>

<p class="DefaultParagraph-1"><b><i>Where:</i></b>  Cleveland and Dayton, OH</p>

<p class="DefaultParagraph-1">The Ohio Aerospace Institute and the U.S. Department of Commerce are pleased to offer U.S. aerospace suppliers three half&#8208;day seminars that will deliver concrete, practical information on the <i>do&#8217;s</i> and <i>don&#8217;ts</i> of exporting aerospace products. Presented by experts in various fields, the seminars are geared especially to small and medium&#8208;sized businesses.  These seminars will cover export controls, trade finance, and working with the FAA.  <!-- TRANSIT - HYPERLINK --><!-- .http://build.trade.gov/tgoaai/tg_oaai_004014. --><a href="./build/groups/public/@tg_oaai/documents/webcontent/tg_oaai_004014.pdf" target="_top">Click here for more information.</a></p></div>
