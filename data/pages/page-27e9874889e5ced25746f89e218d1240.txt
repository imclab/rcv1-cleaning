	  <div class="itemIntroText">
	  	<p>In preparation for the <strong>"Barack Obama Political Message"</strong> (8 tonight on NBC, CBS, Fox, MSNBC, BET, TV One), here are some amusing political-themed Internet videos:</p>
<p>The first is a <a target="_blank" href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QVTfzThBDaE" class="null">"Battlestar Galactica"-themed spot for McCain-Palin</a> that suggests the pair are evil Cylons. (h/t Mary)</p>
<p>The second features <a target="_blank" href="http://www.funnyordie.com/videos/cc65ed650d" class="null">Ron Howard (as Opie and Richie Cunningham) voicing support for Obama</a> with assistance from Andy Griffith and Henry Winkler. (h/t Allan)</p>
	  </div>
	  	  	  <!-- Item fulltext -->
	  <div class="itemFullText">
	  	<p>And the third is a new version of <a target="_blank" href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UDTZCgsZGeA&eurl=" class="null">the old Bud "Wassup!" spot</a> that's been <a target="_blank" href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Qq8Uc5BFogE&eurl=" class="null">updated as a means of support for Obama</a>.</p>
<p>Finally, the liberal MoveOn.org allows Web surfers to customize a video for friends/enemies/frenemies. It's a faux news report on the one voter who failed to vote, leading to a McCain win. Here's one <a target="_blank" href="http://www.cnnbcvideo.com/index.html?nid=ZsIGQvLch4wKRQLgc.N.XTczOTc4Mw--&referred_by=4393999-b9ivkfx" class="null">my friend Stephanie sent me</a>. You can <a target="_blank" href="http://www.cnnbcvideo.com/taf.html?hp=1" class="null">create your own here</a>. And the Hillary Clinton version <a target="_blank" href="http://www.cnnbcvideo.com/index.html?nid=CTs9ta.SoHPPLa.Ojj5LLjMwMDA2MQ--&id=" class="null">is here</a>.</p>
<p>If I found any funny pro-McCain/anti-Obama spots I'd post those too, but I haven't found them other than the <a target="_blank" href="/blogs/tunedin/archive/2008/10/26/murtha-mocked-on-snl.aspx" class="null">"SNL" spots from the other day</a>. If you know of any, post links below in the comments section.</p>
<p>***</p>
<p><img width="250" src="http://c0021359.cdn1.cloudfiles.rackspacecloud.com/images/stories/r_owen/pd.jpg" alt="Kristin Chenoweth stars in " Daisies."="Daisies."" height="167" style="float: right; border: 1px solid black; margin: 10px;" />If you don't plan to watch the Obama show tonight, please check out ABC's <strong>"Pushing Daisies"</strong> (8 p.m., WTAE) as an alternative. It's an excellent, ratings-challenged series that could be canceled. If more viewers don't start watching, it doesn't have a prayer, which would be a shame. America should not be denied the charms of actress Kristin Chenoweth (pictured, right) on a weekly basis.</p>	  </div>
