        <div class="post-body headroom clear-fix" itemprop="articleBody"><a name="aol-share" class="aol-share" title="Reggae and Caribbean Music Marks Tonight's Summer Sounds Concert" data-pid="reggae-and-caribbean-music-marks-tonights-summer-sounds-concert" href="mailto:yourfriend@email.com?subject=Check this out: Reggae and Caribbean Music Marks Tonight's Summer Sounds Concert&body=http://middletown-ct.patch.com/groups/arts-and-entertainment/p/reggae-and-caribbean-music-marks-tonights-summer-sounds-concert"></a>The Middletown Commission on the Arts presents their annual Summer Sounds concert series on the City&#39;s South Green (Union Park) on Tuesday evenings throughout the summer. <br><br>Rain location is the sanctuary of South Congregational Church, directly across the street from the Green on the corner of Main and Pleasant Streets.<br><div id='popular_stories_wrapper' class='clear-fix invisible-mobile'></div><br>Drummer Paul Bozzi, keyboard player and singer Roger Lum You, keyboard player and bassist Guy Wallis, and sax player, singer and ukulele plucker Lyn Ryan McKenna — the band Moto — offer a blend of traditional roots reggae spiced with contemporary touches and more traditional Caribbean soca material.<br><br>The concerts are free and you are encouraged to bring lawn chairs/blankets. Food and beverages (no alcohol is permitted on public lawns) are allowed and the United Methodist Church and South Congregational Church adjacent to the Green both sell food/beverage items.<br><br>Concerts begin at 7 p.m.<br><br>July 23 - Moto - reggae/caribbean<br>July 30 - Trevor Davis Quintet - jazz/swing with vocalist Carolyn Reeves<br>Aug. 6 - Phil Rosenthal Four - bluegrass/old-timey music<br>Aug. 13 - Last Fair Deal - americana/bluegrass<br>Aug. 20 - Sambaleza - brazilian jazz<br>Aug. 27 - Elite Syncopation - ragtime/early jazz<br>Sept. 3 - Middletown Symphonic Band - popular tunes<br>Sept. 10 - Italian Night w/the Angelo Sapia Band<br><br>Visit <a href="http://arts2go.org/" rel="nofollow">arts2go.org</a> for all your arts and entertainment listings.</div>
