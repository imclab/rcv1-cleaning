<div align="center">
<a href="http://www.emily-vancamp.com" target="_blank"><img src="http://www.emily-vancamp.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/nylon.jpg" alt="emily vancamp - nylon" width="450" class="img" /></a>

<blockquote><p>She&#8217;s gearing up for the season premiere of ABC&#8217;s &#8220;Revenge&#8221; and during promotions, Emily VanCamp scored the cover spot on NYLON magazine&#8217;s September 2013 issue.</p>
<p>While rocking a few poses in designer duds from Heather Bell and Topshop for the Jason Nocito-shot spread, the 27-year-old actress dished about her successful series and reveals her constant struggles with the pesky paparazzi.</p>
<p><strong>On &#8220;Revenge&#8221;:</strong><br />
&#8220;They could&#8217;ve hired someone with a bigger name and banked on that. They took a chance on me, and it&#8217;s brought on a whole new collection of opportunities.&#8221;</p>
<p><strong>On being chased by the shutterbugs :</strong><br />
&#8220;It should be illegal for people to stalk you with the cameras. It&#8217;s really invasive. How do you enjoy a moment? I see how people spiral out of control.&#8221;</p>
<p><strong>On her &#8220;girl next door&#8221; reputation:</strong><br />
&#8220;I wouldn&#8217;t have labeled myself that way. I just think I had the look to play those characters. I was a good kid, but I definitely had my moms of rebellion.&#8221;</p></blockquote>
