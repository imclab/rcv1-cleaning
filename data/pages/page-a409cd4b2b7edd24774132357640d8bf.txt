<div id="title"><h1>Summer Arts and Entertainment in Columbus Ohio</h1></div><div id="intro">Columbus summers burst with great arts programs and other entertainment. You'll find info on Actors' Theatre, Picnic with the Pops, the CAPA movie series and other longtime summer community favorites here.</div><p><a href="/od/artsentertainment/a/Horror-Film-Festival-In-Columbus-August-Schedule.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Horror Film Festival in Columbus - August Schedule</a><br>Love a good scare? So does the Gateway Theater and they play host to the only weekly horror film festival in the country. Check out the selection of August films at"Nightmares on High Street."</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/2013-Ohio-Idol.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">2013 Ohio Idol</a><br>Ohio Idol is atatewide singing competition for Ohio residents 12 years of age or older. Also free the month of June - movies, concerts, yoga, baseball games and more!</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Central-Ohio-Summer-Festivals.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Central Ohio Summer Festivals</a><br>Summer festivals are a Columbus, Ohio staple. Here we celebrate diverse cultures - Hispanic, African American, Serbian, Asian, etc.; diverse music - Jazz, blues, alternative; and diverse foods - ribs, strawberries, tomatoes and anything that can be put on a stick or fried.</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Capa-Summer-Movie-Series.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">CAPA Summer Movie Series</a><br>The full 2012 CAPA Summer Movie Series featuring movies of every genre: chick flick Bridget Jones' Diary, classic Saturday Morning cartoons, sci-fi action film The Matrix and classic films The Sound of Music, Alfred Hitchcock's Vertigo and To Kill a Mockingbird.</p><p><a href="/od/musicincolumbusohio/a/Columbus-Symphony-Picnic-With-The-Pops.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Columbus Symphony Picnic with the Pops</a><br>The 2011 Picnic with the Pops schedule with the Columbus Symphony.</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Gateway-Film-Center-Spotlight-Series.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Gateway Film Center Spotlight Series</a><br>Gateway Film Center Spotlight Series focuses on hard-to-get and classic films for every genre of movie goer.</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Columbus-Documentary-Week-Line-up.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Columbus Documentary Week Line-Up</a><br>Get ready to see thought-provoking, award-winning documentaries during Columbus' Documentary Week at the Gateway Theater.</p><p><a href="/od/artsentertainment/a/Free_Concerts.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">2009 Free Summer Concerts in Columbus</a><br>No matter your musical preference, free tunes are in the air this summer. So, bring the lawn chairs, blankets, bug repellant and grab yourself a free &#8220;music break.&#8221;</p><div class="inlineads"><script>zSB(3,2)</script></div><p><a href="/od/artsentertainment/tp/9_No-Cost_Things_2009summer.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">9 Free Things to Do This Summer</a><br>Budgets are tight and we're all cutting back our spending. Send me your list.</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Double-Barrel-June-2013.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Double Barrel - June 2013</a><br>Grab your beans, and saddle up! Gateway Film Center has brought back its summer western film series, Double Barrel. Double Barrel features a different film each week with shows every Saturday, Sunday, and Wednesday at 2:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m.</p><p><a href="/od/summerartsentertainment/a/Double-Barrel-July-2013.htm" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Double Barrel - July 2013</a><br>Grab your beans, and saddle up! Gateway Film Center has brought back its summer western film series, Double Barrel. Double Barrel features a different film each week with shows every Saturday, Sunday, and Wednesday at 2:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. Check out the July listing.</p><p><a href="http://www.gcac.org/fest/" zT="-o1/XJ" target="_blank" class="o" zT="18/1R4/Wa">2009 Columbus Arts Festival </a><br>On June 5-7, the 48-year old Columbus Arts Festival will fill the Discovery District with art, music, food, and hands-on activities. Over 250 nationally acclaimed artists will show their work in this year&#8217;s juried art show. Festival admission is free.</p><p><a href="http://www.theactorstheatre.org" zT="-o1/XJ" target="_blank" class="o" zT="18/1R4/Wa">Actors' Theatre in Columbus, Ohio</a><br>Free theater under the stars! This summer, Actors' Theatre is producing <i>The Three Musketeers</i>, <i>The Tempest</i> and <i>As you Like It</i>. Pack a picnic, grab a blanket and head for Schiller Park in German Village. </p><!--/gc-->
