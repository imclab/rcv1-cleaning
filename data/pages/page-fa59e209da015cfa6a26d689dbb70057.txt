<h4>Fine Arts Society</h4><div style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;&nbsp; <img style="width: 200px; height: 79px;" alt="FASLogo.jpg" src="/docs/CP/1651/FASLogo.jpg" align="left" border="0" hspace="0" vspace="0"><br><br><br><br><br><div style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: bold;"></span><h1><span style="font-weight: bold;"></span>Membership</h1>If you would like to become a member of the Fine Arts Society, please send an email to the Co-Presidents listed below.<br><h1>Mission</h1>The Fine Arts Society is a College of Arts and Science
club with the goal of creating a community of students interested in
art history.&nbsp; The society extends membership to all NYU students.
Through visits to New York City museums and galleries, as well as
walking tours, lectures, symposia, and film screenings, the Fine Arts
Society brings art history majors and non-majors together, with lively
discussions.&nbsp; In addition, the group publishes a newsletter at the
beginning of every month during the school year, with information on
mostly free arts-related events in New York City happening that month.&nbsp;
Their two annual events include a Study Party, held every December on
Reading Day before final examinations, and a Career Symposium, held
every spring. <br><br>The Fine Arts Society encourages students to
participate in events and public programs hosted by NYU, especially
those at the Grey Art Gallery in NYU, as well as programs presented by
