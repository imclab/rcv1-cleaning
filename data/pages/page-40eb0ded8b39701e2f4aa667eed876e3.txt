<h3>Welcome to Fox Auto Parts!</h3>
Fox Auto Parts is a family owned and operated company that is dedicated to serving all of your automotive needs! We strive to provide our customers with a positive buying experience at all times. We recycle over 800 late model vehicles a year in an effort to have the part YOU need in stock! Even if the part you need is not available at our location, Fox Auto Parts has proudly partnered with Team Premium Recycled Parts, a group of over 100 high quality yards around the country that will allow us to locate a quality part and have it shipped in a timely fashion. We also carry a full line of new parts, and proudly offer a full service department staffed with 6 ASE and State certified technicians operating 12 vehicle hoists.
Fox Auto Parts is here to help in any way possible! Give us a call today at 800-851-3277!
