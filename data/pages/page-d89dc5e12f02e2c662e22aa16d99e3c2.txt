What can be done to avoid these types of scams.</p>

					<h3 class="entry-header"><a href="http://blogs.cars.com/safe/2012/07/salvaged-title-fraud-how-to-protect-yourself.html">Salvaged Title Fraud - How to Protect Yourself</a></h3>
		
		<div class="entry-content">
                    <input type="hidden" name="zemanta-related" val="" />
			<div class="entry-body">
				<p>A salvaged title means that the vehicle at some point in time has been claimed a total loss by the insurance company (damage exceeded 50-75% of its actual cash value). Depending on the state, recovered stolen vehicles are issued salvaged titles and government agency vehicles are sometimes issued salvaged titles for vehicles that they have used and are now replacing with new vehicles.</p>
