<p>Lord jesus it&#8217;s a fire, as Sweet Brown would say!</p>
<p>You could <a href="http://www.mytopsportsbooks.com/" target="_blank">bet on sports like football</a> to always give us one wild half-time show, and <a href="http://www.omgcelebritynews.com/categories/beyonce"><strong>Beyonce</strong></a> gave us just that! <p>I&#8217;m so excited to finally see Queen B in concert!!! Her performances have always been top notch and on point, I could only imagine how fabulous her concert would be!</p>
<p>While the announcement that she&#8217;s going on tour is great, I&#8217;m not so crazy about commercial and name of the concert&#8230; Who&#8217;s Mrs. Carter? I&#8217;m sure there&#8217;s an explanation behind the name, so we&#8217;ll have to wait and see!</p>

					<p class="headline_meta">by <span class="author vcard fn">OhEmGee</span> on <abbr class="published" title="2012-09-18">September 18, 2012</abbr></p>
				</div>
				<div class="format_text entry-content">
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-12050" title="Christina Aguilera &amp; Cee Lo Green Out! Shakira &amp; Usher New Judges On The Voice!" src="http://omgcelebritynews.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/Christina-Aguilera-Cee-Lo-Green-Out-Shakira-Usher-New-Judges-On-The-Voice.jpg" alt="" width="416" height="224" /></p>
<p>Surprise, surprise!</p>
<p>It&#8217;s been <a href="http://insidetv.ew.com/2012/09/17/the-voice-usher-shakira/" target="_blank">announced</a> <a href="http://www.omgcelebritynews.com/category/christina-aguilera" target="_blank"><strong>Christina Aguilera</strong></a> and <strong>Cee Lo Green</strong> will be leaving the judges panel on the hit NBC series <em>The Voice </em>for season four!</p>
<p>However, Miz. MyHipzDon&#8217;tLie <strong><a href="http://www.omgcelebritynews.com/category/shakira" target="_blank">Shakira</a></strong> and <a href="http://www.omgcelebritynews.com/category/usher" target="_blank"><strong>Usher</strong></a> will be taking over for Xtina and Cee Lo while the duo take a quick hiatus returning back for season five.</p>
<p>The reason behind this change up NBC <a href="http://insidetv.ew.com/2012/09/17/the-voice-usher-shakira/" target="_blank">says</a>, &#8221; the new additions (judges) are part of a plan by NBC and producers to develop an ongoing tent of talent for <em>The Voice</em> that can be mixed and matched as needed.&#8221;</p>
<p>It makes sense given that these superstar judges also have careers they&#8217;re pursing. Xtina&#8217;s upcoming album <em>Lotus</em> will be dropping November 13th and she&#8217;ll be going on tour (YES!). While Cee Lo is producing new music and working on a scripted comedy series about his life for NBC.</p>
<p>Being a fan of <em>The Voice</em>, I&#8217;m excited to see how Shakira and Usher will blend into the show along with how they get along with <strong>Adam Levine</strong> and <strong>Blake Shelton</strong>! Can anyone imagine the types of singers Shakira will pick for her team? I can&#8217;t.</p>
<p>Thoughts?!</p>
