<p>Whether your passion is music, painting, drawing, dancing or performing, you can tap into the opportunities in the<strong> Arts, Media and Entertainment</strong> industry. Loaded with artistic, creative and high-tech careers this exciting industry offers opportunities in front of or behind the scenes. In addition, companies need experienced multimedia production and graphic designers to create captivating websites as well as game designers and programmers to keep us playing. <strong>Arts, Media and Entertainment</strong> are big business throughout California and world. If you yearn for the creative, have a calling to express yourself, or are fascinated with new media technologies, you may find your career in the <strong>Arts Media and Entertainment</strong> venue.</p>
