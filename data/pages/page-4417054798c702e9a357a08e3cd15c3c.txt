        <h2>
          The Women of Car Culture        </h2>
        <div class="entry">
          <p><img src="http://i566.photobucket.com/albums/ss105/AutocultureOrg/veloce-jun09e.jpg" border="0" alt="Photobucket"></p>
<p>Over at The Selvedge Yard, a piece on boxer, cyclist, race car driver, motorcycle racer, heavy smoker and heavy cusser Violette Morris is worth digesting (<a href="http://theselvedgeyard.wordpress.com/2009/09/29/fast-women-in-history-auto-racings-tough-female-pioneers/">here</a>). As amazing as some of the stories of the men who made car culture are, the women who willingly threw themselves into that snake nest are even amazing-er. Think you&#8217;re a badass? Would you willingly give up a few of your parts to race a car? Didn&#8217;t think so.</p>
