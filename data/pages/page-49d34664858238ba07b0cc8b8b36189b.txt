		<h1 style="text-align:center;">BLOWING VIDEO <a class="zem_slink" title="ROM cartridge" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ROM_cartridge" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">GAME CARTRIDGES</a></h1>
<p style="text-align:center;"><a href="http://jixifox.me/2013/08/24/blowing-video-game-cartridges/blowing-video-game-cartridges/#main" rel="attachment wp-att-5765"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5765" alt="blowing video game cartridges" src="http://jixifox.files.wordpress.com/2013/08/blowing-video-game-cartridges.jpg?w=620&#038;h=439" width="620" height="439" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align:center;">I blowing it hard, I am trying to play these game,<br />
My day is going wack and I need something to play,<br />
I might grab my <a class="zem_slink" title="Atari" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atari" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Atari</a>, <a class="zem_slink" title="Nintendo" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=34.991675,135.766294444&amp;spn=0.01,0.01&amp;q=34.991675,135.766294444 (Nintendo)&amp;t=h" target="_blank" rel="geolocation">Nintendo</a> or my <a class="zem_slink" title="Game Boy" href="http://gameboy.com/" target="_blank" rel="homepage">Game Boy</a>,<br />
Or my <a class="zem_slink" title="Sega Genesis" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sega_Genesis" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Sega Genesis</a>, <a class="zem_slink" title="Super Nintendo Entertainment System" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Nintendo_Entertainment_System" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Super Nintendo</a>, I play hard no toys,</p>
<p style="text-align:center;">I might go on an adventure <a class="zem_slink" title="Mario" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mario" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Mario</a>, Sonic or Duck Hunt,<br />
I am trying to beat someone&#8217;s ass right now, I&#8217;m quite blunt,<br />
For fun let&#8217;s play with friends turn after turn so everyone can go,<br />
But before anything goes down these damn cartridges I got to blow.</p>

		<h1 style="text-align:center;">FIELD OF DREAMS</h1>
<p style="text-align:center;"><a href="http://jixifox.me/2013/08/23/field-of-dreams/fields-of-dreams/#main" rel="attachment wp-att-5760"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5760" alt="fields of dreams" src="http://jixifox.files.wordpress.com/2013/08/fields-of-dreams.jpg?w=620"   /></a></p>
<p style="text-align:center;">Dreamers lack fear &amp; tears but birth aspirations,<br />
Living in the fields and feeling pain,<br />
Their eyes read, and their heart write,<br />
We live pieces of their lives as tears reach our eyes.</p>
<p style="text-align:center;">In the field of dreams anything is possible,<br />
We create art here, we breathe the air,<br />
We release our fear and we tear up in love,<br />
So we stay in our safe havens and fly above.</p>

		<div class="entry-content">
		<h1 style="text-align:center;">IN THE PASSENGER SEAT OF THE RIDE</h1>
<p style="text-align:center;"><a href="http://jixifox.me/2013/08/22/in-the-passenger-seat-of-the-ride/dog-riding-shotgun/#main" rel="attachment wp-att-5756"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5756" alt="dog riding shotgun" src="http://jixifox.files.wordpress.com/2013/08/dog-riding-shotgun.jpg?w=620&#038;h=464" width="620" height="464" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align:center;">-</p>
<p style="text-align:center;">Only if you are lucky, we would go out for a ride,<br />
No matter where we go as long as I am inside,<br />
So you try your best to call out for shotgun,<br />
Because it&#8217;s time to rollout we about to have some fun,</p>
<p style="text-align:center;">I&#8217;m talking on the cell, while the music is blasting,<br />
I look in the backseat someone is twerking and dancing,<br />
We just blew by a <a class="zem_slink" title="Stop sign" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stop_sign" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">stop sign</a>, fast car like we <a class="zem_slink" title="Need for Speed" href="http://www.needforspeed.com/" target="_blank" rel="homepage">Need for Speed</a>,<br />
We about to go to jail, cops behind us, because of this weed.</p>
<p style="text-align:center;"><strong>Geez</strong></p>

		<h1 style="text-align:center;">WHEN MEMES GO WRONG</h1>
<p style="text-align:center;"><a href="http://jixifox.me/2013/08/21/when-memes-go-wrong/confused-baby-funniest-memes/#main" rel="attachment wp-att-5751"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-5751" alt="confused baby funniest memes" src="http://jixifox.files.wordpress.com/2013/08/confused-baby-funniest-memes.jpg?w=620"   /></a></p>
<p style="text-align:center;">Take a look at me,<br />
What do you see?<br />
Is it something to laugh at?<br />
What the hell is so funny?</p>
<p style="text-align:center;">My pain cause you pleasure,<br />
So you shared it to the world,<br />
What did I do to be treated this way?<br />
Memes of people get created every damn day.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;"><strong>Q</strong>: Do you love memes?</p>
