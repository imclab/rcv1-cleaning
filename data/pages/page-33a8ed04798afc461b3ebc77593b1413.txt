<p align="justify"><img src="/images/stories/handsonguitar32.jpg" border="0" alt="electric guitar player" title="electric guitar player" hspace="8" vspace="8" align="left" />Check out our growing list of music festivals in New England! Among the oldest and most well known-- the <a href="http://www.newportjazzfest.net/" target="_blank" title="Newport Jazz Festival">Newport Jazz Festival </a>(est. 1954) and the <a href="http://www.newportfolkfest.net/" target="_blank" title="Newport Folk Festival">Newport Folk Festival</a> (est. 1959), both co-founded by legendary jazz producer, George Wein. Other popular outdoor music venues in New England include The <a href="http://www.litchfieldjazzfest.com/" target="_blank" title="Litchfield Jazz Festival">Litchfield Jazz Festival</a> in Connecticut, <a href="http://www.northatlanticbluesfestival.com/" target="_blank" title="North Atlantic Blues Festival">North Atlantic Blues Festival </a>in Maine, <a href="http://www.rootsontheriver.com/" target="_blank" title="Roots on The River">Roots On The River</a> in Vermont, and the <a href="http://www.greenriverfestival.com/" target="_blank" title="Green River Festival">Green River Festival</a> in Massachusetts. Each year families and individuals from age 0-99 gather to enjoy live music and entertainment at one of New England's many outdoor summer music festivals. One can find arts and craft activities for kids, tasty food and good drink provided by local vendors, as well as an array of artisans selling specialty, New England-made  products and gifts.
