					<div><div style="padding:30px";><div class="arMnSevenIv "><div class="arMnSevenTxt">Buying a brand <a href="newcars/bodystyle/Sedans" target="_blank">new car</a> is very  simple and hassle-free. The problem faced by most is how to sell their car at a  reasonable price that doesn&rsquo;t leave one feeling cheated. For some great tips to  sell your car, browse through some of our expert tips.</div></div></div><div style="padding:30px";><div id="newsDesc" class="arMnTxt" style="margin-top:25px;"><div class="arMnTxtLt"><div class="arYeBoc"><div><h3 class="arYeTxt" style="margin:0;"><img src="/images/articles/bullet_second.jpg" alt="bullet" />&nbsp;Assess your car</h3></div></div><div class="arYeTxtIn">Do not blindly set a price  for your car. Take a sincere look at the market value of the car. If the model  is popular and is in great demand, then there are chances of fast-selling. But  if not, then you have to be little prepared to find the suitable buyer for your  car. Never expect an unrealistic price.</div><br clear="all" /><div class="arYeBoc"><div><h3 class="arYeTxt" style="margin:0;"><img src="/images/articles/bullet_second.jpg" alt="bullet" />&nbsp;Decide Your Price</h3></div></div><div class="arYeTxtIn">Do a little market research. You can also do this online by  referring few car portals. Once you get a clear picture about the price range,  the next step is to consider few factors before you quote the price.
		<ul>
		  <li>Condition </li>
		  <li>Mileage</li>
		  <li>Special features</li>
		</ul>These are the primary factors that a buyer will first examine. If you have  customized your car with modern gadgets, high quality tyres, upgraded engine, a  replaced gear-shift (which is still under warranty), and other accessories, increase  the price accordingly. Maintain all the receipt concerning any major car work so  that you can present it to the buyer while selling the car.<br/><br/>Once you have decided on the price, keep in mind to boost up your figure by  a few thousand rupees as this will mitigate a bit of negotiating room between  you and the buyer.</div><br clear="all" /><div class="arYeBoc"><div><h3 class="arYeTxt" style="margin:0;"><img src="/images/articles/bullet_second.jpg" alt="bullet" />&nbsp;Decide selling location</h3></div></div><div class="arYeTxtIn">The best experimented methods of selling a <a href="usedcars" target="_blank">used car</a> is &ndash; dangling a &lsquo;for sale&rsquo;  sign in the vehicle, giving classified ad in the local newspaper, or trading in  the car at a nearby car dealership. Apart from these traditional techniques,  internet, the latest technology of the era has proven to be very fast and  reliable platform for selling cars.</div><br clear="all" /><div class="arYeBoc"><div><h3 class="arYeTxt" style="margin:0;"><img src="/images/articles/bullet_second.jpg" alt="bullet" />&nbsp;Car Details</h3></div></div><div class="arYeTxtIn">A well maintained car will always impress the buyer. So  ensure that your car scores well both in terms of performance as well as looks.  A little investment on maintenance will fetch you a good price. Minor maintenance  that will cost you little but will enhance the appeal of your vehicle includes  &ndash; <a href="article/0408200802?page=1" target="_blank">car waxing</a>, tidy  interior and trunk, cleaned carpet and upholstery, glossy tyres and hubcaps, oil  change, You can then take a test drive to confirm the car&rsquo;s performance.</div><br clear="all" /></div><div class="arMnTxtRt"><div class="arYeBocRt" style="height:auto;padding-bottom:3px;"><div><h3 class="arYeTxt" style="margin:0;"><img src="/images/articles/bullet_second.jpg" alt="bullet" />&nbsp;Show it off</h3></div></div><div class="arYeTxtInRt">If you have a sign posted on your vehicle, make it visible to  attract a potential buyer.
