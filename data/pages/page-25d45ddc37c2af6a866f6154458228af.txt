Arts &amp; Entertainment
<!-- /com.omniupdate.div -->
	</h2>
                                 </div>
                                 <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="bodyarea1" group="Everyone" button="006" --><!-- com.omniupdate.editor wysiwyg="yes" csspath="/rsc/css/cms/7030_left.css" cssmenu="/rsc/css/cms/bodymenu.txt" width="955" --><!-- /com.omniupdate.div -->
		<br/>
                                 <!-- com.omniupdate.div label="includedContent" group="CommonContent" button="731" path="/common/arts-entertainment/featuredevents.inc" --><!-- com.omniupdate.editor csspath="/rsc/css/cms/common.css" cssmenu="/rsc/css/cms/bodymenu.txt" width="500" div="#OUPreview" --><!-- com.omniupdate.editor csspath="/rsc/css/cms/common.css" cssmenu="/rsc/css/cms/bodymenu.txt" width="515" div="#OUPreview" --><p>Barton Community College offers numerous activities and programs for students to participate in Music, Theatre, Dance, Instrumental, Vocal, and Visual Art. Students can enroll in choir, band, acting, and ballet as well as other fine arts and performing arts classes. Many students plan to major in these programs or simply enroll for personal enrichment. Additionally, many community members enroll in these classes for their own personal enrichment.</p>
<p>Throughout the year, Barton Community College presents various concerts and plays for the college and community from major musicals to intimate student recitals, from student art shows to national and international exhibits, from celebrated dramatic productions to storytelling events. Barton also hosts outside college events from local, regional, and national organizations.</p>
<p>Student and community members have numerous opportunities to participate in Barton’s fine and performing arts activities either as students in classes or audience members.</p>
