<p>The 2014 Scion tC coupe will debut at the 2013 New York<a target="_blank" title=" auto show" href=" http://www.motortrend.com/auto_shows/"> auto show</a> next week, the automaker has just announced. The current, second-generation tC was first introduced for the 2011 model year, and we&#8217;re guessing the 2014 tC will represent more of a major refresh than a full redesign.</p>
<p><a target="_blank" href="http://wot.motortrend.com/2014-scion-tc-coupe-debuting-at-new-york-auto-show-344523.html/2014-scion-tc-from-above-teaser-image" rel="attachment wp-att-344525"><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-344525" title="2014 Scion tC from above teaser image 300x187 photo" src="http://stwot.motortrend.com/files/2013/03/2014-Scion-tC-from-above-teaser-image-300x187.jpg" alt="2014 Scion tC from above teaser image 300x187 image" width="300" height="187" /></a>The front-drive <a href="http://www.motortrend.com/roadtests/coupes/1112_2011_scion_tc_first_test/">Scion tC</a> remains very important to the Scion brand, even with the new <a target="_blank" href="http://www.motortrend.com/roadtests/oneyear/coupe/1302_2013_scion_fr_s_arrival/">rear-drive FR-S</a> stealing the spotlight. Though the boxy xB and FR-S have outsold the tC through the first two months of 2013, the tC contributes more sales than the iQ and xD put together. A redesigned 2014 Scion tC should help boost sales for those who just want a cool-looking coupe and can&#8217;t afford the FR-S&#8217; $5000 price premium.</p>
<p>The current Scion tC is powered by a 180-hp 2.5-liter I-4 mated to a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. <a target="_blank" href="http://www.motortrend.com/roadtests/coupes/1202_mini_cooper_vs_scion_tc_vs_honda_crz_vs_volkswagen_beetle_vs_hyundai_veloster_vs_fiat_500_comparison/">In a <em>Motor Trend</em> comparison</a>, a 2011 tC placed sixth, behind the Fiat 500, Hyundai Veloster, Volkswagen Beetle, Honda CR-Z, and Mini Cooper. The revised 2014 Scion tC, which debuts on March 28, may offer revised front styling with LED daytime running lights as well as retuned steering, a lightly retouched interior, and new wheel styles. Our comparison&#8217;s 2011 tC test car is shown below.</p>
<p>So far, we only have one teaser image of the 2014 Scion tC, but it won&#8217;t be long before the full car is revealed. Stick with <em>Motor Trend</em> for all your New York auto show updates.</p>
