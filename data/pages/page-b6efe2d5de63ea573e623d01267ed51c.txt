<p><h3>The Hottest <i>Dancing With the Stars</i> Gossip</h3>

<ul><li><b>March 2009:</b> Nancy O'Dell and singer Jewel were both forced to leave the <a href="http://realitytv.about.com/od/shallwedance/ig/-Dancing--Stars----Season-8/">Season 8</a> competition due to injuries. Jewel (paired with Dmitri Chaplin) had a fractured tibia in both legs while Nancy (paired with Tony Dovolani) suffered with a painful torn meniscus. The women were replaced with <i>Girls Next Door</i> star Holly Madison and former <i>Bachelor</i> contestant Melissa Rycroft.<p>

<li><b>January 2009:</b> Just six months after Karina Smirnoff cried on <i>The Chelsea Handler Show</i> over her breakup with Mario Lopez, she shocked her fans. Karina and her <i>Dancing With the Stars</i> pal, Maksim Chmerkovskiy, announced that they were <a href="/b/a/258630.htm">engaged to be married</a>. Maksim proposed at midnight as they rang in 2009 together. <p>

<li><b>November 2008:</b> Brooke Burke and Derek Hough <a href="/b/a/258580.htm">won the 7th season</a> of <i>Dancing With the Stars</i>. It was Brooke and Derek's Viennese waltz that earned them a perfect score from the panel of judges. When asked how she felt, Brooke could barely speak and managed to simply say, "Unbelievable!" as the confetti rained down on her head. <p>  


<li><b>November 2007:</b> Following rumors that he and his Season 5 DWTS 19-year-old dance partner, Julianne Hough, were hooking up, Helio Catroneves and Aliette Vazquez <a href="/b/a/258078.htm">called off their engagement</a>. In a statement, Helio said, "I can confirm that Aliette Vazquez and I have called off our engagement and are no longer together. At this time I would prefer to keep my private life private." As you might expect, <a href="http://realitytv.about.com/od/shallwedance/ig/Dancing-With-the-Stars-Photos/Helio-and-Julianne.-0TI.htm">Helio and Juliette</a> continued to deny any romantic connection.</ul><p><!--/gc-->
