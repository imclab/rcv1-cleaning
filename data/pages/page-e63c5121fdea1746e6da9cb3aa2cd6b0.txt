<p><font face="Verdana"><img src="http://pictures.dealer.com//subaruoflasvegassoa/1f9aa22040463872005da37c01025a98.png" href="http://pictures.dealer.com//subaruoflasvegassoa/1f9aa22040463872005da37c01025a98.png" title="subaruoflasvegas.png" class="wysiwyg-image" tabindex="30000" style="height: 34px; width: 378px;"></font><br></p><div style="text-align: center;"><img src="http://pictures.dealer.com//subaruoflasvegassoa/1fbdb55f40463872008730b880633ec5.jpg" href="http://pictures.dealer.com//subaruoflasvegassoa/1fbdb55f40463872008730b880633ec5.jpg" title="cpo-01.jpg" class="wysiwyg-image" tabindex="30000" style="height: 391px; width: 870px;"><br></div><h3>Why Certified Pre-Owned?</h3><p>Only Subaru Certified Pre-Owned
checks your vehicle's past, present and protects your future. Even
before its 152-point safety inspection, every Certified Pre-Owned
Subaru has received a CARFAX<sup>&#174;</sup> History Report. No secrets. No
surprises. Just great value in a vehicle that has our exacting
standards, not once, but twice. And since they come with nationally
recognized, factory-backed, 6-year/100,000-mile CPO Powertrain Coverage
and 24/7 roadside assistance, you can be sure you made the right choice.</p><p><b>Here are all the things that get checked:</b></p><ul style="list-style: disc outside none; padding-left: 30px;"><li><b>Exterior:</b> Check and recondition sheet metal and bumpers, wheels and tires, paint condition, glass, lights, security system and more.<br></li><li><b>Road Test:</b>
Test engine, transmission, steering and brake performance. Check clutch
operation, wheel balance, wheel alignment, emissions and more.<br></li><li><b>Interior:</b> Check and recondition power equipment, controls, features, upholstery, carpet, seat belts, brake and clutch pedals and more.<br></li><li><b>Undercarriage:</b> Check and recondition brakes, steering linkage, exhaust system, axles, struts, tires and more.<br></li><li><b>Under Hood:</b> Check and recondition fluids, filters, hoses, battery and more.<br></li></ul><h3>What Makes It Certified?</h3><p>To qualify for the Certified Pre-Owned Program, each Subaru must meet the following high standards:</p><ul style="list-style: disc outside none; padding-left: 30px;"><li>Must be from the current model year or preceding 5 years.<br></li><li>Must have under 80,000 miles on the odometer.<br></li><li>Must pass a rigorous 152-point inspection. <a href="http://www.subaru.com/content/downloads/pdf/cpo/Subaru_Certification_Checklist.pdf" title="Certification Checklist" target="_blank">See all 152 points</a><br></li><li>Must
receive a clean CARFAX History Report, meaning it does not contain any
DMV-reported incidents like salvage, fire damage or lemon history. <a href="http://www.carfax.com/" title="Carfax Vehicle Reports" target="_blank">More about CARFAX</a><br></li></ul><h3>Mechanical Breakdown Protection</h3><p>The
benefits of a Certified Pre-Owned Subaru continue long after the
inspection process. They stay with you whenever you drive, wherever you
go.</p><p>All qualifying Certified Pre-Owned vehicles will now receive
a standard 6 year or 100,000 mile Powertrain plan with Roadside
Assistance, a $0 deductible, and Carfax report at no additional cost.</p><p>All
Certified Pre-Owned vehicles are eligible for either a 6 year, 100,000
mile Classic or Gold Plus plan (with Roadside Assistance) at their
choice of a $0, $50, or $100 deductible as an upgrade at additional
cost.<font face="Verdana"><br><a title="REQUEST A TEST DRIVE TODAY!" href="http://www.lasvegas.subaru.com/testdrive/index.htm" target="_self" _nodup="31703" label="REQUEST A TEST DRIVE TODAY!" url="/testdrive/index.htm" imagelink="false"><strong><font face="Tahoma" size="5">REQUEST A TEST DRIVE TODAY!</font></strong></a></font></p><font size="3"><span style="font-family: Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"></span></font><hr>
