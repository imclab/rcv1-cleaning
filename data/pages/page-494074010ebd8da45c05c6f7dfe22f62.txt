<h1>Literature</h1>
<div id="leftCol" class="span-12">
  <div class="box">
    <div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/education/Arts_and_education_partnerships_research">Arts and education partnerships research</a></h4>
  <p class="date">30 January 2012</p>
  <p>Arts Victoria and the Department of Education and Early Childhood Development (DEECD) commissioned research and evaluation of partnerships between schools and the professional arts sector in two stages, generating two reports.</p>
</div>
<hr />
<div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/sponsorship_and_philanthropy/its-a-given">Its a given: Tracking the importance of private giving and sponsorship to key organisations</a></h4>
  <p class="date">08 November 2011</p>
  <p>This report tracks and analyses private sector income of Australia Council funded key organisations for the years 2008 to 2010.</p>
</div>
<hr />
<div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/audiences_and_cultural_participation/get_reading!_2010_campaign_research">Get Reading! 2010 campaign research</a></h4>
  <p class="date">31 May 2011</p>
  <p>Get Reading 2010 commissioned market research into Australians� reading habits.</p>
</div>
<hr />
<div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/policy/a_case_for_literature_the_effectiveness_of_subsidies_to_australian_publishers_1995-2005">A case for literature: the effectiveness of subsidies to Australian publishers 1995-2005</a></h4>
  <p class="date">20 October 2010</p>
  <p>This research report was prepared for the Literature Board of the Australia Council for the Arts by Dr Kath McLean, Dr Louise Poland and Jacinta van den Berg on behalf of the Writing and Society Research Group at the University of Western Sydney in May 2010.</p>
</div>
<hr />
<div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/economy/economic_analysis_of_literary_publishing_in_australia">Economic analysis of literary publishing in Australia</a></h4>
  <p class="date">20 December 2008</p>
  <p>This Australia Council-commissioned research report is a study of the economics of the Australian literary publishing sector.</p>
</div>
<hr />
<div class="list-box">
  <h4><a href="http://www.australiacouncil.gov.au/resources/reports_and_publications/subjects/policy/books_alive_-_campaign_research">Books Alive - campaign research</a></h4>
  <p class="date">15 December 2008</p>
  <p>Books Alive is a $2 million government-funded, month-long nationwide campaign to promote books and reading.</p>
</div>
<hr />
