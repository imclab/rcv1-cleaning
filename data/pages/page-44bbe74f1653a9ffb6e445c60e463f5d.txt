<FONT SIZE="7" FACE="COMIC SANS MS" COLOR="PURPLE">SPECIAL EVENTS</font>
<BR>
<br>
<FONT CLASS=black>

<B>The Art of the Quilt</B>
<a href=http://dept.lamar.edu/cofac/quilt.asp>&nbsp;&nbsp; Click here for more information and a list of events.</a><BR>
Sept. - Nov. 2012<BR>
COFAC is proud to share with the Southeast Texas community an interdisciplinary view into the world of quilting funded in part by the National Endowment for the Arts. The Dishman Art Museum will host Quilt National's acclaimed touring exhibition, and the Department of Theatre & Dance will collaborate with the Mary Morgan Moore Department of Music to present the musical Quilters. Regional and national quilt artists, as well as faculty from the Department of History, will present a variety of workshops during the run of the exhibition. <BR><BR>


<B>Le Grand Bal</B>
<A HREF=http://dept.lamar.edu/cofac/lgb.asp>&nbsp;&nbsp;    Click here for more information.</A><br>
March 23, 2013<BR>
7 p.m. - 12 a.m., Dishman Art Museum and Montagne Center<BR>
Sponsored by Lamar University Friends of the Arts<BR>
Le Grand Bal is a gala evening of art, fine dining, dancing and entertainment presented by Lamar's Friends of the Arts.  The black-tie event is the major annual fund-raiser that benefits the College of Fine Arts and Communication.  Activities include a reception and silent art auction from 7 to 9 p.m. in the Dishman Art Museum, with a gourmet buffet, dancing and entertainment from 8 p.m. to midnight in the Montagne Center. Guests will dance to the music of Beaumont's Night & Day Orchestra, under the direction of Wayne Dyess.
