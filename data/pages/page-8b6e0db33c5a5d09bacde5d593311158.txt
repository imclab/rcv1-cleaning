<h1>An Online Library of Literature</h1>
<hr/>
<p>


    <h2>Read. Learn. Think.</h2>

    <p>Welcome to <i>literature.org</i>. This site is here to try to
    bring real books to people through the Internet.

    <p>On this site you will find the <i>full and unabridged</i>
    texts of classic works of English literature. Fiction from
    authors like <a href="/authors/carroll-lewis/">Lewis Carroll</a>,
    the Bronte sisters (<a href="/authors/bronte-anne/">Anne</a>,
    <a href="/authors/bronte-charlotte/">Charlotte</a> and <a
    href="/authors/bronte-emily/">Emily</a>), <a
    href="/authors/london-jack">Jack London</a>, <a
    href="/authors/twain-mark">Mark Twain</a>, <a
    href="/authors/dickens-charles">Charles Dickens</a> and many
    others, and classic scientific works from <a
    href="/authors/darwin-charles/">Charles Darwin</a> and <a
    href="/authors/descartes-rene/">Rene Descartes</a>.

    <p>More books will be added soon, however as this site is
    maintained by enthusiasts rather than professional librarians
    this may be a very slow process. Our sponsor, the people behind the <a
    href="http://www.knowledge.com/">knowledge.com&trade;</a> directory,
    is currently working on new technology that will make this
    updating easier and quicker in the near future.

    <p>Please let us know what you think, and what new books you
    want added. For those who may not be familiar with Copyright
    law, we are unable to make works available that are not in the
    <i>public domain</i>. This mean, basically, nothing where the
    author has not been dead for at least 75 to 90 years.
