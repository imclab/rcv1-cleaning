It also could spawn a unibody, compact SUV for the U.S. market to compete with the Honda CR-V and the Toyota RAV4. There is no B pillar.<br><br>The pentagonal doors define the look of the Zaroot with its front and rear graphic treatment adding a futuristic twist. Nissan designed the Gullwing doors by creating a shortened door that is only a little deeper than the side window so it can be easily opened especially in tight parking areas..
