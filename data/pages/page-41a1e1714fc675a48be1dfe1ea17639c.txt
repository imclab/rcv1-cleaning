						<p><strong>TIRE AND WHEEL DAMAGE OR WEAR</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Normal tire wear or wear-out is not covered. Tire wear is influenced by many variables such as road conditions, driving styles, vehicle weight, and tire construction.</li>
<li>Uniform tire wear is a normal condition, and is not considered a defect.</li>
<li>Road hazard damage such as punctures, cuts, snags, and breaks resulting from pothole impact, curb impact, or from other objects is not covered.</li>
<li>Tire wear due to misalignment beyond the warranty period is not covered.</li>
<li>Damage from improper inflation, overloading, spinning, as when stuck in mud or snow, tire chains, racing, improper mounting or dismounting, misuse, negligence, alteration, improper repair, accident, collision, fire, vandalism, or misapplication is not covered.</li>
<li>Damage to wheels or tire sidewalls caused by automatic car washes or cleaning agents is not covered.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>DAMAGE DUE TO ACCIDENT, MISUSE, OR ALTERATION</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>The Certified or New Vehicle Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Warranty does not cover damage caused as the result of any of the following: collision, fire, theft, freezing, vandalism, riot, explosion, or objects striking the vehicle.</li>
<li>Misuse of the vehicle such as driving over curbs, overloading, racing, or other competition. Proper vehicle use is discussed in the owner manual.</li>
<li>Alteration, modification, or tampering to the vehicle, including, but not limited to the body, chassis, powertrain, driveline, software, or other components after final assembly by Cadillac.</li>
<li>Coverages do not apply if the odometer has been disconnected, its reading has been altered, or mileage cannot be determined.</li>
<li>Installation of non-GM (General Motors) parts.</li>
<li>Water or fluid contamination.</li>
<li>Damage resulting from hail, floods, windstorms, lightning, and other environmental conditions.</li>
<li>Alteration of glass parts by application of tinting films.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>DAMAGE OR CORROSION DUE TO ENVIRONMENT, CHEMICAL TREATMENTS, OR AFTERMARKET PRODUCTS</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Damage caused by airborne fallout, rail dust, salt from sea air, salt or other materials used to control road conditions, chemicals, tree sap, stones, hail, earthquake, water or flood, windstorm, lightning, the application of chemicals or sealants subsequent to manufacture, etc., is not covered.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>DAMAGE DUE TO INSUFFICIENT OR IMPROPER MAINTENANCE</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Damage caused by failure to follow the recommended maintenance schedule intervals and/or failure to use or maintain proper fluids, or maintain fluids between recommended maintenance intervals, fuel, lubricants, or refrigerants recommended in the owner manual is not covered.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>DAMAGE DUE TO CONTAMINATED OR POOR QUALITY FUEL</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Poor fuel quality or incorrect fuel may cause drivability problems such as hesitation, lack of power, stalling, or failure to start. It may also render gauges inoperable or degrade functionality for components such as spark plugs, oxygen sensors, and the catalytic converter.</li>
<li>Damage from poor fuel quality, water contamination, or incorrect diesel fuel or gasoline may not be covered.<br>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>DAMAGE DUE TO IMPACT, USE, OR THE ENVIRONMENT</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>Windshield or glass cracks, chips, or scratches due to impact are not covered. Windshield cracks will be covered for the first 12 months, regardless of mileage if caused by defects in material or workmanship.<br>
</li>
<li>Lights, lenses, mirrors, paint, grille, moldings, and trim are not covered for cracks, chips, scratches, dents, dings, and punctures or tears as a result of impact with other objects or road hazards.</li>
<li>Cracks, chips, scratches, or other damage to the face of a radio or instrument cluster from impact or foreign objects are not covered.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>MAINTENANCE</strong><br>
</p>
<ul>
<li>All vehicles require periodic maintenance.<br>
</li>
<li>Maintenance services, such as those detailed in the owner manual are, the owner's expense.</li>
<li>Vehicle lubrication, cleaning, or polishing is not covered.</li>
<li>Failure of or damage to components requiring replacement or repair due to vehicle use, wear, exposure, or lack of maintenance is not covered.</li>
<li>The items shown below are several examples of what is considered maintenance, and not covered as part of the Certified Pre-Owned Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Vehicle Warranty:<ul>

<li>Brake Pads/Linings</li>
<li>Clutch Linings</li>
<li>Coolants and Fluids</li>
<li>Filters</li>
<li>Keyless Entry (or other remote transmitter/receiver batteries)<sup>2</sup></li>
<li>Limited Slip Rear Axle Service</li>
<li>Tire Rotation</li>
<li>Wheel Alignment/Balance<sup>3</sup></li>
<li>Wiper Inserts</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Any replacement at the time of, or beyond the 172 Point Inspection period is considered maintenance, and is not covered a part of the Certified Pre-Owned Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Vehicle Warranty.</li>
<li>The Certified Pre-Owned Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Warranty only covers components when replacement or repair of these components is the result of a defect in material or workmanship.</li>

<p><strong>IS MY VEHICLE COVERED FROM RUST, TOO?</strong><br>

<p>Cadillac vehicles are designed and built to resist corrosion. All body and sheet metal components are warranted against rust-through corrosion for 6 years, unlimited mileage. Application of after-manufacture rust-proofing products may create an environment that reduces the corrosion resistance built into your vehicle. Repairs to correct damage caused by such applications are not covered under your CPO or New Vehicle Bumper-to-Bumper Limited Warranty. See your Cadillac dealer for terms of this limited warranty.</p>
