      <h1>School of Fine Art</h1>
<h2>Master of Fine Arts (MFA) Degree in Fine Art</h2>
<p>The MFA curriculum in Fine Art allows students to concentrate on Painting, Printmaking or Sculpture with a focus on technical and conceptual development. The departments provide graduate students with studio space, exhibition galleries and state-of-the-art facilities and equipment.</p>
<p>The resulting student portfolios are critically appraised at the graduate student's Final Review and are professionally exhibited to the fine art community in San Francisco's gallery district.</p>
<strong>Learning Outcomes:</strong>
