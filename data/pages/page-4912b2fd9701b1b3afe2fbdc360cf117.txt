<div class="groupDesc" id="groupDesc">
<p><span style="color : #222">Thank you for your interest in Greater Nashville's Art, Culture, and Literature Meetup.  By joining this meetup, you will get to socialize with other arts &amp; culture loving patrons, attend the events with a group, and participate in our literature discussions.  You’ll also have an opportunity to form some new friendships!  We hope you'll join us!</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">This meetup is for those who appreciate: Art, Literature, Museums, Theater &amp; Performance Art, Musicals, Plays, Cultural attractions, Mansions and gardens, Art galleries, Classical Music, Symphony, Orchestra, Ballet, Dance, Opera, Film, Cinema, Documentaries, and Photography.  We'll also visit local parks like Radnor Lake to appreciate the beauty of nature.</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">We will have regular literature discussions, usually with a screening of a film adaptation.  I ask that you only attend if you have either read the novel at some point in your life including school or will read it by the meetup. The reason is that, unlike some other book groups, we actually spend most of the time discussing the novel and due to limited seating (around 20 in my home), only members who can participate in our discussions should attend.  We will also get together at a restaurant on occasion just to discuss literature, what we're currently reading, and the arts in general.  Our first 3 novels were The Great Gatsby, Pride and Prejudice, and Wuthering Heights.  Breakfast at Tiffany's, the Picture of Dorian Gray, A Christmas Carol, and Emma are coming. </span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">To protect our members privacy and since some meetups are held in members' homes, new members are approved.  If you sign up to attend an event, please update your rsvp if you can no longer attend.</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">Before attending an outdoor activity such as a hike, you need to read:</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222"><a href="http://www.meetup.com/NashvilleArts/pages/Information_You_Should_Read_For_Outdoor_Activities/">http://www.meetup.com/NashvilleArts/pages/Information_You_Should_Read_For_Outdoor_Activities/</a></span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">The types of events we may do include TPAC, Nashville Symphony, Tennessee Repertory Theatre, Nashville Opera, Nashville, Ballet, Circle Players, The Frist Center for the Visual Arts, Belcourt Theatre, Ryman Auditorium, The Nashville Film Festival, Chaffin's Barn Dinner Theatre, Darkhorse Theatre, Cheekwood Botanical Garden &amp; Museum of Art, Artists' Cooperative Theatre, Boiler Room Theatre, Nashville Shakespeare Festival, Theatre Ensemble of Nashville, Zeitgeist Gallery, Stanford Fine Art, Vanderbilt University Fine Arts Gallery, Art at the Arcade, TN State Museum, The Parthenon, Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum, Adventure Science Center, and Sudekum Planetarium.</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">You are encouraged to check out these meetup groups as well where additional arts/culture/book events are scheduled:</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">Book meetups for suspense, Sci-Fi, and horror novels are done under this meetup including 1984 which is the book for September: <a href="http://www.meetup.com/Nashville-Horror/">http://www.meetup.com/Nashville-Horror/</a></span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222">The Joy of Dining Out with Friends - I'm organizer and sometimes schedule arts related events such as the Pineapple Room Restaurant at Cheekwood:</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #222"><a href="http://www.meetup.com/Dining-With-Friends/">http://www.meetup.com/Dining-With-Friends/</a></span></p>
