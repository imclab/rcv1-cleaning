<h3><a href="http://www.coloarts.state.co.us./">Colorado Council on the Arts</a></h3>

<p>Investing in the cultural life of communities across the state for over three decades.</p>

<hr>

<p>Please feel free to check the calendars of events provided by many <a
HREF="../communities_dir/communitiesmenu.html">Colorado Community web sites</a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.artslynx.org/colorado">Artslynx Colorado</a>: Comprehensive listing
of Colorado's cultural institutions online - links to theatre, dance, music, and more.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.artstozoo.org">Arts to Zoo Colorado:</a> Information about hundreds
of Colorado's cultural arts organizations - everything from Arts to Zoo!</p>

<p><a href="http://ColoradoMusic.org">Colorado Music Association</a>: A new music support
organization offering monthly meetings and information. </p>

<p><a href="http://www.dmamusic.org">Denver Musicians Association</a>: A hundred year old
organization of over 1,000 professional musicians from the Rocky Mountain Region. </p>
