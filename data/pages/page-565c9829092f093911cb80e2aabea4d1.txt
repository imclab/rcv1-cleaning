            <h3>Live performances</h3>
            <h4><a href="/mccain/">McCain Performance Series</a></h4>
            <p>Head to McCain Auditorium&rsquo;s performance series for nationally and internationally acclaimed artists.
               Performances range from the Moscow National Ballet to the LA Theatre Works&rsquo; <i>War of the Worlds</i> and
               <i>The Lost World</i> to the casts of <i>Annie</i> and <i>Rain: A Tribute to the Beatles</i>.
            </p>
            <h4><a href="http://cstd.k-state.edu/Theatre/">Theatre</a></h4>
            <p>Performances by K-State theatre and dance students take place in Nichols Theatre, McCain Auditorium, and the Purple Masque Theatre.
               Watch K-State productions of classics like <i>Guys and Dolls</i> or <i>Romeo and Juliet</i> and modern shows like <i>Humble Boys</i>.
            </p>
            <h4><a href="/sctd/dance/">Dance</a></h4>
            <p>Dance concerts include a variety of styles&mdash;including ballet, modern, tap, and jazz&mdash;performed by 
               talented students and choreographed by dance faculty, guest artists, and advanced students.
            </p>
            <h4><a href="/music/events.htm">Music</a></h4>
            <p>Each year music faculty and students present more than 150 events featuring solo, ensemble,
and large group presentations for wind, string, keyboard, and percussion instruments and vocal
presentations that include solo recitals, opera workshop, and choir concerts. Most events are free
and take place in All Faiths Chapel or McCain Auditorium.
            </p>
            <h4><a href="/english/visit/">English</a></h4>
            <p>Enjoy poetry and fiction readings by visiting writers and Department of English faculty and students.
            </p>

            <h3>Galleries and exhibitions</h3>

            <h4><a href="http://beach.k-state.edu/">Beach Museum of Art</a></h4>
            <p>The museum offers an in-depth look at Kansas art and brings art and
               artists from around the world to the heart of Kansas.
            </p>

            <h4><a href="http://capd.k-state.edu/events/chang-gallery/">Chang Gallery</a></h4>
            <p>This College of Architecture, Planning, and Design gallery in Seaton Hall is home to work from students, faculty, and practicing architects.
            </p>

            <h4><a href="http://art.ksu.edu/events/chapman.html">Chapman Gallery</a></h4>
            <p>Guest artist and student and faculty work is on display in this 1,400-square-foot, technology friendly Department of Art gallery in Willard Hall.
            </p>

            <h4><a href="http://art.ksu.edu/exhibitions/kemper-union-gallery/">Kemper Art Gallery</a></h4>
            <p>Exhibits from Master of Fine Arts candidates, visiting artists, alumni, and students
               and faculty rotate in this K-State Student Union gallery.
            </p>
