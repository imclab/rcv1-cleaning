<h1>1936 Chevrolet Standard Coupe</h1>
<br />
<p>This 1936 coupe has the two horizontal louvers on the hood which would indicate this is a standard 5 window coupe.&nbsp; They had more rounded fenders and radiator grills, and steel disc wheels were used this year with safety glass.&nbsp; This little coupe may have been owned by a salesman with its huge trunk to carry samples.&nbsp; It was garage kept and well cared for.&nbsp; This Chevy was body up restored, clad&nbsp;in black paint 15 years ago and has a few patina touch ups but still looks great.&nbsp; Loaded with options: radio, heater, whitewalls, rear spare and more.&nbsp; The interior is tan mohair and in good condition.&nbsp; This car fits good, looks straight and is a good driver.&nbsp; Complete video of this vehicle available for $50, refunded with vehicle purchase, and includes overnight shipping of video.&nbsp; Call our classic car division at 815-385-8408 for video and/or vehicle information.</p>
