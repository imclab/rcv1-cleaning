			<div class="entry">
				<p>The mission of the Humanities and Fine Arts Department is to broaden students’ cultural awareness through the study of artistic expression, philosophical thought, religious beliefs and through exploring individual communication techniques resulting in measurably greater understanding of the world around them.</p>
<p>The Humanities and Fine Arts Department consists of programs in Art, Communication, Drama, Film, Foreign Languages, Music, Religion, and Philosophy.</p>
<p>Our primary purpose is to prepare you with the background necessary to seamlessly transfer to the four-year college of your choice with the assurance you have met all the qualifications for entering with &#8220;junior” status. Our department helps you toward the Associate in Arts, Associate in Science, or Associate in Fine Arts degrees which mark the midpoint of your college career.</p>
