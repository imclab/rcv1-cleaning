<p>The 2013 MTV Video Music Awards held last night at the Barclay’s Center in Brooklyn, New York with performances which got social media buzzing&#8212;-Between the amazing performances from Justin Timberlake and Miley Cyrus &#8211; a bunch of stars were given hat tips and trophies for the mantelpiece.</p>

<p><strong>BEST COLLABORATION</strong></p>

<p><strong>BEST ROCK VIDEO</strong></p>
<p>30 Seconds to Mars, “Up in the Air”</p>
<p><strong>BEST POP VIDEO</strong></p>
<p>Selena Gomez, “Come and Get It”</p>
<p><strong>BEST ART DIRECTION</strong></p>
<p>Janelle Monae feat. Erykah Badu, “Q.U.E.E.N”</p>
<p><strong>BEST DIRECTION</strong></p>
<p>Justin Timberlake, “Suit and Tie”</p>
<p><strong>BEST CHOREOGRAPHY</strong></p>
<p>Bruno Mars, “Treasure”</p>
<p><strong>BEST VISUAL EFFECTS</strong></p>
<p>Capital Cities, “Safe and Sound”</p>
<p><strong>BEST CINEMATOGRAPHY</strong></p>
<p>Macklemore &amp; Ryan Lewis feat. Ray Dalton, “Can’t Hold Us”</p>
<p><strong>BEST EDITING</strong></p>
<p>Justin Timberlake, “Mirrors”</p>
<p><strong>BEST HIP-HOP VIDEO</strong></p>
<p>Macklemore and Ryan Lewis, feat. Ray Dalton, ”Can’t Hold Us”</p>
<p><strong>BEST FEMALE VIDEO</strong></p>
<p>Taylor Swift, “I Knew You Were Trouble”</p>
<p><strong>BEST VIDEO WITH A SOCIAL MESSAGE</strong></p>
<p>Macklemore and Ryan Lewis, feat. Mary Lambert, “Same Love”</p>
<p><strong>BEST SONG OF SUMMER</strong></p>
<p>One Direction, “Best Song Ever”</p>
<p><strong>ARTIST TO WATCH</strong></p>
<p>Austin Mahone</p>
<p><strong>BEST MALE VIDEO</strong></p>
<p>Bruno Mars, “Locked Out of Heaven”</p>
