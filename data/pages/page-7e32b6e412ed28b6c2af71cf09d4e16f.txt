                        <h1 class="title">Music Media and Entertainment Technology (MMET)</h1>
        <div class="tabs"></div>
                 
        <div class="node">
  
  
  
  
  <div class="content"><p><strong>(Formerly the Commercial Recording Arts Department)</strong><br /> <span style="font-size: x-small;"> Our goal is to give students the necessary knowledge to compose, record and perform their own songs. Students will understand the historic and cultural connection that music plays in our current world and we will encourage creativity and innovation through the integration of digital tools for music production and promotion into our standard based curriculum.<br /> <br /> All <span style="font-weight: bold;">MMET</span> students will be encouraged to play the guitar, piano and sing by their senior year. Students will acquire a basic level of music theory and a mastery of <em>Garageband</em>, <em>Logic</em>, <em>Reason</em> &amp; <em>ProTools</em> software. Students will also be exposed to 21st century communication skills and have a multitude of performance opportunities and create a digital portfolio to be presented during their Senior Interview. </span></p>
