<h3>Junk Yard Full of Car Parts</h3>
<p>Housing millions of <strong>used parts</strong>, our seven-acre <strong>junk yard</strong> is the place for professional mechanics and private owners to find the <a href="/auto-parts"><u>auto part</u></a> they need. Most <a href="/auto-parts/used-auto-parts"><u>used parts</u></a> are in excellent condition and have many miles of use left in them, making our <strong>auto salvage</strong> yard a great place to get the most for your money. <p>In addition to our <a href="/auto-parts/pick-and-pull"><u>pick and pull</u></a> program for <strong>car parts</strong>, Orthodox Auto Company proudly provides affordable auto glass repair services such as <a href="/auto-glass/windshield-replacement"><u>windshield replacements</u></a>.Windshields are one of the most important safety features in your car, and replacing one can be costly at other places, but that is not the case at Orthodox. Our inexpensive windshields are installed by our experts and are securely placed as to eliminate water and air leaks or the risk of the <a href="/auto-glass"><u>auto glass</u></a> popping out during an accident.</p>
<h3>Where to Sell a Car in Philly</h3>
<p>If you need to <a href="/buy-my-car/sell-my-car"><u>sell a car</u></a> quickly, Orthodox Auto Company will pay you on the spot for any vehicle, regardless of its condition. The <a href="/buy-my-car/how-it-works"><u>car buying process</u></a> is simple:</p>
<ol>
    <li>Call us and describe your vehicle and its condition; let us know if you have the title.</li>
    <li>Receive an instant, free quote over the phone.</li>
    <li>Schedule a time for us to tow it back to our Philly location (or bring it to us if possible).</li>
    <li>Get paid on the spot.</li>
</ol>
<p>Orthodox Auto Company believes every car has a price tag, and we&rsquo;re willing to pay you what we think yours is worth.</p>
<h3>Car Rentals in Philadelphia</h3>
<p>Our <strong>cash car rentals</strong> are designed to provide those with subprime or no credit the opportunity to drive in style through <strong>Philadelphia</strong>. We offer <a href="/car-rentals/car-rental/cheap-car-rental"><u>cheap car rentals</u></a>, <a href="/car-rentals/car-rental/mid-size-car-rental"><u>mid size rental cars</u></a>, and <a href="/car-rentals/car-rental/full-size-car-rental"><u>full size car rentals</u></a> to all drivers over the age of 21 who can provide proof of insurance, a license, and a cash-down deposit.
