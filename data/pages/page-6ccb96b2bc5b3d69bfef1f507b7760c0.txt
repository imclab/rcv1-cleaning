  <div class="content clearfix">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>Automotive is a very broad term which may include sales, auto body, service, parts, management, and many more. The automotive department at Grand Rapids Community College only instructs in the area of automotive service. The automotive program is a nationally accredited program and instructs in all eight areas listed by mechanic certification. GRCC offers many educational options, which include an 18-week program which covers four out of the eight certification areas, an one-year certificate program which instructs all eight areas, and a two-year Associate Degree program. The Associate Degree program transfers to a Bachelor’s Degree program at Ferris State University which allows students to continue their study in Automotive. If hands on work interests you with highly technical and computerized cars, this career is for you.</p>
<p><strong>Mission Statement</strong></p>
<p>The automotive department at GRCC is open to all students who meet basic academic standards, to attain their goals in automotive training; either to prepare for entry-level positions, advancement in the industry, transfer into bachelor degree programs, or personal enrichment.</p></div></div></div>  </div>
