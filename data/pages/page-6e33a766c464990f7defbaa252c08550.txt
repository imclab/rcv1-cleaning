<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
Arts and Entertainment,Celebrity Gossip News - What do you think ...
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-872873991516871693' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<p>Well it's a bit like security marking your property. a tattoo permanently marks you as someone with poor taste. you might as well have 'CHAV' permanently tattooed across your forehead. it does help the police however. Tattoo's are very unique to individuals, and suspects can be identified easily by their tattoos. so ? if you are a law abiding chav, go ahead and get marked. if you are into crime ? beware ? you are a marked man (or woman). you asked for my honest opinion, so no offence meant to anyone.</p> <p>the only tattoo i'd find tasteful is one that comes from some kind of initiation rite. if its something the girl 'earned' through initiatives of some kind then unless its a sleeve, or some kind of facial monstrosity I'd find it attractive, otherwise if its something they just felt inclined to get whether it be the name of a lost sibling, a piece of poetry, or some kind of imagery its a total turn off. </p> <p> yes i recognise that my attraction is pscyhological because I'd find 'earned' tattoos attractive, I still stand that a tattoo without a solid history behind it is a turn off.</p> <p>It fully depends on the tattoos and the situation. For instance I personally think that people with Kanji (asian characters) they can't read are idiots. anyone can can tell you something means strength when it actually means soup? mostly though, they don't bother me. Tattoos are VERY popular these days so all types of people have them.</p> <p>one small discreet tattoo on an area of the body not normally in view? thats really nice like a small rose on the upper arm but anything else and especially down the legs and lower arms make a person look like either an escaped convict or a criminal ! edit: and why someone would want them all over their face and neck God only knows that looks terrible indeed</p> <p>I hate tattoos on anyone. I think they look ugly and common.</p> <p> My dad was in the army, throughout World War 2, and most soldiers those day got tattoos done. when he died at the age of 81, they looked awful, all wrinkly and saggy (sorry Dad)</p> <p>Hi Skycat,<br />Well,I have 7 tats myself..I am Irish and my tats tell a story about my Irish background..I don't see anything bad about having tattoos..It's body art(if done in good taste)..<br />Your Friend,<br />poppy1</p> <p>eh, whatever, don't care.</p> <p> there's a certain amount of trashiness to having someone's name tattooed on your neck, tho. but i've known a girl who had that who i totally liked and respected, so ? whatever, i don't care.</p> <p>I just think most people get tattoos for the wrong reasons. my little brother has about three now and I'm like "whhhhyyy???" and he's planning more. I just don't understand. Maybe I didn't scare him enough as a child?</p> <p>I would not have one myself but I know plenty of people who do have them. I don't think I have a right to comment on what other people choose to do with their bodies.</p> <p>I don't care all that much for them. They tend to look low class most of the time. i guess it all depends on what you get, where at, how big, etc.</p> <p>I think if they are small and on a tasteful part of the body, they can be nice. but I'm not a huge fan of tattoo sleeves, tramp stamps, or facial tattoos.</p> <p>I think when you get them on your face or anywhere else easy viewable you are a dumb a@@</p> <p> It really does handicap you in life</p> <p>i have a few tattoos. people from every walk of life have tats. its not just one type of person.</p> <p>I hate tatoo's i think that they are super tacky. I don't mind the small tatoo's tho, that are not visible when your outside etc.</p> <p>Well, it depends on what tattoos they have. Tattoos can say a lot about a person.</p> <p>I like them but i dont like it when they have it all over their bodys i think thats just gross</p> <p>I think they look dreadful.</p> <p>I have never seen any one with one that it did not make them look like trash</p> <p>I don't like them</p> <p> but i think if there are a few(and not a lot) it's bearable</p> <p>I don't like it especially on a female, but it's not my body</p> <p>I don't mind them, I have two.</p> <p>i like the art work , its very clever and artistic? people who have them ? well i dont people on what they have ?</p> <p>Hope they make it to heaven. you are suppose to respect your temple, putting ink on it especially permanent is disrespecting yourself.</p> <p>so unpure white men who like black women like then pure and virgin like!!!!!</p><p>Source: <a href="http://www.freegby.com/what-do-you-think-about-tattoos-and-the-people-who-have-them-be-perfectly-honest.html">http://www.freegby.com/what-do-you-think-about-tattoos-and-the-people-who-have-them-be-perfectly-honest.html</a></p><p><a href="http://cyberionsystems.com/swg/forum/index.php?action=profile;u=537580">army</a> <a href="http://www.ulastirmasurasi.org/forum/index.php?action=profile;u=285954">shell</a> <a href="http://kingofkings3.gamigo.de/forum/member.php?u=285610">piers morgan</a> <a href="http://hellascert.gr/forum/profile.php?mode=viewprofile&u=538248">avm</a> <a href="http://traffictoplist.com/board/index.php?action=profile;u=873878">21</a> <a href="http://www.politicalhotwire.com/hotwireold/member.php?u=169608">americas got talent</a> <a href="http://yaleherald.com/val//profile.php?mode=viewprofile&u=576184">gang of six</a> </p>
