<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
?Female Fan Walks Out on Dbanj During His Performance? 
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-3777877466560392627' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<img alt="Dbanj (2)" height="266" src="http://www.omg.com.ng/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Dbanj-2.jpg" width="400" /><br />
Reports have it that Wande Coal and Wizkid did not attend the much publicized DKM concert even though there were prior speculations that these artistes would be present at the concert.<br />
How a female fan embarrassed D&#8217;banj:<br />
While Dbanj was performing one of his new tracks, he climbed down the stage and went round to say hi to his fans. Ladies were so excited as they happily danced with him.<br />
<a name='more'></a><br />
He reportedly moved round till he stopped by in front a certain pretty lady dressed in a blue dress in front, close to the stage.<br />
Apparently, the lady was not in the mood to dance but Dbanj did not let her be. Dbanj then tried pulling her up but she insisted that she wasn&#8217;t dancing, so he forced her up and began to drag her towards the stage.<br />
The lady&#8217;s husband approached Dbanj to stop him. He told him, &#8220;she is my wife, she can&#8217;t dance up stage with you&#8221; and Dbanj responded with &#8220;oh, I didn&#8217;t know she was married, I&#8217;m so sorry&#8221; but the lady was already pissed off.<br />
Her husband led her away from the VVIP section to the regular visitors section but after some minutes, they both stood up and left the hall.<br />
Though Dbanj got another lady who was able to do all the bending, twisting, shaking and whinning up stage with him &#8211; that didn&#8217;t stop him from performing to expectation and besides not many people noticed except those in front!
