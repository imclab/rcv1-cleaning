<strong>SUMMARY:</strong> Features a freshly rebuilt and correct date-coded 396cid/325hp with a rebuilt M20 4-speed transmission with original shifter. Complete nut and bolt, factory correct frame-off auto restoration with bucket seats and a black interior. Exterior color is marina blue.<br />

<strong>AUTO RESTORATION DETAILS:</strong> Features a 396/325hp engine with a 4-speed transmission, bucket seats and black interior. A complete nut and bolt, factory correct frame-off auto restoration finished in Marina Blue with correct dated parts. The restoration was completed in December 2008 and has been driven less than 25 miles since. All of the suspension components have been replaced. A freshly rebuilt and correct date-coded 396cid/325hp is matched to a rebuilt M20 4-speed transmission with original shifter. This Chevelle features a correct and completely rebuilt 12-bolt rear end with posi-traction and 3.31 gears. 15&#215;7 Rally wheels are mounted on 245/60/15 BFGoodrich tires in the front and 255/60/15 in the rear.
