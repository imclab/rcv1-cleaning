      <h2 class="title"><a href="/news/2013-05-27/winners-of-first-automotive-grade-linux-user-experience-contest-announced">Winners of first Automotive Grade Linux User Experience Contest Announced</a></h2>
  
  
      <div class="meta">
              <span class="submitted">
          Submitted by Rudolf J Streif on Mon, 2013-05-27 20:42        </span>
      
          </div>
  
  <div class="content">
    <p>During his keynote at the Automotive Linux Summit 2013 Spring in Tokyo Matt Jones, Senior Technical Specialist Infotainment for Jaguar Land Rover and Vice President of GENIVI, announced the winners of the first AGL User Experience Contest:</p>
<ul>
<li>Ford Motor Company - Best New Feature</li>
<li>Reaktor - Best Visual Appearance</li>
<li>Tata Elxsi - Best User Experience</li>
</ul>
<p>The successful winners had less than six weeks from the announcement of the contest and the submission deadline. The contest was designed to be a creative and innovative challenge. The winners truly lived up to the challenge.</p>
<p><a style="font-size: 15px;" href="http://automotive.linuxfoundation.org/2013-agl-user-experience-contest-winners">Read more</a><span style="font-size: 15px;">.</span></p>

      <h2 class="title"><a href="/news/2013-05-13/two-more-weeks-to-automotive-linux-summit-2013-spring">Two More Weeks to Automotive Linux Summit 2013 Spring</a></h2>
  
  
      <div class="meta">
              <span class="submitted">
          Submitted by Rudolf J Streif on Mon, 2013-05-13 11:43        </span>
      
          </div>
  
  <div class="content">
    <p>Automotive Linux Summit 2013 Spring is almost here! In just two more weeks doors will open to our third Automotive Linux Summit. We are back in Japan again like for the inaugural Automotive Linux Summit in November 2011. We have a great lineup of expert speakers presenting on a broad variety of topics related to Linux and open source in automotive applications. See the full <a href="http://events.linuxfoundation.org/events/automotive-linux-summit-spring/schedule" target="_blank">schedule</a>.</p>
<p>GENIVI and many vendors will be present to showcase their solutions.</p>
<p>Please visit the <a href="http://events.linuxfoundation.org/events/automotive-linux-summit-spring" target="_blank">event site</a> for more information and to sign up.</p>
