         <th bgcolor="#333333" scope="col"><b>Books and Author  Trivia </b></th>
       </tr>
     </table>
     <p>*There are two types of literature: <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fiction">fiction</a> and <a href="http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/nonfiction">nonfiction</a>. Within the categories of fiction and nonfiction are categories called <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Literary_genre">literary genre</a>.</p>
     <p>*In the times of Ancient Egypt, Ancient Greece, and Ancient Rome, books as we know them today did not exist. Instead books were written on <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scroll">scrolls</a>. The scroll was awkward because of its size and the fact that it had to be unrolled to read the words.</p>
     <p>*The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scroll">codex</a> was invented around 100 A.D. This consisted of a stack of pages stitched together on the side with a cover to protect the pages. The books we read today are much like the codex.</p>
     <p>*Today many people who like to read do so by using a modern technology, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E-book_reader">electronic e-readers</a>. E-readers are easy to carry around, and allow readers to download books, magazines, newspapers, and more from the internet. The <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smartphone">smartphone</a> is also a new technology which allows readers to download and read books and more.</p>
     <p>*The <a href="http://www.ala.org/alsc/awardsgrants/bookmedia/caldecottmedal/caldecottmedal">Caldecott Medal</a> is awarded once a year to the author of the best children's illustrated (picture) book.</p>
     <p>*The <a href="http://aalbc.com/books/related.htm">Coretta Scott King Award</a> is awarded once a year to an author and an artist whose books for children and young adults encourage expression of the African American experience.</p>
     <p>*The <a href="http://www.ala.org/yalsa/printz">Michael L. Printz Award</a> is awarded once a year to the author who has written the best literature for young adults.</p>
     <p>*The <a href="http://www.ala.org/alsc/awardsgrants/bookmedia/newberymedal/newberymedal">Newbery Medal</a> is awarded once a year to an artist who has written the best children's literature book.</p>
