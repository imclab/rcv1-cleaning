								<h2><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/udon/devil-survivor-official-material-collection-now-in-stores-14-page-preview/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to DEVIL SURVIVOR: OFFICIAL MATERIAL COLLECTION &#8211; Now in stores! 14 Page Preview!">DEVIL SURVIVOR: OFFICIAL MATERIAL COLLECTION &#8211; Now in stores! 14 Page Preview!</a></h2>
								<small>August 26th, 2013 <!-- by ChrisButcher --></small> 
								<div class="entry">
									<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/preview-DevilSurvivor1_001.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-6441" alt="preview-DevilSurvivor1_001" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/preview-DevilSurvivor1_001-1024x728.jpg" width="100%" /></a></p>
<p><strong>DEVIL SURVIVOR: OFFICIAL MATERIAL COLLECTION</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/201305_DevilSurvivor_OfficialMaterialCollection.jpg"><img align="right" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-5738" style="border: 0px; margin: 5px 8px;" alt="201305_DevilSurvivor_OfficialMaterialCollection" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/201305_DevilSurvivor_OfficialMaterialCollection-214x300.jpg" width="214" height="300" /></a>In seven days, Tokyo will fall. Demons have invaded the city, and unless a group of teenagers do something about it, fear and chaos will reign!</p>
<p><em>Devil Survivor: Official Material Collection</em> is the ultimate companion to the hit role playing game, a part of the popular &#8216;Shin Megami Tensei&#8217; series of games that also spawned the smash-hit &#8216;Persona&#8217; series! Although not quite as well known as Persona, the game series packs a wallop with a deep storyline and gorgeous, engaging artwork! Featuring character designs and illustrations by Suzuhito Yasuda, the designer of anime and manga series including <em>Durarara!!, Yozakura Quartet, </em>and even <em>Digimon World Re:Digitize!  </em></p>
<p><em>Devil Survivor: Official Material Collection </em>is an amazing and expansive book. It includes character designs, promotional artwork, storyboards, a hyper-detailed demon guide, and even a series of extensive location designs set around real-world Tokyo! Plus, the book contains  interviews with Designer Suzuhito Yasuda, Director Shinjiro Takada, Scenario Writer Yoh Haduki, and Art Director Akiko Kotoh. Go behind the scenes of the creation of this amazing game!</p>
<p><em>Keep reading to check out a 14-page preview of this book!</em></p>

<h2 style="text-align: center;"><strong>25 YEARS OF MEGA MAN!</strong></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2 style="text-align: center;">UDON celebrates with the Comic-Con debut art book<br />
MM25: Mega Man &amp; Mega Man X Official Complete Works</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h4 style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://udonentertainment.us2.list-manage2.com/track/click?u=067140197ebcd2ac868ab3d6e&amp;id=b6ed10424e&amp;e=902a3a6a66" target="_blank">www.UDONentertainment.com</a> – Booth #4529</h4>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>JULY 8th, 2013 —</strong>This year at Comic-Con, UDON will be celebrating the 25th Anniversary of legendary video game icon MEGA MAN in style,<strong> with the beautiful new art book MM25: MEGA MAN &amp; MEGA MAN X OFFICIAL COMPLETE WORKS.</strong>Chronicling 25 years of the Mega Man series, the book will make its English-language debut at the UDON booth, #4529, in a beautiful<strong> Comic-Con exclusive blue laser foil hard cover edition</strong>!</p>
<p><strong>MM25: Mega Man &amp; Mega Man X Official Complete Works </strong>is an in-depth chronicle of the amazing art created across 25 years of games in the Mega Man series by the good folks at Capcom! Featuring over 400 pages of official game art, concept designs and sketches, rare pin-ups, and all-new tribute art, this is a stunning art book collection that every fan of the<em> Blue Bomber </em>will want on their shelves. UDON Entertainment has faithfully translated the entire book into English, including production notes, creator comments, and three all-new exclusive interviews with the creators from across the history of the series!</p>
<p><strong>In a Comic-Con Exclusive, UDON Entertainment will debut a very limited, hard-cover edition of MM25 at San Diego Comic-Con International, July 17th-21st. </strong>This limited edition will feature high-quality hardcover binding, a beautiful deluxe blue laser foil dust jacket, and special &#8220;schematic design&#8221; varnishing under the dust jacket! This item will be available from the UDON booth, #4529, on a first-come, first-served basis for $100.</p>

								<h2><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/udon/udon-entertainment-announces-6-comic-con-exclusives/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to UDON ENTERTAINMENT ANNOUNCES 6 COMIC-CON EXCLUSIVES!">UDON ENTERTAINMENT ANNOUNCES 6 COMIC-CON EXCLUSIVES!</a></h2>
								<small>June 21st, 2013 <!-- by ChrisButcher --></small> 
								<div class="entry">
									<p>Announced <a href="http://www.comic-con.org/exclusives/udon-entertainment" target="_blank">today</a>, <strong>UDON Entertainment</strong> will have an amazing range of exclusive and rare new books at this year&#8217;s San Diego Comic-Con International! These exciting new editions span some of our most popular licenses, including Capcom&#8217;s <strong><em>Mega Man</em></strong><em> </em>and <strong><em>Street</em> Fighter</strong>, and Double Fine Productions&#8217; <strong><em>Brutal</em><em> Legend</em></strong>.</p>
<p>After the sell-out success of Comic-Con 2012&#8242;s <em>Marvel vs. Capcom: Official Complete Works Limited Edition HC, </em>the UDON crew has worked hard to come up with a range of amazing editions that will debut at San Diego, and if they sell-0ut there, they&#8217;re gone for good!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/IMG_4894.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6210 aligncenter" alt="IMG_4894" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/IMG_4894-889x1024.jpg" width="480" height="553" /></a></p>
<p><strong>San Diego Comic-Con Exclusive:</strong></p>
<p><strong>MM25: MEGA MAN &amp; MEGA MAN X OFFICIAL COMPLETE WORKS LIMITED EDITION HARDCOVER</strong></p>
<p>Celebrating 25 years of the Blue Bomber, <em><strong>MM25</strong></em> is the art book that fans have been clamoring for! Weighing in at over 400 pages, this book captures the art and design behind every iteration of the classic Mega Man and Mega Man X characters, including character art, concept sketches, game covers, rare pinups, over 100 pages of material never-before-published in English! <strong>Exclusively for San Diego Comic-Con, this special edition hardcover features a beautiful deluxe blue laser foil dust jacket, and amazing hard-cover production with awesome &#8220;schematic&#8221; design.</strong> $100</p>
<p>&#8211;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/J-C-S_TEASER-800px.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6213 aligncenter" alt="J-C-S_TEASER-800px" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/J-C-S_TEASER-800px.jpg" width="480" height="342" /></a></p>
<p><strong>San Diego Comic-Con Exclusive:</strong></p>
<p><strong>STREET FIGHTER CONVENTION EDITION &#8220;VS.&#8221; Hardcovers! </strong></p>
<p><strong>UDON&#8217;s fan-favourite comics set in the <em>Street Fighter </em>universe return, with brand new stories and amazing convention-exclusive editions!</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_akuma.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6216 aligncenter" alt="sdcc_akuma" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_akuma.jpg" width="497" height="315" /></a></p>
<p>-<strong> San Diego Comic-Con will see the debut of </strong><em><strong>Street Fighter Origins: Akuma,</strong> </em>the long-awaited origin story of the most terrifying fighter in the world! Featuring story by Chris Sarracini and art by Joe Ng, this is the book that die-hard fans have been waiting for. At SDCC, fans will be able to get the exclusive &#8220;VS.&#8221; Convention edition, featuring an awesome Akuma vs. Gouken dustjacket illustration by James Ghio, with a bonus &#8220;Sakura on the beach&#8221; image on the reverse! $40</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_classic.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6215 aligncenter" alt="sdcc_classic" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_classic.jpg" width="497" height="315" /></a></p>
<p>- <strong>Start the UDON <em>Street Fighter </em>saga from the beginning, with <em>Street Fighter Classic Volume 1!</em> </strong>Collecting material from the first 10 issues of UDON&#8217;s <em>Street Fighter </em>series, this brand new oversized hard cover edition of the material has amazing printing, great binding, and will match up on your shelves with our new <em>Origins </em>and <em>Super Street Fighter </em>graphic novels! This Comic-Con exclusive features brand-new Ryu vs. Sagat dust jacket illustration by James Ghio, with hidden &#8220;Chun-li on the Beach&#8221; image inside! $40</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_super1.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6214 aligncenter" alt="sdcc_super1" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/sdcc_super1.jpg" width="493" height="315" /></a></p>
<p>- <strong>Fast forward to the all-new future of Street Fighter in <em>Super Street Fighter Volume 1: New</em><em> Generation!</em></strong><em> </em><em>Super Street Fighter</em> moves the timeline forward to the fan-fav Street Fighter III<em> </em>era, but, with all of the popular Street Fighter IV characters included for the first time! Exclusive to Comic-Con International, this limited edition features a brand new Guile vs. Juri dust jacket illustration by James Ghio, with hidden &#8220;Juri on the Beach&#8221; image inside, and can be signed by UDON artists during the convention! $60</p>
<p>&#8211;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/SDCC2013_UDON_BRSLIP.jpg"><img class="wp-image-6217 aligncenter" alt="SDCC2013_UDON_BRSLIP" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/SDCC2013_UDON_BRSLIP.jpg" width="480" height="378" /></a></p>
<p><strong>San Diego Comic-Con Exclusive: </strong></p>
<p><strong>THE ART OF BRUTAL LEGEND: HEAVY METAL SLIPCASE EDITION</strong></p>
<p>More Metal than Metal, this stunning tome of concept art and design work from the<strong> Double Fine Studios game Brutal Legend</strong> is guaranteed to blow art book fans away! <strong>The Comic-Con International Limited Edition features an exclusive heavy-duty silver metallic slip case,</strong> perfect to protect this collectible item no matter how much head-banging it inspires! Truly awesome! $80</p>
<p>&#8211;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/wow_tribute_hc.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-6218 aligncenter" alt="wow_tribute_hc" src="http://www.udonentertainment.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/wow_tribute_hc.jpg" width="497" height="649" /></a></p>
<p><strong>San Diego Comic-Con Exclusive:</strong></p>
<p><strong>WORLD OF WARCRAFT TRIBUTE: LIMITED EDITION HARDCOVER</strong></p>
<p><strong>UDON Entertainment&#8217;s new <em>World of Warcraft Tribute</em> artbook</strong> pays homage to Blizzard&#8217;s popular massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) with fantastic artwork from an international selection of artists! <strong>The hardcover edition is a Comic-Con exclusive and features a great hardcover binding, gold foil stamping, and stunning exclusive artwork by Blizzard&#8217;s own Arnold Tsang</strong>, who will be signing at the UDON booth during the convention! $100.00</p>
<p>&#8211;</p>
<p>Keep checking this page for more updates on UDON&#8217;s plans for San Diego Comic-Con International 2013!</p>
