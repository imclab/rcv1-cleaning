									<p>Try focusing on one illustrator or one style of art for one week. Study this style in depth by examining as many books that feature this style of illustration as you can find. Compare and contrast this style with other styles the children may have seen or studied. One common illustrator that children are intrigued by is Eric Carle. His tissue paper collage art makes an impact in his books, such as "The Very Hungry Caterpillar" and "The Grouchy Ladybug." After studying Carle's style, allow children to try creating art in this manner. Provide lots of tissue paper squares, scissors, glue and space to create.
