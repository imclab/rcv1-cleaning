<blockquote>
<img src="/pix/const/updating-s.gif" width="130" height="100" border="1" align="right">
<font size=+2>T</font>he art scene in Malta is very varied
and relatively intense for the size of the population. As with many other
aspects of Maltese society, the sacred and the profane are intertwined
to offer a hybrid culture which is unique not only to the mediterranean but also to this
long standing archipelago. The Maltese also expand their energy on art
related amusements and popular entertainments such as the cinemas and
dance clubs which are scattered all over the islands.
</blockquote>
