				Your Future In Automotive was created by FISITA: the International Federation of Automotive Engineering Societies, to help students and young people everywhere find out more about studying engineering at university and about the many exciting career opportunities for engineers in today’s automotive industry. <a id="homeMore">More</a>			</div>
			<div class="homeHidden" id="homeHidden">
				<div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>Your Future In Automotive was created by FISITA: the International Federation of Automotive Engineering Societies, to help students and young people everywhere find out more about studying engineering at university and about the many exciting career opportunities for engineers in today’s automotive industry.</p>
<p>FISITA was founded in Paris in 1948. Today our members are the national engineering societies in 37 countries, together with 60 of the leading vehicle manufacturers, suppliers, energy companies and technology providers who belong to our Honorary Committee.</p>
<p>In total, we represent more than 185,000 automotive engineers around the world.</p>
<p>FISITA organises the biennial World Automotive Congress, as well as the annual World Automotive Summit – where leaders from the auto industry come together with scientists and policy makers to plan the future of the automobile in society. </p>
<p>We have a FISITA magazine: ATZautotechnology; we publish technical papers and organise programmes for students and young engineers to help them gain practical experience and contacts within the global auto industry.   </p>
<p>We are a non-profit, non-governmental association. Our mission is to share knowledge among the world's automotive engineers and contribute to the development of automotive technology world-wide.</p>
<p>By helping engineers from different nations to work together, we help to move the automotive industry and the engineering profession closer to our shared goal of safe, sustainable, efficient and affordable mobility for all.</p>
