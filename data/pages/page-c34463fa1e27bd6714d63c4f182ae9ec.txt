<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">

<br />
Beyonce fans are outraged over the pop superstar's budding friendship with Kanye West's new girlfriend Kim Kardashian. <br />
<a name='more'></a><br />
<blockquote>
Beyoncé and Kim Kardashian hung out over the weekend and RadarOnline.com has exclusively learned the Single Ladiessinger's fans are outraged.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
The two were front and center at Jay-Z andKanye West's Birmingham, U.K. "Watch The Throne" concert supporting their significant others, and the two girls bonding over their men has already put a kink in Beyoncé's reputation.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
"Kim Kardashian is not the sort of girlfriendthat Beyoncé fans envision the new motherhaving," an insider told RadarOnline.com.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
"Beyoncé has an air of class and sophistication to her, while Kim is famous because of a sex tape and a failed marriage."</blockquote>
<blockquote>
But with Beyoncé's husband and Kim's boyfriend touring together, the two girls are sure to be spending more time together and it won't do any favors for Beyoncé's reputation.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
"Beyoncé and Kim Kardashian in the same picture let alone the same sentence is tragic," one fan tweeted.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
Another fan tweeted, "if your role model is Kim Kardashian, I can't take you seriously, Beyoncé is a way better role model. She's respectful of herself."</blockquote>
<blockquote>
"Beyoncé tries to stay out of the tabloids, while they're Kim's livelihood," the source said.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
"It's an odd pairing."</blockquote>
