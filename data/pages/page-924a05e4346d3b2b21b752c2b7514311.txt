        <div id="matrix_1409855504" class="sortable-matrix" data-matrixId="1409855504"><div class="n"><h1>Casa Grande Valley Fine Arts Association (CGVFAA)</h1></div><div class="n"><p> </p>
<p>Welcome to the website for the Casa Grande Valley Fine Art Association! We are a nonprofit organization and our mission is to promote and encourage all forms of fine art in the Casa
Grande Valley area.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Membership is open to anyone – professional and amateur fine artists in any medium, or just plain enthusiasts for the local arts scene. To learn more see <a href="http://cgvfaa.org/become-a-member/">Become a Member</a> .</p>
<p> </p>
<p>CGVFAA provides members the following opportunities to show and sell their art:</p>
<ul>
<li>Art in the Alley – Opportunity to show their art with other CGVFAA members inside at Casa Grande Main Street’s monthly street fair in historic downtown Casa Grande.</li>
<li>Artist of the Month – Each month, a different member is highlighted at Art in the Alley, and their art and bio is featured in the Casa Grande Dispatch.</li>
<li>Fall Art Show and Sidewalk Sale.</li>
<li><a href="http://www.southwesternpremierartists.com/show.php?p=dx3" target="_blank">Spring Fine Art Explosion</a> – In conjunction with Southwestern Premier Artists, a two-day outdoor
festival of fine art, food and entertainment in Casa Grande’s Peart Park.</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<p>To learn more about these opportunities see <a href="http://cgvfaa.org/events/">Events</a>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Members can also show and sell their art on our website in our <a href="http://cgvfaa.org/member-galleries/">Member Galleries</a>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>CGVFAA provides opportunities for members to network and learn from each other at our <a href="http://cgvfaa.org/events/events-meetings/">monthly  meetings</a>. Thinking about joining us? You’re welcome
to attend!</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>MONTHLY MEETINGS</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><strong>When:</strong>   Second Monday of each month (Sept-June)</span></p>
<p><span><strong>Where:</strong>  In a Pear Tree, back room</span></p>
<p><span>             412 N Florence St in Historical Downtown</span></p>
<p><span>             Casa Grande, AZ﻿</span></p>
<p><span><strong>Time:</strong>    6pm﻿</span></p>
