  <h2>Fine and Performing Arts</h2>

  <div class="meta">
  
  </div>

  <div class="content"><h4>
	Departments:</h4>
<div align="center">
	<a href="/creative-writing">Creative Writing</a>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<a href="/dance">Dance</a>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<a href="/music">Music</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="/theatre">Theatre &amp; Film</a>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a href="/visual-arts">Visual Arts</a>
	<hr />
</div>
<p><strong>Fine &amp; Performing Arts Mission Statement:</strong></p>
<p>The Division of Fine &amp; Performing Arts at Paradise Valley Community College provides courses and special events as well as an &quot;artists&#39; community&quot; for students at all levels who are planning avocations and/or vocations in the artistic fields of Creative Writing, Dance, Music, Theatre and Visual Arts.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Division of Fine &amp; Performing Arts values the creative process and the creative product as well the student&#39;s individual creative &quot;voice.&quot; The Division is devoted to the idea of interdependence of work in the arts with the broader worlds of ideas and action which will, ultimately, lead to a vibrant and responsible culture.&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Objectives:</strong></p>
<p>&bull; to develop a personal sense of aesthetics, including a conscious awareness of discipline specific basics, aesthetic fundamentals, aesthetic application and aesthetic principles&nbsp;</p>
<p>&bull; to develop and enhance a conscious awareness and respect for others&#39; ways of thinking,&nbsp;<br />
	working and expressing themselves&nbsp;</p>
<p>&bull; to develop and enhance various artistic methods of problem solving, including diverse approaches&nbsp;<br />
	to expressive, analytical and developmental tools to every human situation&nbsp;</p>
<p>&bull; to develop and enhance an awareness of how the arts reflect and reveal cultures</p>
<p>&bull; to develop and enhance our students&#39; ability to understand the human experience, both past and present</p>
