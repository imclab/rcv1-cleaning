                <h1>Arts &amp; Entertainment in Naples</h1>
                <h2>The sophisticated, successful Naples' lifestyle is reflected in the city's many art galleries and select theaters. From museum quality pieces to unique home accessories to fine works by local artists, you will find a wide variety of exceptional art. A world renowned center for the arts highlights the theatrical opportunities, and popular entertainment is found throughout the town. 
</h2>
