                <h3>Diverse Ideas and Forms of Expression</h3>
                
                <p><span class="lead">Indiana University and <a href="../about/bloomington.shtml">Bloomington</a> squeeze a city's worth of culture into a college town, bringing together a wealth of cultural resources and the perspectives and talents of students from all 50 states and more than 125 countries.</span></p>
<p>IU Bloomington's renowned music school, world-class performance spaces and museums, and outstanding programs in fine arts and theater and drama represent just a few of the opportunities for you to experience diverse ideas and forms of expression&#8212;and to create your own.</p>
<h4>Cultural Centers, Events, and Resources</h4>
<dl>
<dt><a href="https://onestart.iu.edu/ccl-prd/GroupCalendar.do?methodToCall=publicCalendar&amp;pubCalId=GRP1445">Arts and Entertainment Events Calendar</a></dt>
<dt><a href="http://artsfest.indiana.edu/">113 Days of Art</a></dt>
<dd>A summer-long series of music, cinema, theatre, art, and dance</dd>
<dt><a href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7eacc/">Asian Culture Center</a></dt>
<dd>Provides student resources, educational support, and community outreach to promote awareness and understanding of Asian cultures</dd>
<dt><a href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7efnecc/">First Nations Educational and Cultural Center</a></dt>
<dd>Provides educational opportunities that further awareness of Native American people</dd>
<dt><a href="http://imu.indiana.edu/">Indiana Memorial Union (IMU)</a></dt>
<dd>A hub of campus activity, featuring bowling, movies, concerts, lectures, adventure trips, creative classes, and a hotel and conference center</dd>
<dt><a href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7elacasa/">Latino Cultural Center (La Casa)</a></dt>
<dd href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7eintlserv/ic/">Offers community, academic, and service programs to promote awareness and understanding of Latino/Latina culture</dd>
<dt><a href="http://www.indiana.edu/%7enmbcc/">Neal-Marshall Black Culture Center</a></dt>
<dd>Offers educational, cultural, and social programs that promote awareness about the Black experience</dd>
</dl>
