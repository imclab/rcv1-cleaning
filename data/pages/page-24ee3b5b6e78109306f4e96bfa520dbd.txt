<h1>Automotive Schools and Colleges</h1>
<p class="blurb"><img border="0" class="floatLeft" src="http://www.trade-schools.net/graphics/automotive.jpg" alt="Automotive Schools" />With proper training from one of these automotive schools, you can become more than just a grease monkey; you can become one of the most skilled and knowledgeable people in the garage. By choosing to pursue formal automotive training, you could soon be a valued worker in a field that requires qualified technicians to keep up with dynamic work environments. The business of servicing cars and trucks is changing regularly as new models emerge and more sophisticated and complex technologies take hold.</p>

<p class="blurb">Plus, it's becoming more and more difficult to learn everything you need to know from on-the-job training alone, making automotive trade schools that much more valuable. By attending an automotive trade school, you can learn to service all aspects of passenger cars and heavy-duty trucks, from transmissions to engines to brakes.</p>

<p class="blurb">You could soon have the necessary training to pursue entry-level positions in service bays, dealerships, transport companies, and more. Automotive trade schools can also give you the chance to test your skills through a real-world practicum before graduation, providing you with the opportunity to make contacts in the industry.</p>

<p class="blurb">You have the keys to a successful future at your fingertips. Request more information from the automotive schools near you today!</p>
