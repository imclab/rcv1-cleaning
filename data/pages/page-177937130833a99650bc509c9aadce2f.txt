			<h2>  George Clooney speechless Against Gay Fan</h2>
 


              
  
    <center></center> 
			<div class="entry">

 			  <p><a href="http://judyhalone.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/George-Clooney.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-2696" title="George Clooney" src="http://judyhalone.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/09/George-Clooney-300x198.jpg" alt="George Clooney" width="300" height="198" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://judyhalone.com/category/celebrity/george-clooney-celebrity/"><strong>George Clooney</strong></a> — who is widely considered one of Hollywood’s sexiest actors — was left speechless by a gay fan who stripped off and asked him for sex. The 48-year-old actor was promoting new movie <em>The Men Who Stare at Goats</em> Tuesday when the infatuated admirer declared his feelings.<span id="more-2695"></span></p>
<p>The unidentified male stood up in the press conference and said, “I am gay, George, and I think I am in love with you… Please take me.”</p>
<p>He then proceeded to strip to his underwear, leaving his tie in place, and added, “May I kiss you, please. Just one kiss?”</p>
<p>Taking it all in his stride, George calmly replied, “It’s hard when you take a big chance and it doesn’t really work.</p>
<p>“It’s always embarrassing when you take one real big swing for the fences and it just falls flat. It was a good try, though. You know, the tie looks good, and it’s just long enough.”</p>
<p>In the film, George plays Lynn Cassady, a man who claims the CIA has a secret, psychic unit who are working to end war.</p>
<p>Fielding questions about his private life during the conference, George — who is dating Italian TV presenter Elisabetta Canalis — also joked he was planning a same-sex wedding.</p>
<p>Referring to<em> The Men Who Stare at Goats</em> director <strong>Grant Heslov</strong>, George said, “Grant and I are actually announcing our wedding while we are here. I don’t quite know how to answer that question, but I can read your mind again and I know what you’re thinking.”</p>
