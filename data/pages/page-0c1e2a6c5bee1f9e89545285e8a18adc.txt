                    <h1><span class="comment-count"><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/2013/03/01/obama-mixes-up-star-wars-with-star-trek-twitter-geeks-explode/#comments" rel="nofollow" style="font-weight:bold;">6</a></span><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/2013/03/01/obama-mixes-up-star-wars-with-star-trek-twitter-geeks-explode/">Obama Mixes Up Star Wars with Star Trek; Twitter Geeks Explode</a></h1>
                    <p><a rel="attachment wp-att-125714" href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/2013/03/01/obama-mixes-up-star-wars-with-star-trek-twitter-geeks-explode/picard-facepalm/"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-125714" title="picard-facepalm" src="http://cdn.evilbeetgossip.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/picard-facepalm.jpg" alt="picard facepalm star trek meme" width="450" height="301" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/barack-obama/" target="_blank">President Obama</a> offended thousands of <em><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/star-trek/" target="_blank">Star Trek</a></em> AND <em><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/star-wars/" target="_blank">Star Wars</a></em> nerds by mixing up the franchise&#8217;s popular references.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/obamas-jedi-mindmeld-star-wars-425553" target="_blank">The Hollywood Reporter</a> explains his gaffe,</p>
<blockquote><p>Addressing reporters about the so-called budget sequestration that would gut government spending at midnight, Obama said that he could not force congress to pass a bill to prevent the cuts&#8217; implementation. He is a president, he said, not a dictator, and can&#8217;t use a &#8220;Jedi mind meld&#8221; to force Republicans&#8217; hands.</p></blockquote>
<p>His flub was combining a &#8220;Jedi mind trick&#8221; (from <em>Star Wars</em>) with a &#8220;Vulcan mind meld&#8221; (from <em>Star Trek</em>.) It&#8217;s almost a &#8220;malapropism&#8221; in which, one replaces &#8220;an incorrect word in place of a word with a similar sound.&#8221; (Thanks, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malapropism" target="_blank">wikipedia</a>.) Mind trick, mind meld, preeeeettty close. Twitter freaked out, but I&#8217;m pretty sure 90% of it was in good fun. Man I hope it was in good fun.</p>
<p>Obama is a <em>Star Trek</em> fan, apparently. He invited old school <em>Star Trek</em> cast member Nichelle Nichols to the White House in 2012, and they posed in a photo doing the Vulcan salute.</p>
<p>Live long and may the force be with you, Obama.</p>

<p>NERDS, GET IN HERE, WE&#8217;RE TALKING ABOUT <em><a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/star-wars/" target="_blank">STAR WARS</a></em> IN HERE. Mark Hamill AKA Luke Skywalker of the <em>Star Wars</em> trilogy, is in talks about possibly returning to the franchise. &#8220;Who cares about Mark Hamill?&#8221; you ask. I CARE. A LOT OF US CARE. THERE ARE 4 &#8211; 8 PEOPLE READING THIS WHO CARE ABOUT MARK HAMILL.</p>
<p>Via <a href="http://www.hollywoodreporter.com/heat-vision/mark-hamill-new-star-wars-422643" target="_blank">Hollywood Reporter</a>:</p>
<blockquote><p>They&#8217;re talking to us. <a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/george-lucas/" target="_blank">George [Lucas]</a> wanted to know whether we&#8217;d be interested. <strong>He did say that if we didn&#8217;t want to do it, they wouldn&#8217;t cast another actor in our parts; they would write us out. I can tell you right away that we haven&#8217;t signed any contracts.</strong> We&#8217;re in the stage where they want us to go in and meet with Michael Arndt, who is the writer, and Kathleen Kennedy, who is going to run Lucasfilm. Both have had meetings set that were postponed &#8212; on their end, not mine. They&#8217;re more busy than I am.</p></blockquote>
<p>Omg, Mark Hamill, you adorable national treasure. &#8220;They&#8217;re more busy than I am.&#8221; That is some beautiful honesty right there.</p>
<p>In January it was announced that <a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/jj-abrams/" target="_blank">J.J. Abrams</a> is directing the first sequel, <em>Episode VII</em>. Then, there was discussion that <a href="http://www.evilbeetgossip.com/category/harrison-ford/" target="_blank">Harrison Ford</a> is reprising his role as Han Solo, but reports conflict as to whether or not it&#8217;s official just yet. <em>Rolling Stone</em> says it is, but check out their source.</p>
<p>From <a href="http://www.rollingstone.com/movies/news/report-harrison-ford-will-reprise-han-solo-in-new-star-wars-films-20130219" target="_blank">Rolling Stone</a>:</p>
<blockquote><p>Harrison Ford will return as the original space cowboy, Han Solo, in the new installments of the <em>Star Wars</em> franchise, according to <a href="http://latino-review.com/2013/02/15/exclusive-harrison-ford-return-han-solo-star-wars-episode-vii/" target="_blank">Fox News Latino</a> columnist Umberto &#8220;El Mayimbe&#8221; Gonzalez. The reporter said he &#8220;triple checked&#8221; his &#8220;legitimate sources&#8221;&#8230;</p></blockquote>
<p>Oh cool, well we all know how legit that Umberto &#8220;El Mayimbe&#8221; Gonzalez is.</p>
<p>Hamill goes onto speculate,</p>
<blockquote><p><strong>I&#8217;m assuming, because I haven&#8217;t talked to the writers</strong>, that these movies would be about our offspring &#8212; like my character would be sort of in the Obi-Wan range [as] an influential character. … When I found out [while making the original trilogy] that ultimate good news/bad news joke – the good news is there&#8217;s a real attractive, hot girl in the universe; the bad news is she&#8217;s your sister – I thought, &#8216;Well, I&#8217;m going to wind up like Sir Alec. I&#8217;m going to be a lonely old hermit living out in some kind of desert igloo with a couple of robots.&#8217;</p></blockquote>
