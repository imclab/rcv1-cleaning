            <div class="mod-main-overview-text-title">
                In a Nutshell</div>
            <div class="mod-main-overview-text">
                The <strong>2010 Suzuki SX4 Crossover</strong> is an economical compact four-door hatchback that is manufactured with front- or all-wheel drive, the latter of which offers three modes: full/part-time/front-wheel. Equipped with a 2.0-liter I-4 engine, the Suzuki SX4 Crossover comes with either a six-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). <p></p>
Boasting an airy cabin, Garmin Nuvi navigation system, and loads of features, the Suzuki SX4 Crossover provides function and versatility. The crossover's safety equipment is above average, especially on crash tests, and its fuel economy rates in the 23 mpg city / 29 mpg highway range.</div>

                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    New for 2010</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    For 2010, the Suzuki SX4 Crossover received re-styling, a more powerful 150-horsepower 2.0-liter engine, and a pair of new transmissions: a six-speed manual transmission and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Interior features were also upgraded.</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    Essentials</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    <strong>Engine</strong>: 2.0L I-4<br/>
<strong>Transmissions</strong>: 6-speed manual, CVT<br/>
<strong>Body Style</strong>: 4-door hatchback<br/>
<strong>Trim</strong>: Base Sport Utility</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    On the Outside</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    The Suzuki SX4 Crossover received an updated front fascia and new wheels for 2010.</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    From the Inside</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    Sporting a new instrument cluster, a height-adjustable driver seat, and new navigation features, the Suzuki SX4 Crossover features a quality interior, built with reliable materials, and head and leg room for tall drivers.</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    Driving Performance</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    Because the Suzuki SX4 Crossover's optional all-wheel drive adds over 100 pounds to its curb weight, the hatchback feels less nimble and slower to respond than the front-wheel drive configuration. However, the new 2.0-liter engine with 150-horsepower is an improvement, as are the new six-speed manual transmission and CVT.</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    Safety Equipment</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    Standard safety systems on the Suzuki SX4 Crossover include stability control, as well as driver and front passenger front, side, and curtain airbags.</div>
                
                <h3 class="sz11 b">
                    Gas Mileage</h3>
                
                <div style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
                    Crossover FWD, SportBack GTS: <strong>22 mpg city/30 mpg highway</strong><br/>Crossover FWD, Crossover Technology FWD, SportBack GTS (CVT): <strong>23 mpg city/30 mpg highway</strong><br/>Crossover AWD: <strong>22 mpg city/20 mpg highway</strong> (manual)<br/>Crossover AWD, Crossover Technology AWD, Crossover Touring AWD: <strong>23 mpg city/29 mpg highway</strong></div>
