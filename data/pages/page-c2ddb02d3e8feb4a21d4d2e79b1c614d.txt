        <h1>&nbsp;</h1>
        <h1>Welcome to the College of Fine and Performing Arts</h1>
        <p>&nbsp; </p>
        <p>The College of Fine and Performing Arts (CFPA) offers nearly 300 performances, special presentations and gallery events each year.  Our <a href="" target="">2013-2014 Season</a> is no exception, with a phenomenal line-up featuring the talents of our students and faculty, special guest lecturers, professional performers, and visual artists.   These include some of our many successful alumni who come back to campus to share their expertise with the next generation of artists preparing for their own careers. </p>
        <p>Providing our students with a rigorous arts experience is the central mission of the CFPA.  The very best arts faculty and students are found at Western and their talents are acknowledged across the nation.  I invite you to join us for some of our many events as we celebrate the arts and showcase the stars of tomorrow in some of the highest quality arts events the area has to offer. </p>
        <p><img src="images/kitSpicer.jpg" width="139" height="209" align="left" hspace="15px" alt="Kit Spicer" />Please help us keep in touch by <a href="http://visitor.constantcontact.com/d.jsp?m=1102261807906&amp;p=oi" target="_blank">signing up for our weekly email</a>, &ldquo;This Week in the Arts.&rdquo;  We look forward to keeping you updated with our efforts to make the College of Fine and Performing Arts the best of its kind in the country.        </p>
        <p>&nbsp;</p>
        <p>Kit Spicer, Interim Dean<br />
          College of Fine and Performing Arts<br />
        Western Washington University</p>
