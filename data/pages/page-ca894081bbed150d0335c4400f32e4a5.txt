<div style="padding-top:10px">
<script type="text/javascript"><!--

<div class="entry-content">
		<p>Just a few minutes back, Volvo released the first teaser of its Frankfurt Show concept through a Youtube video.</p>
<div id="attachment_93234" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 548px"><a href="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Thomas-Ingelath-Volvo-concept-coupe.jpg" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-internal="0"><img class="size-full wp-image-93234" style="margin-top: 1px; margin-bottom: 1px;" title="Thomas Ingelath Volvo concept coupe" alt="Thomas Ingelath Volvo concept coupe" src="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Thomas-Ingelath-Volvo-concept-coupe.jpg" width="538" height="257" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Volvo&#8217;s social media team has confirmed that the concept that embodies the future of Volvo design is Frankfurt-bound.</p></div>
<p>The clipping goes as to how Volvo&#8217;s chief designer, Thomas Ingelath, who joined the company last year, observed that Volvo&#8217;s designs were &#8216;vanilla&#8217; and not &#8216;cutting edge&#8217;. Towards the end of the short clip, he&#8217;s seen sketching a concept car, assuring viewers that the &#8216;vanilla days&#8217; are over.</p>
<div id="attachment_93232" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 550px"><a href="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe-rear.jpg" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-internal="0"><img class="size-large wp-image-93232" style="margin-top: 1px; margin-bottom: 1px;" title="Volvo Concept Coupe rear" alt="Volvo Concept Coupe rear" src="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe-rear-1024x574.jpg" width="540" height="303" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Gotcha! A badge on the car&#8217;s hood reveals the name of the concept. Taillight graphic is part of the clean sheet design and the sculpted C-Pillar and wing section is reminiscent of the Audi A7 Sportback.</p></div>
<p>In the same video, Volvo&#8217;s edit team has packed three teaser images, as frames, elapsed by 1 second of the video.  After numerous loops, pauses and reflexive clicking of the play button, we were able to screen-capture all three frames.</p>
<p><iframe src="//www.youtube.com/embed/vY9qrYuH_Ws" height="317" width="550" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p>In one frame that reveals the rear of the car, a badge that reads &#8220;Concept C Coupe&#8221; is clearly seen.</p>
<div id="attachment_93231" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 552px"><a href="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe-front.jpg" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-internal="0"><img class="size-large wp-image-93231" style="margin-top: 1px; margin-bottom: 1px;" title="Volvo Concept Coupe front" alt="Volvo Concept Coupe front" src="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe-front-1024x576.jpg" width="542" height="305" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">The Concept C Coupe&#8217;s front-end is a perfect match to the elusive concept car image that was floating on the internet over the weekend.</p></div>
<p>Voracious auto buffs will know that a mysterious concept car image is doing the rounds on the internet. Guess what, the headlight and front pockets of car in video matches those on the long-hooded mystery car.</p>
<p>Volvo&#8217;s definitely on the verge of revealing a new design direction, one that we would see on future generation cars based of the Scalable Platform Architecture that debuts on the next gen XC90 next year.</p>
<p>The concept car, besides signaling a new direction in design, may also serve as a precursor to the coupe version of the next gen S80 (tentatively C80) <a title="Volvo plans four all-new models starting with the XC90 replacement next year" href="http://indianautosblog.com/2013/02/volvo-plans-four-all-new-models-starting-with-2015-xc90-64093">reported by sections of the British press</a> prior to the Geneva Motor Show.</p>
<div id="attachment_93233" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 550px"><a href="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe.jpg" data-slb-active="1" data-slb-internal="0"><img class="size-large wp-image-93233" style="margin-top: 1px; margin-bottom: 1px;" title="Volvo Concept Coupe" alt="Volvo Concept Coupe" src="http://indianautosblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/Volvo-Concept-Coupe-1024x575.jpg" width="540" height="303" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">The Coupe profile is revealed in one shot. The lower window line gets an upward flick right at the end of the daylight opening.</p></div>
<p>We can assure readers that this car will arrive in Frankfurt as Volvo&#8217;s social media team has uploaded an image of Mr. Ingelath with the caption: <em>The world-first exclusive teaser of Volvo’s imminent transformation&#8230;Did someone say Frankfurt?</em> followed by the link to the Youtube video.</p>
