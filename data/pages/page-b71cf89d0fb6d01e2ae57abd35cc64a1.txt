            <blockquote><a href="http://www.nzhistory.net.nz/culture/literature-in-new-zealand-1930-1960">History

<br>
        <a href="http://www.nzetc.org/tm/scholarly/tei-corpus-19thcenturynovels.html">
        19th Century New Zealand Novels, a digital collection from the New Zealand Electronic Text Centre. </a><br>

        The novels of nineteenth 
century New Zealand are part of an under-read and undervalued period in New Zealand 
cultural history.&nbsp;Although the term "Maoriland" may evoke a world of 
saccharine fantasy in which Maori warriors in heroic attitudes and Maori maidens 
in seductive ones inhabited outmoded Victorian literary forms, recent critics 
have argued that there was a lively and significant colonial 
literature,&nbsp;full of complexities, contradictions, evasions and&nbsp;the 
beginnings of a self-consciously New Zealand 
literature.<br>
