<p><font size="1" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#666666">Thank you for your interest in Dollar Thrifty Automotive Group, Inc. (DTAG). We believe that our employees are the key to our success.</font> </p>

<p><font size="1" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif" color="#666666">Please follow the steps below and get ready to put on your seatbelt because we want to put you in the driver’s seat of your career!</font></p>

<p>

3. Click on <b>Save Search/Set up Job Agent</b>. <br>
4. Input a name for your search. Check the box to set up the Job Agent using those criteria. <br>
5. Input an email address for your notifications. <br>
6. Click on <b>Save Search/Job Agent (if applicable) </b>. <br>
7. You’ll receive emails when a job is posted fitting your requirements. </font></p>
