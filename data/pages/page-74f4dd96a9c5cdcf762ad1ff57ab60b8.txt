<div class='title'>
<a href="javascript:animatedcollapse.toggle('announce1678')">Franklin School Pumpkin Fair set for Saturday October 13</a>
</div>
<div id='announce1678' style='display:none;'>
<p>WESTFIELD, NJ – The Franklin Elementary School Parent Teacher Organization (PTO) will hold its annual fall Pumpkin Fair on Saturday Oct. 13, from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m. - rain or shine - at Franklin School, 700 Prospect Street, Westfield.</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>This fun-filled school tradition offers something for everyone in the family, and features games, inflatables, obstacle courses, face painting, glitter tattoos, creepy crafts and everyone’s favorite – the musical cookie walk.</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>There will also be gift basket raffles, lots of live goldfish and plenty of food. Visitors to the fair can enjoy cotton candy, pizza, hot dogs, drinks and baked goods galore.  </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Proceeds benefit the Franklin PTO. The Pumpkin Fair is open to everyone, not just Franklin families, so come out and have some fun!</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>

<div class='title'>
<a href="javascript:animatedcollapse.toggle('announce1675')">HEDGEHOG & FEATHER THEATRE CO. ANNOUNCES A HALLOWEEN EVENT </a>
</div>
<div id='announce1675' style='display:none;'>
<p><strong>HEDGEHOG &amp; FEATHER THEATRE CO. ANNOUNCES A HALLOWEEN EVENT <br />FOR THE FAMILY <em>CREATURE DOUBLE FEATURE : FRANKENSTEIN – DRACULA<br /><br /></em></strong></p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Hedgehog &amp; Feather Theatre Co. is thrilled to announce the Halloween Family Event of the season with <em>Creature Double Feature : Frankenstein - Dracula</em> on Friday, October 19<sup>th</sup>, Saturday, October 20<sup>th</sup>, Friday, October 26<sup>th</sup>, Saturday, October 27<sup>th</sup> at 7:30PM each performance.  All tickets are $15. The spooky production will take place at St. Paul’s Episcopal Church Parish Hall, 414 E. Broad Street, Westfield. For more information and to purchase tickets online please visit <a href="http://hedgehogandfeather.org/" rel="nofollow">hedgehogandfeather.org</a>, or e-mail <a href="mailto:info@hedgehogandfeather.org" rel="nofollow">info@hedgehogandfeather.org</a></p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p><em>Creature Double Feature : Frankenstein - Dracula</em> is being Produced by Sarah Hedgepeth and Julie Fetter, Directed by Ty Jacobs, Sets and Props by Julie Fetter, Costumes by Alisa Korunow, and Technical Stage Manager Mary Kate Miller.</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Based on the classic books by Mary Shelley and Bram Stoker, adapted by Ty Jacobs, and with musical selections of Mussorgsky and Tchaikovsky, this will be the Halloween Event that the whole family can enjoy. Back to back at last, the two originators of scare, Frankenstein’s Monster and the first vampire, Dracula…all performed by kids and for kids! “Frankenstein” is the tale of a mad scientist named Dr. Victor Frankenstein who puts together a creature by parts he took from the cemetery! But the poor Monster is misunderstood and too scary looking to make friends and ends up causing terrible trouble. “Dracula” is the tale of a very nasty guy who has been ‘alive’ for centuries, although by all accounts, he is dead! Count Dracula lives by biting people on the neck and drinking their blood. Only a mysterious Professor Van Helsing can save the day and stop Dracula from his evil deeds.</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Our cast includes: Yustina Alexander-Himlan (Fanwood), Marlo Avido (Westfield), Kacey Brennan (Westfield), Katie Ceraso (Westfield), Lily Ceraso (Westfield), Elizabeth Cotter (Mountainside), Joe Ensslin (Westfield), Julia Guarneri (Fanwood), Nicolas Guerriero (Westfield), Andrew Kapadia (Westfield), Max Kleiman (Westfield), Marcelina Krowinska (Westfield), Sarah Kudron (Cranford), Tony Peer (Westfield), Matt Ryan (Berkeley Heights), Jonathan Samanski (Westfield), Evan Schachter (Springfield), Julie Singer (Westfield), Lauren Singer (Westfield), and Christopher Slavik (Hoboken).</p>

<a href="javascript:animatedcollapse.toggle('announce1590')">Westfield Symphony Goes Big Band on Sunday, September 9</a>
</div>
<div id='announce1590' style='display:none;'>
<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Get "in the mood" with David Wroe and WSO on Sunday September 9 at <em>Swing Time!</em>, an afternoon of blistering hits from the big band era. <br /><br /><strong><em>'CHEEK TO CHEEK' WITH FULL DANCE FLOOR!</em></strong><strong><em><br /></em></strong> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p>Come bounce and sway to the irresistible rhythms of big band and swing standards from the 30s to the 50s as WSO changes gear to deliver the authentic sounds of a modern swing band <br />in full flight.</p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p> </p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p><strong>Sunday | September 9 | 3pm</strong><br />L’Affaire | 1099 US Route 22 | Mountainside, NJ<br /><br />General admission seating $30<br />Reserved table seating $55 (located next to dance floor)<br /><br />Cash bar. Reserved tables of 6 and 8 available.<br /><br /><strong>For tickets &amp; information go to <a href="http://www.westfieldsymphony.org" rel="nofollow">www.westfieldsymphony.org</a></strong></p>


<p>&#13;</p>

<p><br />Should you choose ….bring your dancing shoes! With space for both listening and dancing, <em><em>Swing Time!</em></em> will delight music lovers and swing dancers of all ages.</p>
