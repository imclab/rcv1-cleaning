<p style="text-align: center;"><font style="font-size: 16px;"><strong><font style="font-size: 14px;">Come to our MONTHLY MEMBERSHIP MEETINGS!</font><br />
</strong></font><em><u>Meetings are held the 1st Tuesday of every month at 6:30pm;&nbsp;<br />
join us in the banquet room at Kep's Grill on Muller Road, Washington.<br />
</u></em><br />
Each meeting includes a short update on news and events, an artist's demonstration or presentation, a 'show and tell' time, and then wrap up with a little time to network. Practicing artists (novice and expert) are encouraged (but not required)&nbsp;to bring an item to share at 'show and tell'. Each month we have a theme that inspires some to create new work, but its always great to see anything community members are creating in the name of art!<br />
Check the local paper for information on presentations, demos, and themes too. See you there!!<br />
