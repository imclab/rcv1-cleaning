<p>Of course, we&#8217;ve already seen <a title="Jaguar C-X16 Concept" href="http://www.carsuk.net/jaguar-c-x16-revealed-it-may-be-the-xe-but-its-no-e-type-jag/">much of what the F-Type Coupe will offer in the C-X16 Concept</a> &#8211; the basis for the F-Type &#8211; when that was revealed a year ago, but we hadn&#8217;t expected to see it as the F-Type Coupe until at least Geneva 2013.</p>
<p>But <a title="Motor Authority" href="http://www.motorauthority.com/news/1079528_2014-jaguar-f-type-coupe-check-back-at-the-l-a-auto-show?utm_source=feedburner&amp;utm_medium=feed&amp;utm_campaign=Feed%3A+MotorAuthority2+%28MotorAuthority%29" target="_blank">Motor Authority</a> are this morning claiming the F-Type Coupe will arrive at the L.A. Auto Show alongside the F-Type Roadster adding a third debut model for Jaguar Land Rover at L.A. to sit alongside the F-Type Roadster and 2013 Range Rover.</p>
<p>If true, it could mean the F-Type Coupe hitting showrooms by next summer and at prices a bit lower than the F-Type Roadster although, at least initially, the range will be the same with the choice of the F-Type, F-Type S and F-Type V8 S, with an F-Type V8 R to follow.</p>
<p><strong>Update</strong>: <em>It appears that Motor Authority&#8217;s man who knows, didn&#8217;t. So back to the original plan with the F-Type Coupe arriving at the Geneva Motor Show in 2013.</em></p>
