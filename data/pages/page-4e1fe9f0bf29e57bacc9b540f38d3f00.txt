</script></div><p>Germans from BMW revealed a concept that prefigures the successor of 5 Series at the Beijing Auto Show. The model has a length of five meters and is a four-door coupe that combines the advantages of a GT with the one of a sports model. The ceiling of this concept is 100 mm lower than the current BMW 7 Series F01 and the design was done as a development of a conventional sedan in three volumes.<br />
<span id="more-5691"></span></p>
<div align="center"><div id="attachment_5692" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 510px"><a title="BMW Gran Coupe Concept at Beijing Auto Show" href="http://www.bmwcoop.com/wp-content/images/2010/04/BMW-Gran-Coupe-Concept-2.jpg" target="_blank"><img class="size-full wp-image-5692" src="http://www.bmwcoop.com/wp-content/images/2010/04/BMW-Gran-Coupe-Concept-2.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="310" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BMW Gran Coupe Concept at Beijing Auto Show</p></div></div>
<p>BMW Gran Coupe will rival models such as Porsche Panamera, Audi A7 and next generation Mercedes-Benz CLS. Also, people at BMW will keep their design values that have established, this model following to present a number of trends that the future models of the manufacturer in Munich will follow.</p>
