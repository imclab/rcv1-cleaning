              <h3>Learn to Manage the Fields of Sports, Arts and Entertainment at Point Park in Downtown Pittsburgh</h3>
              <p>Point Park University's sports, arts and entertainment management program in Downtown Pittsburgh is one of the most innovative in the United States. Students earn a business degree while focusing in the three industries of sports, arts and entertainment management.</p>
              <h3>Courses Taught</h3>
              <p>
                <a href="/Academics/Schools/Business/UndergraduateProgram/SportArtsandEntertainmentManagement/SAEMFaculty">Taught by practicing professionals</a>, this program combines a solid background in business, marketing and management with the added expertise in sport, arts and entertainment management. This type of course work is not typically found in undergraduate programs.<br /><br />Business courses in the SAEM program include:</p>
              <ul>
                <li>Principles of Management;</li>
                <li>Business Law;</li>
                <li>Intro to Accounting I and II;</li>
                <li>Human Resource Management;</li>
                <li>Corporate Finance; and </li>
                <li>Strategic Planning.</li>
              </ul>
              <p>SAEM majors then have the flexibility to take industry-specific courses of their choice such as:</p>
              <ul>
                <li>Event Management; </li>
                <li>Businesses of Concert Promotion and Touring;</li>
                <li>Performing Arts Management;</li>
                <li>Talent Management;</li>
                <li>Media Management;</li>
                <li>Essential Fundraising Principles; and</li>
                <li>Facility and Venue Design Management.</li>
              </ul>
              <h2>
                <a name="Sports">
                </a>Sports Management</h2>
              <p>Sure there are lots of sports management programs out there. But none have the depth of experience and advantage of crossover exposure like Point Park University's SAEM program. In addition, there are national and college sports teams located just steps from Point Park's campus.</p>
              <h2>
                <a name="Arts">
                </a>Arts Management</h2>
              <p>The SAEM program builds on the strength of Point Park's nationally ranked <a href="/Academics/Schools/COPA">Conservatory of Performing Arts</a> – a perfect platform for training on the business side of this career path. SAEM courses have helped musicians aspiring to launch their own careers and provided dancers with skills in studio business operations. In addition, Pittsburgh’s Cultural District is just blocks away, offering students with the opportunity to work in theatre, ballet, opera and dance performance. The Carnegie Museums, Carnegie Science Center, Highmark SportsWorks and the Andy Warhol Museum are also closeby.</p>
              <ul>
              </ul>
              <h2>
                <a name="Entertainment">
                </a>Entertainment Management</h2>
              <p>The SAEM program will educate you in the development of concert and talent management, booking agencies, record labels and ticketing to name a few areas. You will also learn what it takes to work in management at a stadium, arena, theater or any other type of venue or facility.</p>
              <h3>Landing Internships and Careers</h3>
              <p>Students can complete up to three internships for academic credit  with at least one internship required to graduate. Point Park  University's vibrant campus in   <a href="/About/AboutPittsburgh">Downtown Pittsburgh</a> is within walking distance to top-tier sports arenas, renowned arts  organizations and live entertainment venues, offering the perfect  setting for SAEM majors to land outstanding <a href="/Academics/Schools/Business/UndergraduateProgram/SportArtsandEntertainmentManagement/SAEMInternships">internships</a>, giving them the experience they need to land <a href="/Academics/Schools/Business/UndergraduateProgram/SportArtsandEntertainmentManagement/SAEMCareers">careers</a> in Pittsburgh and beyond.</p>
              <h3>Academic Partnership with Stage AE</h3>
              <p>Point Park University and <a href="http://www.promowestlive.com/index_pittsburgh.php?venue=stageae" target="_blank">Stage AE</a>, one of Pittsburgh’s newest concert venues, have formed an academic partnership to offer  students in the SAEM program with an opportunity to learn about the  entertainment industry from a <a href="/Academics/Schools/Business/UndergraduateProgram/SportArtsandEntertainmentManagement/StageAEClassroom">dedicated classroom space at Stage AE</a> on Pittsburgh's North Shore.</p>
              <h3>Earn an M.B.A. with a concentration in SAEM</h3>
              <p>Point Park also offers a concentration in sport, arts and entertainment management through its <a href="/Academics/Schools/Business/GraduatePrograms/MBA">M.B.A. degree program</a>.</p>
