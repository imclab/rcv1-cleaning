<p>Navigation: <a  class="text" href="http://thecarculture.wikia.com/wiki/The_Car_Culture_Wiki">The Car Culture Wiki</a> &gt;
</p><p>Automotive racing is nearly as old as automobiles themselves. Since the advent of the automobile, people have been racing them against nearly everything, including horses, airplanes, motorcycles, and even people. However, the most obvious and common are other automobiles. Automotive racing has had a long a rich history, with many forms continuing until today in many different countries.
</p>
