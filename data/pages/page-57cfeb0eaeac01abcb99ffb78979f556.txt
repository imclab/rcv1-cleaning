<p>The book was designed to give you an insider's look at how the <i>auto repair business</i>

<p>The 67-page guide to the auto repair business will point out common <span style="color: red;">auto service scams</span>

mechanic and not a writer. You may find a few grammar and spelling 
errors. 

</p><p>I do not plan on editing the guide and the material is 
copyrighted so please do not send me a list of corrections. The reason 
that I am giving this report away is that I hope that you will find some
 value in it. And there is no question that the book supports the 
reasons for do-it-yourself auto repair (the focus of the YouFixCars.com 
website). 

</p><p>If you never take your vehicle into a shop and perform all of the
 auto repairs yourself you will not fall pray to automotive service 
scams. I also hope that this will be an opportunity for you to get to 
know me better. I would like you to tell your friends and family about 
the YouFixCars.com website. I hope to be your primary destination for 
car repair information.
