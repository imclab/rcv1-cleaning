            <td valign="top"><p class='style1'>The Keuka Arts Festival 2014</p>
		<p><p>
	The Keuka Arts Festival is a fine art and skilled craft show held annually along the Keuka Lake Outlet Trail, only a few blocks from downtown Penn Yan. The event is free and will feature live entertainment and activities for families.</p>
</p>
		<p><span class='style2'>Date & Time:</span>
		<p>
	Saturday June 14th and Sunday June 15th: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.<br />
	The festival will be held rain or shine.</p>
</p>
		<p><span class='style2'>Artists:</span>
		<p>
	For 2014, the festival expects over 110 vendors that include categories such as painting, photography, pottery, jewelry and more.</p>
</p>
		<p><span class='style2'>Food & Wine:</span>
		<p>
	Local restaurants and wineries will offer an array of culinary delights, and our farmers market will feature 100% local, seasonal produce. A selection of fine Finger Lakes wines is available for tasting, as well as fresh lemonade and ice cream. Picnic tables located throughout the festival area provide visitors an opportunity to sit and relax while enjoying their refreshments.</p>
</p>
		<p><span class='style2'>Entertainment:</span>
		<p>
	A variety of live music will fill the air throughout the festival featuring everything from Bluegrass to Acoustic Rock. The entertainment line-up will be announced at the end of May, so make sure to check this website often for updates.</p>
