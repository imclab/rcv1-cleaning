<h1><a href="http://automotivereisen.com/" rel="bookmark" title="About Automotive Reisen">About Automotive Reisen</a></h1>
</div>

<div class="post-meta">
</div>

<div class="post-content">
<div id="attachment_8" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 161px"><img class="size-full wp-image-8" title="About AMR" src="http://74.220.219.62/~automou4/wp-content/uploads/2008/12/index11.jpg" alt="Walking tours to romantic villages are included for shopping or just relaxing" width="151" height="230" /><p class="wp-caption-text">Walking tours to romantic villages are included for shopping or just relaxing</p></div>
<p> </p>
<p>Automotive Reisen offers factory, museum, motorsport, and historic military tours to historic and cultural European cities. Patrick Yanahan, president of Automotive Reisen has over 25 years of European travel as well as vintage motorsport and collector automobile experience. This life passion has helped him create the travel offerings and successful tours that have left his groups speechless. The tour offerings will be well balanced, combining motorsport and marque manufacturer destinations as well as a host of other activities, exciting for the man, woman, and the couple. </p>
<p>Automotive Reisen will custom tailor tours for all groups, car clubs, dealer organizations as well as special projects for European auto manufacturers. We handle all travel, lodging, tours and cultural visits with affordable experiences to locations not on standard travel agency tours. </p>
