                        <b>USA Manufacturer and Worldwide Distributor since 1978</b>

                        <p align="justify" class="main_13">
                        World Upholstery &amp; Trim is a wholly owned division of XKSS Inc., a California 
                        corporation. XKSS was formed in 1978 and was initially designed to supply 
                        authentic interior parts that were no longer supplied by the original 
                        manufacturer.
                        </p>
                        <p align="justify" class="main_13">
                        Today, Located in Santa Paula, CA World Upholstery &amp; Trim is a manufacturer and 
                        online supplier of soft interior trim products for a wide range of classic and 
                        collectable automobiles.
                        One of our primary goals is to perpetuate the originality of interesting 
                        automobiles for future generations. Authenticity, originality and information 
                        are our primary products. Our company targets professional restorers and 
                        do-it-yourself owners not satisfied with trim shop designed interiors. Our 
                        inventory fills the needs of a select segment of the upscale automobile market.
                        </p>
                        <p align="left" class="main_16">
                        <b>Authenticity, World-Class Quality and Experience</b>
                        </p>
                        <p align="justify" class="main_13">
                        We are specialists dedicated to providing authenticity, 
                        high quality and excellent service. No one else in the business can claim the 
                        amount of experience we have in reproducing soft trim parts. This experience is 
                        necessary especially when dealing with older models that may have gone through 
                        some changes over the years. We use both original or reproduction materials in 
                        all of our products whether its leather, vinyl, canvas or carpet. Italian 
                        carpet, German canvas, English wool and many more imported materials are readily 
                        available. Whether it&#39;s heat-sealed, perforated or embossed our upholstery kits 
                        are reproduced and hand-crafted using patterns made from actual factory parts. 
                        Everything we offer is the finest available. No machine sewing necessary.
                        </p>
                        <p align="left" class="main_16">
                        <b>Hand-Made Products Perfected in Over 30 Years</b>
                        </p>
                        <p align="justify" class="main_13">
                        World Upholstery only manufactures parts that are either difficult to find from 
                        other vendors or parts that are made substandard from other vendors. Most of our 
                        products are no longer supplied by the original manufacturer. All products are 
                        supplied in original materials when available and are sewn in original designs 
                        with ease of installation in mind. Substitutions are only made if original 
                        materials are no longer in commercial existence. Years of pattern and product 
                        development have given us the knowledge of original material manufacturer and 
                        suppliers as well as a large supplier base for manufactured items. We continue 
                        to restore our interesting automobiles and developed new products from those 
                        restorations. Our very experienced production crew manufactures all our products with the strict
                        adherence to quality and authenticity and we will work with you or your installer to make sure that everything is
                        made to your satisfaction.
                        </p>
                        <p align="justify" class="main_10">
                        <i>Disclaimer: Not all of our products are suitable for the amateur restorer and no installation instructions are offered
                        for parts that we feel should be installed by a professional. Most products are made-to-order.</i>
