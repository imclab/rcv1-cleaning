<div class="meta">
	<a href="http://www.driveculture.com/author/admin/" title="Posts by admin" rel="author">admin</a> | October 14, 2010 | <a href="http://www.driveculture.com/2010/10/14/pleasanton-auto-mall-custom-car-show/#comments" rel="bookmark" title="Comments for Pleasanton Auto Mall Custom Car Show">Comments (0)</a>

							<p>Second time is a charm&#8230;.Congrats to Adam Toy (organizer) for another successful event this past weekend. The event brought in 180+ show cars, music, food and charity event. Speaking of charity, the show raised over $2,600 to Coins for Campers (<a title="http://www.ttff.org/" href="http://www.ttff.org/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.ttff.org']);" target="_blank">www.ttff.org</a>) good job everyone to who donated, participated and to the organizers ! !</p>
<p>Photos by <a title="http://www.jsjbrothers.com/" href="http://www.jsjbrothers.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.jsjbrothers.com']);" target="_blank">John San Juan of JSJbrothers.com</a> and Jeff Cabacungan.</p>
