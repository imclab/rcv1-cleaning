                    <h2 class="modeloverview-subheading">Overview</h2>            <p class="yat-collapsible collapsed intro" property="rdfs:comment" itemprop="description">The 2013 3 Series Convertible is a 2-door, 4-passenger luxury convertible, available in 3 trims, ranging from<span class="ellips">...</span> <a href="#" class="more toggle">More Info</a><span class="full"> the 328i to the 335is. <br><br><br><br>Upon introduction, the 328i is equipped with a standard 3.0-liter, I6, 230-horsepower engine that achieves 17-mpg in the city and 26-mpg on the highway.
