<div id="attachment_1554" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 310px"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1554" title="xinsrc_372090624145701519564233" src="http://chinaluxculturebiz.files.wordpress.com/2009/10/xinsrc_372090624145701519564233.jpg?w=300&#038;h=201" alt="Bugatti's choice to open a showroom in Beijing shows the company's intention to expand in the China market" width="300" height="201" /><p class="wp-caption-text">Bugatti&#39;s choice to open a showroom in Beijing shows the company&#39;s intention to expand in the China market</p></div>
<p>China&#8217;s growing automotive demand has been great for automakers of all stripes, from up-and-coming budget domestic brands to the world&#8217;s most expensive and exclusive marks. <p>Now <a href="http://www.bugatti.com/">Bugatti</a>, the high performance French automaker, has opened its first-ever showroom outside of France, located on Beijing&#8217;s swanky Jinbao Street. From <a onclick="return mugicPopWin(this,event);" oncontextmenu="mugicRightClick(this);" href="http://news.alibaba.com/article/detail/cars/100183067-1-bugatti-opens-its-first-showroom.html">Alibaba News</a>:</p>

<div id="attachment_1383" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 310px"><a href="http://www.zoomilife.com/images/wang-chuan-fu.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1383" title="wang-chuan-fu" src="http://chinaluxculturebiz.files.wordpress.com/2009/09/wang-chuan-fu.jpg?w=300&#038;h=200" alt="BYD's Wang Chuanfu is now worth over US $5 billion, putting him at the top of China's &quot;Rich List&quot;" width="300" height="200" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BYD&#39;s Wang Chuanfu is now worth over US $5 billion, putting him at the top of China&#39;s &quot;Rich List&quot;</p></div>
<p>Last week, <a href="http://chinaluxculturebiz.wordpress.com/2009/09/24/warren-buffett-gives-little-known-chinese-clothier-a-global-boost/">we saw what the endorsement of a financial heavyweight like Warren Buffett can do </a>for a little-known Chinese company, with the stock of Dayang Trands skyrocketing 71% following Buffett&#8217;s praises of the company&#8217;s bespoke suits. In the automotive sector, today <a href="http://www.chinaherald.net/2009/09/buffetts-backing-helps-car-maker-to.html"><em>China Herald</em> </a>(via <a href="http://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601080&amp;sid=aJ2CNtienA9g">Bloomberg</a>) points out that Buffett&#8217;s investment in previously low-key Chinese battery and hybrid/electric car maker <a href="http://www.byd.com/">BYD </a>has not only given the brand global visibility, it has made the company&#8217;s head, Wang Chuanfu, a very rich man and putting him at the top of the <a href="http://www.hurun.net/listreleasecn387.aspx">Hurun Report&#8217;s China Rich List </a>&#8211; <a href="http://www.hurun.net/listreleaseen388.aspx">beating China&#8217;s longtime #1, Zhang Yin</a>.</p>
<blockquote><p><em>[Wang's] wealth jumped to $5.1 billion, exceeding Nine Dragons Paper Holdings Ltd. founder </em><a href="http://search.bloomberg.com/search?q=Zhang+Yin%3Fs&amp;site=wnews&amp;client=wnews&amp;proxystylesheet=wnews&amp;output=xml_no_dtd&amp;ie=UTF-8&amp;oe=UTF-8&amp;filter=p&amp;getfields=wnnis&amp;sort=date:D:S:d1"><em>Zhang Yin’s</em></a><em> $4.9 billion, according to an e-mailed statement from the Hurun Report today.</em></p>
