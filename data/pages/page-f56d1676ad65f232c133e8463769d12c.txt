<h1 class="headerimage">Automotive</h1>
<img src="images/automotive-header.jpg" alt="autmotive images">
</article>

<article>
<p>The Automotive Technology Program provides classroom instruction and laboratory (hands-on) experience for the following Career Studies Certificates: </p>

<p class="no-margin">The <a href="/Academics_And_Student_Services/Academics/Programs/Career_Studies_Certificate/auto-technician-csc.asp">Automotive Technician</a> certificate includes training in the ASE areas of engine repair, suspension & steering, brakes, and electrical systems.</p>  
<p><strong>24 credits</strong></p>

<p class="no-margin">
The <a href="/Academics_And_Student_Services/Academics/Programs/Career_Studies_Certificate/auto-diagnostician-csc.asp">Automotive Diagnostician</a> certificate includes training in the ASE areas of automotive transmission, manual drive train, climate control, and entire performance.</p>
<p><strong>26 credits</strong></p>

<h2>Automotive Technology Admissions</h2>

<p>High school students interested in becoming technicians in the automotive field are encouraged to enroll. Currently enrolled high school students need to see their high school counselor for information before registering for the program.</p>
 		
<p><strong>Credits:</strong></p>
<p>High school students receive high school land College credits. Adult students receive College credit toward the applicable Career Studies Certificate.</p>

<p><strong>Dual Enrollment Automotive</strong> info for current HS students 540-423-9126</p>

<p>Location:</p>

<p>Classes are held at our new Automotive Technology Center location. </p>

<p><strong>Automotive Technology Center</strong><br/>
42 BlackJack Road<br/>
Fredericksburg, Va 22401<br/>
</p>
<p>For directions contact Brent Wilson at:</p>

<p><strong>Phone:</strong>834-1978<br/>
<strong>Email:</strong><a href="mailto:bwilson@germanna.edu">bwilson@germanna.edu</a></p>

<p>View <a href="http://www.germanna.edu/admissions/documents/student-to-do-list-june2012.pdf" target="_blank">New Student To Do List</a></p>
