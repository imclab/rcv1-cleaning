It is intended as a primer to the Master in Transportation and Car Design;</p>

<div id="details" class="column">
  <h2 class="title">Automotive Industry And Design Culture</h2>
  
  <p>This course explores the history and the main facts of the motor-car industry relating this to its developments in the assembly/manufacturing processes, to the evolution of a variety of cultural references and to the changes in life-styles.The goal here is enabling students to gain a conscious understanding of modern car design and of the future possibilities offered in this field with a particular focus on sustainable issues.</p>
