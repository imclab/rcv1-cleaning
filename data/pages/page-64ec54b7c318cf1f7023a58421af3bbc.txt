The remainders were the standard  Prius and the Prius Plug-in, which has a battery that can be charged  from an electrical outlet.</p><br /><br /><a href="http://money.cnn.com/2012/10/23/autos/toyota-prius-sales-california/index.html?section=money_autos" class="infoLink" target="_blank" style="font-weight: bold; padding-left: 3px;" rel="nofollow">Read Article</a></span></p>
