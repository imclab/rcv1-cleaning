												<span itemprop="articleBody"><p><a href="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/001-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[38102]"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-38119" alt="001-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak" src="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/001-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak-770x512.jpg" width="770" height="512" /></a></p>
<p>Just before an official reveal, the images of the upcoming 2014 BMW 4 Series leak onto the internet and we have them to share with you below.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/002-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[38102]"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-38118" alt="002-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak" src="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/002-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak-770x513.jpg" width="770" height="513" /></a></p>
<p>The all-new 2014 BMW 4 Series is slated to replace the current 3 Series coupe. The range will start off things with a 2.0-liter turbo 4-cylinder engine in a 428i model and then go to the range-topping 435i featuring the 3.0-liter inline 6-cylinder. Of course in true BMW fashion you will be able to select an M Sport trim. Of course we will still away the aggressive M4 to drop at a later date, but for now we get the idea on what the 4 Series will look like and can draw our own speculations for an upcoming M4.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/016-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak.jpg" rel="prettyPhoto[38102]"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-38107" alt="016-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak" src="http://www.automotiveaddicts.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/016-2014-bmw-4-series-coupe-leak-770x601.jpg" width="770" height="601" /></a></p>
