The standard monthly report compiles official vehicle registration information provided by the department of motor vehicles in each of Cross-Sells 27 partner states. We produce over 5,000 individually tailored reports for automotive dealers, financial institutions, and media companies each month.

<li>Identify strengths and weaknesses versus the competition</li>
<li>Details sales by Vin, Make, Model, Owner City, Owner Zip, Seller Name, Seller Code, and Lienholder where allowed by state law.</li>
