<p><strong>Strong reasons to list your website in web directories...</strong></p>
<p>Web directories list the websites by theme (topic). The links 
  in a <em>web directory</em> are collected, edited and organized by humans. <a href="http://www.yahoo.com" target="_blank">Yahoo</a> 
  and <a href="http://www.dmoz.org" target="_blank">Dmoz ODP(Open Directory Project)</a> 
  are considered as the best human-edited web directories on the Internet and 
  they are one of the most trusted and relied traffic sources. But, getting into DMOZ is not easy. Editors at DMOZ have very strict guidelines and if your website does not adhere, your website will not be listed. </p>
<p> Since these directories 
  get thousands of submissions every day, it is sometimes difficult to get listed 
  on them and your URL may take a long time ( atleast 4 - 6 months) to be reviewed 
  and listed. Some of the top directories offer paid review. Yahoo offers a paid listing support. Generally good directory listings 
  give a surprise boost to your <strong>link popularity</strong>. <a href="http://www.allthewebsites.org/webdirectory/directories,17.html">View 
  a list of popular web directories.</a></p>
  
<p><strong>Is redirect link from <em>web directories</em> ( for 
  example, go.cgi?to=www.mywebsite.com ) is counted in link popularity?</strong></p>
<p class="divider">No. Only direct links ( &lt;a href=&quot;http://www.mywebsite.com&quot;
