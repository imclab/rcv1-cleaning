<h1>Visitors &amp; Conventions &gt; <br />The Arts &amp; Entertainment</h1><p><img class="new_pics_padding" height="150" alt="" width="224" align="right" src="images/sce/Grass-Valley-Center-for-the.jpg" /><br />Grass Valley and Nevada County offer a rich repertoire of theater, music, and entertainment.&nbsp; The community generously supports the arts.&nbsp; Throughout the year, a number of theater groups, the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.catsweb.org">Community Asian Theater (CATS)</a> and The <a target="_blank" href="http://www.foothilltheatre.org">Foothill Theatre Company</a> to name a few, delight local audiences. The <a target="_blank" href="http://www.thecenterforthearts.org">Center for the Arts</a> and the historic <a target="_blank" href="http://www.minersfoundry.org">Miners Foundry Cultural Center</a>, as well as other venues, host a diverse mix of theater, film, and other events.&nbsp; And of course, the newest movie releases can be viewed at the various <a target="_blank" href="http://www.sierratheaters.com">Sierra Theaters</a>.</p><p><img class="stocking_padding" height="226" alt="" width="150" align="left" src="images/sce/Noriko-Kishi.jpg" /></p><p>&nbsp;</p><p>The beautifully forested <a target="_blank" href="http://www.nevadacountyfair.com">Nevada County Fairgrounds</a> is home to a series of unique events which draw attendees from miles around year round.&nbsp; The popular Draft Horse Classic and Nevada County Fair are local classics.&nbsp; <a target="_blank" href="http://www.musicinthemountains.org">Music in the Mountains</a> offers a full spectrum of classic music spring, summer, winter and fall. &nbsp;</p><p>The <a target="_blank" href="http://www.neighborhoodcenterofthearts.com/">Neighborhood Center for the Arts</a> provided adults with developmental disabilities an opportunity to grow to the best of their abilities through the arts and community integration.</p><p>Check the <a class="red" href="events.aspx">Community Calendar</a> for details on events each month.&nbsp; You will find a wide variety of random fun things to do in a small town steeped in rich history&nbsp;and a vibrant arts community!</p><p>&nbsp;</p></td>
