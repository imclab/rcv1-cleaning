			</span>
        	<span class="paragraph-1">
				<p>Most of the “Eat &amp; Greet” events will take place before the book festival, which will be held Sept. 7 in the Downtown Arts District.</p>

                    <p>Debbie Macomber, a No. 1 New York Times bestselling fiction writer, will attend a lunch at Old Salem Visitors Center at 11:30 a.m. Sept. 6. She will give a presentation and sign books. Tickets are $45, which includes a boxed lunch and a hardcover copy of Macomber’s new book, “Rose Harbor in Bloom.”</p>
                
                    <p>Sherri Brooks Vinton, the author of “Put ’em Up!”, “Put ’em Up! Fruit” and “The Real Food Revival,” will talk and sign books at Whole Foods at 4 p.m. Sept. 6. Admission is free. Tickets are not required.</p>
                
                    <p>Adrian Miller, a culinary historian and barbecue judge, will give a presentation and sign books at a breakfast at Sweet Potatoes Restaurant at 8:30 a.m. Sept. 7. Breakfast will be prepared from Miller’s forthcoming book, “Soul Food: The Surprising Story of an American Cuisine, One Plate at a Time.” Tickets are $52, which includes breakfast and a copy of Miller’s book.</p>
                
                    <p>Elana Amsterdam, the author of “Gluten-Free Cupcakes” and the forthcoming “Paleo Cooking from Elana’s Pantry,” will give a presentation and sign books at 4:30 p.m. Sept. 7 at Mary’s Gourmet Diner. Tickets are $48, which include a copy of “Paleo Cooking from Elana’s Pantry” and the chance to sample recipes.</p>
                
                    <p>Tickets are on sale now at <a href="http://bookmarks.roundtablelive.org">http://bookmarks.roundtablelive.org</a>.</p>

    function updateSectionListFooter() {         
        var allSectionsFooter = '';
        var allTypesFooter = '';
        $('#searchSectionsFooter :checked').each(function(){
            if ($(this).val() == 'all'){
                $('#sectionCollectionFooter').removeAttr('name');
                $('#typeCollectionFooter').removeAttr('name');    
            }
            else if($(this).attr('class') == 'section-box regular'){
                $('#sectionCollectionFooter').attr('name','c[]');
                if (allSectionsFooter == ''){
                    allSectionsFooter = $(this).val();
                }

                    allSectionsFooter += (',' + $(this).val());
                };

                if (allTypesFooter == ''){

                else{
                    allTypesFooter += (',' + $(this).val());
                };
            };
        });

        $('#typeCollectionFooter').val(allTypesFooter)
