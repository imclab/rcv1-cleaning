<h1 id="ctl00_ContentSection__PageTitle">Search Chevrolet Certified Pre-Owned in Omaha, NE</h1>
<div class="custompage customcontent">
<p style="text-align: center;"><img style="width: 502px; height: 183px;" alt="GM Certified Cars and Truck, Omaha Nebraska" src="/dealerimages/Dealer%204163%20Images/GM%20Certified.jpg" longdesc="H&amp;H Chevrolet has a Huge Selection of GM Certified Cars and Trucks" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Buying a used car is not what it used to be. No more worries about purchasing a new set of problems. General Motors allows you to buy your next pre-owned vehicle with the same confidence you would have with a new vehicle. The GM Certified program is available on eligible Chevrolet, Buick, GMC, Pontiac, and Saturn vehicles 2006 or newer. Eligible vehicles can only have 75,000 miles or less.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">GM wants owners to have peace of mind when purchasing a certified vehicle. To deliver this each car is put through a rigorous 172-point inspection and reconditioning process. GM certified technicians adhere to strict, factory-set standards to ensure the engine, chassis, and body are in excellent condition on every certified vehicle. All certified vehicles also come with a vehicle history report. Our process ensures every certified used car will meet our exacting standards and more importantly yours. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">The GM Certified program extends far beyond the initial purchase. We want to ensure your continued satisfaction with all certified vehicles. To accomplish this GM has established the Owner Care program. Each GM Certified vehicle comes with a 2 year/30,000 mile standard CPO maintenance plan. This plan covers the following:</span></p>
<ul>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Oil Changes based on your vehicles Oil Life Monitor System</span> </li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Tire Rotations</span> </li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Multi-Point Vehicle Inspection</span> </li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Should you decide to sell your car the Owner Care program is transferrable to subsequent owners if it is still within the 2 year/ 30,000 mile range. The ability to transfer your Owner Care plan adds value to your vehicle. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Other benefits of the GM Certified program are:</span></p>
<ul>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">12-month/12,000 mile Bumper-to-Bumper Warranty</span> </li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">5 year/100,000 mile Power train Limited Auto Warranty </span>
    <ul>
        <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">24 Hour Roadside Assistance</span> </li>
        <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">Courtesy Transportation</span> </li>
    </ul>
    </li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">3-day/150 mile customer satisfaction guarantee. If you&rsquo;re not completely satisfied with your new certified purchase within 3 days or 150 miles you can bring it back to the dealer and exchange it for a vehicle that is a better fit. </span></li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">3 month free trial of OnStar Directions and Connections including OnStar Turn by Turn navigation. </span></li>
    <li><span style="font-family: calibri; font-size: 18px;">3 month free trial of Sirius XM Satellite Radio</span> </li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: center;">The GM Certified Program allows you to get more for your money. Purchasing a certified pre-owned can lower the total cost of ownership for your vehicle saving you money. Piece of mind is a standard 2year/30,000 mile Owner Care program.&nbsp;<br />
