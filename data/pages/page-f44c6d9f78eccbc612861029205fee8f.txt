        <h2>Automotive Technology</h2>
        
        <div id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolder1_ContentBlock1">
	<span style="display:none"> </span><script language="JavaScript" type="text/javascript" defer="defer"><!--

<div id="paragraph-top" title="paragraph-top" name="paragraph-top"><p>Jobs for trained automotive technicians are waiting. In fact, the U.S. Department of Labor estimates that 100,000 technicians are needed nationwide. To qualify for one of these jobs, you need the right skills. </p><p><strong>Education will take you farther </strong></p><p>With extensive electronic and computerized circuitry, late-model automobiles are highly complex machines. Automotive technicians must be skilled in using modern test equipment, special tools, emission controls, overdrive transmission and other test equipment. Greenville Tech’s Automotive Service Training program will teach you to use the latest equipment – tools that are equal to or better than what you’ll find in the workplace. </p></div>
<div id="accordion">

<h3>Exclusive specialized training</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>Beyond general automotive training, Greenville Tech also offers these specialized options:</p><ul><li><strong>General Motors</strong> – the only GM ASEP training program in South Carolina </li><li><strong>Nissan/Infiniti</strong> – the only Nissan/Infiniti training program in South Carolina </li><li><strong>Southeast Toyota – </strong>the only Toyota training program in South Carolina </li><li><a title="Motorsports" href="/automotive/motorsports/"><strong>Motorsports</strong></a> – the only college-level motorsports program in South Carolina (General Technology Associate in Applied Science degree with a concentration in Motorsports), a two-year program that provides extensive experience in Motorsports, with cross training in automotive and auto body techniques </li></ul></div>

<h3>A career you can count on</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>The level of training provided in this program is well-respected by area employers. </p><p>Graduates are prepared for employment as technicians, service writers, and parts managers at dealerships and independent repair shops. <br /><br />Learn about real-time job openings and salary information through <a title="Career Coach" href="https://gvltec.emsicareercoach.com/#action=loadCourseInfo&amp;Search=&amp;SearchType=course&amp;OccID=51-4121.06&amp;CourseID=106662" target="_blank">Career Coach</a>. <br />      </p></div>

<h3>Is Automotive Technology right for you?</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>If you enjoy cars and trucks, high-tech equipment, and hands-on work, this could be the field for you. <br /><br />Students are responsible for </p><ul><li>Having and maintaining a dealership, service center, or affiliate sponsor by the first day of class and throughout the program </li><li>Purchasing required tools </li><li>Meeting personal protective equipment requirements of Greenville Tech </li><li>Meeting sponsor’s employment requirements including valid driver’s license and drug testing where required </li></ul></div>

<h3>What you’ll learn in the program</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>Students learn to identify, analyze and solve problems. General theory and basic information includes </p><ul><li>Engine fundamentals </li><li>Automotive electricity </li><li>Manual drive train/axle </li><li>Brakes </li><li>Engine reconditioning </li><li>Ignition systems </li><li>Fuel systems </li><li>Suspension &amp; steering </li><li>Automatic transmission </li><li>Automotive air conditioning </li></ul><p>Hands-on experience is emphasized </p><ul><li>Students alternate between working at a local dealership and going to college in 8- to 12-week intervals. </li><li>Students receive an hourly wage and are assigned to an experienced technician who guides and supervises training. </li></ul><p><strong>Professional Credentials</strong>  </p><p>Graduates are encouraged to take the Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) test to gain certification as an Automotive Service Excellence Technician. </p></div>

<h3>Certification and Accreditation</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence/National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation (ASE/NATEF) certification ensures that training meets or exceeds industry standards.</p><p>Greenville Technical College is accredited by the <a title="Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools " href="http://www.sacscoc.org/" target="_blank">Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools</a> to award associate degrees, diplomas and certificates.</p></div>

<h3>Program details and courses</h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p><strong>Automotive Service Training Program (ASTP) <br />Associate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program trains students in the testing, diagnosis, and servicing of motor vehicles, preparing them to become proficient, entry-level automotive technicians. The ASTP program is certified by The National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation (NATEF). </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Automotive_Technology_-_ASTP/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>General Motors Automotive Service Education Program (GM-ASEP) <br />Associate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program trains students in the testing, diagnosis, and servicing of General Motors motor vehicles, preparing them for employment as entry-level automotive technicians at General Motors dealerships or AC Delco shops. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Automotive_Technology-GM_ASEP/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>Nissan/Infiniti Technician Training Program <br />Associate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program covers testing, diagnosis, and servicing of Nissan/Infiniti motor vehicles, preparing graduates to become entry-level automotive technicians at Nissan/Infiniti dealerships. Students can customize their training with up to seven course modules. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Automotive_Technology-Nissan/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>Southeast Toyota Training Program <br />Associate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>The only Toyota training opportunity in South Carolina, this program trains students in the testing, diagnosis, and servicing of Toyota motor vehicles, preparing them for employment as entry-level automotive technicians at Toyota dealerships. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Automotive_Technology-Toyota/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>Motorsports Marketing <br />Certificate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program teaches students how to market and promote events to race teams and prospective businesses. Students will gain a working knowledge of the marketing of race drivers, cars, and venues to potential sponsors that can support racing and use it as an advertising medium. The program is designed to help students understand the many areas of motorsports and their marketing needs, allowing graduates to enter the workforce as motorsports marketing assistants. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Motorsports_Marketing/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>Motorsports Performance Engines <br />Certificate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program trains students in the skills required for proper performance engine building, setup, and testing. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Motorsports_Performance_Engines/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… </p><hr /><p><strong>Race Chassis Building and Setup <br />Certificate in Applied Science </strong></p><p>This program trains students in the proper method and procedures of a race chassis setup. </p><p><a title="Read more from our current catalog" href="/Race_Chassis_Building_and_Setup/">Read more from our current catalog</a> (pdf)… <br /><br /></p></div>

<h3>Gainful Employment </h3>
<div id="accordion-content" title="accordion-content" name="accordion-content"><p>For more information about our graduation rates, the median debt of students who completed the program, and other important information, we encourage you to review our gainful employment disclosure information for these programs: </p><ul><li><strong><a title="Motorsports Marketing" href="/gainful-employment/motorsports-mktg/">Motorsports Marketing</a></strong></li><li><strong><a title="Motorsports Performance Engines" href="/gainful-employment/motorsports-perf-eng/">Motorsports Performance Engines</a></strong></li><li><strong><a title="Race Chassis Building and Setup" href="/gainful-employment/race-chassis/">Race Chassis Building and Setup</a></strong><br /><br /></li></ul></div>
