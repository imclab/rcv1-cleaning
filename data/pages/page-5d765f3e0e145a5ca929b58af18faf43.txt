Better than used but not quite new; these manufacturer backed programs are designed to bring a little peace of mind to the used car buying process and ownership.</div>

This process offers the buyer peace of mind regarding the history and condition of their vehicle, and allows manufacturers and dealers to maximize profit and build brand loyalty.</div>
