<H2 class=pageTitle>Alliance Media Arts and Entertainment Design High School</H2><!-- ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ -->
<DIV class=school>
<DIV>Alliance Media Arts and Entertainment High School is a free public charter school authorized by the Los Angeles Unified School District.</DIV>
<DIV>&nbsp;</DIV>
<DIV>Alliance Media Arts and Entertainment High School provides students in East Los Angeles with a college-ready curriculum that intersects with their interests in media arts and design. The school's goal is to&nbsp;ensure that students have the knowledge and skills to excel in core subject areas and succeed in college, while promoting their interests and talents media arts and technology.&nbsp; Students are challenged to apply their skills through digital photography, film, animation, web design, broadcasting, music, and graphic design.</DIV>
<DIV>&nbsp;</DIV>
<DIV class=infobox>
<H4 id=enrollment>Enrollment for Current School Year</H4>
<P>370 students in grades 9-12</P>
<H4 id=opened>Date Opened</H4>
<P>August 2009</P>
<H4 id=address>Address</H4>
<P>113 South Rowan Avenue<BR>Los Angeles, CA 90063<BR><A href="/school_locations/">See map</A></P>
<H4 id=phone>Phone</H4>
<P>(323) 859-0750</P>
<H4 id=fax>Fax</H4>
<P>(323) 859-0758</P>
<H4 id=website>Website:</H4>
<P><a target="_blank" href="http://www.meadahs.org/" target=_blank>meadahs.org</A></P>
<DIV></DIV><p>Apply in <a href="/forms/application_updated/en/index.jsp">English</a> or <a href="/forms/application_updated/es/index.jsp">Spanish</a>.</p></DIV>
<H3 id=performance>Performance</H3>
<DIV>Alliance Media Arts and Entertainment High school achieved a 2012 Academic Performance Index (API) score of 684. CST results: 34% Advanced Proficient in English, 8% Advanced/Proficient in Math. For more on the school's 2011-2012 Performance Indicators, <A href="/performance/10-11/Dashboards/14-Media Arts Performance Dashboard 10-11.pdf">click here</A>.</DIV>
<DIV>&nbsp;</DIV>
<H3 id=community>Community</H3>
<DIV>Alliance Media Arts and Entertainment High School is located in East Los Angeles, in the neighborhood surrounding Garfield and Roosevelt High Schools.</DIV>
<DIV>&nbsp;</DIV>
<H3 id=demographics_and_average_daily_attendance>Demographics and Average Daily Attendance</H3>
<DIV>99% Latino and 1% Asian, 22% English Language Learner (ELL) students, and 94% free or reduced meal program participants. Average daily attendance is 95%.</DIV>
<DIV>&nbsp;</DIV>
<h3 id="principal_profile">Principal Profile</h3>
<div><img style="WIDTH: 148px; HEIGHT: 196px" class="principal_photo" src="/schools/mediaarts/SANCHEZ Web.jpg" height="2514" width="1510">Art Sanchez has been a high school administrator for the past fifteen years. He has served as an assistant principal, principal, and central office administrative director. He began his teaching career in 1988 as an English Language Learner instructor and physical education teacher. Mr. Sanchez was a head football coach prior to entering into school administration.  Art received his bachelor&#8217;s degree in Physical Education from California State University, Los Angeles and his master of arts degree from Azusa Pacific University.</div></div>
