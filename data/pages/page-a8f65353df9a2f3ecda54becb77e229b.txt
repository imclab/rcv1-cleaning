<h2>COVERAGE> Speed&#038;Stance Swarms SpeedVentures</h2>

<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2287.jpg" alt="Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>
<p>This weekend&#8217;s inaugural <strong>Speed&amp;Stance</strong> meet was crazy!!! I can&#8217;t believe so many people turned up to the event, because we organized and promoted the entire thing in only five days. FIVE DAYS! Thanks to our friends at <a href="https://www.facebook.com/SpeedVentures"><strong>Speed Ventures</strong></a>, we were able to throw this gigantic car event in the parking lot of <strong>Auto Club Speedway</strong> in Fontana!</p>
<p>We advertised the meet to run between 12 noon and 5pm, but every single one of the infield parking lots at the speedway were FULL by 2:45pm! There was a crazy line of cars backed up from the infield parking, through the tunnel, wrapping all the way around to the Cherry Avenue entrance! I was told that once the parking lots allocated to the event hit capacity, the people at the gates had to turn away well over 100 cars! Insane.</p>
<p>Since traffic and road congestion became an issue, the good folks from Auto Club Speedway quickly opened up a couple more parking lots so that cars could pre-stage before rolling in to the actual show area, which saw both <em>autocross</em> and high speed <em>road racing</em> with Speed Ventures on the ROVAL course, and a beginner level car control clinic for <strong>Scion FR-S</strong> and <strong>Subaru BRZ</strong> owners called the <strong>86 Dynamic Driving Academy</strong>.<span id="more-22249"></span></p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2222%20copy.jpg" alt="AE86 MotorMavens Cipher Garage 86FEST Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>
<p>While the rest of our FR-S and BRZ friends who attended <strong>86FEST</strong> learned how to do donuts, J-turns, and navigate a tight, technical course in the 86 Dynamic Driving Academy, <strong>Mike Kim</strong> and I decided to park our matching red cars (Mike&#8217;s 86FEST Scion FR-S and my <strong>Cipher Garage</strong> zenki AE86 hatchback) in front of the MotorMavens booth to promote our <em>upcoming 86FEST event at Auto Club Speedway on August 10 &amp; 11 weekend</em>. Mark your calendars!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2192%20copy.jpg" alt="AE86 MotorMavens Cipher Garage 86FEST Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>
<p>The area around the MotorMavens booth was packed all day long, and we were very happy to have our booth anchored together with our close friends from <strong>DaYUUM!</strong>, <strong>Fatlace</strong>, <strong>Eco Shine Auto</strong>, <strong>Dub Nation</strong>, and <strong>California Car Cover</strong>, which was displaying its <strong>Golden Shine</strong> line of car care products, as well as this super low and light jack, which was specially designed just for lowered cars!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/IMG_2242.jpg" alt="Bernie Chacon Dub Nation Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>

LOL) from Eco Shine Auto and <strong>Bernie Chacon</strong> from Dub Nation had the low jack on display at the booth. Bernie even jacked up his super low 2010 Volkswagen Golf TDI (aka Project Mk6) so everyone could see the capabilities of the jack!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/IMG_2250.jpg" alt="GRID2 GRID 2 Codemasters Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens" width="672" height="448" /></p>

You can <a href="https://www.facebook.com/gridgame/app_328014080634035"><strong>pre-order the game</strong> by clicking here</a>.</p>

<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2304%20copy.jpg" alt="Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>

<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/IMG_2218%20copy.jpg" alt="Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>

Thank you for coming out, James!!! That car is so beautiful!!!</p>

We all joke about how everyone wants to be like Noel, and even use the #CanibeNoel hashtag on Instagram, but seriously&#8230; this guy built an awesome looking car. It&#8217;s the most original and lowest of all the Rocket Bunny equipped FRS builds in the United States. Respect.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2168%20copy.jpg" alt="Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>

<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_1961.jpg" alt="Allen Berg Racing School Fontana Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>
<p>Most of our show attendees naturally look to power-adding mods for their engines when they think of driving faster. However, most of these guys don&#8217;t realize that all cars are only as fast as the driver&#8217;s ability. Fast cars don&#8217;t make fast lap times &#8211; fast drivers do.</p>
<p>This being said, we invited <strong>Allen Berg Racing Schools</strong> to come out and set up a display at our Speed&amp;Stance event. They brought out one of their open wheel school cars to speak to event attendees and show off a car that was capable of higher <em>speeds</em> and had a lower <em>stance</em> than most of the cars in attendance. Look at the front camber too.</p>
<p>The number on the nose cone was even 86!!! What a coincidence??!?!?!?!</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/speed-and-stance-IMG_2028.jpg" alt="Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>
<p>This guy is the man. Our long time friend <strong>Sean Lee</strong>, the founder of the <strong>Purist Group</strong> of car afficionados took a break from his driving instruction duties at the 86DDA course to chill with us and park his beautiful white <strong>Porsche 911 GT3RS</strong> at the MotorMavens booth next to my old red AE86. This car has been <a href="http://motormavens.com/2010/03/carspotting-ae-performance-porsche-gt3-rs/">previously featured on MotorMavens</a>, and don&#8217;t be fooled into thinking this GT3RS is a Cars&amp;Coffee showboat. This car is built and well tuned, and Sean stands apart from many Porsche owners out there, because actually knows how to drive it fast.</p>
<p>When you see the headlights of a seasoned canyon runner and track day veteran like Sean Lee in your rearview mirror, you definitely want to GTFO of the way. And like other well known Porsche owners like Nakai-san and Shinji from <strong>Rauh Welt</strong> in Japan, Sean says his love for performance driving all started behind the wheel of his red 1985 Corolla GT-S coupe. AE86 for the win.</p>
<p><img class="alignnone" src="http://www.motormavens.com/emAlbum/albums/Antonio%20Alvendia/Events/Speed%20And%20Stance/Speed%20and%20Stance%201/_fullsize/IMG_2362.jpg" alt="Cipher Garage AE86 SSR MkIII Sprinter Trueno RSpec Autoworks Speed And Stance Speed&amp;Stance Auto Club Speedway MotorMavens Speed Ventures HellaFlush Stance Nation Dayuum Fatlace State of Stance " width="672" height="448" /></p>

And thanks to my boys <strong>Anthony Juarez</strong>, <strong>Johnny Juarez</strong>, <strong>Midget</strong>, and <strong>John Sayno</strong> of <strong>Team Infinit</strong> for helping me transport the MotorMavens booth back from the event!</p>
<p>Thanks to Mike Kim and <strong>Daniel La Blanc</strong> from <a href="http://www.facebook.com/oemaudioplus"><strong>OEM Audio Plus</strong></a> for helping during teardown of the booth even though he was late for an important dinner date with his wife, and thanks for fay jai <strong>Danny Truong</strong> of<a href="http://www.facebook.com/infinitwheels"><strong> Infinit Wheels</strong></a> for letting me use his Honda Ruckus to get around the event and snap photos all day!</p>

THANK YOU to everyone who came out to the event! The turnout was incredible for only FIVE DAYS of promotion! Can you imagine what it might have been like if we promoted it for like&#8230; <em>two weeks?</em></p>
