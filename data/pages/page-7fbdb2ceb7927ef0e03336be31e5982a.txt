	You&rsquo;re a creative person, a person who loves to honor your God-given talents, whether that&rsquo;s on a stage, in a studio, behind an instrument or in front of a microphone. If you want to take your passion to the next level, become a student in PBA&rsquo;s School of Music &amp; Fine Arts.<br />
	<br />
	<span class="subHeading">At PBA,&nbsp;you&rsquo;ll:</span></p>
<ul>
	<li>
		Benefit from an<span class="subHeading3"> array of creative </span>majors such as art education, graphic arts and voice performance, as well as a variety of minors. PBA is <span class="subHeading3">one of just three Christian institutions </span>in the nation to offer a dance major.</li>
	<li>
		Experience a <span class="subHeading3">Christ-centered education.</span></li>
	<li>
		Learn from <span class="subHeading3">professors who are committed Christians </span>and who are active professionally.</li>
	<li>
		Enjoy a<span class="subHeading3"> 13:1 student-to-faculty ratio </span>and an average class size of just 17.</li>
	<li>
		<span class="subHeading3">Enhance your knowledge </span>and skills through clubs and organizations like the Ballroom Dance Club or the Songwriters Artist Guild.</li>
	<li>
		<span class="subHeading3">Experience exciting performance opportunities </span>through more than a dozen vocal, choral and instrumental groups.</li>
	<li>
		<span class="subHeading3">Participate as a </span>dance major in several dance concerts, showcases and productions, ranging from classical to contemporary.&nbsp;<span class="subHeading"> </span></li>
	<li>
