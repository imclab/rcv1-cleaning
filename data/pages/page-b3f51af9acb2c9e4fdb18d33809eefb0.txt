<p>The College of Fine Arts prepares students for life-long engagement with local and global communities through education in Art, Music, or Theatre. Our dedicated faculty inspire students by challenging them to strive for excellence in creative scholarship/research.</p>

<h4> </h4>

<h4>Considering Art, Music or Theatre?<br />
</h4>

<p>With state-of-the-art facilities and faculty comprised of dedicated teachers and working professionals, the College of Fine Arts combines high educational standards with extraordinary opportunities for real-world experience.</p>

<p>We invite you to visit our <a id="CP___PAGEID=2376,departments.cfm,4|" href="/fine-arts/departments.cfm">Departments</a> section to find all the details about our world-class programs in <a id="CP___PAGEID=128142,/fine-arts/art/index.cfm,22|" href="/fine-arts/art/index.cfm">Art</a>, <a id="CP___PAGEID=128508,index.cfm,124|" href="/fine-arts/music/index.cfm">Music</a> and <a id="CP___PAGEID=125591,/fine-arts/theatre/index.cfm,123|" href="/fine-arts/theatre/index.cfm">Theatre</a>. Or view the events calendar to find out about the many events going on each week, including performances, gallery exhibits, contests, festivals and camps.</p>

<p>As the only college offering the B.F.A., M.F.A., Bachelor's of Music and Master's of Music degrees in the state of South Dakota, you will no doubt find something that's a fit for your interests!</p> </div>
