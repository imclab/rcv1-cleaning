<h1>Fine Arts Programming</h1>

<p>&nbsp;</p>


<img src="/student-life/images/100609_UWS_0692.jpg" width="470" height="313" alt="Music class at UW-Sheboygan" />

<p>The University of Wisconsin-Sheboygan offers a diverse series of Fine Arts programming throughout the academic year. From concerts to <a href="../clubs/theatre-drama/">theater</a> and art exhibits to special events, UW-Sheboygan is alive with cultural and performing arts entertainment.</p>

<p>Staff, students and the community are encouraged to participate in the variety of events and exhibits held on the campus each year. Most fine arts and performing arts events are held in the Fine Arts Building and Fine Arts Gallery. Events planned on the campus for the academic year are listed below.</p>

<p>For more information about upcoming events at UW-Sheboygan, please contact the UW-Sheboygan Box Office at (920) 459-6652 or e-mail <a href="mailto:carrie.hoppe@uwc.edu">carrie.hoppe@uwc.edu</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
