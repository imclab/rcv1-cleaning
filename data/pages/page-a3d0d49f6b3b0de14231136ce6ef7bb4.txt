            <p align="left" class="style3" style="margin-bottom: 0;">&nbsp;</p>
            <p align="left" class="style3" style="margin-top:0;">Pleasanton hosts a number of arts activities and entertainment programs for all ages.&nbsp; For additional information <span style="font-family:verdana;font-size:12px;color:#000000;">on the below items</span>, click on the links to the left.</p>
            <p align="left" class="style1 style2 mmhide_sectiontext">The City offers a variety of performing, visual, and literary arts through its Civic Arts program. These include a presenting series of professional entertainment, our annual free Shakespeare in the Park, Movies in the Park, the popular Poetry, Prose &amp; Arts Festival and poetry reading series, as well as public art pieces around town, and arts events for youth.</p>
            <p>The Community Services Department offers a wide variety of arts classes each quarter. Classes cover areas such as ceramics, origami, dance, drawing, painting, music, and photography.</p>

            <p align="left" class="style1 style2" style="margin-top: 0">Groups such as Pleasanton Cultural Arts Council, Pleasanton Art League, and Pleasanton Community Concert Band also offer arts programs or activities around town. The Pleasanton Downtown Association also coordinates a number of events with an art component, such as the Pleasanton Heritage Festival, Concerts in the Park, and First Wednesday Street Parties. The Museum on Main Street provides a link to past cultures of the area through guest lectures, exhibits and tours.</p>
