<h1 class="postheader"><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/08/27/new-jiangnan-chunzhou-suv-is-a-bit-of-a-ford-raptor-in-china/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to New Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV is a bit of a Ford Raptor in China">New Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV is a bit of a Ford Raptor in China</a></h1>
<p class="date">Published on August 27, 2013 by Joey Wang</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/chunzhou-suv-1.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-48075" title="Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV" alt="New Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV is a bit of a Ford Raptor in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/chunzhou-suv-1-458x305.jpg" width="458" height="305" /></a></p>
<p>This is <a href="http://auto.sohu.com/20130827/n385154376.shtml" target="_blank">the new</a> Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV SUV, it will be launched on the China car market in the first half of 2014, priced between 80.000 and 100.000 yuan (13.000 &#8211; 16.300 USD). The vehicle&#8217;s headlights and grille look very much <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/03/02/spotted-in-china-ford-f-150-raptor-crew-cab-in-white/" target="_blank">Ford Raptor</a>.</p>
<p>The Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV SUV, it is simply called &#8216;SUV&#8217;, is made by a company called Jiangnan Chunzhou Automobile (<a href="http://www.jiangnanauto.com/" target="_blank">web</a>), also known as Jiangnan Auto. Confusingly, back in March we saw spy shots of <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/03/29/spy-shots-jiangnan-chunzhou-suv-is-ready-for-the-chinese-car-market/" target="_blank">another Jiangnan Chunzhou SUV SUV</a>, with a different front and more expensive, but is hasn&#8217;t hit the market yet.</p>
