<p><a href="http://www.femalefirst.co.uk//justin-timberlake//" class="exposure-link " data-linktrack="LinkTracker,articleExposure,justin-timberlake">Justin Timberlake</a> has told fans he's "not even close" to being done after being honoured at the MTV Video Music Awards last night (25.08.13).</p>
<p>The 32-year-old singer picked up four prizes at the annual event - including a lifetime achievement gong titled the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award - but insisted the triumphant evening only marks the beginning after he made his return to music earlier this year following a hiatus to focus on his acting career.</p>
<p>In an open letter on his website Justintimberlake.com, he wrote: "The last time I wrote you an open letter, it was in January and I promised that it would be a 'Big 2013.'</p>
<p>"Since then, from The Grammys to the release of the first part of 'The 20/20 Experience' to the crowd's response at the VMAs, it's been a wild ride.</p>
<p>"I hope you've enjoyed it as much as I have ... This letter is to tell you that we're not done .. .Not even close."</p>
<p>Justin's track 'Mirrors' picked up gongs for Video of the Year and Best Editing, while 'Suit &amp; Tie' - his collaboration with Jay Z - took home the award for Best Direction.</p>
<p>The 'Cry Me A River' hitmaker - who reunited with his former *NSYNC band mates at the event - also said he "can't wait" for fans to hear his upcoming new record.</p>
<p>He continued: "On Sept 30th, the second half of 'The 20/20 Experience', 'The 20/20 Experience 2 of 2', comes out.</p>
<p>"Since all these songs were written together over a year ago, it's been so hard to wait this long to release them. I can't wait for all of you to hear The 20/20 Experience in full."</p>
