	<h2>Bachelor of Arts Degree in Arts & Entertainment Management</h2>
<p><span>Are you looking to combine your passion for the arts with a meaningful career that involves management, planning, promotion, development and marketing? Then look no further than the Arts & Entertainment Management Program at Dean College. Put simply, a major in Arts & Entertainment Management is a business program for those interested in the business side of the arts and entertainment world.</span>
<p><br />
<p><span>The B.A. in Arts & Entertainment Management offers a well-rounded curriculum that features management, technical and performance studies with in-depth course work ranging from business and communications to media arts to dance and theatre management. Dean’s program is the only one of it’s kind offered on the undergraduate level in the Northeast with concentrations available in:</span>
<br />
<br/ />
<ul>
    <li>Media Arts</li>
    <li>Communications</li>
    <li>Performing Arts </li>
</ul>
<p><br />
By providing a solid balance between theoretical knowledge and practical experience, graduates are well-prepared to launch into a career in the arts or entertainment industries upon graduation. Internship opportunities within some of the most notable venues in Boston and Providence allow Dean students to build their resumes during their undergraduate years and develop important contacts and networks that will serve them throughout their careers. </p>
<br />
<p>Learn More about the <a href="http://www.dean.edu/Academics/BachProgramsArtsEntertainmentMgt.cfm" target="_blank">Arts & Entertainment Management </a>curriculum! </p>
