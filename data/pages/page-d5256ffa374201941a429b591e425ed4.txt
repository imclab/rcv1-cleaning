<h1>Automotive and Vehicle Degree and Programs</h1>

<div id="intro">
<p>If you love cars (or trucks, or motorcycles, or boats), you can train to become a repair, maintenance or auto body specialist in less than a year. Self-paced distance learning programs allow you to master engines, drive trains, brakes, transmissions, electrical systems, tires, or exhaust systems-all at your own pace. Or take courses in body repair, automotive detailing, diesel technology, and small engine repair.</p>
<h2>Put Your Automotive Training to Work</h2>
<p>Many students choose to combine their career training with on-the-job apprenticeships, using their skills to work on real problems. You can also add advanced online classes in automotive specialties or other systems.</p></div>
