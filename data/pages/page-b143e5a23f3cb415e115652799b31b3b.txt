<div class="divider-post span8" style="margin-bottom:0px;"></div>

<div class="entry-content">

<p>There is a skill to modifying cars, and that is to make them either blend in to the crowd in the case of a sleeper or to make them stand out to help the owner look like a creative, individual person. And there is this <a title="Chevrolet" href="http://www.chinacartimes.com/category/cherolet-china">Chevrolet</a> Cruze. This Cruze, found by Tycho of the Car News China, manages to define China&#8217;s lack of help for the mentally impaired in a single series of photos.</p>
<p>The Cruze has been come the car of choice for young white collar workers that are looking to buy their first car, a big mission accomplished from <a title="Shanghai GM" href="http://www.chinacartimes.com/category/shanghai-gm">Shanghai GM</a>. But we really do have to feel sorry for the poor <a title="Chevrolet" href="http://www.chinacartimes.com/category/cherolet-china">Chevrolet</a> guys that have to drive this thing into the dealership every few months for a dealership.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.chinacartimes.com/wp-content/chevrolet-cruze-china-pink-3.jpg" onclick="return TrackClick('http%3A%2F%2Fwww.chinacartimes.com%2Fwp-content%2Fchevrolet-cruze-china-pink-3.jpg','')"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-33915" alt="chevrolet cruze china pink 3 839x1024 Car News China Discovers Worlds Ugliest Chevrolet Cruze" src="http://www.chinacartimes.com/wp-content/chevrolet-cruze-china-pink-3-839x1024.jpg" width="839" height="1024" title="Car News China Discovers Worlds Ugliest Chevrolet Cruze" /></a></p>
<p>If cars were self aware, we&#8217;re quite positive that this one would be looking to throw itself and its owners off the nearest Beijing suspension bridge. </p>
<p>Head over to <strong><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/07/26/chevrolet-cruze-is-very-pretty-in-pink-in-china/" onclick="return TrackClick('http%3A%2F%2Fwww.carnewschina.com%2F2013%2F07%2F26%2Fchevrolet-cruze-is-very-pretty-in-pink-in-china%2F','Car+News+China+on+an+awful+Chevvy')" title="Car News China on an awful Chevvy">Car News China</a></strong> to see the rest of this monster. </p>
