<h2><a title="The 2013 MTV Video Music Awards, The Good, The Bad, And The WTF" href="http://www.dippedincream.com/the-2013-mtv-video-music-awards-the-good-the-bad-and-the-wtf/" rel="bookmark">The 2013 MTV Video Music Awards, The Good, The Bad, And The WTF</a></h2>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/vmas.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32326" alt="VMAs" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/vmas-500x333.jpg" width="500" height="333" /></a></p>
<p>I don't expect much from the <strong>VMA</strong>s anymore. I didn't even watch it last year, but nothing could have prepared me for the horror that took place on stage during Sunday night's show. I'll get to that in a moment, but surprisingly, it wasn't <em>all</em> bad. A boy band reunited, we got to see some old favorites, and despite some glaring sound problems, most of the performances not involving teddy bears were great.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div style="background-color: #000000; width: 520px;">
<div style="padding: 4px;">
<p><iframe src="http://media.mtvnservices.com/embed/mgid:uma:video:mtv.com:942062/cp~id%3D1712596%26vid%3D942062%26instance%3Dmtv%26uri%3Dmgid%3Auma%3Avideo%3Amtv.com%3A942062" height="288" width="512" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p style="text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; padding: 4px; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px;">Get More: <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/ontv/vma/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">2013 VMA</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Artists.MTV</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/music/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Music</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/lady-gaga/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Lady Gaga</a></p>
</div>
</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Lady Gaga </strong>opened the show paying tribute to herself with her performance of "<em>Applause." </em>She changed into numerous costumes and wigs, and looked pretty damned good doing it.</p>
<p><em id="__mceDel"><span style="color: #000000;"><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/miley.gif" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-32322" alt="Miley mess" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/miley.gif" width="500" height="265" /></a></span> </em><br />
Now let's talk about <em>this</em> mess. <strong>Miley Cyrus </strong>had the gall to walk, or what she probably thought was strutting, out on stage in some horrid bathing suit surrounding by strippers with teddy bears strapped to their backs. Remember all those anti-drug commercials from the 80s and 90s? <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em>"This is your brain on drugs."</em></span> If this was our brain on Miley, oops "Molly", then that is a trip I never want to take. Then <strong>Robin Thicke </strong>joins her looking like he just got cast in the low budget sequel to '<em>Beetlejuice.' </em>The whole thing was weird, and not in a good way. There was no need for us to see those uncooked chicken cutlets she calls an ass, and Robin, dude, what the hell? It was only two months ago that we were jammin' to "<em>Blurred Lines" </em>and this is how you do us?</p>
<p><em id="__mceDel"><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/kanye.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32328" alt="Kanye" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/kanye-332x500.jpg" width="332" height="500" /></a></em></p>
<p><em id="__mceDel"> </em><strong>Jared Leto</strong>, whose ombre hair is probably natural, introduced <strong>Kanye West</strong> who performed<em> "Blood On The Leaves."</em> He was mostly shown in silhouette against a backdrop of the woods. Nice, but we've seen better from Ye. It <em>was </em>refreshing after that Miley madness though.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/pharrell-with-niles-and-df.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32337" alt="pharrell with niles and df" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/pharrell-with-niles-and-df-500x332.jpg" width="500" height="332" /></a></p>
<p>That big to-do about <strong>Daft Punk </strong>having to cancel their appearance on <em>'The Colbert Report'</em> because they were scheduled for the VMAs, well, it proved to be quite underwhelming. The duo appeared on stage with collaborators <strong>Pharrell Williams </strong>and <strong>Nile Rodgers </strong>to present <em>Best Female Video</em> and didn't say anything, natch, but I appreciated them stopping by. They hung out in the audience for the rest of the night.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div style="background-color: #000000; width: 520px;">
<div style="padding: 4px;">
<p><iframe src="http://media.mtvnservices.com/embed/mgid:uma:video:mtv.com:949301/cp~id%3D1712596%26vid%3D949301%26instance%3Dmtv%26uri%3Dmgid%3Auma%3Avideo%3Amtv.com%3A949301" height="288" width="512" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p style="text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; padding: 4px; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px;">Get More:<br />
<a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/ontv/vma/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">2013 VMA</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Artists.MTV</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/music/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Music</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/justin-timberlake/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Justin Timberlake</a></p>
</div>
</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>An hour into the show, what all the 90s kids were waiting for, <strong>Justin Timberlake</strong>'s big performance. He spun, he twirled, he brought back some old hits and mixed them in with the new, but that didn't matter. Where was <b>'N SYNC</b>?!!! The guys only joined him on stage for <em>"Girlfriend"</em> and <em>"Bye Bye Bye".</em> Clearly not enough stage time! I doubt we'll be hearing about a reunion tour, but it's nice to see that their moves are still on point. Timberlake's buddy, <strong>Jimmy Fallon, </strong>presented him with the <em>Video Vanguard</em> award, which he seemed genuinely honored to receive.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/macklemore.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32338" alt="macklemore" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/macklemore-500x332.jpg" width="500" height="332" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Macklemore &amp; Ryan Lewis </strong>were big winners, taking home two of those Moonmen. They took to the stage for <em>"Same Love"</em> with <strong>Mary Lambert </strong>and <strong>Jennifer Hudson</strong>. I can't co-sign that powder blue suit, but I know they made a lot of folks in Seattle proud.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div style="background-color: #000000; width: 520px;">
<div style="padding: 4px;">
<p><iframe src="http://media.mtvnservices.com/embed/mgid:uma:video:mtv.com:943312/cp~id%3D1712596%26vid%3D943312%26instance%3Dmtv%26uri%3Dmgid%3Auma%3Avideo%3Amtv.com%3A943312" height="288" width="512" frameborder="0"></iframe></p>
<p style="text-align: left; background-color: #ffffff; padding: 4px; margin-top: 4px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px;">Get More:<br />
<a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/ontv/vma/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">2013 VMA</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Artists.MTV</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/music/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Music</a>, <a style="color: #439cd8;" href="http://www.mtv.com/artists/bruno-mars/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.mtv.com']);" target="_blank">Bruno Mars</a></p>
</div>
</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>TLC </strong>introduced <strong>Drake</strong> who gave us <em>"Started From The Bottom"</em> and his awkward profile. <strong>Jaden Smith </strong>was really into it. <strong>Bruno Mars </strong>won the award for <em>Best Male Video</em>, and then almost blinded us during <em>"Gorilla."</em> Again, the sound was an issue here;  it was still nice to hear one of the best songs from<em> 'Unorthodox Jukebox' </em>live!</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/riri.gif" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-32339" alt="riri" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/riri.gif" width="500" height="265" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Katy Perry </strong>closed the show, performing her single <em>"Roar"</em> under the Brooklyn Bridge. There was a whole boxer theme going on, and jump roping! But it ended abruptly and no one really seemed here, there or anywhere for it. <strong>Rihanna </strong>won the night even though she didn't perform and wasn't nominated for anything. Her shady looks during Miley's performance, and putting popcorn under some girl's seat, made her the person to pay attention to.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/smiths.png" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32340" alt="Smiths" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/smiths-500x282.png" width="500" height="282" /></a></p>
<p>Also have to give a shout out to the <strong>Smith </strong>family for their reactions during that talent holocaust. So until next year, when we have to do this all over again!</p>

<h2><a title="The Best and Worst Dressed Of The VMAs Red Carpet" href="http://www.dippedincream.com/the-best-and-worst-dressed-of-the-vmas-red-carpet/" rel="bookmark">The Best and Worst Dressed Of The VMAs Red Carpet</a></h2>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>There were dozens of lists of the most memorable <strong>VMA</strong> fashion moments, and I doubt any from last night's show will make any future lists. Though the ladies looked fab, there weren't any crazy outfits, not even from <strong>Lady Gaga</strong>. The VMAs are where you get crazy, and literally, show your ass, but we'll have to settle for some high slits and bare midriffs.</p>

<p>To promote the theme of her new single "<em>Roar," </em><strong>Katy Perry </strong>wore this animal print <strong>Emanuel Ungaro </strong>dress. She was a bit upstaged by the lovely girl behind her.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/miley.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-32304" alt="Miley" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/miley-332x500.jpg" width="332" height="500" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Miley Cyrus</strong>, she tries so damned hard. Here she is in vintage <strong>Dolce &amp; Gabbana </strong>looking like a reject extra from '<em>Selena.' </em></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>Selena Gomez </strong>looked much better in <strong>Versace</strong>.</p>

<h2><a title="*NSYNC Creates Official Twitter Account" href="http://www.dippedincream.com/nsync-creates-official-twitter-account/" rel="bookmark">*NSYNC Creates Official Twitter Account</a></h2>
<div id="attachment_32272" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/nsync-cap.jpg" ><img class="size-medium wp-image-32272 " title="*NSYNC cap" alt="OH." src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/nsync-cap-500x490.jpg" width="500" height="490" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">OH.</p></div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>So. That happened! There I was, trolling the 'net, when both Twitter and Facebook seemingly exploded. The reason? <strong>*NSYNC</strong> was now live, and <em>verified</em>, on Twitter. But, wait! They're not a group anymore! <em>Au contraire</em>. This is actually something we just don't know.</p>
<p>Signs, like the "rumored" reunion at the <strong>VMAs</strong> are certainly pointing to the affirmative, regardless of <strong>Lance Bass</strong>' apparent denial. Is the boy band reuniting for a single VMA performance in honor of <strong>Justin Timberlake </strong>receiving the 'Video Vanguard' award? Or is something more long-term in the works? Or...was the original plan for a one-shot, with the ensuing fan reaction, enough to make the former members rethink their solo statuses? Again, we just don't know. But it's very telling that the account obtained over 30, 000 followers in the 4 hours since it'd been created. Clearly, the fans are still around (akin to those of the <strong>Backstreet Boys/NKOTB</strong>) and there is money to be made. To answer the question posed in their first official tweet, I'm going to say that the mic is <em>definitely</em> on.<em></em></p>

<h2><a title="To Russia with an &#8216;LOL! No&#8217;: Wentworth Miller Sets &#8216;Em Straight" href="http://www.dippedincream.com/to-russia-with-an-lol-no-wentworth-miller-sets-em-straight/" rel="bookmark">To Russia with an &#8216;LOL! No&#8217;: Wentworth Miller Sets &#8216;Em Straight</a></h2>
<div id="attachment_32241" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 392px"><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/WM1.jpg" ><img class="size-full wp-image-32241 " alt="Say what?!" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/WM1.jpg" width="382" height="382" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Say what?!</p></div>
<p>I'm starting to think that Russia lives in a bona fide dream world, the real-life embodiment of the ubiquitous sno-globe. Somehow, they seem to think that their harsh and disgusting human rights violations against their LGBTQ citizens will have nary an effect on how the world sees/treats them. Pause and shake your head at that irony, folks! It's exactly as ridiculous as it sounds.</p>
<p>Cue the written "voice" of one <strong>Wentworth Miller</strong>. You may remember him from things such as <strong>Prison Break</strong> and, let's be real, quite a few of your fantasies. The man is beautiful, well-spoken, and...gay. Apparently, this wasn't much of a secret as it was something a number of people had suspected over the years. In fact, there was more shock over his 41 yrs of age (he must drink the same virgin's blood as <b>Johnny Depp</b>) and exactly <em>how</em> he chose to "come out" i.e. make his sexual orientation wide-spread knowledge rather than speculation.</p>
<p>Russia, specifically the <strong>St. Petersburg International Film Festival</strong>, extended an invitation to the Brit  to be a "guest of honor" during the fest which runs from Sept. 13th to the 22nd. Wentworth, with a class the Russian government would do well to emulate, wrote an open letter with a clear and emphatic response:</p>
<p><em>"...as a gay man, I must decline."</em></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/WM2.jpg" ><img class="size-full wp-image-32243 aligncenter" alt="WM2" src="http://www.dippedincream.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/WM2.jpg" width="500" height="385" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>He continues on to note that, despite being part Russian himself, he <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em>"cannot in good conscience participate in a celebratory occasion hosted by a country where people like myself are being systematically denied their basic right to live and love openly"</em></span>.</p>
<p>Lawwwwwwd. I don't know about you but I can smell the singed flesh of that burn from here! We may have Russia attempting to behave as if everything were peachy keen but <strong>Wentworth</strong> is having none of it! To the point of revealing an aspect of his life that he'd, before now, fiercely guarded. And, truly, if there's ever a reason to officially out oneself, doing it to bring even <em>more</em> attention to a country's ass-backward policies regarding a significant number of their population <strong><em>is</em> </strong>100% worth it.</p>
