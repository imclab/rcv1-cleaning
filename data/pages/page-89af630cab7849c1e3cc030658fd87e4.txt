        		<p>BMW 1 Series M:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.autodebut.com/2011/bmw-1-series-m-coupe/"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1404" title="Orange BMW 1M" src="http://autodebut.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/bmw_1_series_m_coupe_2.jpg" alt="Orange BMW 1M" width="596" height="305" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">BMW has finally made the ultimate driving machine again. The BMW 1M will be packed with a twin &#8211; turbocharged engine producing 340 horsepower and 369 lb torque with a 6 speed manual. The 1M will be able to reach 60 in 4.7 seconds and run around the Nürburgring in 8 minutes and 12 seconds.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Our full <a href="http://www.autodebut.com/2011/bmw-1m-bmw-1-series-m-coupe-review/#more-4221">overview on the BMW 1M</a></p>
