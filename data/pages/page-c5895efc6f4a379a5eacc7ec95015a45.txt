			<h1>
				&#8216;Blogologues&#8217; brings Internet humor to life			</h1>

			
			<div class="article-content">

				<div class="wp-caption"><img src="http://www.metro.us/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/8bbb11abf4762ebfd1f3a766871e2b85.jpg" class="attachment-medium wp-post-image" alt="Snapshots from &quot;Blogologues,&quot; running now through January at Under St. Marks." /><div class="wp-caption-text"></div></div>
				<p>For an affordable, lighthearted evening of comedy, check out<br />
&#8220;Blogologues&#8221; at Under St. Marks in the East Village. Conveniently<br />
located and affordably priced ($15 includes admission and a free beer),<br />
this show presents the best of the Internet&#8217;s worst in a series of skits<br />
stolen from your favorite websites and blogs. </p>
<p>Kicking off the six-month series, which primarily runs on the last<br />
Monday of each month, &#8220;I Need A Vocation&#8221; focused on the theme of<br />
employment <span>&ndash;</span><br />
or lack thereof. Segments touched upon such topics as office etiquette,<br />
pie charts, work affairs and the joys of being a career housewife.<br />
Though the skits were acted out so as to<br />
mine the depths of their humor by an ensemble of highly talented<br />
comedians, one complaint might be that the material itself came from a<br />
limited number of sources and dated as far back as 2007. Given the<br />
gigantic scope of material available on any given topic on any given<br />
week (especially if you&#8217;re seeking unemployment humor these days), more<br />
topical and timely material would have given the show that extra oomph.<br />
Each month brings a new topic; the Lively Productions team also accepts<br />
suggestions for<br />
future sources and themes.</p>
<div class="im">
Still, for the price and the caliber of comedy, it&#8217;s hard to go wrong.<br />
The space itself is relaxed and fun: an unpretentious black box where<br />
you can hit the restroom, stop by the bar and then grab some Cheetos from a bowl on<br />
the ledge within ten paces of your seat. Plus, did we mention you get a<br />
free beer? </p>
<p>You can choose from 7 p.m. or 9 p.m. performances; upcoming dates are:</p>
<p>Monday, Sept. 26, 2011<br />
Monday, Oct. 24, 2011<br />
Monday, Nov. 28, 2011<br />
Friday. Dec. 30, 2011<br />
Monday, Jan. 30, 2012<span style="line-height: 24px;font-family: georgia,'bitstream charter',serif;font-size: 16px"></span></p>
</div>
