				<h1>Welcome</h1>
				<p>Welcome to our new, exciting and informative website. This is a stellar year for us as we celebrate our 40<sup>th</sup> Anniversary! We want to celebrate in old and new ways in keeping with our history and traditions. The introduction of our new and improved website is a new way. In 1972 when we were founded the Internet was in its infancy and now we have a new way of sharing our stories. We hope you will take a few minutes and visit our Heritage section to remember, our Events page to learn about upcoming events and our Education page learn about new ways of sharing arts and culture.</p>
<p>We are so excited to be celebrating our 40<sup>th</sup> year! These are very trying times for arts organizations and non-profits. We are thrilled to still be here bringing arts and culture to this community through our Delta Arts Center, through partnerships with local schools and universities and special visual and musical events.</p>
