<p>Carmel arts and entertainment is the stuff of lore. In 1910, the San Francisco Call reported that 60 percent of Carmel&rsquo;s houses were built by citizens who were &ldquo;devoting their lives to work connected to the aesthetic arts.&rdquo; Today the tradition continues with live music and theater for the masses and bon vivant alike. Carmel&rsquo;s venues are historic, including the restored Sunset Theater, the Golden Bough Theater, and the Forest Theater, the first outdoor theater west of the Rockies.</p>
