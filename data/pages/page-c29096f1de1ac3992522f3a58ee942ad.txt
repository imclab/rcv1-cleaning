<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
TV Entertainment Hosts Serve The Community
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-2455584155414902238' itemprop='description articleBody'>
A question I am often asked is, &quot;How can I become a Host?&quot; <br><br>Now that&#39;s a loaded question. The question should be &amp;hellip;&quot;Do I have what it takes to become a host?&quot; What are the core technical skills and values you need to have in order to become a host? Its as simple as the three T&#39;s. Do you have the three T&#39;s? These are THINK. TALK.TEACH. <br><br>THINK FAST.<br><br>Do you think fast? What I mean by that is... can you visually recognize see text and process text with speed and talk out the ideas in a conversational manner? You see, this is something that great hosts have in common. They process their thoughts very quickly. They think visually; they think in bullet points. They see text in whole phrases not just word-by-word. They see what&#39;s ahead but are present to the moment at the same time. Now this skill cannot be developed overnight. It takes time. If you are a fast, natural talker, you have it. You process pretty quickly. If you speak slowly, you process in an even, methodical, measured way. <br><br>This does not mean if you process slowly or speak slowly that you cannot become a TV Host. Those who speak too fast and slush their words together need to work on their speech as well. So the key is to process quickly and speak in a controlled manner by punching words, adding inflection when you are going from one point of the story to another, and using diction to sound clear by leaving a space in between each word. <br><br>Now, the next T-Core Value or Technique is TALK.<br><br> Do you speak quickly and briskly? Do you have great diction and speed so you sound clear, concise and smart? Do you speak so fast you slur your words together? Or,do you speak too slowly for TV? Now speaking quickly does not mean talking fast. It means to talk with urgency, excitement and energy but it also means to speak with precise clarity. This type of clear, fast communication works brilliantly on television. Clear communication with energy is the key to sounding like a pro on TV. It&#39;s a skill that is developed over time. Repetitive reading text aloud can help accelerate the think and talk process. <br><br>You see, speaking for the camera or TV HOSTING is a MIND, MOUTH and BODY CONNECTION. When these are aligned properly, then and only then are you really a host. Most of the time I spend with clients is teaching them verbal mastery, connecting their mind (thinking) with their mouth (talking) and their body (teaching heart). <br><br>Lets move on to T-Core Value #3: TEACH. <br><br>As TV Hosts we are Teachers. We like to teach the world. We are leaders. So if you like to explain process, if you like to give directions and help people than this is the career for you. Provided that you have the other T-skills mentioned above. Passion for learning and teaching is a must when becoming a speaker or host. Having a passion to pass on information is of great importance. Having the passion and curiosity to want to draw out information from people also helps when you are interviewing people. All of this passion for passing information, informing the public, teaching them... is a core value for TV HOSTING. <br><br>In then end, when you think about TV Hosts they are community servers. They serve the community daily weekly monthly. We are a core necessity in society. To transfer the news, create order on a game show, or help you pick out the coolest gift on QVC, it&#39;s all community service. We serve the public with a smile... and hopefully, get paid while we do it.<br><br><br>----------------------------------------------------<br>Master Talent Teachers is an award-winning team of top entertainment industry teaching professionals providing FREE valuable videos, insider tips and products to empower your craft and career! For even more valuable information <br><br>Go to =&gt; <a href="http://www.mastertalentteachers.com">http://www.mastertalentteachers.com</a> now and watch FREE in-depth videos.<br><br><br><br>EasyPublish this article: <a href="http://submityourarticle.com/articles/easypublish.php?art_id=294132">http://submityourarticle.com/articles/easypublish.php?art_id=294132</a>
