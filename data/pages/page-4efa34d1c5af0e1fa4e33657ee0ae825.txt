		<h1 class="entry-title">Welcome to Arts &amp; Events</h1>
	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1712" alt="home-photos" src="http://arts.santa-clarita.com/files/2012/01/home-photos.jpg" width="559" height="187" /></p>
<p>There is always something going on in the <a href="http://www.santa-clarita.com/">City of Santa Clarita</a>.  Whether it&#8217;s music in the park, a unique art exhibit, a national cycling tour, or outdoor recreation, the City entertains thousands of residents and visitors each year.   Seasonal events are our specialty, from the <a href="http://www.cowboyfestival.org/">Cowboy Festival</a> in the spring, and <a href="http://arts.santa-clarita.com/events/concerts-in-the-park/">Concerts in the Park</a> in the summer, to <a href="http://thursdaysatnewhall.com/">Thursdays@Newhall</a> in the fall, and the <a href="http://scmarathon.org/">Santa Clarita Marathon</a> in the winter.  So grab a friend or your family and get out and explore Santa Clarita’s year-round calendar of fun for everyone!</p>
