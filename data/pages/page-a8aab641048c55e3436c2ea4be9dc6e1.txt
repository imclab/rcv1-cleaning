Gene's Auto Repair is <a type="1" href="http://www.diamondcertified.org/Meaning.html" target="_blank">Diamond Certified</a>, <a type="1" href="http://www.ase.com/" target="_blank">ASE certified</a>, <a type="1" href="http://www.aaa.com/AAA_Travel/Auto/automotive_services.htm" target="_blank">AAA approved</a> and a <a type="1" href="http://www.bar.ca.gov/80_BARResources/01_CAP&GoldShield/GoldShield_Stations.html" target="_blank">Gold Shield Station</a>.  Gene's Auto Repair holds <a type="1" href="http://www.bar.ca.gov/70_SiteWideInfo/02_Tools/03_VerifyLicense.html" target="_blank">four licenses</a> with the Bureau of Automot<img src="images/photo_keys.jpg" width="233" height="350" align="left" class="border" style="margin-right:10px; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:5px" alt="Auto Repair Vallejo" />ive Repair: ARD, Smog, Brake and Lamp

        blocks from the Mare Island water front. <br />

      <h2>We are Diamond Certified</h2>
      <p>As of<em> </em>May 2008, Gene's Auto Repair earned the prestigious Diamond Certified<sup>®</sup> award.  The Diamond Certified award is presented only
        to Automotive Industry and other local service companies that
        rate highest in quality in a detailed customer satisfaction study of
        their customers served during the previous four-year period. Each
        qualifying company must also pass a license, insurance, complaint
        status and legal rating.  To maintain Diamond Certification,
        American Ratings Corporation conducts ongoing customer satisfaction
        surveys and checks credentials on a regular basis.</p>
      <p>As a Diamond
        Certified company, Gene's Auto Repair is proud to be
        recognized for exceptional customer satisfaction and
        professionalism. <a type="1" href="http://www.diamondcertified.org/Meaning.html" target="_blank">Click to find out more</a> </p>
      <h2>What is a Gold Shield Station?</h2>
      <p>A Gold
        Shield Station is a licensed Smog Check station, which meets higher
        performance standards.  A Gold Shield Station provides a variety
        of inspection and repair services to California motorists. In addition
        to its regular smog check inspection and repair services, Gold Shield
        stations can inspect and certify Directed Vehicles as indicated on the
        DMV renewal notice, issue certificates to Gross Polluters, and perform
        state subsidized repairs. <a type="1" href="http://www.bar.ca.gov/03_BARPrograms/Smog_Check.html" target="_blank"><br />
        Click to find out more</a></p>
      <h2>Our Technicians</h2>
      <p>Our
        ASE certified technicians are trained and certified in engine repair,
        suspension and steering, brakes, electrical/electronic systems, heating
        and air conditioning, engine performance, and automotive advanced
        engine performance.  All of our technicians are certified smog
        technicians licensed to perform emission diagnostics and smog
        repairs.  Plus they have the combined experience of over 50 years
        in general automotive repair. <a type="1" href="http://www.ase.com/Template.cfm?Section=Motorists2" target="_blank"><br />
        Click to find out more</a></p>
