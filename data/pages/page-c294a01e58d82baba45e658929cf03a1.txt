<p class=MsoNormal><b style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'>CODA Holdings, Inc. to
Restructure Business around Energy Storage<o:p></o:p></b></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt'>May 1,
2013 -- Los Angeles, CA -- CODA Holdings, Inc. (the Company or CODA) announced
today that it is focusing its business strategy on the growing energy storage
market.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>CODA plans to implement the
restructuring of its business through a voluntary filing under Chapter 11 of
the U.S. Bankruptcy Code. <span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> </span>The voluntary petition
was filed today in the District of Delaware.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>This process is intended to enable the Company to complete a sale,
confirm a Plan and emerge from bankruptcy in a stronger position to execute its
new business plan.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>The Company expects
the sale process to take 45 days to complete.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0in;margin-bottom:.0001pt'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></p>

<p class=MsoNormal><span style='mso-ascii-font-family:Calibri;mso-fareast-font-family:
"Times New Roman";mso-hansi-font-family:Calibri;mso-bidi-font-family:Calibri'>FCO
MA CODA Holdings LLC, an affiliate of </span>Fortress Investment Group, is
leading a consortium of lenders intending to provide debtor-in-possession (DIP)
financing to enable the Companys energy storage business to remain fully
operational during the restructuring process.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>The Company has filed a motion with the bankruptcy court for approval of
the consortium, or its designee, as the stalking horse bidder in the sale
process to acquire the Company post-bankruptcy.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>In addition, the Company will seek to monetize value of its existing
automotive business assets.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span><span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>    </span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal>&quot;After concluding a comprehensive review of our
strategic options, the Board of Directors, management team and senior lending
group have concluded that focusing on the Companys energy storage business
presents the best opportunity moving forward, said Phil Murtaugh, Chief
Executive Officer, CODA Holdings, Inc.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'> 
</span>We believe the restructuring process that we have entered into today
will enable the Company to complete a sale and confirm a Plan that maximizes the
value of its assets, serving the best interests of our stakeholders.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>CODA diversified its business and formed CODA Energy two
years ago.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>CODA Energys products are
based on the same core technology, which includes its proprietary battery
management and thermal management systems, found in CODAs vehicles adapted for
stationary energy storage applications.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>The
Company designs and builds scalable, custom energy storage solutions optimized
for generation, distribution and behind-the-meter applications for commercial,
residential and industrial end users.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>   </span>The
Company is currently shipping product, and has a robust pipeline of new
customers and existing installations in the field.</p>

<p class=MsoNormal>CODAs legal advisor in connection with the restructuring is
White &amp; Case LLP.<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Emerald Capital
Advisors serves as its Chief Restructuring Officer and restructuring advisor,
and Houlihan Lokey serves as its investment banker for the restructuring.<span
style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>Sidley Austin LLP is serving as FCO MA CODA
Holdings LLCs<span style='mso-spacerun:yes'>  </span>legal advisor.<b
style='mso-bidi-font-weight:normal'> <o:p></o:p></b></p>
