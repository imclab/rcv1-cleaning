<p>Things got physical for <a href="http://imnotobsessed.com/2013/08/26/alec-baldwin-food-dad/" target="_blank"><strong>Alec Baldwin</strong> and a paparazzi </a>today in NYC.  According to <a href="http://www.tmz.com/2013/08/27/alec-baldwin-nypd-photog-attack/" target="_blank">TMZ</a> both <strong>Alec</strong> and his wife <strong>Hilaria</strong> were out walking when they were approached by a paparazzi looking to take their picture. <strong>Hilaria</strong> ducked into a local tea store to avoid the pap and<strong> Alec</strong> called the police. I guess <strong>Alec</strong> must have come out of the store because apparently he grabbed the photog.</p>
<p>TMZ spoke to the pap who said <em>&#8220;All I can say is I was doing my job asking him normal questions and he flipped. That&#8217;s all.&#8221; </em>although a source close to Baldwin claims that the paparazzi was the agressor.</p>
<p>The police did arrive on the scene but both <strong>Alec</strong> and the photographer have agreed to not press any charges.  I can absolutely believe that the paparazzi was aggressive and that <strong>Alec</strong> would be protective of his wife.  <a href="http://imnotobsessed.com/2013/08/24/alec-baldwin-hilaria-thomas-baby-girl/" target="_blank">She just a baby a few days ago</a>! I wonder if the baby was with them? Either way I think that photographers should be able to take their pictures but there&#8217;s no need to get so close and speak to the celebrities if they don&#8217;t want to be bothered. Thoughts?</p>

					<p><a href="http://imnotobsessed.com/?attachment_id=502246" rel="attachment wp-att-502246"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-502246" alt="vmas press room brooklyn" src="http://cdn.imnotobsessed.com/wp-content/uploads/wenn206000182.jpg" width="520" height="319" /></a></p>
<p>Sunday night&#8217;s VMA&#8217;s gave us<a href="http://imnotobsessed.com/2013/08/26/nsync-vma-performance-scaled-justin-timberlake/" target="_blank"> just a short<strong> N&#8217;Sync</strong> reunion,</a> but rehearsing for those 15 seconds on stage was enough to remind the band members -<strong> Joey Fatone, Chris Kirkpatrick, Lance Bass, JC Chasez</strong> and <strong>Justin Timberlake,</strong> just what they loved and hated about each other!  In a radio interview about the performance <strong>Chris Kirkpatrick</strong> <a href="http://www.usmagazine.com/entertainment/news/chris-kirkpatrick-n-sync-vmas-reunion-reminded-me-why-we-hate-each-other-2013278" target="_blank">said</a> <em>&#8220;It was funny because all of us getting back together, we remembered what we love about each other and what we hate about each other,&#8221; the singer, 41, said. &#8220;It was just a bit of fun.&#8221;</em></p>
<p>It also doesn&#8217;t sound like <strong>Chris</strong> is too eager to do it again! He addressed <a href="http://imnotobsessed.com/2008/09/10/nsync-reunion-coming-up/" target="_blank">the physicality of the performance</a> and said <em>&#8220;Honestly, when he talked to us I thought we were just coming out with mic stands and singing. When I came home from rehearsals my girl&#8217;s like &#8216;Why are you drenched?&#8217; . . .I&#8217;m too old for this! I was so worried about not messing up for him.&#8221;</em></p>
<p>Ha!  It did kind of look like they were a bit out of shape:)  I enjoyed the performance though!</p>
