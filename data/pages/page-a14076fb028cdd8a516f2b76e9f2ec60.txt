Who doesn&#39;t want to have palatial houses to live in and swanky cars to drive in? Reading entertainment news fulfills that desire in an indirect way. Celeb gossip brings about a certain amount of difference to the humdrum lives that they are otherwise leading. It is this escape that feeds the craze of paparazzi scoops and other ways to get the updates of what happens behind the glitzy screen.</p> <p>Why do you think celebrity gossip articles and posts always contain references to the personal lives of the personalities we are interested in? The answer is not complex. People want to live life the way these personalities are perceived to be living through the pages of celeb gossip. They want to date attractive people like the real characters in the entertainment news sections do. They want to explore forbidden paths like celebs do. They want to push the boundaries of what is permissible in society. They want to know through these Hollywood gossip columns how these celebs can get away with something that they would have got stuck with.</p> <p>The celebrity gossip rumors sometimes lead people askew. Lots of what life is in the pages of the entertainment news sections may not be reality or even fact. But readers of the celeb gossip pages are smart and they know how to sift the chaff from the grain. When they read some outrageous news item published as a paparazzi scoop, they discard that immediately as piece of trash. You cannot fool the updated reader anymore. Because they take such an active interest in the lives of the celebs, they are well aware of what is actually going on. The varied number of sources is another reason that nothing that happens can be entirely published as gospel truth.</p><div class="print image_sharing_exclude"><script type="text/javascript"><!--

There is the ever-magnetic attraction of knowing more about the celebs who have always lived behind shadows. With the coming of the celeb gossip sections, people are now in touch with the side of the personality which had remained hidden for long. It&#39;s this sneak peek behind the scenes action that interests people. Frankly nobody can jump over the barricade and get a ring-side view of what is happening. This element of curiosity gave birth to Hollywood gossip. The difference in the lives of the subjects and the readers made the contrast even more palpable.
