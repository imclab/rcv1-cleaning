<h2 style="text-align: center">Newport Arts and Entertainment</h2>
<h3 style="text-align: center">Art, Theater, Music and Film.  It's all here!</h3>
<p><style type="text/css"><!--body{background-image:url(http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/images/back-image-14.gif);background-attachment:fixed;background-repeat:repeat-y;background-position:top center;}--></style>

Arts and entertainment is <b>another reason to visit</b> historic Newport, Rhode Island.  <br><center><script type="text/javascript"><!--

</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/newport-art-museum-1.jpg" width="400" alt="newport art museum" title="nam outside"></p>
<p><a href="#mag">Newport Arts Scene Magazine</a><BR><a href="#nam">Newport Art Museum</a><BR><a href="#theaters">Theaters</a><BR><a href="#events">Events</a><BR><a href="#entertainment">Entertainment</a><BR><BR>

<h3 style="text-align: left"><a name="mag">Newport Arts Scene Magazine</a></h3>
<p>Newport Arts Scene Magazine is an <b>entertainment magazine</b> covering arts and artists in the Newport, RI area. It's managed, manned and <b>produced by the artists and arts lovers</b> in the area. <br><br><a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-arts-scene-magazine.html">Click here</a> or on the image below for more about Newport Arts Scene Magazine.
</p><p align="center"><a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-arts-scene-magazine.html"><img src="/images/nasm-4.jpg" width="500" alt="Newport Arts Scene Magazine" title="nasm site"></a></p>
<hr size=1px style="width:100%;margin-left:0px;">
<h3 style="text-align: left"><a name="nam">Newport Art Museum</a></h3>
<p>76 Bellevue Avenue<br>401-848-8200<BR><BR>The Art Association of Newport was <b>formed during the summer of 1912</b>.  Its purpose was to exhibit fine art and to promote arts education in Newport.<BR><BR>The Association is now known as the <b>Newport Art Museum</b>, and is located on historic <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/bellevue-avenue.html">Bellevue Avenue</a> near the <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/hotel-viking.html">Hotel Viking</a> and <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/redwood-library.html">Redwood Library</a>.
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/newport-art-museum-2.jpg" width="400" alt="newport art museum" title="nam inside"></p>
<p>The museum features works by many artists, including:<ul><li>Lydia Field Emmett<li><b>Gilbert Stuart</b><li>Howard Gardiner Cushing<li>Helena Sturtevant<li>Dale Chihuly<li>Rita Rogers</ul>It also gives <b>local artists</b> many opportunities to show off their work throughout the year.  The <b>Newport Annual</b> and the <b>Artists' Studio Gallery</b> are a couple of those events.<BR><BR>Many other interesting events are held here throughout the year as well, including <b>Newport Gallery Night</b>, Wet Paint 2009, <b>Lunch With the Artist</b> and Art on the Lawn.<BR>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/newport-art-museum-3.jpg" width="400" alt="newport art museum" title="nam 3"></p>
<p>The museum is only open during the day, and hours vary depending on the time of year.  <b>Admission is $10 for adults</b>, $8 for seniors, $6 for students and military personnel (ID required) and children under 5 enter free.
</p><hr size=1px style="width:100%;margin-left:0px;">
<h3 style="text-align: left"><a name="theaters">Theaters</a></h3>
<p>Newport is also home to some interesting and historic theaters.  After a day at the <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-ri-beaches.html">beach</a> and some <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-ri-attractions.html">sightseeing</a>, <b>why not check out a show</b>?<BR><BR><b>Firehouse Theater</b>, located right off of Broadway (4 Equality Park Place - 401-849-3473), puts on shows year round in their 49 seat theater.<BR><BR>Their shows are usually on <b>Fridays and Saturdays at 8 pm</b>.  They also perform on Thursdays and Sundays, but check with them for exact times.<BR><BR><center><b>Firehouse Theater off of Broadway</b></center>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/firehouse-theater-1.jpg" width="400" alt="newport firehouse theater" title="firehouse theater wide"></p>
<p>The <b>Newport Playhouse & Cabaret Restaurant</b> is another great spot to spend an evening.  This unique, family-owned establishment has been delighting its visitors since opening in 1983.  Newport arts and entertainment doesn't get any better than this!<BR><BR>Enjoy the monstrous buffet, full liquor service and <b>fabulous performances</b> during the day or at night.  This is a handicapped-accessible and smoke free location.<BR><BR>Newport Playhouse & Cabaret Restaurant<br>102 Connell Highway<br>401-848-PLAY (7529)<BR><BR><center><b>Newport Playhouse & Cabaret Restaurant</b></center>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/newport-playhouse-2.jpg" width="400" alt="newport playhouse" title="playhouse entrance"></p>
<p>The <b>Opera House Cinema</b> and <b>Jane Pickens Theater</b> are located next door to each other on Touro Street, in historic Washington Square.  In addition to showing feature films, these  locations also host the <b>Newport International Film Festival</b> every summer.<BR><BR><center><b>Opera House Cinema</b></center><center>401-846-3456</center>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/opera-house-newport.jpg" width="400" alt="opera house newport ri" title="opera house"></p>
<p><center><b>Jane Pickens Theater</b></center><center>401-846-5252</center>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/jane-pickens-theater.jpg" width="400" alt="jane pickens theater" title="jane pickens"></p>
<h3 style="text-align: left"><a name="events">Events</a></h3>
<p>There are so many <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-ri-events.html">Newport RI events</a> going on throughout the year that I wouldn't want to try and list them all here.  You'll just have to take my word for it, <b>there's always something going on here</b>!  <BR><BR>Here's some of the most popular:<ul><li><b>Newport International Film Festival</b><li>Newport Summer Comedy Series <li><b>Newport Folk Festival</b><li>St. Patrick's Day Parade <li><b>Newport Jazz Festival</b> <li>Taste of Rhode Island </ul>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/newport-film-festival.jpg" width="400" alt="newport film festival" title="film festival sign"></p>
<p><a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-ri-events.html">More about Newport RI events</a>
</p><h3 style="text-align: left"><a name="entertainment">Entertainment</a></h3>
<p><b>The entertainment doesn't end</b> with the Newport festivals.  Throughout the year (but especially during the summer), there are countless great times to be had and <b>musical performances</b> to enjoy all around the city.<BR><BR>Another thing Newport is famous for is the <b>crazy bar/club scene</b> during the summer season.  People flock to Newport every summer to be a part of the action.  <BR><BR><center><b>The Landing - it gets pretty crazy here after dark</b></center>
</p><p align="center"><img src="/images/landing-newport.jpg" width="400" alt="landing newport ri" title="landing"></p>
<p>Whether you're into rock bands, <b>dancing at the clubs</b> or drinking your friends under the table at one of our <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/newport-rhode-island-bars.html">Irish pubs</a>, <b>Newport is the place to be during the summer</b>.</p><p><b>Some of the best spots</b>:<ul><li>O'Brien's Pub (great people watching spot right on <a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/thames-street.html">Thames Street</a>)<li>The Pelham (live bands)<li>Newport Blues Cafe (blues music)<li>The Landing (college crowd)<li><a href="http://www.newport-discovery-guide.com/clarke-cooke-house.html">The Boom Boom Room</a> (dance club)<li>Rhino Bar and <b>The Mamba Room</b> (drinking and dancing across the hall from each other)</ul>Whatever form of arts and entertainment you're looking for, you're sure to find it in the "City by the Sea", Newport, Rhode Island.<BR><BR><b>Have fun</b>!
