									<h2><span itemprop="name">Country Fans Boo Jessica Simpson</span></h2>
										<div class="comments-link">
									</div><!-- .comments-link -->
					</header><!-- .entry-header -->

				<div class="entry-content">
			<p style="text-align: center;"></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"> </p>
<p style="text-align:center;"><img src="http://assets.poponthepop.com/photos/xlarge_p/jessica-simpson-and-eric-johnson-walking.jpg" alt="Jessica Simpson and Eric Johnson Walking" class="curs" onclick="window.location.href='http://poponthepop.com/gallery/nggallery/image/jessica-simpson-and-eric-johnson-walking'" class="curs" onclick="window.location.href='http://poponthepop.com/gallery/nggallery/image/jessica-simpson-and-eric-johnson-walking'" class="curs" onclick="window.location.href='http://poponthepop.com/gallery/nggallery/image/jessica-simpson-and-eric-johnson-walking'"  data-type="Photo" data-id="11004"></p>
<p>Jessica was <a href="http://www.usmagazine.com/jessica-simpson-booed-at-first-country-concert" target="_blank">booed by many</a> at the concert, but there were a few fans in the mix as well.</p>
<p>She told the crowd,</p>
<blockquote>
<p>&#8220;I don&#8217;t know what your perception is of Jessica Simpson or what tabloid you buy, but I just want you to know that I&#8217;m just a girl from Texas. I&#8217;m just like you. I&#8217;m doing what I love and dating a boy.&#8221;</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Yeah, yeah, we know.  You can&#8217;t be without a boy for two seconds..</p>
<p>Simpson dedicated &#8220;You&#8217;re My Sunday&#8221; to her &#8216;boy&#8217; Tony Romo, who was there, and sang a ton of covers, such as  <strong>Willie Nelson</strong>&#8216;s &#8220;On the Road Again.&#8221;</p>
<p>One guy told the local paper, &#8220;I&#8217;ll have to drink a lot of beer to sit through her concert.&#8221;</p>
<p>Her new album drops September 9th.</p>
