<p>Since we haven&#8217;t posted anything about this movie yet, it had to come. </p>
<p>The winter is closing in and while some doesn&#8217;t care much for the cold weather others look forward to an awesome time. If your&#8217;e ether one or another, just take a look at this awesome movie from the producers of &#8220;That&#8217;s It, That&#8217;s All&#8221;.  </p>
<p><img src="http://www.the-xworld.com/wp-content/img/TheArtofFlight1.png" alt="theartofflight1" /></p>
<p>Two years in the making &#8220;The Art of FLIGHT&#8221; got released in September and follows Travis Rice, John Jackson, Mark Landvik, Scotty Lago, Jake Blauvelt, Nicolas Muller, Gigi Ruf, DCP and Pat Moore on new global adventures.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.the-xworld.com/wp-content/img/TheArtofFlight2.png" alt="theartofflight2" /></p>
<p>You can see that these guys have used a lot of their time on this movie and the result is just pure amazing. Not just the riding but also the editing. Because of the stunning footage it&#8217;s not a must to &#8216;love&#8217; snowboard to watch this movie. </p>
