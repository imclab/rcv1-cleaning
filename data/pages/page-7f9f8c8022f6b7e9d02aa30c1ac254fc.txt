      <h1 class="postTitle"><a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/2014-corvette-stingray-convertible-geneva-debut-confirmed-2013-geneva-auto-show/" rel="bookmark">2014 Corvette Stingray Convertible Geneva Debut Confirmed [2013 Geneva Auto Show]</a></h1>

      
      
      <div id="creditsHolder" style="margin:10px 0 0 0;">

        <div id="fbLikeHolder1" style="display:inline-block;"></div>

        <small class="post-credits">
        January 31, 2013 at 2:17 pm by <a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/author/andrew-wendler/" title="Posts by Andrew Wendler" rel="author">Andrew Wendler</a>        </small>
      </div>

      <div class="post">

        <!-- Gigya Social Share Floater -->
        <div id="divButtons"></div>

        <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103821" title="2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible1.jpg" alt="" width="626" height="382" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103821" title="2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible1.jpg" alt="" width="626" height="382" /></noscript></p>
<p>Hot on the heels of the <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/news/2014-chevrolet-c7-corvette-stingray-z51-photos-and-info-news">2014 Corvette Stingray</a> coupe’s show-stealing debut in Detroit, Chevrolet has confirmed to <em>Car and Driver</em> that the Stingray convertible will make its first appearance at the <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/2013-geneva-auto-show">2013 Geneva auto show</a>. Rumors of the droptop C7 have been swirling ever since <a href="http://www.autoweek.com/article/20130128/GENEVA/130129805"><em>Autoweek</em></a> first reported the Vette convertible&#8217;s Swiss reveal, and although not unexpected, many figured Chevy would give the fixed-roof Stingray a little more time under the spotlight before being asked to share.<span id="more-103818"></span></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103822" title="2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible-02" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible-021.jpg" alt="" width="626" height="382" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103822" title="2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible-02" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2015-Chevrolet-Corvette-convertible-021.jpg" alt="" width="626" height="382" /></noscript></p>
<hr noshade="noshade" />
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/2012-chevrolet-corvette-z06-vs-2013-nissan-gt-r-vs-2012-porsche-911-carrera-s-comparison-test">Comparison Test: 2012 Chevrolet Corvette Z06 vs. Porsche 911 Carrera S, 2013 Nissan GT-R</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/reviews/2013-chevrolet-corvette-427-convertible-instrumented-test-review">Instrumented Test: 2013 Chevrolet Corvette 427 Convertible</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/2012-chevrolet-camaro-zl1-vs-ford-mustang-shelby-gt500-comparison-test-review">Comparison Test: 2012 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 vs. 2013 Ford Mustang Shelby GT500</a></li>
</ul>
<hr noshade="noshade" />
<p>The Stingray convertible—which Chevrolet claims is not represented by <a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/first-renderings-of-2015-chevrolet-corvette-convertible-hit-the-internet/">these renderings</a> that surfaced on the web, despite our thinking otherwise—is set to go on sale as a 2014 model, with production beginning as early as November and cars arriving in showrooms before the end of the year. As Chevrolet works to increase its global recognition, it makes sense that it would choose to reveal the softtop variant of its most-recognizable nameplate in front of an international audience. It’ll be interesting to see how the all-American Corvette convertible holds it own in the presence of all that European hardware.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/2013-geneva-auto-show"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103687" title="2013 Geneva auto show full coverage" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2013-Geneva-auto-show-full-coverage-626.jpg" alt="2013 Geneva auto show full coverage" width="626" height="38" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103687" title="2013 Geneva auto show full coverage" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2013-Geneva-auto-show-full-coverage-626.jpg" alt="2013 Geneva auto show full coverage" width="626" height="38" /></noscript></a></p>
