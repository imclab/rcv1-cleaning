				<h1 class="rt-pagetitle">
			Art Events		</h1>
							
		
		
		
		
					<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;">&nbsp;<span style="color: #000000;">Winter/Spring 2012-2013 Art Events</span></span><br /><em><span style="font-size: 12pt; color: #0000ff;"></span></em></h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span class="footer">See also <a href="/galleries/art-galleries-boulder-county-colorado" target="_self">Galleries</a> and <a href="/museums/art-museums-history-museums-in-boulder-county-colorado" target="_self">Museums</a></span></p>
<p><em><strong>Monthly Friday-night receptions and artwalks are a tradition at galleries and in art districts throughout Boulder County. In addition to opening receptions at galleries, you’ll often find live entertainment, food and drink, and other businesses with extended hours.</strong></em></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Downtown Boulder:</strong> First Fridays, 6-8pm on and around the Pearl Street Mall</li>
<li><strong>NoBo Nite Out:</strong> First Fridays, 6-9pm in the NoBo Art District, along North Broadway between Iris Avenue and Lee Hill Road</li>
<li><strong>Lafayette:</strong> Third Fridays, 6-9:30pm at more than a dozen shops and restaurants along Public Road</li>
<li><strong>Longmont:</strong> Second Fridays, 6-9pm along Main Street between Third and Sixth avenues</li>
<li><strong>Niwot: </strong>First Fridays, 5-8pm; participating locations in Niwot change each month</li>
</ul>
<strong>Arts &amp; Ales</strong><br /><strong>June 20, July 18, Aug. 15:</strong> Monthly gatherings sponsored by Longmont Council for the Arts pair artist demonstrations and craft brews at Left Hand Brewing Company, 1265 Boston Ave. in Longmont. 5-8pm. <a href="http://www.artslongmont.org">www.artslongmont.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>ArtWalk Longmont</strong><br /><strong>July 19:</strong> ArtWalk is a free evening of art exhibitions, music, live theater and dance, and children’s activities on Longmont’s Main Street between Third and Sixth avenues. View the work of featured artists at dozens of downtown businesses, and stroll the conveniently blocked-off streets, 6:30-9pm. <a href="http://www.artwalklongmont.org">www.artwalklongmont.org</a>. <br /><br /><strong>Boulder Art Association</strong> <br />Rotating works by BAA members are on display year-round at businesses throughout the county; check the website for schedule. The association also holds monthly meetings. 303-444-9922; <a href="http://www.boulderartassociation.org">www.boulderartassociation.org</a>.<br />
<ul>
<li>June 6-9: Annual Members’ Show, held jointly with Longmont Artists’ Guild, at Boulder County Fairgrounds in Longmont</li>
<li>Aug. 23-Sept. 6: Annual Juried Show, at Dairy Center for the Arts</li>
</ul>
<br /><strong>Broomfield Art Guild</strong><br /><strong>July 11-Aug. 4:</strong> BAG’s artist-members present their regional show in the Broomfield Auditorium, 3 Community Park Road. See <a href="http://www.broomfieldartguild.org">www.broomfieldartguild.org</a> for details. <br /><br /><strong>Cherry Creek Arts Festival</strong><br /><strong>July 5-7:</strong> Annual nonprofit event is a celebration of visual, culinary and performing arts. In Denver’s Cherry Creek North Shopping District. <a href="http://www.cherryarts.org">www.cherryarts.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>City of Boulder Pottery Lab Spring/Summer Sale</strong><br /><strong>June 1-2:</strong> Annual sale at the Pottery Lab, 1010 Aurora, helps raise funds to keep the lab going, offering beginning and intermediate classes for children, adults, teens, seniors and people with mild traumatic brain injuries. 10am-5pm. <br /><br /><strong>Estes Park Art Events</strong><br />Estes Park hosts several big arts events each summer; for more information check <a href="http://www.estesparkcvb.com">www.estesparkcvb.com</a>. <br />
<ul>
<li>June 8-9: Wool Market, at Stanley Park Fairgrounds</li>
<li>Aug. 31-Sept. 2: Labor Day Arts &amp; Crafts Show, at Bond Park</li>
</ul>
<br /><strong>Farmers’ Market Fine Art &amp; Fine Craft Fairs</strong><br />Juried shows are held in conjunction with Boulder and Longmont Farmers’ Markets one Saturday per month. Boulder dates are June 15, July 13 and Aug. 17; Longmont dates are June 29, July 27 and Aug. 31. <a href="http://www.boulderfarmers.org">www.boulderfarmers.org</a>. <br /><br /><strong>Golden Fine Arts Festival</strong><br /><strong>Aug. 17-18:</strong> Annual juried event showcases fine arts plus live music, children’s art activities, free horse-drawn carriage and train rides, demonstrations at Clear Creek History Park, and food/beverage vendors. <a href="http://www.goldenfineartsfestival.org">www.goldenfineartsfestival.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>Longmont Art in Public Places Tours</strong><br />The Longmont Bike Map (available online or at the Longmont Museum &amp; Cultural Center, 400 Quail Road) shows the locations of all the works in the collection, so you can design your own walking, biking or driving tour. AIPP also hosts several guided tours via bike or walking; check ci.longmont.co.us/museum/aipp or the Facebook page.<br /><br /><strong>Longmont Council for the Arts</strong><br />LCA hosts 2nd Friday receptions, Arts &amp; Ales events, demonstrations, house concerts and more, all year long. For a schedule of events, see <a href="http://www.artslongmont.org">www.artslongmont.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>Loveland Sculpture &amp; Art Shows</strong><br /><strong>Aug. 9-11:</strong> Three special arts events take place on the same weekend in Loveland—two nationally recognized sculpture shows and a fine arts festival. Shuttle service is available throughout the city for the weekend’s events. <br />
<ul>
<li>Sculpture in the Park, Aug. 10-11: 30th annual juried event showcases more than 2,000 works by sculptors from all over the world; in Benson Sculpture Garden, 2908 Aspen Drive just off 29th Street. 970-663-2940; <a href="http://www.sculptureinthepark.org">www.sculptureinthepark.org</a>&nbsp;</li>
<li>Loveland Sculpture Invitational, Aug. 9-11: On the grounds of Loveland High School, just across the street from Benson Park. 970-663-7467; <a href="http://www.lovelandsculpturegroup.org">www.lovelandsculpturegroup.org</a> </li>
<li>Art in the Park Festival, Aug. 110-11: 49th-annual arts and crafts festival includes more than 200 participants; in Loveland’s North Lake Park. 970-663-2407; <a href="http://www.lincolngallery.com">www.lincolngallery.com</a>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<br /><strong>Nederland High Peaks Art Festival</strong><br /><strong>June 22-23:</strong> Outdoor juried exhibition, now in its 13th year, also features live music and food booths, and a portion of proceeds benefit the Nederland Community Library Foundation. <a href="http://www.nederlandchamber.org">www.nederlandchamber.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>Open Arts Fest</strong><br /><strong>July 20-21:</strong> This visual-arts event brings more than 100 artists from around the country to Pearl Street Mall. It includes artists’ demonstrations, a children’s art tent and a silent auction. 10am-8pm Saturday and 10am-5pm Sunday. <a href="http://www.openartsboulder.org">www.openartsboulder.org</a>.<br /><br /><strong>Pecha Kucha Longmont</strong><br /><strong>Aug. 16:</strong> Drawing its name from the Japanese term for the sound of “chitchat,” this free event features presentations of 20 images on the screen for 20 seconds each, so each presenter has about 7 minutes to discuss their subject. 7pm at Firehouse Art Center, 667 4th Ave., Longmont. <a href="http://www.firehouseart.org">www.firehouseart.org</a>.
