<span class="festival-header">Artists Wanted</span><br />
      <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA">The Scottsdale Arts Festival is now accepting applications from artists in all media interested in showing their work at this award-winning event. Applications must be submitted online before Monday, Oct. 14. <a class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" href="https://www.zapplication.org/event-info-public.php?fair_id=2811" target="_blank">Click here</a> to apply.</p>
      <span class="festival-header">About</span><br />
<blockquote class="Body-Style1-SCPA">
<span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" style="color: #009999;">200</span> awe-inspiring artists from coast to coast<br />
<span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" style="color: #009999;">20</span> toe-tapping Arizona bands and entertainers<br />
<span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" style="color: #009999;">12</span> mouth-watering gourmet food trucks<br />
<span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" style="color: #009999;">3</span> smile-inducing days of family-friendly fun<br />
and <span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" style="color: #009999;">you</span>!
</blockquote>
<p class="Body-Style1-SCPA">Named one of the top arts festivals in the nation by the readers of <em class="Body-Style1-SCPA">AmericanStyle Magazine</em> and Best Arts Festival by <em class="Body-Style1-SCPA">Phoenix New Times</em>, the Scottsdale Arts Festival has been recognized for its high-quality fine arts since 1971. The Festival showcases <span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">nearly 200 jury-selected artists</span> from throughout the United States and Canada who work in painting, sculpture, glass and ceramics, jewelry, photography and more. Works of art are available for purchase directly from the artists and through the Festivals online art auction.</p>
                    <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA">Be sure to 'Like' us on <a href="http://www.facebook.com/ScottsdaleArtsFestival" class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">Facebook</a> for all the latest news!</p>
          <img src="images/2010-11/festival/festival-people.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="100" style="margin-bottom: 20px;">
      <span class="festival-header">Admission</span><br />
      <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA">Tickets may be purchased in advance  or at the entrance on the day of the Festival.</p>
        <blockquote>
        <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA"><span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">Single-Day Pass:</span> $8 (valid for any single-day)<br>
        <span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">Two-Day Pass:</span> $12 (valid for any two days)</p>
        <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA"><span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">Special Pricing:</span><br>
        
        $5 for students (student pricing not available online)<br>
        FREE for children 12 and under<br>
        FREE for members of Scottsdale Center for the Performing Arts and SMoCA<br />
        <a class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold" href="members/join.aspx" target="_blank">Become a member</a> and claim your free Festival tickets!</p>
        </blockquote>
        <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA">Rain or shine.<br>
        Pets are not allowed with the exception of trained service animals.<br>
        No food/beverages or coolers (except for water and childrens snacks).</p>
          <span class="festival-header"><a name="Location"></a>Location</span>
  <p class="Body-Style1-SCPA"><span class="Body-Style1-SCPA-Bold">Scottsdale Center for the Performing Arts</span><br>
        7380 E. Second St. &ndash; Scottsdale<br>
        (Four blocks south of Indian School Road and three blocks
        east of Scottsdale Road)</p>
