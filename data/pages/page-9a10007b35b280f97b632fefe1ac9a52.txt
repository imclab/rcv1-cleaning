              <h1>Car Repair</h1>
              <h2>Keeping you on the road</h2><p>Cars don't last forever, but when they break down, repairs can be frustrating, time-consuming and hard on the wallet - especially if you have to deal with a mechanic who's sleazy or dishonest.</p>

<p>Modern cars are more technical than ever, with computers regulating more and more of the car's functions. This makes it trickier for you to make repairs to yourself. But it's still worthwhile to repair the basics yourself. Most of the price you pay at a garage is going to be for labor, so if you can do it yourself you'll save big money.</p>
