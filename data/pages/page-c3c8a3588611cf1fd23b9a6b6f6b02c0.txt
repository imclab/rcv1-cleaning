            <h1>Mercedes-Benz Concept Style Coupe leaked</h1>
            
                <style type="text/css">
                    .topobject{ width:580px;float:right; text-align:center;}
                </style>
                
        <div class="topobject">
        
            <a href="http://content.worldcarfans.co/2012/4/18/medium/1439537362301321514.jpg"><img width="580" src="http://content.worldcarfans.co/2012/4/18/big/72937286887742682.jpg" alt=" Mercedes-Benz Concept Style Coupe leaked" /></a>
            <div class="mainphotocredit">Mercedes Concept Sytle Coupe leak - 18.4.2012</div>
			
        </div>
        
                <div class="clear"></div>
            <div class="articlead2" id="Worldcarfans_ArticlePages_Middle_BTF_Center_300x250"></div><script type="text/javascript">googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('Worldcarfans_ArticlePages_Middle_BTF_Center_300x250'); });</script> <h2 class="abstract">Previews the production CLA / CLC</h2>
            <div class="articlecontent">
               <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
               <p>The first pictures of the Mercedes Concept Style Coupe have leaked to the web, ahead of its <a href="../112031642486/beastie-boy-mike-d-to-host-mercedes-clc-concept-debut">debut</a> at the Transmission LA: AV CLUB art and music festival.</p>
<p>Designed to preview the upcoming <a href="../112032942912/mercedes-officially-confirms-the-cla--clc-four-door-coupe">CLA / CLC</a>, the concept is a four-door coupe that draws styling cues from the <a href="../112030541980/2013-mercedes-a-class-officially-revealed-video">A</a>- and <a href="../110100128767/2012-mercedes-cls-debuts-in-paris">CLS-Class</a>.  Official specifications haven't been released, but the concept rides on the MFA platform and reportedly measures 4637mm long, 1891mm wide and 1398mm tall with a wheelbase that spans 2699mm.</p>
<p>Power is provided by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder petrol engine with 211 PS (155 kW / 208 hp).  However, the production model will likely offer a variety of engines with outputs ranging from 109 PS (80 kW / 108 hp) to 211 PS (155 kW / 208 hp).</p>
<p>On a side note, it turns out our suspicions were correct as the terribly <a href="../112041743478/mercedes-teases-clc-interior-in-design-video">mundane design video</a> was indeed a teaser of the&nbsp;Concept Style Coupe.</p>
