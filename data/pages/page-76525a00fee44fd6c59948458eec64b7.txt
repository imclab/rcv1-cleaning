<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-56099" title="FuelCulture Hyundai Genesis Coupe Turbo Concept" src="http://www.automobilesreview.com/uploads/2012/10/FuelCulture-Genesis-Coupe-Turbo-Concept.jpg" alt="FuelCulture Hyundai Genesis Coupe Turbo Concept" width="651" height="434" /><br />
FuelCulture and <a href="http://www.automobilesreview.com/category/car-makes/hyundai/">Hyundai</a><strong> </strong>has presented their project – a special edition <strong>Genesis Coupe</strong> at the <a href="http://www.automobilesreview.com/pictures/sema/">2012 SEMA Motor Show</a>.</p>
<p>The car comes with a lot of upgrades (see below), but the most important are under the hood. The 2.0 liter turbocharged petrol engine gets an ARK DT-S exhaust system, ECU remap, Injen CAI and Chase Bays AN fuel line kit. The result is superb – 315HP (235 kW) and 328Nm (242 lb-ft) of torque. In other words, the RWD sports car becomes unique fun toy.</p>
<p>The other upgrades include a suspension mods and interior upgrades for more comfort. It boasts a diamond-stitched front and rear seats as well as the door panels. The audio system is also improved by Rockford Fosgate.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: x-large;">Exterior</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Infinite Auto Design Custom Widebody Rear Kit (50mm)</li>
<li>Infinite Auto Design Custom Sideskirts</li>
<li>Infinite Auto Design Custom Front Bumper</li>

<li>VIS Racing Proline Rear Lip</li>
<li>Dupont Hot Hues Candy Apple Red Paint</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: x-large;">Suspension</span></p>
<ul>
<li>BC Racing Coilovers</li>
<li>Energy Suspension Bushings</li>
<li>Whiteline Front Sway Bar</li>
<li>Whiteline Rear Sway Bar</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: x-large;">Wheels/Tires</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Chasebays Brake Lines</li>
<li>Continental Extreme Contact DW 225/35/19 Front Tires</li>
<li>Continental Extreme Contact DW 265/30/19 Rear Tires</li>
<li>Password JDM Lugnuts</li>
<li>Rotiform IND 19&#215;9 Front Wheels</li>
<li>Rotiform IND 19&#215;11 Rear Wheels</li>
<li>Baer Disc Brake Rotors</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>ARK DT-S Exhaust System</li>
<li>Boost Controller</li>

<li>Chase Bays Electronic Boost Solenoid</li>

<li>Password JDM Oil Cap</li>

</ul>
