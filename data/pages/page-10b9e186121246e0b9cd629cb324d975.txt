				<p>Shoops (photos edited with Photoshop) have long internet history. MMA fighting became mainstream recently after 15 years of living under the radar in USA and naturally have strong internet following. There are many funny shoops, but MMA is ideal for getting shooping material from almost every event. Face expressions and unusual body positions during the fight secure a lot of shoop photos.</p>

<p>I saw first shoop a few years ago and I don't even remember what was in that photo, I remeber that it had some Pride (Japan MMA promotion) theme. Only six months ago I became aware of whole internet subculture dedicated to shooping photos and making animated gifs soon after major MMA events.</p>

<p>Sherdog forum and few other popular websites and forums are habitats for MMA related shoops. I regularly visit this places in search for new photos.</p>

<p>Some of the best shoops are:</p>

<p>- Mirko Cro Cop destroying a car with his left kick on Street Fighter bonus level</p>

<p>- Andrei Arlovski as King Leonidas from movie "300" (almost no need for special efects here as Andrei is freaky similar to Leonidas)</p>

<p>- Bruce Lee at birthday partie screaming</p>

<p>- Mirko Cro Cop sleeping (after Gabriel Gonzaga fight)</p>

<p>- Chuck Liddel covered with blanket by Rashad Evans (after Evans knocked out him)</p>

<p>- Wanderlei Silva attack Keith Jardine with axe (they don't call him Axe Murderer for nothing)</p>

<p>- Fedor buries Arlovski after the fight</p>

<p>- Crazy face of Rashad Evans after fight with Lyoto Machida</p>

<p>- Maybe best shoops ever: Michael Bisping as prince Charles and English soldier (from Dan Henderson fight)</p>

<p>- Van Damme and Bolo Yeung as rockstars (cleverly taken clips from the movie)</p>

<p>- Demian Maia jump after Nate Marquardt punched him</p>

<p>- Brock Lesnar and Frank Mir Family Guy gif - funnies gif ever</p>

<p>- Anderson Silva as Grim Reaper</p>

<p>- Kimbomania: everyone wanna look like Kimbo Slice</p>

<p>- Light heavyweight title belt as gift from Cecil Peoples to Lyoto Machida (controversial Shogun Rua fight)</p>

<p>- Brock Lesnar beating four lightweights</p>

<p>- Dan Henderson and Uriah Faber as Han Solo and Luke Skywalker</p>

<p>And all of this treasure is at various websites acros net, never easy to find. I collected best ones and hosted them on my website dedicated only to MMA shoops: <a target="_new" rel="nofollow" href="http://mmafunny.blogspot.com/">Funny fights</a>.</p>
