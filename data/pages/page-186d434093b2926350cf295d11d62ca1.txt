<div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_ctl00_label" style='display:none'>Main Content</div><div id="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_ctl00__ControlWrapper_RichHtmlField" class="ms-rtestate-field" style="display:inline" aria-labelledby="ctl00_PlaceHolderMain_ctl00_label"><h1>Epicor<sup>®</sup> for Automotive – Business Software Solution</h1>
<p>Servicing your automotive customers effectively and adapting to their increasing demands requires a flexible IT infrastructure. After more than 20 years of providing solutions for 9,000 midmarket manufacturers, Epicor knows that you need powerful <strong>automotive industry</strong> <strong>software </strong>that’s not only simple to set up and use, but also easy to integrate with other systems. The answer is an <strong>automotive manufacturing software </strong>solution built from the ground up using a true <a id="Service-oriented Architecture | Epicor" title="Service-oriented Architecture | Epicor" href="/Solutions/Pages/Serviceoriented.aspx">service-oriented architecture</a> (SOA) combined with a Microsoft® .NET user interface.</p>
<h2>Automotive Manufacturing Software</h2>
<p><strong>Epicor for Automotive </strong>is a global enterprise resource planning (ERP) and <strong>automotive industry software </strong>solution designed for <strong>automotive manufacturers </strong>and businesses that provide products and services to the automotive industry. Our <strong>automotive software </strong>solution goes beyond traditional ERP to provide financial management, product management, customer relationship management (CRM), <a title="Supply Chain Management" href="/SOLUTIONS/Pages/Supply-Chain-Management.aspx">supply chain management (SCM)</a>, and more. SOA makes this robust functionality available as secure Web services for easy integration with your Microsoft Office applications and the global supply chain. The tools used for integration are components of the Epicor <a id="Productivity Pyramid | Epicor" title="Productivity Pyramid | Epicor" href="/Solutions/Pages/ProductivityPyramid.aspx">Productivity Pyramid</a>. 
<h2>Automotive Manufacturing Software and Solutions</h2>
<ul>
<li>Support for release accounting through use of demand management and purchase contracts 
<li>Canned EDI workflows and report formats for electronic communication of release and ASN information to and from your automotive customers 
<li>Improved lead times and waste reduction through implementation of lean strategies for automotive manufacturers 
<li>Support for AIAG-compliant labels, strong product lifecycle management (PLM) for product data and document management, and advanced quality management for handling PPM, APQP, PPAP, and TS 16949 
<li>Exceptional project management capabilities with <b>automotive manufacturing software</b> solutions 
<li>Complete visibility with a comprehensive end-to-end solution containing built-in dashboards</li></ul>
<p>With award-winning integrated applications, <strong>Epicor for Automotive</strong> is a powerful business and automotive industry software solution that provides the flexibility and scalability you need to grow your company profitably by optimizing your most important resources.</p>
<p>Have questions about our comprehensive <strong>software for the automotive industry</strong>? Please call us at <b>+1-800-999-6995</b>, or <b><a title="" href="/Company/Pages/AskanEpicorExpert.aspx" target="_blank">ask an epicor expert</b></a></b></b></b>.</p></div>
