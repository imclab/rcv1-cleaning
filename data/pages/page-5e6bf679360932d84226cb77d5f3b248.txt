											<p id='intelliTxt' class="intro" data-module="article-intro">When you go into a used car dealership, you often have a choice between a certified car or a car with an extended warranty.  A dealer offering a certified pre-owned car guarantees the car has been inspected thoroughly, all needed repairs have been performed and the car is in the best mechanical condition possible. A certified car costs more and is usually warranted for a period of time.  A simple extended warranty does not guarantee a car has been inspected or repaired by the dealer; it simply means the dealer will warrant the car for a given period of time beyond that of the standard manufacturer's warranty.  A car with only an extended warranty will generally not cost as much as a certified car.
