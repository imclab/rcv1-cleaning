			(BANG) -Selena Gomez thinks a fan tattooing her likeness on them is ''intense''.<br /><br />
The 20-year-old singer-and-actress is slightly perturbed by enthusiastic followers who brand themselves with tattoos of her face or name and show them off to her.<br /><br />
Asked about the weirdest thing a fan has done, she said: ''I've had somebody tattoo me on their leg actually. That was pretty intense.''<br /><br />
Although she found the body ink disturbing, she added to French radio show, C'Cauet: ''It was cool though.''<br /><br />
Many of the 'Come and Get It' hitmaker's devoted fans have tattoos in her honour and they frequently decide to bare their decorated flesh to her in unusual places.<br /><br />
A young female admirer proudly showed Selena a tattoo she had gotten of her name located on her lower back while the singer made her way through Los Angeles International Airport in May 2012.<br /><br />
Selena took a moment to thank the enthusiastic young girl, who also has a tattoo bearing the name 'Demi' - in honour of fellow former Disney star, Demi Lovato.<br /><br />
The 'Spring Breakers' star also has two tattoos of her own, including a musical note on the side of her right hand and the Roman numerals 'LXXVI' on the back of her neck.<br /><br />
The star had the numerical marking added last September by celebrity tattooist Bang Bang in New York City.<br /><br />
He said at the time: ''Selena was nervous it might have been painful, so she held her best friend's hand the whole time. But she did a great job.''</p></div>
