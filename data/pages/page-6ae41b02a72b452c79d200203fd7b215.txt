		<p align="center"><a href="http://cdn.sheknows.com/thebuzz/2008/06/cher-red-hair.jpg" title="cher-red-hair.jpg"></a><a href="http://cdn.sheknows.com/thebuzz/2008/06/cher-red-hair.jpg" title="cher-red-hair.jpg"></a><img border="0" vspace="6" width="468" src="http://cdn.sheknows.com/thebuzz/2008/06/cher-red-hair.jpg" hspace="8" alt="Cher has a fan who's gone too far" height="477" /> <a href="http://cdn.sheknows.com/thebuzz/2008/06/cher-red-hair.jpg" title="cher-red-hair.jpg"></a></p>
<p>Do you believe in love after love after love? Or was it love after life after life? Whatev.</p>
<p>All we’re sayin’ is the legendary singer and actress, Cher recently encountered a disorderly fan and we do mean disorderly. A 36 year-old Nashville man, Calvin Hutton Houghland was asked to leave a club where Cher was performing (as for the location? The infamous Tootsies Orchid Lounge. Marvy!)</p>
<p>After initially complying he returned and grabbed the Cherster by the waist as she chilled in a sectioned off area of the lounge. He was charged with disorderly conduct and public intoxication. Now we bet he wishes he could turn back time! (haha!)</p>
