<p>Ever have a good car that you simply love to hate on? Mine was a 1989 Toyota Tercel like the one you see here and looking back on it, I feel truly guilty about the way I treated it. It had an AM/FM radio with two speakers, crank windows, cloth seats and that was it. </p>
