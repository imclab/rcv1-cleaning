<div class="quick_keys"><font color="#660000"><b><font size=+2>The early
20'th Century and especially the Mid Teen's, 30's andearly 40's produced
some of the most beautiful cars ever built.&nbsp;</font></b> <b><font size=+2>The
economic depression of this period cause fierce competition to give the
few that could still afford one of these cars, the most beautiful automobile
that could be manufactured. An example is the Duesenberg SJ that was built
to satisfy the tastes of the Hollywood market. It was a phenomenal creation
weighing two and a half tones and used a supercharged 7 liter twin overhear
camshaft straight eight engine that could propel the car at more then 160
miles per hour an unheard of speed at that time. It like many of the other
manfacturesof that period fell by the wayside but some are still with us
today.</font></b></font>
<br><b><font color="#660000"><font size=+2>Although some of these cars
continued into the 1940's you will see that by the end of the depression
most of them lost their charm and became just another car.</font></font></b></div>
