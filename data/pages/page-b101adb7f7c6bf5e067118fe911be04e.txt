				<p>I was just in Atlanta and stopped by the Autoshow, I took a bunch of pictures and wanted to share some of the cooler rides that I saw! Here is the first: The Lexus LF-C &#8211; even though it was originally debuted at the 2004 New York Auto Show, I still think this is one sick car!</p>
<h4>Specifications</h4>
<p>I got these from a sign on the display:</p>
<ul>
<li>Convertible Hardtop with 4-Position Glass Roof<br />
(Coupe, Targa, Speedster &amp; Fully-open Convertible)</li>
<li>One-touch button operates Convertible Hardtop</li>
<li>Front-mounted V8 engine</li>
<li>Rear Wheel Drive</li>
<li>6-Speed sequential shift automatic transmission</li>
<li>21-inch read wheels with 285/30ZR21 tires</li>
<li>20_inch front wheels with 235/35ZR20 tires</li>
<li>Cross-drilled front &amp; rear disc brakes</li>
<li>Retractable rear view cameras (instead of mirrors on the doors)</li>
<li>Rear view camera on bumper (instead of inside rear view mirror)</li>
<li>Rear view cameras display their images on the dashboard screen</li>
<li>Machined crystal tail lamps with LED lighting technology</li>
<li>Suede-trimmed bucket seats</li>
<li>3-Dimensional gauge cluster</li>
<li>Translucent crystal center console and door trim with blue backlighting</li>
<li>Drive-By-Wire steering</li>
<li>Formula One inspired steering wheel and shifter</li>
</ul>
