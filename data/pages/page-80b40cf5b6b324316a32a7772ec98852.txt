        <div class="post-body headroom clear-fix" itemprop="articleBody"><a name="aol-share" class="aol-share" title="Pechanga Gets Noticed By TV Hotshots During Recent Emmy Awards" data-pid="pechanga-gets-noticed-by-tv-hotshots-during-recent-emmy-awards" href="mailto:yourfriend@email.com?subject=Check this out: Pechanga Gets Noticed By TV Hotshots During Recent Emmy Awards&body=http://temecula.patch.com/groups/arts-and-entertainment/p/pechanga-gets-noticed-by-tv-hotshots-during-recent-emmy-awards"></a><p>Pechanga Resort &amp; Casino caught
the attention of top television peeps following an Emmy win handed to a
producer who filmed at the popular Temecula venue.<br></p>

<p>This past weekend, Dr. Travis Fox of
MindFox Productions took home a win in the Special Events Coverage category of
the 2013 National Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences Emmy® Awards/Pacific Southwest Chapter. The
footage and audio of “The Fab Four: The Ultimate Tribute,” for which Fox scored
the Emmy, was recorded inside Pechanga Resort &amp; Casino’s theater on January
6, 2012. </p><div id='popular_stories_wrapper' class='clear-fix invisible-mobile'></div>

<p>The PBS special began airing in June
2012 and has been seen by millions of viewers across the country. The show featured
the Beatles tribute band, which has been noted as the best around due to their
precise attention to detail and “note for note renditions of Beatles songs,”
according to the band’s website. The television show was also nominated in the
area of Director Post Production.</p>

<p>Fox and other winners in the Pacific
Southwest regional Emmy Awards were presented with their winged, golden statues
June 15 at an awards ceremony in Las Vegas. </p>

<p>A Fab Four concert takes the audience
through the history of The Beatle’s journey, complete with an actor playing the
part of Ed Sullivan introducing the four lads from Liverpool on his show and
showcasing music from every era of the musicians’ careers. </p>

<p>Haven’t seen the Fab Four yet? They plan
to return to Pechanga for a round of concerts, Jan. 10-11, 2014. Tickets are
$15, $25 and $35 and go on sale in October 2013. For more information,  call toll free (877) 711-2946 or visit <a href="http://www.pechanga.com/" rel="nofollow">www.Pechanga.com</a>.</p></div>
