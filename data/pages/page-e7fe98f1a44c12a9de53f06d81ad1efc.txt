<h1>All things art in Bel Air</h1>
<p>Bel Air&#8217;s Arts &amp; Entertainment District develops, promotes and supports diverse artistic and cultural pursuits. The District preserves a sense of place, provides unique local experiences and supports economic revitalization.</p>
<p>We offer tax incentives and technical assistance to artists as well as to businesses that support the arts. We welcome developers to build and renovate, and artists to live here and pursue creative dreams.</p>
