<h3>Welcome to LMC Automotive</h3>
<p>LMC Automotive is the leading provider of automotive production, sales and powertrain forecasts and automotive industry market intelligence. LMC Automotive started operation in 2011, following the demerger of the forecasting division of J.D. Power &amp; Associates, though the group has been in continuous operation since the early 1990s. See <a href="/news-events/press-releases/" title="Press releases">press release</a>.</p>
<p>Our market analysis and forecasts, carried out from offices across the globe, are supported by experienced professionals with broad, long and varied experience in the automotive sector. Our forecasting and analysis products speak for themselves, but they are supplemented by an unmatched level of customer support from our global team.</p>
<p>By targeting the key issues defining the automotive industry, LMC Automotive provides the most focused, responsive, timely and cost-effective market planning and analysis products available.</p>
<p>If you need to understand the outlook for vehicle markets, vehicle production or the powertrain sector, or if knowledge of critical new future models is key to your business, then we can help you. <a href="/contact/" title="Contact Us">Contact us</a>.</p>
