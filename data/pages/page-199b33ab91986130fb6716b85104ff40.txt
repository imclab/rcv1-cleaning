<p>Normally the best time to post a stolen car alert is right after the vehicle has gone missing &#8211; wait too long to get the word out, and the car has likely changed identities and/or time zones. That said, sometimes they hang around, and Richard Kauffman of Millersburg, Ohio, believes that to be the case with his stolen 1971 Oldsmobile Cutlass Supreme convertible.</p>
<p>Kauffman bought the Cutlass in 1994 for $1,500 and proceeded to restore it with a buddy of his in Moss Green with gold stripes and a white top and interior. An Olds 350-powered and air-conditioning-equipped car, the Cutlass also saw a few modifications along the way, including a Hurst dual-gate shifter, custom stereo and CD changer, and a Hotchkiss two-inch front drop kit. Kauffman also added a tilt steering column and Tic Toc Tach. &#8220;I wouldn&#8217;t say it was mint,&#8221; Kauffman said. &#8220;I drove it. I like to drive my cars around.&#8221;</p>
<p><span class="pinterest-button"><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fblog.hemmings.com%2Findex.php%2F2013%2F04%2F15%2Fstolen-car-alert-1971-oldsmobile-cutlass-supreme-convertible%2F&media=http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_04_800-150x105.jpg&description=Stolen+car+alert+%26%238211%3B+1971+Oldsmobile+Cutlass+Supreme+convertible" class="pin-it"  count-layout="horizontal"></a><a href="http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_04_800.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-91841" alt="stolenCutlass_04_800" src="http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_04_800-150x105.jpg"  width="150" height="105" \/></a></span> <span class="pinterest-button"><a href="http://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/?url=http%3A%2F%2Fblog.hemmings.com%2Findex.php%2F2013%2F04%2F15%2Fstolen-car-alert-1971-oldsmobile-cutlass-supreme-convertible%2F&media=http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_01_1200-150x105.jpg&description=Stolen+car+alert+%26%238211%3B+1971+Oldsmobile+Cutlass+Supreme+convertible" class="pin-it"  count-layout="horizontal"></a><a href="http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_01_1200.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-thumbnail wp-image-91839" alt="stolenCutlass_01_1200" src="http://images.hemmings.com/wp-content/uploads//2013/04/stolenCutlass_01_1200-150x105.jpg"  width="150" height="105" \/></a></span></p>
<p>Stolen on the morning of May 6, 2012, from the Canfield Fairgrounds in Canfield, Ohio, the Cutlass was at the time wearing Ohio license plates CPE9032 and VIN 34671M186059. Kauffman is offering a $1,000 reward for any information leading to the return of his car. Anybody with information about the car&#8217;s whereabouts should contact Kauffman at 330-893-3283 or the Mahoning County Sheriff&#8217;s Office at 330-480-5000, reference report number 6-1205-007.</p>
