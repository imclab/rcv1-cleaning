    <h1>Arts/Entertainment</h1>
	<p><a class="button-style-search" href="/things-to-do/attractions#search"><span>Search Attractions</span></a></p>
<div class="clear">&nbsp;</div><p><span>Let the Finger Lakes entertain you! You can visit inspiring galleries that show off local and cultural talent from the region and throughout the world, or discover a museum and the fun activities each one has to offer you! Not only will you see a lot, but you will also hear and laugh a lot with the various music festivities and theater showings throughout the Finger Lakes!<strong><br /> <br /> <strong><span>Art</span></strong></strong><br /> <br /> Here in the Finger Lakes there are an&nbsp;</span>abundance of art galleries waiting for you to stop by and visit! You can view local artistic talent at&nbsp;<a href="/things-to-do/attractions/art-galleries-1/east-hill-gallery-at-the-rochester-folk-art-guild">EastHill Gallery at the Rochester Folk Art Guild</a>, where you can see or purchase pottery, clothing designs, weaving, woodworking and graphic designs. While in Ithaca, take in the <a href="/things-to-do/attractions/art-galleries-1/community-arts-partnership-of-tompkins-county">Community Art Partnership</a> or&nbsp; Visit the&nbsp;<a href="/things-to-do/attractions/art-galleries-1/memorial-art-gallery">Memorial Art Gallery</a>&nbsp; in Rochester.&nbsp; To see many different forms of art visit&nbsp; <a href="/things-to-do/attractions/art-galleries-1/john-d-barrow-art-gallery">John D. Barrow Art Gallery</a>&nbsp;and see over 200 historical paintings! Remember to visit each artsy shop before you leave and take a piece of the Finger Lakes home with you!</p>
<p><span><strong> <br /> <strong><span>Museums</span></strong></strong><br /> <br /> Spend a day in Corning, NY at the<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="http://www.cmog.org">Corning Museum Glass</a><span>&nbsp;</span>where you can see live glassblowing demonstrations everyday and shop their huge glass collection!&nbsp; Other museums that you must check out include the<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="http://www.eastmanhouse.org/">George Eastman House International Museum of Photography and Film</a><span>&nbsp;</span>and the<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="/things-to-do/outdoor-recreation-1/museums-historical-sites/rochester-museum-science-center">Rochester Museum and Science Center</a><span>&nbsp;</span>in Rochester, or the<a href="/things-to-do/attractions/museums-historical-sites-1/rockwell-museum-of-western-art"> Rockwell Museum of Western Art</a><span>&nbsp;</span>in Corning. Visiting a number of museum's in Ithaca will be easy when you visit <a href="http://www.discoverytrail.com/">Ithaca's Discovery Trail</a> which represents roughly 7 area museums. With over 135 museums in the Finger Lakes, there is plenty of culture and history to be shared!<strong><br /> <br /> <strong><span>Music</span></strong></strong><br /> <br /> With so much to do and so much to see, the Finger Lakes also offer something your ears will want to listen to!<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="/about-the-region/services/events/clemens-center">Clemens Center</a><span>&nbsp;</span>for performing arts located in Elmira, NY offers live concerts, Broadway series, dance recitals, educational shows for kids in school and more!&nbsp;Or visit<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="/things-to-do/attractions/museums-historical-sites-1/sodus-bay-lighthouse-museum">Sodus Bay Lighthouse Museum</a><span>&nbsp;</span>on a Sunday during their summer concert series which open with a July Fourth concert and end with a Labor Day Weekend concert. </span><span>Also, don&rsquo;t miss local music talent playing at the Finger Lakes wineries throughout the summer and fall seasons!<strong><br /> <br /> <strong><span>Theatre</span></strong></strong><br /> <br />Theatres in the Finger Lakes include regional playhouses that are still taking bows several generations after their first curtain call&hellip;</span><span>Well-known theatres include the<span>&nbsp;</span><a href="/things-to-do/attractions/performing-arts-culture-1/merry-go-round-playhouse">Merry-Go-Round Playhouse</a><span>&nbsp;</span>in Auburn and <a href="/things-to-do/attractions/performing-arts-culture-1/smith-opera-house">Smith Opera House</a> in Geneva.&nbsp; For a list of more <a href="/things-to-do/arts-entertainment/theater">click here</a><br /></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
