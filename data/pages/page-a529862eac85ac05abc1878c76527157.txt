	<h3 class="kicker">The Best in Children's Literature</h3>	<h1>Children&rsquo;s Books & Authors</h1>		<p class="intro">We've gathered here many resources for sharing the joy of reading with children. By highlighting great books and authors we hope you'll find the books that unlock the love of reading in the children you care about.</p>
