<p>
	<iframe width="372" height="209" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/gDNuZZaysH4?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
<p>
	At Lipscomb Academy, we believe creativity is one of the unique ways we are &ldquo;made in God&rsquo;s image.&rdquo; As such, we seek to broaden students&rsquo; horizons into the many intriguing dimensions of fine arts. Students may participate in a variety of creative activities such as art, chorus, band and drama/theater. Fine arts teachers combine classroom instruction with participatory and experiential learning methods, giving students complete insight into these creative pursuits.<br />
	<br />
	Lipscomb Academy&rsquo;s connection with Lipscomb University creates expanded opportunities - collaborations with university level professors are the norm rather than the exception. From first-class art studios and rehearsal halls to a variety of performance venues to collegiate-level instructional experiences, Lipscomb Academy gives students the knowledge and tools to realize their full creative potential.<br />
	<br />
	Our focus on fine arts education allows students to cultivate greater artistic appreciation while developing their own unique and personal God-given talents.</p>
