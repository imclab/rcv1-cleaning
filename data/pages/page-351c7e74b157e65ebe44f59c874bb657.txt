	                    <h2>Quality Used Auto and Truck Parts</h2>
<p>Allied Auto Salvage is just six acres, with about <strong>400 vehicles on-site</strong>. Bill noted, &#8220;We&#8217;re only <strong>50 miles from downtown Los Angeles</strong> and that&#8217;s an everyday delivery area.&#8221;</p>
<p>&#8220;We&#8217;re not an ultra late-model yard,&#8221; said Chris, &#8220;because the cars here are so clean and rust-free. People keep their cars a little longer, so we can cater to the body shops and the mechanical customers.&#8221;</p>
<p>Allied Auto Salvage is a member of The State of California Auto Dismantlers Association (<acronym title="State of California Auto Dismantlers Association">SCADA</acronym>) and the Automotive Recyclers Association.<br />
Allied Auto is proud to offer quality used auto parts to the Riverside, Los Angeles (LA) area.</p>
