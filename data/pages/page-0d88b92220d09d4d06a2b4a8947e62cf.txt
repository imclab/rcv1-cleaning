      <h1 class="postTitle"><a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/kia-previews-cross-gt-large-crossover-concept-2013-chicago-auto-show/" rel="bookmark">Kia Previews Cross GT Large Crossover Concept [2013 Chicago Auto Show]</a></h1>

      
      
      <div id="creditsHolder" style="margin:10px 0 0 0;">

        <div id="fbLikeHolder1" style="display:inline-block;"></div>

        <small class="post-credits">
        January 30, 2013 at 11:16 am by <a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/author/alexander-stoklosa/" title="Posts by Alexander Stoklosa" rel="author">Alexander Stoklosa</a>        </small>
      </div>

      <div class="post">

        <!-- Gigya Social Share Floater -->
        <div id="divButtons"></div>

        <p><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-103647" title="Kia Cross GT concept" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Kia-Cross-GT-concept-626x382.jpg" alt="Kia Cross GT concept" width="626" height="382" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-103647" title="Kia Cross GT concept" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Kia-Cross-GT-concept-626x382.jpg" alt="Kia Cross GT concept" width="626" height="382" /></noscript></p>
<p>Kia is going crossover coconuts, it seems, and has announced a second crossover-concept debut for this spring, this time for the 2013 Chicago auto show. The Cross GT concept, which Kia is previewing with the photo above, will appear in the Windy City before the vaguely named <a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/kia-urban-concept-vehicle-teased-loses-tiger-nose-grille-2013-geneva-auto-show/">Urban Vehicle</a> two-door crossover-ish-looking show car bows at the Geneva show in March. <span id="more-103646"></span></p>
<p>Although Kia hasn&#8217;t specified how large the Urban Vehicle concept is, the company is being quite forthcoming with the Cross GT, stating that it previews a premium crossover larger than the <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/news/2014-kia-sorento-photos-and-info-news">Sorento</a>. We&#8217;re betting that this means the Cross GT could someday morph into a big three-row crossover to go against the likes of the Chevy Traverse, Nissan Pathfinder, and Ford Explorer. What&#8217;s interesting is that the Cross GT appears to feature Kia&#8217;s current &#8220;Tiger Nose&#8221; grille, a styling touch deliberately missing from the Urban Vehicle&#8217;s front end. The Cross GT also has a sharply raked roofline that terminates in a chopped rear end reminiscent of <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/news/kia-trackster-concept-news">Kia&#8217;s Track&#8217;ster concept</a> from last year&#8217;s Chicago show, as well as a large, vertical fender vent located just aft of the front wheels.</p>
<hr noshade="noshade" />
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/reviews/2011-kia-sportage-sx-turbo-awd-road-test-review">Instrumented Test: 2011 Kia Sportage SX Turbo AWD</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/news/2012-kia-soul-eco-16-instrumented-test-review">Instrumented Test: 2012 Kia Soul 1.6 Eco</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/reviews/2013-kia-rio5-sx-manual-instrumented-test-review">Instrumented Test: 2013 Kia Rio5 SX Manual</a></li>
</ul>
<hr noshade="noshade" />
<p>We dig the Cross GT&#8217;s look, even when we put on our back-to-reality glasses that show a crossover with a normal roofline, smaller wheels, and (fingers crossed) no fender vent. Critically, Kia could use a large, three-row crossover in its lineup following the mercy killing of the wrong-car-at-the-wrong-time, body-on-frame, V-8–powered <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/jeep-grand-cherokee-vs-kia-borrego-nissan-pathfinder-toyota-4runner-comparison-tests">Borrego</a> SUV.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/2013-chicago-auto-show"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103685" title="2013 Chicago auto show full coverage" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2013-Chicago-auto-show-full-coverage-6262.jpg" alt="2013 Chicago auto show full coverage" width="626" height="38" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-103685" title="2013 Chicago auto show full coverage" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/2013-Chicago-auto-show-full-coverage-6262.jpg" alt="2013 Chicago auto show full coverage" width="626" height="38" /></noscript></a></p>
