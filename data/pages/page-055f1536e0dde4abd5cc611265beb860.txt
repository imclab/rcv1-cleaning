<p>There are basically three types of customization possible, those that enhance the appearance of your car, those that enhance the performance of your car or those that enhance the sound of your car.  These customizations cover everything from tinting and unique paint jobs, suspension or engine upgrades to custom mufflers and high-powered stereo systems. Some car owners dabble in each one of these areas, while others just focus on one; either an awesome stereo or a lowered suspension.</p>



<p>Aftermarket and performance car parts have been gaining popularity for the last few years. As a result you now can get your parts from places as diverse as big box department stores, to specialty stores and websites. Where you shop will depend on what you need and what you're willing to spend. Custom high-end audio components are easy to be found, but if you're looking for highly customized mufflers or some whacky type of van conversion kit, you may need to look harder and spend more.</p>
