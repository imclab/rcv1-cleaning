				<h2>1952 Ferrari 212 Inter Geneva Coupe Vignale</h2>
<h5>The 1952 Ferrari 212 restoration was completed at Gassman Automotive in January 2013. There were 6 of these cars made, and this is the first. After being displayed at the Geneva World Auto Show in 1952, the car changed owners several times, and engine once. The car was found in Connecticut in 2010, and the original engine was found soon after. It was then brought to Gassman, and many hours of research and hard work were poured into the restoration of this incredible Ferrari.</h5><br>
<h3>Gassman Automotive and owners Mike and Jane Green are extremely honored to show the Ferrari at the 63rd annual Pebble Beach Concours d'Elegance on August 16, 2013.</h><br><br><br>

					<h2 class="posts_title">1952 Ferrari 212 Inter Geneva Coupe Vignale</h2>
						
					<div class="entry">

						<p style="font-size: 20px; text-align: center;">Gassman Automotive completed this restoration in January 2013. It was restored to factory original specifications and an amazing piece of art.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>August 18, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The Ferrari was proudly displayed at the 63rd annual <a href="http://www.pebblebeachconcours.net/">Pebble Beach Concours d&#8217;Elegance</a>. While it did not bring home any trophies, Gassman Automotive is extremely honored to attend such an event with such a brilliant car.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>June 10, 2013:</strong><br />
The rear end was torn apart to find a problem in the differential. We assumed it was alright after being sent to us from another company so we didn&#8217;t touch it, however have now discovered that the bearings were bad. We rebuilt the assembly, and are putting the car back together again, very carefully!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 22, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://youtu.be/LlgsfPbZ4pE">The Ferrari has left the building</a>!!! It is <a href="http://youtu.be/w4152fc7ekY">being driven down</a> to West Palm Beach, Florida for the Cavallino Classic XXII car show which is being held on Saturday, January 26, 2013.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.gassmanautomotive.com/awards/cavallino-classic-xxii-2013/">Click to view Gallery of Cavallino Classic XXII</a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 22, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">15 minutes until the car carrier will be here, and the guys at Gassman are reassembling the Ferrari. We found out that we had incorrect shocks on the vehicle, and in the pursuit of perfection, they needed to be corrected. Video of the car leaving the shop to come!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 10, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The Ferrari is finished! The carpets were completed today, and seats installed. After a long day of putting on the finishing touches, <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gCKXQfr5ITU">the car was driven for the first time</a>&#8230; All the way into the paint booth for wash and wax and detailing for the professional photos today (photos to come soon!)</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 7, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The dash of the car has been completed, including all trim pieces. George has made and sewn the door panels, and is now finishing up the carpet and will be installing the seats soon. The hood and all of the hardware and latches are installed, and the springs have been wrapped in a protective layer of leather. The rear windshield is reinstalled, sealed, and the trim pieces are completed. Door handles are installed and working.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 4, 2013:</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Both of the doors and the hood have been put onto the car. The windows in the doors are installed as well. The lights in the headlights have been wired and are in working condition. The rear view mirror was fitted and installed. Unfortunately, the headliner and the rear wind shield had to be uninstalled in the pursuit of perfection. The countdown timer has now reached its end, and the final race to completion is on.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>January 1, 2013:</strong></p>
