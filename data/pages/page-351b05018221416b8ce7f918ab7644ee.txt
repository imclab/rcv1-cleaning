                	<h1>Automotive </h1>

                    <p>Kuehne + Nagel creates value across the automotive industry via our extensive global network, industry expertise and powerful technology.</p>
			<p class="banner_link"></p>
			<div id="banner_sub_shadow"></div>
              	</div><a id="c5030"></a><div class="csc-textpic-text"><p class="bodytext">We have both the resources and expertise to provide a highly competitive edge in automotive logistics across the globe. The tried and trusted service we offer, gained from long experience in the sector, is designed specifically for the automotive industry. Our global logistics network, state-of-the-art information technology and highly-qualified experts, all contribute to a spirit of innovative thinking, and to a record of highly successful implementation. </p>
<p class="bodytext">Our automotive service supports the entire product life cycle of a vehicle:</p></div><a id="c5221"></a><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Inbound Logistics</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">Kuehne + Nagel's inbound logistics services supply domestic and international car production sites with all material necessary for efficient manufacturing. These include the consolidation of products from multiple suppliers, and flexible management of incoming goods to ensure that lead times are met. We also offer customs clearance and transportation services to the warehouse / manufacturing site.<br /><br /></p></div>
</div><a id="c5601"></a><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Production Logistics</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">Our range of services includes:</p><ul><li>Warehousing / Sub-assembly</li><li>Kitting</li><li>Pre-assembly of components</li><li>Various forms of production line feeding such as<ul><li>Just-In-Time (JIT) deliveries</li><li>Just-In Sequence (JIS) services</li><li>Removal and processing of empty load units.</li></ul></li></ul></div>
</div><a id="c5602"></a><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Outbound Logistics</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">Outbound Logistics services include the multi-modal distribution of goods for suppliers, production plants or distributors. </p></div>
</div><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>After-Sales Services</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">In after-sales services / spare parts services, we operate complex distribution networks and warehouses using fully automated warehouse equipment and providing value added services to ensure that dealers receive the support necessary to keep customers satisfied.</p></div>
</div><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Packaging Services</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">We provide: </p><ul><li>Wood packaging</li><li>Industrialised packaging</li><li>Returnable packaging solution</li><li> Temperature controlled and specialised packaging customised to meet our client’s requirements. </li></ul><p class="bodytext">The need for the right packaging design and concept is important for the automotive industry especially given the&nbsp; complexity, lean production processes&nbsp; and&nbsp; globalised supply chains. We also provide two specialised packaging concepts for the automotive industry: </p>
<p class="bodytext"><b>Corrosion intercept services (sub-point to packaging)</b><br />One of our unique value-added services for the automotive industry is its corrosion intercept service, which is a substitute for oil, wax or polish. We are the only logistics provider with the rights to use this effective anti-corrosion material in the packing process.&nbsp; This is just one of many services available from our packaging solution centre.</p>
<p class="bodytext"><b>Completely / semi-knocked down services (sub-point to packaging)</b><br />We offer the entire spectrum of logistics services for preparing, transporting and importing vehicles which are packaged as completely knocked down (CKD) or semi-knocked down (SKD).</p></div>
</div><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Tyre Logistics</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">We provide a set of specialised logistics services for plant  logistics warehouses and tyre distribution centres, allowing tyre  manufacturers to focus on production while receiving reliable  international logistics support from a world class provider.</p></div>
</div><div>
                <h2 class="glide_heading" onclick="toggleGlider(this);"><img width="15" height="15" class="closed" src="/fileadmin/templates/portal/images/trans.gif" /><span>Reverse Logistics</span></h2>

                <div class="glide glide_box"><p class="bodytext">We cover the entire supply chain for the automotive industry including managing return flows. In the field of automotive logistics that means the handling of returnable units, repair and maintenance services as well as return goods under warranty from dealers and distributors. </p></div>
</div><a id="c5217"></a><div class="inner_padding"><p class="bodytext">Kuehne + Nagel provides complete, multi-modal transport services, leveraging our capabilities as the world’s leading seafreight forwarder and top-class airfreight solutions provider.</p>
<p class="bodytext">For more information, please download <a href="fileadmin/user_upload/documents/your_industry/Automotive/documents/Kuehne___Nagel_Automotive_Brochure.pdf" title="Kuehne___Nagel_Automotive_Brochure.pdf (2.2 MB)" target="_blank" >our brochure</a> or  <a href="nc/general_contact/?email_to=automotive%40kuehne-nagel.com&contact_type=loc&referer=292&hash=c68854fc5446124c40e96776190f43ef&cHash=0df557b5cefad0fb69e76dab3953625a">contact us</a>. </p></div><!--TYPO3SEARCH_end--></div>
