<h2 align="center">Sponsored by</h2>
<h1 align="center">The Michael E. Horn Family Foundation</h1>
<p align="justify">ARTLAND, an exciting new dimension in education, is an interactive art studio created especially for children ages 4 to 10. It is a special place designed to challenge and stimulate the imagination while allowing children to create works of art at their own pace.</p>
<p align="justify">ARTLAND features an art laboratory fully equipped with supplies and materials for making works of art and is complemented by child-sized easels and tables. Instruction is provided by art students from area colleges for seasonally themed projects. ARTLAND is FREE and open to all.</p>

<h2 align="center">October 4, 5, &amp; 6
</h2>
<p align="justify">The 2nd Annual Owensboro Museum of Fine Art PAINTOUT, an en plein air event, will be presented in conjunction with Owensboro’s Buffalo Festival October 4, 5, &amp; 6.</p>
<p align="justify"> The three day event will be highlighted by an exhibition and artist’s reception at the museum on Sunday, October 6 where MERIT AND PURCHASE AWARDS will be presented for winning entries during a public reception from 4:30 to 6:30 p.m. Participating artists may also exhibit in a special sales event, The Buffalo Arts &amp; Crafts Fair, staged in the museum’s Ryan Park from 1:00 to 4:00 p.m. on that date.</p>
<p align="justify"> Artists may enter the BUFFALO FESTIVAL PAINTOUT by completing the registration form at www.omfa.us or by calling 270-685-3181. Registration fee is $20 prior to August 1 and $35 after that date. The fee includes the BUFFALO ARTS &amp; CRAFTS FAIR AND ARTIST’S RECEPTION.</p>
