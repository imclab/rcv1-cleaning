<div id="intro">There are many <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/artsandentertainment/tp/Oklahoma-City-Movie-Theaters.htm">Oklahoma City movie theaters</a>, but rather than settling for mediocrity, you should choose one of the best movie theaters in Oklahoma City for your next outing.  For some, high concessions and loud movie-goers have forced just catching it on DVD, but you won't be disappointed if you catch your movie in the right place, though.  Here is information on the best movie theaters in Oklahoma City, including location, showtimes, ticket buying and more.</div><div class="lsItm"><h3>1. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/warrentheatres.htm">Moore Warren Theatre</a></h3><div class="imgw"><q><a href="http://0.tqn.com/d/okc/1/0/Z/A/Warren1.jpg" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank" title='View Full-Size'><img src="http://0.tqn.com/d/okc/1/6/Z/A/Warren1.jpg"></a></q><cite>&#169; Warren Theatres</cite></div><div class="hasimg">Trust me when I tell you that going to the movies simply doesn't get any better than this. Located in <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/neighborhoods/p/mooreokc.htm">Moore</a>, the Warren Theatre sets a new standard. With movie memorabilia everywhere, a classic theater decor, a diner, mammoth screens, state-of-the-art sound, game room and even a soundproof "cry room," just the "regular" experience is memorable enough. But the <i>balcony option</i> will blow you away. Available on two screens each week, adults 21 and over can sit in oversized chairs, enjoy a cocktail from the bar and be served full meals before and during the movie. Once you've been, you will feel cheated every time you see a show anywhere else. <ul><li><a href="http://www.anrdoezrs.net/click-3332365-10504407?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fandango.com%2Fmoorewarrentheatre_aauuo%2Ftheaterpage" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank">Check Showtimes and Buy Tickets</a> <li>1000 Telephone Rd. (Moore) <li>(405) 703-3777</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/warrentheatres.htm">More: Full Moore Warren Profile</a></div></div></div><script>zSB(3,3)</script><div class="lsItm"><h3>2. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcharkins.htm">Harkins Bricktown Cinemas 16</a></h3>Whether you're out for date night in <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/bricktown/Oklahoma_Citys_Bricktown.htm">Bricktown</a> or are just looking for one of the best theaters in OKC, the Harkins Cine Capri Bricktown is a great choice. It opened in late 2004, part of the continuing boom in economic development in Bricktown and has all the elements of a top of the line movie-watching experience, including daycare available for the parents. It's the cream of the crop. <ul><li><a href="http://www.tkqlhce.com/click-3332365-10504407?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fandango.com%2Fharkinscinecapribricktown_aaswz%2Ftheaterpage" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank">Check Showtimes and Buy Tickets</a> <li>150 E. Reno Ave. <li>(405) 231-4747</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcharkins.htm">More: Full Harkins Bricktown Profile</a></div></div><div class="lsItm"><h3>3. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcamctheaters.htm">AMC Quail Springs Mall 24</a></h3>More screens. More times. Great setting. The theater at <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/shoppingandservices/p/quailsprings.htm">Quail Springs</a> only comes in behind the Bricktown Harkins because it's a bit older. Don't let that fool you, though. The theaters are in good shape, and the experience is top notch. If you can handle mall crowds, you'll definitely enjoy yourself. <ul><li><a href="http://www.dpbolvw.net/click-3332365-10504407?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fandango.com%2Famcquailspringsmall24_aaktw%2Ftheaterpage" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank">Check Showtimes and Buy Tickets</a> <li>2501 W. Memorial Road <li>(405) 775-0262</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcamctheaters.htm">More: Full AMC Quail Springs 24 Profile</a></div></div><div class="lsItm"><h3>4. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okccinemark.htm">Cinemark Tinseltown</a></h3>This is another one of the big theaters with a bunch of screens, stadium seating and digital sound. It even has a game room for the kids while you wait for your movie to start. I rank it below some of the others, but overall, it's a solid place to catch a movie and has better prices than most. <ul><li><a href="http://www.dpbolvw.net/click-3332365-10504407?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fandango.com%2Fcinemarktinseltown_aaitg%2Ftheaterpage" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank">Check Showtimes and Buy Tickets</a> <li>6001 Martin Luther King Blvd. <li>(405) 424-0461</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okccinemark.htm">More: Full Cinemark Tinseltown Profile</a></div></div><div class="lsItm"><h3>5. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcamctheaters.htm">AMC Crossroads Mall 16</a></h3>I've only been to the Crossroads Mall 16 a couple of times, but I've been fairly pleased with it. It's clean, comfortable and has enough screens that the movie you want will probably be on, and it seems to have improved since it changed from a Regal Cinema to an AMC. If you're down in south OKC, you should check it out. You likely won't be disappointed. <ul><li><a href="http://www.jdoqocy.com/click-3332365-10504407?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fandango.com%2Famccrossroadsmall16_aamdi%2Ftheaterpage" zT="-o1/XL" target="_blank">Check Showtimes and Buy Tickets</a> <li>1211 E. I-240 <li>(405) 632-4872</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/movies/p/okcamctheaters.htm">More: Full AMC Crossroads Profile</a></div></div><div class="lsItm"><h3>6. <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/museumseducational/p/okcartmuseum.htm">Noble Theater at the OKC Museum of Art</a></h3><B>Bonus Pick:</b> Some people don't even know about the Noble Theater. From the <a href="http://okc.about.com/od/museumseducational/p/okcartmuseum.htm">Oklahoma City Museum of Art</a>, this unique gem will give you the opportunity to see rare and classic films as well as hard-to-find quality movies in a restored classic theater. Whereas many of the establishments on this list focus on the "blockbusters," the Noble Theater presents award nominees, foreign films, short films, classics and much more.<br> <ul><li>415 Couch Drive <li>(405) 236-3100</ul><div class="lsLks"><a href="http://okc.about.com/od/museumseducational/p/okcartmuseum.htm">More: Museum Info and Movie Specials</a></div></div><!--/gc-->
