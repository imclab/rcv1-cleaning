               <p>According to a recent report, Ford will introduce a Focus coupe concept at the North American International Auto Show.</p>
<p>Details are limited, but <em>Auto Express</em> is reporting the coupe will be low, long, and dramatic.  While this is a little vauge, we can expect a massive grille, a center-mounted exhaust and distinctive C-pillars which will mimic the legendary Capri.</p>
<p>A variety of engines will be available, but the range-topping model will boast a turbocharged 2.0-liter EcoBoost with 250 PS (184 kW / 247 hp) and 360 Nm (265 lb-ft) of torque.  This will likely enable the ST coupe to accelerate from 0-60 mph in six seconds, before hitting an electronically limited 155 mph (250 km/h). &nbsp;If that hasn't tickled your fancy, the car will also have a sport-tuned suspension and a torque vectoring system.</p>
