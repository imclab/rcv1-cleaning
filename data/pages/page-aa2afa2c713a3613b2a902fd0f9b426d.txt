<p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica"><strong>Seller's Responsibilities:</strong></font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">(Provide the following items to the buyer)</font></p>

    <li>
    <div><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">The Colorado Certificate of Title.</font></div>
    </li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial">If there is more than one owner named on the front of the Certificate of&nbsp;Title,&nbsp;all owners must print and sign their name as seller.&nbsp;</font></li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial">Enter purchase price and date.</font>
    <ul type="disc">
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial">After Hours Purchase <a target="_blank" href="http://www.michie.com/colorado/lpext.dll?f=templates&amp;fn=main-h.htm&amp;cp=">C.R.S. 42-3-115(2)(b), C.R.S. 42-6-113</a>;&nbsp; A buyer of a vehicle may operate a vehicle on the highway prior to registering the vehicle when:</font>
        <ul type="disc">
            <li><font size="2" face="Arial">The buyer has purchased the motor vehicle within the last 36 hours from a person who is not a licensed motor vehicle dealer.</font></li>
            <li><font size="2" face="Arial">The vehicle was purchased either on a Saturday, on a Sunday, on a legal holiday, or between 5p.m. and 8 a.m. </font></li>
            <li><font size="2" face="Arial">The vehicle is being driven from the place where the seller stored the vehicle to the place where the buyer intends to store the vehicle.</font>&nbsp;</li>
        </ul>
        </li>
        <li><font size="2" face="Arial">The buyer must carry in the vehicle:</font>
        <ul type="disc">
            <li><font size="2" face="Arial">A bill of sale that identifies the vehicle by year, make and VIN and shows the time and date of sale and is signed by both the buyer and the seller.</font></li>
            <li><font size="2" face="Arial">Proof of insurance.</font></li>
        </ul>
        </li>
    </ul>
    </li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial">Fill in the name(s) and addresses of the buyer at this time.</font></li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">Complete odometer disclosure reading and indicator information on the Certificate of Title or it may be disclosed on the DR2173 Motor Vehicle Bill of Sale form which can be purchased through Standard Register at 303-860-8000. </font></li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">An</font><font size="2"><font face="Arial, Helvetica"> emissions inspection certificate is required if the buyer lives in an&nbsp;</font><a href="/cs/Satellite?c=Page&amp;cid=1185957917898&amp;pagename=Revenue-MV%2FRMVLayout"><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">emissions area</font></a></font><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">.</font></li>
    <li><font size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica">If the title has been branded &ldquo;Rebuilt from Salvage&rdquo;, the seller must complete the&nbsp;<a href="/cs/Satellite?c=Document_C&amp;childpagename=Revenue-MV%2FDocument_C%2FRMVAddLink&amp;cid=1211966065460&amp;pagename=RMVWrapper">Rebuilt from Salvage Disclosure</a>. If reason for Salvage is unknown, a vehicle&nbsp;<a href="/cs/Satellite?c=Page&amp;childpagename=Revenue-MV%2FRMVLayout&amp;cid=1220610980199&amp;pagename=RMVWrapper">record search</a>&nbsp;must be completed to determine the reason the vehicle was salvaged.</font>&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
