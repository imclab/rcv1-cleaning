<p>Starting Saturday with a performance by comedian Julie Goldman, the third annual event will be held at the  from Jan. 14-28.</p><div id='popular_stories_wrapper' class='clear-fix invisible-mobile'></div>
<p>The two-week festival will feature a number of comedians and performing artists, as well as a laughter yoga workshop. The unique exercise routine is designed to trigger chemical reactions in the brain and strengthen core muscles through laughter.</p>
<p>Here is a complete rundown of the festival&#39;s schedule:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Jan. 14, 7:30pm:</strong> </li>
<li><strong>Jan. 15, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 19, 7:30pm; Jan. 20, 10am: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 21, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 22, 12pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 22, 2pm; Jan. 23, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 25, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 26, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
<li><strong>Jan. 28, 7:30pm: </strong></li>
</ul>
