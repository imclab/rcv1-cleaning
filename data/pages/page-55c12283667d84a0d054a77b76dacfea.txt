<h1><span>Who</span> We Are</h1>
<h2>Professional musicians, dancers, actors and artists at your service!</h2><p>The <a href="/about-us/faculty/">faculty</a> and staff at the Academy of Fine Arts are tried, true and dedicated to developing your creativity and expressive potential through the arts. Whether your goal is to create or perform professionally or for your own enjoyment, our university trained faculty is here to provide expert guidance in whichever field you choose.</p>

<div class="colthirdstwo">
<h1><span>What</span> We Do</h1>
<h2>Music classes, Art classes, Theatre classes, Dance classes</h2><p>We offer an array of classes, lessons, performances and exhibitions in the four major branches of art - Music, Art, Theatre, and Dance.</p>

<div class="colthirdsthree">
<h1><span>Why</span> Our Classes</h1>
<h2>Lively, convenient atmosphere &amp; exceptional faculty</h2>
		<p>Four different branches of the arts in one space makes for a lively atmosphere &mdash; and, convenience for parents and students wanting to investigate and excel in more than one discipline. The <a href="/about-us/faculty/">exceptional faculty</a>, each member chosen through a rigorous search and audition process, has trained literally thousands of students over the years and made a significant contribution to the arts in Montgomery County.</p>
