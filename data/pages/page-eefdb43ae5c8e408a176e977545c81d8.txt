<h4>Fine Arts &amp; Communication Studies</h4><p>The Department of Fine Arts and Communication Studies offers Bachelor of Arts degrees in the following areas:</p><ul><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=35211&amp;libID=37633" title="Art Education - Default">Art Education</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=13132&amp;libID=15554" title="Prospective Students">Art Studio, with an emphasis in graphic design</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=38767&amp;libID=41186" title="Commercial Music Program">Commercial Music</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=2047&amp;libID=4702" title="USC Upstate: Fine Arts &amp; Communication - Bachelor of Arts in Communications">Communication,  with concentrations in theatre, communication studies, mass media and public relations </a></li></ul><p>The mission of the Department is to enhance the intellectual, ethical, cultural, and professional development of fine arts and communications students. The faculty is committed to preparing students to become competent professionals in music, art, theatre, communication studies, journalism and mass communications. Minors are also offered in the following areas:</p><ul><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=13202&amp;libID=15624" title="Art History">Art History</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=38767&amp;libID=41186" title="Commercial Music Program">Commercial  Music </a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=2061&amp;libID=4716" title="Communication Studies Minor">Communication Studies</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=2055&amp;libID=4710" title="Journalism and Mass Communications">Mass Media</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=39666&amp;libID=42091" title="Public Relations - default">Public  Relations</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=10664&amp;libID=13088" title="Shoestring Players - Default">Theatre</a></li><li><a href="/WorkArea/linkit.aspx?LinkIdentifier=id&amp;ItemID=32450&amp;libID=34874" title="Minors in Music">Jazz Studies</a></li></ul><p> </p>
