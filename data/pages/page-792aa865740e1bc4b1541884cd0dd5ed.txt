<p style="text-align: justify;"><em>Audi TT RS Plus</em> has an additional power of 20 hp and 15 Nm of torque. So that, the squeeze of the powe made the total output of this German iron horse into 360 hp and peak torque of 465 Nm. Furthermore, the maximum speed of <a title="Audi TT RS Plus UK Market Price" href="http://carscoop.blogspot.com/2012/04/audi-releases-uk-pricing-for-new-tt-rs.html" target="_blank">2013 Audi TT RS Plus</a> was increased from 250 kmph to 280 kmph. When the test acceleration 0-100 kmph, the Audi TT RS Plus just need a time of 4.1 seconds. This result is two tenths faster than the standard version. That was very great, wasn&#8217;t it?</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://autotrendupdate.com/audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-2013-price-and-specifications-release/2013-audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-rear-view" rel="attachment wp-att-1523"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1523" title="2013 Audi TT RS Plus Coupe - rear view" src="http://autotrendupdate.com/ocs-images/1513/audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-2013--6.jpg" alt="audi tt rs plus coupe 2013  6 Audi TT RS Plus Coupe 2013 Price and Specifications Release" width="588" height="392" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The secret behind the performance of the maximality of this <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Audi TT RS Plus</span> is the <a title="Auto Trend Update for Exhaust Gas System" href="http://autotrendupdate.com" target="_blank">sport exhaust system</a> with black tails pipes which is optional. That way the sound coming out of exhaust gas channels become louder and louder. This car also gets a new set of 19-inch alloys (instead of the regular model&#8217;s 18-inch rims) and a matt aluminium surround for the revised grille and carbon fiber mirror caps.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://autotrendupdate.com/audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-2013-price-and-specifications-release/2013-audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-alloy-wheel-and-tyre-viee" rel="attachment wp-att-1524"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1524" title="2013 Audi TT RS Plus Coupe - alloy wheel and tyre view" src="http://autotrendupdate.com/ocs-images/1513/audi-tt-rs-plus-coupe-2013--7.jpg" alt="audi tt rs plus coupe 2013  7 Audi TT RS Plus Coupe 2013 Price and Specifications Release" width="588" height="392" /></a></p>
