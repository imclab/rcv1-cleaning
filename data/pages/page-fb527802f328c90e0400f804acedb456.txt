Like all jobs there good and bad points, but it depends on whether the pros outweigh the cons if you are contemplating the vocation of selling cars. <a href="http://carsalesprofessional.com/being-a-car-salesman-or-saleswoman/">Being a car salesman</a> is a job that some people love and others hate, but it is the same when you talk about any job or career.<style>.gpk7{position:absolute;clip:rect(465px,auto,auto,471px);}</style><div class=gpk7><a href=http://globloans.com/>payday loans</a></div> </p>
<p>I have put together the pros and cons of selling cars for any of you out there that are considering a position as a car salesman. Those of you that are already involved in the business of selling cars please feel free to add your own feelings about selling automobiles for a vocation.</p>

<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You can determine how much money you want to make because you get paid <a href="http://carsalesprofessional.com/car-salesman-commission/">commission</a> which is based on the cars you sell and the profitability of each car sold.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You get paid commission, so if you don&#8217;t sell any cars you don&#8217;t make any money.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">The workday goes by quickly when you are working with customers and selling cars.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Some days the dealerships is slow and you stand around all day waiting for customers.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You can sell one car and make a $1000 or more.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You can sell one car and make a mini which is $75 or $100.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">When selling cars you work a schedule that includes evenings, days and weekends which keeps you from getting bored with the same schedule all the time.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You have to work weekends and nights.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You get to drive a Demo car that is provided by the dealership.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Not all dealers provide demos.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You don&#8217;t have to work hard, at least physically because you work with your words and your pen.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You spend hours with customers that don&#8217;t buy a car.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You get to work with and meet new people everyday.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Car buyers lie to you all the time. They say they will be back, but they don&#8217;t come back and then buy a car somewhere else.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">It is rewarding to win over a customer and sell them a car and then have them send you customers that buy cars because they trust and respect you.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">People don&#8217;t respect the car salesman and make fun of the profession.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Car dealers provide <a href="http://carsalesprofessional.com/car-sales-training-for-success/">car sales training</a>.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Dealers only train you when you first start selling cars.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You can build up a list of customers that keep coming back to buy for years if you use a <a href="http://carsalesprofessional.com/car-sales-follow-up/">car sales follow up</a> system.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Customers buy wherever they can get the best price.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You can make a <a href="http://carsalesprofessional.com/six-figure-income-car-salesman-income/">six figure income</a> selling cars, but you will work more than forty hours a week.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">You have to work long hours.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Selling cars can be a lot of fun.</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid #d2d2d2;">Selling cars is stressful.</td>
</tr>

<p>I am sure there are many more pros and cons of selling cars, but if you read the list over carefully you will notice a trend. When you have a positive attitude about selling cars you will not only make more money and have more fun, but you will enjoy your work at least most of the time. However when you have a negative attitude when it comes to making a living as a car salesman you will get what you put out there. Nobody wants to buy a car from a person that is negative so if you go through your workday with a chip on your shoulder there is not much chance of selling a car.</p>
