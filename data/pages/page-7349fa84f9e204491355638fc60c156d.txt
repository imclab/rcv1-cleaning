		<h2>
			<a href="http://decaturartsfestival.com/festival-events/fine-arts-exhibition/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Fine Arts Exhibition">Fine Arts Exhibition</a>
		</h2>
		
		<img width="545" height="250" src="http://decaturartsfestival.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/03/daf-fine-arts.jpg" class="attachment-full wp-post-image" alt="daf-fine-arts" />
		<div class="entry">
 			<h3>May 21-June 2</h3>
<p>Dalton Gallery at Agnes Scott College<br />
Dana Fine Arts Building<br />
141 E. College Ave. (<a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=s_q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=141+E.+College+Ave.+30030&amp;aq=&amp;sll=37.0625,-95.677068&amp;sspn=63.9851,135.263672&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=141+E+College+Ave,+Decatur,+Georgia+30030&amp;z=17" target="_blank">map</a>)</p>
<h4>Opening Reception</h4>
<p>Tuesday, May 21 • 5:15-7 pm<br />
Be among the first to view an intriguing collection of paintings, prints, photographs, sculpture and mixed media selected by this year’s distinguished jury. Meet the artists and enjoy refreshments. Held in conjunction with the May meeting of the <a href="http://decaturdba.com/" target="_blank">Decatur Business Association</a>. </p>
<h4>Exhibition Hours</h4>
<p>Open daily: 2-5 pm<br />
Friday, May 24: 2-10 pm<br />
Saturday, May 25-Sunday, May 26: noon-6 pm<br />
Sunday, June 2: noon-6 pm</p>
<h4>Last Call Closing Reception</h4>
<p>Sunday, June 2 • 4-6 pm<br />
Join the Decatur Arts Alliance and selected artists for brief discussions of the artists’ works. </p>
<p>The Fine Arts Exhibition attracts submissions from regional and national artists and is reviewed by respected members of the Atlanta arts community. This year’s jurors are Nell Ruby, Chris Appleton, and Temme Barkin-Leeds.</p>
