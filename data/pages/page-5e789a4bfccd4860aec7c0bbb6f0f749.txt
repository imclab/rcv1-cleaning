<h2>Arts and Entertainment</h2>
<p>South Puget Sound Community College participates in&nbsp;an important and strategic community partnership with the <a href="http://www.washingtoncenter.org/">Washington Center for the Performing Arts</a> to manage the public operations of the Kenneth J. Minnaert Center for the Arts.</p>
<p>The Kenneth J. Minnaert Center for the Arts features top speakers, <a href="/community-and-business/entertainment/theatre">theater</a>, performing arts and <a href="/community-and-business/entertainment/music">music</a> throughout the year. Also featuring the <a href="/community-and-business/entertainment/gallery">Minnaert Center for the Arts Gallery</a>, the center showcases national, regional, state and local acts and artists.</p>
<p>If you are interesting in renting space at the Minnaert Center, please call (360) 753-8585. Tickets for events at the Minnaert Center are available online at <a href="http://www.OlyTix.org">OlyTix.org</a>&nbsp;or by calling (360)753-8586.</p>
