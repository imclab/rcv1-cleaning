<div class="combinationContent">
  <H1>
  Northampton Arts & Entertainment
  </H1>
   
 

</div>
<div>
<p>
Northampton is renowned as an arts and entertainment destination in Western Massachusetts, named &#x201C;Number One Best Small Arts Town in America&#x201D; by author John Villani and one of the &#x201C;Top 25 Arts Destinations&#x201D; in the nation by AmericanStyle magazine.  Northampton is home to internationally known musicians, artists, writers, and performers presenting their work in local theaters and galleries.  There are virtually unlimited options for arts and entertainment in and around Northampton. Here are some of the resources you can use to keep up to date on the latest in the world of the arts:<br />
<hr>
</p>
</div><div class="combinationContent">
  <H2>
  <hr>Arts and Tourism Organizations
  </H2>
   
 

</div>
<div class="combinationContent">
  <H3>
    <A HREF="http://www.academyofmusictheatre.com/" target="_blank">
  Academy of Music Theatre
    </A>
  </H3>
   
 

<p >
The Academy offers first class performing arts and film presentations in an historic theatre of national significance that aesthetically is downtown's jewel, at the intersection of Main, South, Elm and State Streets. The Academy also encourages the use of the venue for social, educational and professional events.
</p>
</div>
<div class="combinationContent">
  <H3>
    <A HREF="http://www.northamptonma.gov/arts/" target="_blank">
  Northampton Arts Council
    </A>
  </H3>
   
 

<p >
City Arts Council sponsored events calendar.
</p>
</div>
<div class="combinationContent">
  <H3>
    <A HREF="http://www.explorenorthampton.com/" target="_blank">
  Greater Northampton Area Chamber of Commerce
    </A>
  </H3>
   
 

<p >
Arts and entertainment listings, events, venues in Northampton and around the Pioneer Valley.
</p>
</div>
<div class="combinationContent">
  <H3>
    <A HREF="http://www.nohoarts.org" target="_blank">
  Northampton Center For The Arts
    </A>
  </H3>
   
 

<p >
The Northampton Center for the Arts (NCFA) is a private, non-profit arts organization which promotes the arts in greater Northampton. Located downtown, NCFA provides low-cost exhibit and performance space for area artists and arts organizations in an elegant turn-of-the-century performance hall and two adjoining galleries. NCFA is dedicated to programming theater, music, art and dance that is culturally diverse.
</p>
</div>
<div class="combinationContent">
  <H3>
    <A HREF="http://www.northamptondowntown.com/" >
  Northampton Business Improvement District
    </A>
  </H3>
   
 

<p >
The Northampton Business Improvement District is a group of local residents, business owners and advocates working together to support the vibrant downtown of Northampton, Massachusetts as an exciting cultural, shopping, dining and residential destination.
</p>
</div>
