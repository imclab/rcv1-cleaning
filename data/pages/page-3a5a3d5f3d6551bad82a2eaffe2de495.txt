		Once you increase boost in your Coupe GT turbo system, it's a good time to consider replacing the restrictive factory downpipe. An aftermarket Coupe GT downpipe offers a larger diameter and smoother bends than the original, resulting in what is often a substantial horsepower increase. Depending on the design, downpipes may also come equipped with integrated high-flow catalytic converters and/or exhaust diverters, which re-route wastegate gases for decreased exhaust turbulence.
