	<div class="entry-content">	
		<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.pakart.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Southwest-Black-Fine-Art-Show-web.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-773" title="Southwest-Black-Fine-Art-Show-web" src="http://www.pakart.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Southwest-Black-Fine-Art-Show-web.jpg" alt="" width="294" height="426" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">April 13 &#8211; 15, 2012 is the Southwest Black Fine Art Show 2012!</p>
<p>In 2010 over 3,000 collectors, educators, and art lovers came together for a weekend full of exhibits, workshops, and sales to showcase and celebrate Black Fine Art.</p>
<p>The Southwest Black Fine Art Show (SWBFA) has attracted artists, collectors, educators, curators and families from all over the country.  The SWBFA Show was created to provide a quality platform for artists, collectors, galleries, educators, fine art professionals, and anyone interested in black fine art to interact with the nation&#8217;s best black artist. The SWBFA Show is committed to the exclusive presentation of original art produced by artists of African and African-American descent. Our goal is to make this show a national destination point where collectors come to find quality black art and artists can come to share their unique and valuable gifts.</p>
<p>The SWBFA Show was founded by Dallas artist, Frank Frazier.  He has been assisted by local volunteers, group of artists, art educators, collectors and curators from across North Texas and the nation to make the show a continuous success.</p>
<p>Early Registration Deadline:  Febuary 29, 2012</p>
<p>Final Deadline:  March 15, 2012</p>
