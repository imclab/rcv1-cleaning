<H3><FONT FACE=VERDANA COLOR="008040">Questions and Answers</FONT></H3>

<BLOCKQUOTE>

<FONT FACE=VERDANA SIZE=2>

<TABLE BORDER=0 WIDTH=400>
<TR>
<TD ALIGN=LEFT VALIGN=TOP><FONT FACE=VERDANA SIZE=2>

<P><B><U>Chrysler Sebring Convertible Fluid Fill</U></B></P>

</FONT>
<P><FONT FACE=VERDANA SIZE=3 COLOR="FF0000"><B>Q. </B></FONT><FONT FACE=VERDANA SIZE=2>
1998 Chrysler Sebring Convertible. The top wouldn't go up. Found a leak by the passenger side cylinder. It was the line. How do I fill the hydraulic fluid? Do I need a special tool? Where do I fill it, pump? I have a Chilton's manual, it tells me to fill the fluid, but doesn't tell me how.</P>

<P>Thanks,<BR>
John</P>

</FONT>
<TD ALIGN=LEFT VALIGN=TOP><IMG SRC="mechanic.jpg" ALT="Chrysler Sebring Convertible Fluid Fill"></A></TD></TR>
</TABLE>

<P><FONT FACE=VERDANA SIZE=3 COLOR="FF0000"><B>A. </B></FONT>On top of one end of the convertible top pump is a rubber fill plug, remove it and fill it to the bottom of the fill hole. Here is the proper procedure.</P>

<OL>
<LI>Rotate and raise the motor/pump assembly so the reservoir is in the up position. The rubber filler plug will be at the top.
<LI>Raise the motor and pump fitting to at least 2" above the horizontal rear seat back reinforcement.
<LI>Tilt the motor and pump assembly to approximately the 10 o'clock position, with the rubber filler plug still at the top.
</OL>

<P CLASS=MSOBODYTEXT STYLE='TEXT-INDENT:. 2IN'><FONT COLOR="FF0000"><B>CAUTION:</B></FONT> The motor and pump assembly must remain in the current position until the rubber filler plug is reinstalled in step 7.</P>

<OL start=4>
<LI>Remove the rubber fill plug from the reservoir.
<LI>Fill the hydraulic system by adding Dexron 2 Automatic Transmission Fluid to the bottom of the fill hole.
</OL>

<P CLASS=MSOBODYTEXT STYLE='TEXT-INDENT:. 2IN'><FONT COLOR="FF0000"><B>CAUTION:</B></FONT> Do not operate the top switch in the "up", closing the top, direction.</P>

<OL start=6>
<LI>With two people, activate the top switch in the "down" (opening the top) position to fully retract the cylinder rods and hold for approximately 5 seconds, until the motor and pump stalls. A percolating or gurgle sound/feeling is an indication that the procedure is working.
<LI>Top off the pump with Dexron 2 and insert the filler plug.
</OL>

</BLOCKQUOTE>
