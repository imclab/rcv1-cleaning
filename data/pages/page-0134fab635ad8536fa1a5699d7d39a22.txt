    <h1>Fine Arts Events</h1>
    
  <p>For every season,  Marietta College offers a plethora of Fine Arts Events which are open to students, faculty, staff, and the general public. Throughout the year, one can peruse <a href="http://www.marietta.edu/Student_Life/Fine_Arts_Events/art_schedule.html">exhibitions of student art</a>, attend productions of student and faculty-produced <a href="http://www.marietta.edu/Student_Life/Fine_Arts_Events/theatre_schedule.html">plays</a>, and enjoy stirring <a href="http://www.marietta.edu/Student_Life/Fine_Arts_Events/concerts.html">musical performances</a> by the many college musical ensembles.</p>

<p>Marietta College also hosts nationally and internationally known performers, speakers and authors, both through our acclaimed <a href="http://www.marietta.edu/Student_Life/Fine_Arts_Events/esbenshade.html">Esbenshade</a> series, and in special <a href="http://www.marietta.edu/Student_Life/Fine_Arts_Events/concerts.html">concerts</a> featuring performers such as Juliana Hough, OAR, Fuel, and Taylor Swift, to name a few.</p>
