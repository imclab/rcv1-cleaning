		</h2>
    <div class="content clearfix">
        <div id="image">
            <a href="/news/2034"><img alt="Taylor Swift Fans Hate Tank" src="http://uploads.kidzworld.com/news/feature_image/2034/Taylor-Swift-Trouble-news.jpg" /></a>
            <div class="caption center">
                Taylor Swift fans won't take anyone shaming their Queen!
            </div>
        </div>
        <div class="text">
            <div class="article_image center"><img style="float:none;" src="http://uploads.kidzworld.com/article/36091/taylor-swift-t-news.jpg" alt="Taylor Swift" /><div class="article_caption">The Bad Kids Clothing tank features 10 of Taylor's exes</div></div><p>
	<a title="Taylor Swift Fun Facts!" href="/article/28272-taylor-swift-fun-facts"><strong>Taylor Swift</strong></a> fans, known as "Swifties", have taken to message boards and writing strongly worded letters of complaint to a clothing company that is printing a tank top with all the last names of Taylor's alleged exes (we say alleged since many of them are uncomfirmed romances.) Online retailer <strong>Bad Kids Clothing</strong> was inspired to make the tank tops when they created one custom version for a friend and posted it to instagram, and were floored by the popularity of it.</p>
<p>
	However the Swifties were swift in their response, feeling that the shirt was not only unfair to Taylor, but untrue! This isn't the first time they've tried to put a stop to T's that comment on Taylor's romantic life, Abercrombie &amp; Fitch used to make a shirt that said "More BFs than T.S." that her fans got involved with stopping.</p>
