  <tr> <td><a name="article"><h3>Why</a> You Need a Vehicle Bill of Sale</h3> 
  By <a href="index.phtml" title="Used Auto Sales Website">AutoCrisis.com</a> 
  <p>A <b>vehicle bill of sale is a written agreement 
  by which a used auto owner assigns or transfers their rights or interest in 
  the vehicle to the buyer.</b> For <a href="packages.phtml" title="Sell your Used Auto here!">used 
  auto sales</a>, having this form will be of great help in the selling process.<br> 
  A vehicle bill of sale may serve as one of the documents that establishes a 
  buyer's proof of ownership when trying to obtain auto insurance or prove ownership.</p><p>AutoCrisis.com 
  provides the below free auto bill of sale for use upon the sale of a motor vehicle 
  or automobile. Both the seller and the buyer complete the vehicle bill of sale. 
  Please note that your local DMV may not provide free auto bill of sale forms.<br> 
  The type of information normally included in a used vehicle bill of sale includes 
  the following:</p><ol>
					 <li>Year, Make and Model of the vehicle.
					 <li>Description of vehicle.
					 <li>Buyer and Seller's names and signatures.
					 <li>Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).
					 <li>Purchase price of the vehicle.
					 <li>Odometer reading, etc.</ol>
					 
  <p>The 
  buyer retains the original copy of the used auto bill of sale, and provides 
  it to the DMV with the other acceptable proofs of ownership required when purchasing 
  a tag.<br> Additionally, the vehicle bill of sale may be notarized. The Buyer 
  and Seller can make copies of the bill of sale, for record keeping purposes.

  Remember! A bill of sale of a motor vehicle, is not sufficient proof of auto 
  ownership. So, the vehicle purchaser may need other forms of proof of ownership, 
  such as the vehicle Title.<br> AutoCrisis.com is happy to provide you with this 
  free sample auto bill of sale. Also, we do not require you to register in order 
  to access this form.<br> <b>When looking for used car sites to sell your used 
  cars by owner, AutoCrisis.com, is 'the' place to go!</b></p>
