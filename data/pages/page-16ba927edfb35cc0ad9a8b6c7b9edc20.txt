						<h2 class="posts_title">&#8216;X Factor&#8217; elimination shocks fans; LA Reid quits</h2>
			
						
<div class="meta">
	Published by EOTM News Editor on <a href="http://eotmblog.com/2012/12/x-factor-elimination-shocks-fans-la-reid-quits/">December 13th, 2012</a> - in <a href="http://eotmblog.com/Entertainment News/breaking-news/" title="View all posts in Breaking News" rel="category tag">Breaking News</a>, <a href="http://eotmblog.com/Entertainment News/entertainment-news/" title="View all posts in Entertainment News" rel="category tag">Entertainment News</a>, <a href="http://eotmblog.com/Entertainment News/reality-tv/" title="View all posts in Reality TV" rel="category tag">Reality TV</a>, <a href="http://eotmblog.com/Entertainment News/tv/" title="View all posts in TV" rel="category tag">TV</a></div>
							<div class="entry">
								<div class="KonaBody"><p><a title="LA Reid quits" href="http://www.usatoday.com/story/life/people/2012/12/13/x-factor-results-shocks-fans-la-reid-quits/1768509/" target="_blank"><strong>(USA Today)</strong> </a>Just hours before <em>The<strong><a title="la reid quits" href="http://eotmblog.com/2011/12/x-factor-winner/" target="_blank"> X Factor</a></strong></em> semifinals went on the air Thursday, judge/mentor <strong><a title="la reid quits x factor" href="http://eotmblog.com/Celebrity%20News,%20Breaking%20News/x-factor/" target="_blank">L.A. Reid</a></strong> told <em>Access Hollywood</em> he was quitting his judge&#8217;s gig.</p>
<blockquote><p>&#8220;I have decided that I will not return to <em>The X Factor</em>next year,&#8221; he told <em>Access&#8217;</em> Shaun Robinson in an exclusive interview. &#8220;I have to go back and I have a company to run that I&#8217;ve kind of neglected and it saddens me a little bit, but only a little bit.&#8221;</p>
<p>L.A. said he needs to get back to business as the chairman and CEO of Epic Records. And he has no hard feelings for Simon Cowell or anyone else. &#8220;It was a nice break, it was a nice departure from what I&#8217;ve done for the past 20 years, but now I gotta get back to work.&#8221;</p>
<div id="attachment_18237" class="wp-caption alignright" style="width: 235px"><a href="http://eotmblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/la-reid1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-18237" title="la reid" src="http://eotmblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/la-reid1-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">(Photo: Nino Munoz, Fox)</p></div></blockquote>
<p>But first he has to finish up work on Season 2. And Reid went about business as usual on Thursday&#8217;s results show.</p>
<p>The Top Four final <em>X Factor</em> acts got together on stage to kick things off singing the appropriate <em>I&#8217;m Coming Home</em>.</p>
<p>And who would be <em>going home</em>?</p>
<p>Beloved country crooner Tate Stevens? Highly unlikely.</p>
<p>Teen powerhouse Carly Rose Sonenclar? Doubtful, although she wasn&#8217;t flawless on Wednesday. &#8220;I feel really confident about her performance,&#8221; said mentor Britney Spears.</p>
<p>Boy band Emblem3? Not if their crazed fans came through for them.</p>
<p>Fifth Harmony girl group? &#8220;I think it&#8217;s going to be difficult for my girls &#8216;cuz the other three are so strong,&#8221; said their mentor Simon Cowell, but he added, &#8220;Like I said, I do believe in miracles.&#8221;</p>
<p>And, without further ado &#8230; surprise! Fifth Harmony was sent through to the finals.</p>
<p>It was the first act announced by hosts Mario Lopez and Khloe Kardashian Odom. Britney Spears had a look of surprise and horror on her face. (Twitter loved it.) Simon smiled. &#8220;I could not be happier,&#8221; he said. L.A. admitted, &#8220;I&#8217;m very shocked.&#8221; Britney said she was &#8220;a little disappointed.&#8221;</p>
<p>To give everyone a few minutes to calm down, Bridgit Mendler sang <em>Ready or Not</em>.</p>
<p>And then the second act going through was announced: Tate Stevens!</p>
<p>&#8220;For every country music fan out there, I am one of the biggest country music fans. Thank you very much!&#8221;</p>
<p>Bruno Mars kept the mood up by performing <em>Locked Out of Heaven,</em> rocking the stage with his dancin&#8217; band and a laser light show.</p>
<p>And then it was time for the big news: Carly Rose Sonenclar was through to the finals! &#8220;I don&#8217;t even know what to say,&#8221; said the teen, who looked extremely relieved. &#8220;Thanks for voting. Emblem3, I know you&#8217;re going to go far. I&#8217;m going to buy your album.&#8221;</p>
<p>The cute surfer-boy banders were booted off. Singer Wes Stromberg thanked their &#8220;awesome&#8221; fans for &#8220;voting their butts off.&#8221; Simon said, &#8220;I&#8217;m very, very disappointed, but,&#8221; he told the guys, &#8220;I mean this: You&#8217;re going to have a huge career.&#8221;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
