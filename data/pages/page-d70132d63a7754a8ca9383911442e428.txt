Zipcar will be an integral part of these vibrant communities of well-informed, connected people who enjoy urban life and transportation options.</p>
<div id="callout">
	<h3>We envision a future where car-sharing members outnumber car owners in major cities around the globe.</h3>

Our leading brand will be synonymous with fun, honesty, clever innovation, and social good. Car-sharing strategies will be a requisite part of the planning for all college campuses, urban, residential, and commercial developments, and city planning agencies.</p>
<p>We will become a premier employer for talented, passionate people who seek to turn great ideas into a profitable, growing company while doing good for our world.</p>
