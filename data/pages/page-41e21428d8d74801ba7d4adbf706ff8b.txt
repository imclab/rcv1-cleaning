<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
Usher Fan Disrupts The Voice 
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-526006924963080819'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-hz1rUGwTeNo/UXVE4p9bk_I/AAAAAAABTOQ/Rm81aqRuqXw/s1600/Screen+Shot+2013-04-22+at+10.09.07+AM.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="258" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-hz1rUGwTeNo/UXVE4p9bk_I/AAAAAAABTOQ/Rm81aqRuqXw/s400/Screen+Shot+2013-04-22+at+10.09.07+AM.png" width="400" /></a></div>
<br />
A crazed Usher fan had to be escorted out of a taping of The Voice for disrupting the show to get Usher's attention...<br />
<br />
<a name='more'></a>From <a href="http://www.nypost.com/p/pagesix/voice_cracks_for_usher_fan_4aFBbY4bzAbUVhY2cJyOZO%20" style="color: red;">Page Six </a><br />
<blockquote>
An overzealous female fan of R&amp;B singer Usher was tossed out of a taping of NBC&#8217;s &#8220;The Voice&#8221; Thursday afternoon after she repeatedly yelled out to the superstar from the audience to get his attention.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
The unidentified woman also repeatedly &#8220;blew a whistle&#8221; during taping to distract Usher, who is a judge on the hit singing-competition show along withShakira, Adam Levine and Blake Shelton.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
According to sources close to the show, the 20-something audience member blew her whistle for about a minute during filming at NBC studios in Burbank, Calif.</blockquote>
<blockquote>
We&#8217;re told that only the show&#8217;s celebrity judges were on stage during the incident, sitting in their oversize red rotating judges chairs.</blockquote>
