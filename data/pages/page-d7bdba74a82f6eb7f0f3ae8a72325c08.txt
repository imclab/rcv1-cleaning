					<p><strong>Welcome to NorCal Car Culture!</strong></p>
<p>We hope to be an important source of information for people in northern California who love classic cars, hot rods, trucks, motorcycles and all kinds of racing.</p>
<div id="attachment_1055" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 307px"><a href="http://www.norcalcarculture.com/about/my-1953-chevy-pickup/"  rel="attachment wp-att-1055"><img src="http://www.norcalcarculture.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/00000014-297x300.jpg" alt="" title="My 1953 Chevy Pickup" width="297" height="300" class="size-medium wp-image-1055" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">My first car: a 1953 Chevy Pickup.</p></div>
<p>We cover Northern California car shows, swap meets, auto racing, and classic car auctions. We review auto related books, products, websites and businesses. We have articles on people from Northern California that love cars, that race, or just work in the automotive industry. We post photos of the cool cars, trucks and motorcycles we see, as well as event posters and automobilia. We maintain a directory of car clubs, race tracks, racing associations, local drivers, and northern California auto businesses.</p>
<p>Any ideas or suggestions for new items to cover or post are appreciated. Please feel free to email us with information on your car club, business, event or driver web page.</p>
<p>We hope you enjoy our website and return often.</p>
