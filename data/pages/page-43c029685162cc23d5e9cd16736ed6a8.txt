<h2>Savannah Real Estate Owners Delight in Vibrant Arts and Entertainment Scene</h2>-->

<p><img src="/media/galleries/savannah+ga/area+info/lifestyle+articles/savannah+arts+entertainment/scadssidewalkartfestival.jpg" alt="Savannah Art and Entertainment" title="Savannah Art and Entertainment" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="400" height="300" align="top" /><br />Savannah real estate owners enjoy a vibrant arts and entertainment scene and the city&#39;s many cultural offerings through its museums, art centers, theaters, galleries and festivals. <br /><br /><a href="http://www.telfair.org/" target="_blank" title="Savannah Telfair Museum of Art">The Telfair Museum of Art </a>is located on historic Barnard Street in downtown Savannah, Georgia. Three buildings make up the Telfair, two of which are National Historic Landmarks. The buildings are the historic Telfair Academy of Arts and Sciences, the historic Owens-Thomas House, and the contemporary Jepson Center, which opened to the public in March 2006.  Visitors to the Telfair enjoy educational resources, gallery exhibits, sculpture gardens and more.<br /><br />Savannah, Georgia&#39;s extensive list of museums include the <a href="http://www.mercerhouse.com/home.htm" target="_blank" title="Savannah Mercer Williams House Museum">Mercer Williams House Museum</a> (songwriter Johnny Mercer), Girl Scouts founder Juliette Gordon Low House, the Ralph Mark Gilbert Civil Rights Museum, Roundhouse Railroad Museum, the Ships of the Sea Maritime Museum and the <a href="http://www.mightyeighth.org/word/index.php" target="_blank" title="Savannah Mighty Eight Air Force Museum">Mighty Eighth Air Force Museum</a> in neighboring Pooler.<br /><br /><img src="/media/galleries/savannah+ga/area+info/lifestyle+articles/savannah+arts+entertainment/_list/bcms1155.jpg" alt="Savannah Art Opening" title="Savannah Art Opening" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="224" height="148" align="left" />Savannah, GA, is home to the renowned <a href="http://www.scad.edu/" target="_blank" title="Savannah College of Art and Design">Savannah College of Art and Design</a> (SCAD), and therefore has a bevy of galleries and art venues.  The college hosts exhibits throughout the year in various locations within its urban campus. City Market, located in the Historic District is home to the City Market Art Center, and is considered an artist&rsquo;s haven with galleries and studios featuring paintings, carvings, handmade jewelry, fine art and stained glass. <br /><br />There is something for every Savannah art enthusiast&rsquo;s taste.  Performing arts enthusiasts enjoy the <a href="http://www.savannahtheatre.com/" target="_blank" title="Savannah Theater">Savannah Theater</a>, Savannah Actor&rsquo;s Theater, the History Theater, Savannah Community Theatre, SCAD Theater, and the Savannah Children&rsquo;s Theater. <br /><br />Savannah is home to the Savannah Film Festival, a weeklong annual event that began in 1998. The Savannah College of Art and Design hosts the festival that features independent clips and full-length films from both professional and student filmmakers: truly a favorite among locals and owners of Savannah real estate. <br /><br />The <a href="http://www.savannahmusicfestival.org/" target="_blank" title="Savannah Music Festival">Savannah Music Festival</a> is held for nearly three weeks each year from late March to early April, offering educational programs, dance, film and narrative performances. <br /><br />The downtown lunch crowd enjoys the Summer Concerts in the Squares, a weeklong end-of-July celebration of classical, jazz and American standards, performed live beneath a canopy of live oaks in several of the city&rsquo;s 22 historic squares downtown. Other live music events are held regularly in City Market and in several of the local Savannah restaurants. Some favorite venues for Savannah real estate owners are Tubby&rsquo;s Tankhouse on River Street, North Beach Grill on Tybee Island, and downtown at the Sentient Bean&rsquo;s open mic night and local band events. <br /><br /><img src="/media/galleries/savannah+ga/area+info/lifestyle+articles/savannah+arts+entertainment/_list/thesavannahtheatresbroadway.jpg" alt="Savannah Entertainment and Theater" title="Savannah Entertainment and Theater" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="224" height="168" align="right" />One cannot consider Savannah, Georgia, entertainment without mentioning the <a href="http://savannahsaintpatricksday.com/" target="_blank" title="Savannah St. Patrick's Day Parade">Savannah&rsquo;s St. Patrick&rsquo;s Day celebration</a>.  Savannah&rsquo;s St. Patrick&rsquo;s Day party is the second largest event of its kind in the country. The parade marked its 183rd year in 2007, with 350 entries, 50 bands and an estimated 700,000 people attending.<br /><br />Other annual events among the Savannah, Georgia, entertainment scene include Oktoberfest on River Street, the Savannah Folk Music Society&rsquo;s Folkfest in City Market, Savannah Blues Festival and stage shows such as Annie, Cats and Hairspray at the Savannah Civic Center <br /><br />The Jewish Food Festival, Savannah Greek Festival, NOGS (North of Gaston Street) Tour of Homes and Gardens, First Saturdays on River Street are other highly anticipated annual events in Savannah. Locals and owners of Savannah real estate also enjoy the Tybee Island Beach Bum Parade, a community water fight and parade.  //</p>
