            <div class="c11text">
              <p>Who  wouldn&#8217;t want the power to shop like a cash buyer for a good used car?</p>
              <p>Whether  you&#8217;re planning to buy a  used car at a dealership, online or from a private  party, you can shop like a cash buyer with used car loans from Wells Fargo.</p>
              <h2>Alternatives to dealer auto loans</h2>
              <p>Even though a dealer can line up used car financing for you, it&#8217;s still a good idea to secure your own car finance option before you&#8217;re ready to buy a used car.</p>
              <p><a href="/car-loan-application/main/begin.do?statea=usedcarloan3">Apply for a Wells Fargo auto loan today!</a></p>
              <h2>Online or private party purchases</h2>
              <p>Used car financing is a little different when you make the purchase from a neighbor, online or through the classifieds. Your car finance 
                options are to pay cash or line up your own used car loan.</p>
              <p>Before talking with the seller, visit your bank, credit union or apply online for a used car loan. That way you can secure the <a href="used-car-financing.html">used car financing</a> before committing to the seller.</p>
              <p><a href="/car-loan-application/main/begin.do?statea=usedcarloan4">Complete the Wells Fargo car finance application online!</a></p>
              <h2>Flexible opportunities for used auto loans</h2>
              <p>You may already know what vehicle to buy, or maybe you want to know what you can spend first.</p>
              <p>Either way, our used car finance opportunities are flexible enough to give you a pre-qualified amount<sup>1</sup> before you start looking. Or, Wells Fargo may be able to help you line up your used auto loan right away if you&#8217;re ready to buy a used car now.</p>
              <h2>Already know what car to buy?</h2>
              <p>If you&#8217;ve already decided on buying a used  car and know what it costs, Wells Fargo may be able to help you with your used auto loan.</p>
              <table cellspacing="0" class="c21" summary="this is the table with headers">

                </tr>
                <tr class="c21group h11">
                  <td ><p>If you&#8217;ve found the vehicle you want and you&#8217;re ready to buy, we can help you line up financing in just four easy steps.</p>
                    <p><a href="https://www.wellsfargo.com/autoloans/resources/autofinancing">Used auto financing tips</a></p>
                    <ol>
                      <li> <strong>Complete our short online loan application</strong><br />
                        Applying  takes just a few minutes, there&#8217;s no application fee and no obligation! </li>
                      <li><strong>Receive your credit decision</strong><br />
                        Most  customers receive their credit decision immediately.<sup>2</sup> </li>
                      <li><strong>Sign the paperwork</strong><br />
                        Once  approved, you can close your used car loan at a local Wells Fargo store where  you&#8217;ll get a check for the price of the vehicle you&#8217;ve chosen. </li>
                      <li> <strong>Buy a used car with confidence</strong><br />
                        With approved financing in hand, you can buy your used car with the feel of a cash buyer. </li>
                    </ol>
                    <h2><strong>Used car loans from Wells Fargo offer these benefits:</strong></h2>
                    <ul>
                      <li>Easy online loan application</li>
                      <li>Qualification to shop like a cash buyer</li>
                      <li>Fast credit decisions and loan processing</li>
                      <li>Competitive interest rates</li>
                      <li>Discounts if you&#8217;re a Wells Fargo customer and/or you make automatic payments<sup>3</sup></li>
                      <li>More!</li>
                    </ul></td>
                </tr>
              </table>
              <h2>Not sure what car you want or how much you qualify for?</h2>
              <p>If you&#8217;re not sure what vehicle you&#8217;re looking for, that&#8217;s ok. Wells Fargo can pre-qualify you for a used auto loan and tell 
                you how much you qualify for before you start shopping.</p>

                </tr>
                <tr class="c21group h11">
                  <td ><p>Knowing that your financing is already approved and how much you can spend lets you  shop for a vehicle with confidence.</p>
                    <ol>
                      <li> <strong><a href="/car-loan-application/main/begin.do?statea=usedcarloan5">Complete our short online used car application</a></strong><br />
                        Applying for a used car loan takes just a few minutes, there&#8217;s no application fee and no  obligation! </li>
                      <li> <strong>Receive your credit decision</strong><br />
                        Most customers receive their credit decision immediately.<sup>2</sup> </li>
                      <li> <strong>Find a vehicle within your budget</strong><br />
                        Knowing the loan amount you&#8217;re approved for helps you narrow down  your search and stay within your budget 
                        when you negotiate the final price. </li>
                      <li> <strong>Finalize the used car loan</strong><br />
                        After you&#8217;ve chosen your vehicle, visit your local Wells Fargo store to close your used car loan. 
                        We&#8217;ll finish up the paperwork and pay the seller for your car. </li>
                    </ol>
                    <h2><strong>A pre-qualified used car loan from Wells Fargo offers:</strong></h2>
                    <ul>
                      <li>Easy online loan application</li>
                      <li>Fast credit decisions &amp; loan processing</li>
                      <li>Competitive  interest rates</li>
                      <li>Time to find the right vehicle&#8230;loan approval good for 30 days</li>
                      <li>Qualification to shop like a cash buyer</li>
                      <li>Personal help to complete paperwork and finalize the purchase when you close your used  car loan at the nearest Wells Fargo store</li>
                      <li>More!</li>
                    </ul>
