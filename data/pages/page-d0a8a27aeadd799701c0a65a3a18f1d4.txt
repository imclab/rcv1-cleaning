<h1><a href="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/white-spacer.jpg" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-99" title="white-spacer" src="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/white-spacer.jpg" alt="white spacer" width="400" height="27" /></a>&#8220;Discover Why People Rave About Forest Hill Auto Service”</h1>
<p>Could it be that they know what they are doing?</p>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://auto-repair-md.com/contact-forest-hill-car-care/" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-83" title="auto-repair-review-image2a" src="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/auto-repair-review-image2a.jpg" alt="auto repair review md" width="388" height="201" /></a></p>
<p>&#8220;Dave and Bob have been working on my cars for <strong>over thirteen years</strong>. They are <strong>honest and trustworthy</strong>. I would <strong>recommend them</strong> to all my friends.&#8221; &#8211; John Pullara</p></blockquote>
<p>At some point in your life you&#8217;ve probably had a bad experience with an auto repair service. It&#8217;s sad, but most of us have experienced it. Since most people don&#8217;t know a lot about car repairs I guess that makes it easy for other shops to take advantage of people. The good news is that you do have a choice&#8230;</p>
<p><strong>A good repair shop knows that the most important part of their business is loyal, repeat customers. Our goal is to provide you with such great service that you become one of our many loyal customers.</strong></p>
<p>As a customer you deserve great service at a fair price.  You don&#8217;t want to wonder if the auto repair shop is charging you for work you don&#8217;t really need. You don&#8217;t want to feel like they&#8217;re just guessing at what&#8217;s wrong with your vehicle. You don&#8217;t want to be quoted one price and charged another for &#8220;unexpected repairs&#8221;&#8230;</p>
<p>Is it possible that they may find something that wasn&#8217;t expected? Yes, once in a while it does happen. However a good auto service center should be able to give you an accurate account of what to expect.</p>
<p><strong>So where do you turn to and who can you trust?</strong></p>
<p>Let Forest Hill Auto Service Center be your first choice… You can expect the best customer service because our reputation is always on the line…</p>
<p>If you car has a problem we&#8217;ll take care of it like it’s our own. We know that you have to rely on your car to get around.</p>
<p>By bringing your car in for regular oil changes and check ups like your brakes, tires, hoses, belts, and fluids &#8211; your car will last you a lot longer and provide you better gas mileage.</p>
<h2>80 Years of Auto Repair Experience At Your Service&#8230;</h2>
<p>Bob and Dave (the owners) have a combined 80 years of auto service experience. They&#8217;ve run Overlea Car Care for 17 years and you can now get that <strong>great service right here in Forest Hill, Maryland</strong>. Forest Hill Car Care is now open and able to help you with all your car care and repair needs.</p>
<h3>Not many auto repair centers can show you proof of how happy and loyal their customers are&#8230;</h3>
<blockquote>
<h3><a href="http://auto-repair-md.com/contact-forest-hill-car-care/" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-89" title="auto-repair-review-image1a" src="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/auto-repair-review-image1a.jpg" alt="auto repair review maryland" width="400" height="167" /></a></h3>
<p>Overlea Auto Repair can be described as &#8220;In &amp; Out Service&#8221; repair shop. My vehicle can be serviced in the morning <strong>without any appointment</strong>, for <strong>any type of service</strong>, and will be out by noon. My employment is directly across the street from Overlea Auto Repair and I can spy on the mechanics working on my vehicle. I have <strong>trusted the same mechanics</strong> for the last to (2) years with my vehicles from an <strong>oil change to replacing an axle</strong>. &#8211; Tracy Creek</p></blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://auto-repair-md.com/contact-forest-hill-car-care/" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-92" title="auto-repair-review-image3a" src="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/auto-repair-review-image3a.jpg" alt="auto repair review maryland" width="370" height="336" /></a></p>
<p>My name is Robert Chard. I and my family have been taking our vehicles to Overlea Auto Service Center since April 2003. Our cars have always gotten <strong>very good care at reasonable costs</strong>. They not only take care of the problem you have it there for, the also, while doing your initial problem take time to check to make sure you don&#8217;t have any other problems, such as lights that don&#8217;t work and necessary fluids that are low, tire air pressure and even wiper blades.</p>
<p><strong>They are good mechanics with good work ethics and are totally honest</strong>. I recommend them without any reservations. &#8211; Robert Chard</p></blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://auto-repair-md.com/contact-forest-hill-car-care/" ><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-95" title="auto-repair-review-image4a" src="http://auto-repair-md.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/auto-repair-review-image4a.jpg" alt="auto repair review maryland" width="392" height="155" /></a></p>
<p>My experience with Overlea Auto started one Saturday morning years ago when I stopped by and asked for an oil change service. They <strong>pulled my car in right away and took care of me</strong>. It was always difficult for me to find a service station where you would get treated honestly. Well I found one in Overlea Auto. The owners, Bob and Dave, have been servicing all my cars in my family since that first Saturday 5 or 6 years ago. I&#8217;m so glad that I stopped there that day.</p>
<p>Everyone there is <strong>friendly</strong> and above all, they do <strong>excellent work at a fair price</strong>. I hope to be taking my cars there for a long time. I have been <strong>recommending them to anyone who needs good honest mechanics</strong>. Thanks Overlea Auto for all you do for the Loewe family. &#8211; Gary Loewe</p></blockquote>
<p>If you&#8217;re looking for auto repair service you can trust, good work ethics, honest mechanics and excellent car care at a fair price&#8230;</p>
