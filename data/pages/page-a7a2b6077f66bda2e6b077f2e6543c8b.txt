			<div id="tabs-3" class="hometabcontent">
	
				 <h3>Architecture</h3>
				 <p>Home to such architectural marvels as Le Corbusier's <a
				href="http://www.ves.fas.harvard.edu/ccva.html">Carpenter Center</a> and a <a
				href="http://www.gsd.harvard.edu/">Graduate School of Design</a> that has produced many influential architects of the last century, Harvard has had a long conversation with the built environment. The conversation continues with
				courses in the <a href="http://haa.fas.harvard.edu/">Department of History of Art and Architecture</a> and increasing connections between students in the
				GSD, the College, and across the University.</p>

			<div id="tabs-6" class="hometabcontent">
	
				 <h3>Film</h3>
				<p>Home to the renowned <a href="http://hcl.harvard.edu/hfa/">Harvard Film Archive</a>, the <a href="http://www.filmstudycenter.org/">Film Study Center</a>, and the curricular offerings of the <a href="http://www.ves.fas.harvard.edu/">Department of Visual and Environmental
				Studies</a>, Harvard is committed to film in all its variations, from its
				earliest flickers to cutting-edge documentaries.</p>

<!-- MUSIC -->
			<div id="tabs-7" class="hometabcontent">
	
				 <h3>Music</h3>
				<p>With legendary professors, incomparable visiting artists, and students who have changed the landscape of music, Harvard offers both an extraordinary curricular program in music and musicology through the <a
				href="http://www.fas.harvard.edu/~musicdpt/">Department of Music</a>, and a varied extracurricular music scene, with countless <a
				href="http://ofa.fas.harvard.edu/groups/index.php?category=Music">student groups</a> performing throughout the year.</p>
				 
				 <p><a href="events_trumba.php?category=6">View a list of music events</a> ></p>

<!-- THEATER -->
			<div id="tabs-8" class="hometabcontent">
	
				 <h3>Theater</h3>
				<p>Whether it is in the professional productions of the <a
				href="http://www.amrep.org/">American Repertory Theater</a>'s mainstage, the innovative performances led by <a href="http://ofa.fas.harvard.edu/groups/index.php?category=theater">student theater groups</a> in black box spaces, the exhaustive archive of the performing arts held in the <a href="http://hcl.harvard.edu/libraries/houghton/collections/htc/index.cfm">Harvard Theatre Collection</a>, or the exciting courses offered by the <a href="http://webdocs.registrar.fas.harvard.edu/ugrad_handbook/current/chapter4/drama.html">Committee on Dramatics</a>, the life of the theater thrives at Harvard.</p>
				 				 
				 <p><a href="events_trumba.php?category=7">View a list of theater events</a> ></p>

<!-- VISUAL ARTS -->
			<div id="tabs-9" class="hometabcontent" style="background:#dda700;">
	
				 <h3>Visual Arts</h3>
				<p>The visual arts are found in abundance on Harvard's campus. The practice of the graphical, sculptural, digital, video, and mixed media arts thrive in the studios of Harvard's <a href="http://www.ves.fas.harvard.edu/">Department of Visual and Environmental Studies</a>. A powerful tradition of art history, theory and criticism continues in the <a href="http://haa.fas.harvard.edu/">Department of the History of Art and Architecture</a>. <a href="http://ofa.fas.harvard.edu/groups/index.php?category=visual">Student-led groups</a>, visiting artists, gallery spaces, and a passionate community of artists all contribute to the dynamic culture of visual arts found on campus.</p>
				 
				 <p><a href="events_trumba.php?category=8">View a list of visual arts events</a> ></p>
