				    <h3>
										<a href="http://www.asheville.com/cgi-bin/click.cgi?id=flatrockplayhouse&url=http://www.flatrockplayhouse.org/?reflink=ashevillecom" target="_blank">Flat Rock Playhouse</a>				    </h3>

				    <p>Flat Rock, NC<br />
The State Theatre of North Carolina, and one of the finest local playhouses in the United States. Highly acclaimed throughout the theater world. The Playhouse’s dual mission of producing the performing arts and providing education in the performing arts includes a ten-show professional series; a summer and fall college apprentice and intern program; performances and cabaret series by the YouTheatre; year-round classes and workshops for students from kindergarten through adults. Flat Rock Playhouse now hosts over 90,000 patrons annually and is a major contributor to the local economy and the Arts in North Carolina.</p>
