<h1>Certified Pre-Owned FAQ</h1>
<ul class="faq-menu">

<div class="faq-container">
<div data-cid="C1" class="faq-category-content">
<ul>
<li data-question="Q1">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Is every pre-owned Lincoln a Certified Pre-Owned Lincoln?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
The fact is, not every Lincoln vehicle can make the cut as a Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned vehicle. Only after an exhaustive selection process do we certify it.
<div class="disclaimer"></div>
</div>
</li>
<li data-question="Q2">
<div class="faq-question">
<span class="action-button"></span>
<p>Why should I consider a Lincoln Certified Pre-Owned luxury vehicle?</p>
</div>
<div class="faq-answer">
Three words – peace of mind. Before your vehicle is delivered, it receives a unique 200-point inspection.
