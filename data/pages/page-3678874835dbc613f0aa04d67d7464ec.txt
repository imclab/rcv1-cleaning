<h1>China Car Culture: fooling the seat-belt reminder systems</h1>
<p class="date">Published on March 14, 2013 by Tycho de Feijter</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-1.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40523" title="China car culture" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-1-458x273.jpg" width="458" height="273" /></a></p>
<p>Chinese drivers hate to wear their safety belts. Instead, they wear <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/03/02/fooling-the-police-with-a-safety-belt-t-shirt-in-china/" target="_blank">specially designed clothing</a> to <em>pretend</em> they are buckled up. But that won&#8217;t stop the seat-belt reminder lights and beeps, which are all extremely annoying.</p>
<p>It is possible to click the belt in the buckle behind your back but that is uncomfortable. It is also possible to fiddle with the electronics but that is difficult. Creative and innovative Chinese companies finally found an easy solution.</p>
<p><span id="more-40519"></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-2.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40524" title="Seat-belt heas China" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-2-458x295.jpg" width="458" height="295" /></a></p>
<p>Belt heads without belts! Fit in every buckle. No beeping. No flashing. Good to go. Mentioned Chinese innovators sell zillions of different kinds, all priced between 3 and 15 yuan, or between 48 cent and 2.4 USD. One Chinese seller neatly explains the problem:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-2a.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40526" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-2a-458x612.jpg" width="458" height="612" /></a></p>
<p>&#8216;Ding ding!&#8217; Aaaah! I checked a few online stores. My favorites:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-3.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40527" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-3-458x198.jpg" width="458" height="198" /></a></p>
<p>Cartoons. Fun daddy, <em>and</em> for the children.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-4.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40528" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-4-458x336.jpg" width="458" height="336" /></a></p>
<p>Laughing about seat belts.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-5.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40529" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-5-458x402.jpg" width="458" height="402" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/tag/bling/" target="_blank">Bling</a>! Modeled after products from the crazy Japanese <a href="http://www.garson.co.jp/" target="_blank">tuning company DAD</a>.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-6.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40530" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-6-458x303.jpg" width="458" height="303" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-7.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40531" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-7.jpg" width="457" height="452" /></a></p>
<p>No beeps and it opens your <del>beer bottle</del> cola bottle.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-7a.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40533" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-7a-458x268.jpg" width="458" height="268" /></a></p>
<p>Such a small thing, and still so useful.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-8.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40532" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-8-458x305.jpg" width="458" height="305" /></a></p>
<p>Every brand available, no matter what you drive. Only 5.5 yuan, so you can take a different car every day. Today, I drive a Buick:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-9.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-40525" alt="Fooling the seat-belt reminder in China" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/china-safety-belt-9-458x524.jpg" width="458" height="524" /></a></p>
<p>Click! Buckled up I am.</p>
