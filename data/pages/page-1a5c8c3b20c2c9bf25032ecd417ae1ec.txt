    <h2 class="content_title">At your service...</h2>
    <img alt="Seatco Services" id="about_us_img" src="./images/seatco_129.jpg">
    <p>Seatco provides <b>high-quality automotive trim repair, restoration, and installation services</b> - at a fair price.</p>
    <p>We work on cars, trucks, motorcycles, RVs, boats, limousines, fleet vehicles, and more.</p>
    <p>Need a new convertible top?</p>
    <p>Our trim and restoration services include repair or replacement of <b>upholstery, carpets, dashboards, soft tops, convertible tops, sunroofs</b> (and moon roofs).  We'll fix or replace any <b>fabric, vinyl, plastic windows, glass windows, frames, top motors, latches, straps</b>, or any other related mechanism.  We can fix or replace <b>headliners</b> and make <b>leather interior upgrades</b>.</p>
    <p><b>Need something else?</b>  All told, Seatco can service nearly <em>any interior auto trim need</em>.</p> 
    <p>Seatco offers full, factory-original restorations for your vintage auto's trim.  If needed, we'll <b>fabricate unavailable parts</b>, painstakingly duplicating the original through old photos, drawings, and other research methods.  Our auto-body partners work with us for complete, end-to-end restorations.</p>
    <p>If custom is your style, we've worked with NFL athletes, singers, and other money-is-no-object owners to create <b>custom car interiors</b> like no other.</p>
    <p>Seatco regularly services <b>fleet vehicle</b> tops and interiors and offers volume-discount rates for this type of work.</p>
