<div class="itemtext">
<p>LA Galaxy fans are certainly making their disappointment clear about the injury keeping <strong>David Beckham</strong> off the pitch. They held up &#8220;Wanted&#8221; posters of the star striker scrawled with &#8220;missing&#8221;, &#8220;too scared&#8221; and &#8220;I lost.&#8221;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a rel="attachment wp-att-201587" href="http://anythinghollywood.com/2007/08/david-beckhams-fans-are-disappointed/beckhamfan_upset_1/"><img class="size-full wp-image-201587 aligncenter" title="beckhamfan_upset_1" src="http://anythinghollywood.com/wp-content/2007/08/beckhamfan_upset_1.jpg" alt="beckhamfan upset 1" width="400" height="365" /></a></p>
<blockquote><p>Other banners like &#8221;Bench it like <strong>Beckham</strong>&#8220;, <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/pages/live/articles/showbiz/showbiznews.html?in_article_id=472336&amp;in_page_id=1773" target="_blank">referring to</a> <strong>Beckham</strong> on the sidelines.
