 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1681" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA AWARDS UK 2013 CELEBRATION IN 2 DAYS EXTRAVAGANZA">BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA AWARDS UK 2013 CELEBRATION IN 2 DAYS EXTRAVAGANZA</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Aug 5th</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>
   </div>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1682" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 514px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/BEFFTA-AWARDS-2013-25TH-AND-26TH-OCTOBER.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-1682" title="BEFFTA AWARDS 2013 - 25TH AND 26TH OCTOBER" src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/BEFFTA-AWARDS-2013-25TH-AND-26TH-OCTOBER.png" alt="BEFFTA AWARDS 2013 - 25TH AND 26TH OCTOBER " width="504" height="720" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA AWARDS 2013 - 25TH AND 26TH OCTOBER </p></div>
<p>Europe&#8217;s biggest award ceremony for black and ethnic personalities n entertainment, film, fashion, television and arts (BEFFTA AWARDS) will be celebrating their 5th year anniversary in style over two days in London. The invite only awards ceremony is expected to attract over 7000 guests from Europe, USA, Africa, Asia and the UK.</p>
<p>BEFFTA awards UK 2013 is officially on 25th and 26th of October. The glamorous awards ceremony honours and celebrates musicians, comedians, actors, actresses, djs, models, fashion designers, film directors, music producers, authors, dancers, choreographers, promoters, TV presenter, radio personalities, radio stations and more.</p>
<p>The awards ceremony founder Pauline Long said,  &#8220;the team is working hard behind the scenes to ensure a truly talented community is celebrated in a magnificent way. Many of these talented personalities put in a lot of hard work in their chosen industries and it is on fair that they are appreciated.&#8221;</p>
<p>With a record breaking BEFFTA awards UK public nomination just recently concluded on 29th July the team is expected to announce the final nomination list this month.</p>
<p>To get involved with BEFFTA awards UK as an award category sponsor or volunteer or red carpet presenter please contact the team at info@beffta.com</p>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1661" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA AWARDS UK PUBLIC NOMINATIONS FOR THE GRAND 5TH YEAR ANNIVERSARY IS OFFICIALLY OPEN">BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA AWARDS UK PUBLIC NOMINATIONS FOR THE GRAND 5TH YEAR ANNIVERSARY IS OFFICIALLY OPEN</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Jun 3rd</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>
   </div>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1662" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 617px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/beffta-full-11.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-1662 " title="BEFFAT UK 5TH YEAR GRAND ANNIVERSARY - PUBLIC NOMINATIONS 2013 " src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/beffta-full-11.jpg" alt="BEFFTA UK 2013 5th year grand anniversary email info@beffta.com " width="607" height="432" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA UK 5TH YEAR GRAND ANNIVERSARY - PUBLIC NOMINATIONS 2013</p></div>
<p>Europe&#8217;s biggest award ceremony celebrating black and ethnic personalities in entertainment, film, fashion, television and arts has officially opened it&#8217;s grand 5th year anniversary public nominations.  BEFFTA awards, founded by entrepreneur, TV presenter and philanthropist Pauline Long in 2009 is back for the 5th year to celebrate greatness within the African and Caribbean community.</p>
<p>The 5th year BEFFTA UK anniversary is expected to be celebrated in a very grand style bringing together several international and UK stars under one roof with a common goal to celebrate and honour both unknown and known hard working and extremely talented personalities in entertainment, film, fashion, television and arts.</p>
<p>With legendary personality Sir Trevor McDonald celebrated last year at BEFFTA UK 2012 with a lifetime achievement award, BEFFTA awards UK team looks forward to celebrating yet another deserving legend who will be revealed soon.</p>
<div id="attachment_1664" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 620px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/33867_371640492913907_1441300216_n.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-1664 " title="Sir Trevor McDonald receives BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD with a standing ovation at the prestigious BEFFTA UK 2012" src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/33867_371640492913907_1441300216_n.jpg" alt="Sir Trevor McDonald receives BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD with a standing ovation at the prestigious BEFFTA UK 2012" width="610" height="406" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Sir Trevor McDonald receives BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD with a standing ovation at the prestigious BEFFTA UK 2012</p></div>

<p>The unique global awards ceremony BEFFTA(Black Entertainment Film Fashion Television and arts awards)  founded by UK based entrepreneur and philanthropist Pauline Long is officially launching in Jamaica on 9th February. BEFFTA celebrates and honours African and Caribbean personalities in UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Africa and the Caribbean in entertainment, film, fashion, television and arts. The BEFFTA Caribbean press launch in Jamaica will be held at the exclusive South Beach Cafe in Kingston.</p>
<p>Amongst high profile personalities in entertainment expected to attend the launch are Freddie McGreggor, Oliver Samuel, Abbyssinians and many more. Sean Paul who won Best International Caribbean act at the UK BEFFTA 2012 will be presented with his award at the launch.</p>
<p>BEFFTA Caribbean in Jamaica launch will be hosted by BEFFTA international manager Verona White and Miss Jamaica UK Gemma Feare.</p>
<p>Artist Nature is expected to entertain guests at the launch.</p>
<p>BEFFTA Caribbean aims to promote and honour unsung talented artists and personalities in film, fashion, television and arts but most importantly celebrate both unknown and known personalities under one roof.</p>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1639" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA FILM ICON BEST AWARD I HAVE RECEIVED ON A GLOBAL LEVEL, SAYS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE">BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA FILM ICON BEST AWARD I HAVE RECEIVED ON A GLOBAL LEVEL, SAYS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Nov 15th</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>
   </div>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1645" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 495px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/omo101.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-1645" title="BEFFTA FILM ICON OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE " src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/omo101.jpg" alt="BEFFTA FILM ICON OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE " width="485" height="676" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA FILM ICON OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE </p></div>
<p>Top international movie star Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde  picked her BEFFTA FILM ICON award at a private presentation at London&#8217;s  leading film studios East End Studios. Speaking to the press in London, the star expressed her gratitude for being honoured with such a high profile award.</p>
<div>Omotola Jalade-Ekeinde has become the  first movie star to be awarded with the special BEFFTA honorary award in  the film category, other high profile personalities who received BEFFTA  special award this year include Sir Trevor McDonald OBE recipient of  BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT award.</div>
<div>The humanitarian and Amnesty  international activist said, &#8220;this is the best award I have received on a  global level, it is such an honour and I&#8217;m truly humbled that 16 years  of my work in the film industry has been rewarded in a special way  internationally. There are so many people to thank for bringing me to   this level, special thanks goes to my husband for always being there,  everyone that I have worked with but most importantly my fans. I love my  fans so much, they are very supportive&#8221;.</div>
<div>BEFFTA founder Pauline Long commended Omotola for her  contribution to the film industry but also for her great charitable work.</div>
<div>The BEFFTA FILM ICON award was  presented to Omotola by PR guru Theodora Ibekwe in the presence of  London press and invited guests.</div>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1582" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA 2012 WINNERS ANNOUNCED AT A STAR-STUDDED AWARDS CEREMONY">BREAKING NEWS: BEFFTA 2012 WINNERS ANNOUNCED AT A STAR-STUDDED AWARDS CEREMONY</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Oct 28th</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1596" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 501px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Sir-trevor-and-plong.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1596  " title="BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD-WINNER SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE AND BEFFTA FOUNDER PAULINE LONG" src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/Sir-trevor-and-plong-1024x893.jpg" alt="BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD-WINNER SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE AND BEFFTA FOUNDER PAULINE LONG" width="491" height="428" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD-WINNER SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE AND BEFFTA FOUNDER PAULINE LONG</p></div>
<p>Europe&#8217;s biggest award ceremony for black and ethnic personalities in showbiz BEFFTA celebrated personalities at an elegant event. The prestigious 4th annual BEFFTA awards UK took place in London on 27th October at  a glittering red carpet  award ceremony attended by personalities in entertainment, film, fashion, television and Arts. Among the stars on the night were Sir Trevor McDonald,  Lemar, Catwalk professor, footballer Fabrice Muamba, singer and xfactor star Starboy Nathan and more.</p>
<p>Sir Trevor McDonald OBE  received a standing ovation from the 3000 audience as he received the BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD. Lorna Sutara Gayle picked up BEFFTA ENTERTAINMENT ICON AWARD while young singer/songwriter straight from the Brit School was honoured with BEFFTA YOUNG ACHIEVER AWARD.</p>
<p>&#8220;I&#8217;m extremely happy to see BEFFTA fulfilling it&#8217;s purpose and the  purpose being honouring and celebrating a truly talented and  hard-working community. People came together to network and appreciate  each other&#8217;s accomplishments. I&#8217;m so grateful for everyone that  attended, at BEFFTA awards everyone is special. We honoured a legend,  Sir Trevor McDonald with a standing ovation. We honoured unsung talent  and as always we will continue to appreciate everyone that contributes to entertainment,  film, fashion, television and arts awards. We look forward to yet  another special BEFFTA 2013 which will be our grand 5th year anniversary inLondon&#8221;, said BEFFTA founder Pauline Long.</p>
<p>BEFFTA awards is a special inclusive award ceremony that is committed to celebrating both unknown and known under one roof.  It is an award ceremony that aims to inspire the community to achieve at the highest level.  In the meantime BEFFTA Caribbean, Africa, Canada and USA are under way.</p>
<p>Winners of BEFFTA awards UK  include film director Obi Emelonye, musician Lady Leshurr and many more.</p>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1570" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: ACTRESS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE HONOURED AS BEFFTA FILM ICON">BREAKING NEWS: ACTRESS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE HONOURED AS BEFFTA FILM ICON</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Oct 25th</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>
   </div>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1578" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 359px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/photoomo.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-1578" title="BEFFTA FILM ICON: ACTRESS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE " src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/photoomo.jpg" alt="BEFFTA FILM ICON: ACTRESS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE " width="349" height="640" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA FILM ICON: ACTRESS OMOTOLA JALADE EKEINDE </p></div>
<p>The multiple award-winning actress Omotola Jalade Ekeinde has been recognised with a special honorary award BEFFTA FILM ICON for her contribution to film industry in Africa, Europe and globally.  BEFFTA(Black Entertainment Film Fashion Television and Arts) awards is Europe&#8217;s biggest award ceremony celebrating achievements of black and ethnic personalities in showbiz globally. The Nollywood mega star who has appeared in over 300 hundred movies since starting her career at the tender age of 17 is popularly known for playing lead roles with films that smash the box office. Her recent films include Last Fight To Abuja, Amina, Ties That Bind and Ije.</p>
<p>Hailed as cinema box office queen, Omotola played lead role in the movie Ije, Africa&#8217;s biggest and highest earning movie in the cinemas to date. Omotola continues to appear on the screens globally with her latest movies Up Creek Without a Paddle and Blood on the Lagoon in post production .</p>
<p>Omotola is a World Food Programme ambassador and Amnesty international activists. The generous actress is a role model who gives back to the youth through her own charity Omotola Youth Empowerment Programme(OYEP).</p>
<p>BEFFTA founder Pauline Long praised Omotola&#8217;s great talent and commended her for being a mentor to the young ones aspiring to get into the film industry.</p>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1561" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: DJ ABRANTEE RECEIVES SPECIAL HONORARY BEFFTA EXCELLENCE AWARD">BREAKING NEWS: DJ ABRANTEE RECEIVES SPECIAL HONORARY BEFFTA EXCELLENCE AWARD</a></h2>

 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>

<p>The triple award-winning club, television and radio Dj Abrantee  popularly known for his drive time radio show on Choice FM has been  honoured with a special honorary award the BEFFTA EXCELLENCE AWARD for  his wonderful and endless contribution to urban and Afrobeats music both  in UK and internationally.</p>
<p>Dj Abrantee has supported several  artists actively including some of the world famous artists Jay -Z,  Neyo, Akon, T-Pain, Wizkid, Ice Price , Psquare and more.</p>
<p>2011 was a big year for Afrobeats as Dj Abrantee toured and UK and Ireland promoting Afrobeats and Afrobeats musicians which led to the genre being recognised in the mainstream UK music scene.</p>
<p>The music promoter and Dj will receive the BEFFTA EXCELLENCE AWARD on 27th October at the prestigious 4th annual BEFFTA UK awards in the presence of an international audience.</p>

    <div id="attachment_1540" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 494px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/395168_10150433303140778_1389801014_n.jpg"><img src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/395168_10150433303140778_1389801014_n.jpg" alt="BEFFTA MEDIA ICON - PRINCESS &#039;DEUN ADEDOYIN-SOLARIN" title="BEFFTA MEDIA ICON - PRINCESS &#039;DEUN ADEDOYIN-SOLARIN" width="484" height="624" class="size-full wp-image-1540" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA MEDIA ICON - PRINCESS 'DEUN ADEDOYIN-SOLARIN</p></div>
<p>The multi-award winning veteran broadcaster and media consultant who is best known for her prime-time TV show Princess of Arize Princess &#8216;Deun Adedoyin-Solarin has been awarded the special honorary award BEFFTA Entertainment Icon. With a successful career in media and broadcasting for just over 35 years, the Nigerian born journalist, motivational speaker and media consultant started her career as a broadcaster on NBC TV in 1975.  </p>
<p>Princess Deun, an extremely respected media personality in Europe was celebrity host for the Nigerian leading talk show Morning Ride on NTA2 Channel 5 before creating her current popular TV show Princess of Arize on BEN TV SKY channel 182. Princess of Arize show is positioned as the leading innovative and indigenous media programme that continues to break new grounds. The TV show celebrates and features businesses, individuals and special events within the African and Caribbean community in the Diaspora and beyond.  </p>
<p>As personalities in showbiz prepare to walk on the red carpet in London on 27th October at the prestigious 4th annual BEFFTA UK awards,  this will be a special night as Princess Deun will be picking the BEFFTA honorary award in the presence of an international audience from USA, Canada and Africa. </p>

 <h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?p=1485" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: BREAKING NEWS: SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE HONOURED WITH BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD">BREAKING NEWS: SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE HONOURED WITH BEFFTA LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD</a></h2>

 <div class="post-date">
   <p class="day">Oct 1st</p>
 </div>

 <div class="post-info clearfix ">
  <p class="author alignleft">Posted by <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?author=1" title="Posts by BEFFTA Editor ">BEFFTA Editor</a> in <a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/?cat=1">NEWS FLASH</a>      </p>
   </div>

    <div class="post-content clearfix">
    <div id="attachment_1487" class="wp-caption alignnone" style="width: 528px"><a href="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/TrevorMcDonald460NEWS.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-1487" title="BEFFTA MALE LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD RECIPIENT SIR TREVOR MCDONALD" src="http://www.beffta.com/news/wp-content/uploads/2012/10/TrevorMcDonald460NEWS.jpg" alt="BEFFTA MALE LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD RECIPIENT SIR TREVOR MCDONALD" width="518" height="310" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">BEFFTA MALE LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD RECIPIENT SIR TREVOR MCDONALD OBE</p></div>
<p>Veteran journalist, presenter and newsreader Sir Trevor McDonald OBE has been honoured with a special honorary award BEFFTA MALE LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT award.  The multi award-winning news anchor will pick  up his special award in the presence of an international audience at the prestigious 4th annual BEFFTA awards UK taking place on 27th October in London.</p>
<p>Sir Trevor McDonald was the first male black  television news reader and  British broadcaster with the highest awards for his contribution to journalism over a period of  over 30 years.  In 1999 he was knighted for his excellent contribution to journalism.  The broadcaster who is well know for presenting News at Ten on  ITN and ITV&#8217;s current affairs flagship programme Tonight with Trevor McDonald is also know for the charitable causes that he was involved in such as Princes Trust, Nelson Mandela Foundation, The Royal National Institute of the Blind, The Royal Commonwealth Society for the deaf, Macmillan Cancer Research and Leukaemia Trust and many more.</p>
