                        <h1>Welcome to Rapco Automotive!</h1>
                        <h4 class="head_sub">Complete Auto Care, Maintenance &amp; Accessories</h4>
                        <p>Rapco Automotive is the oldest specialty auto repair shop in Delaware Valley! We’ve been providing top quality auto repair, maintenance services, truck and auto accessories, window tinting and many other automotive repair services in Upper Darby and Philadelphia since 1953 – almost 60 years in business! Our family owned and operated auto repair shop has spanned three generations, and our longevity in the industry shows our dedication to honest auto repair reliable customer care.</p>
                        <p>We have two great locations to serve you, one in Upper Darby and the other in Philadelphia. At both locations, our ASE Certified technicians utilize state-of-the-art technology to handle auto repair and maintenance services on all makes and models of foreign and domestic vehicles. At the Philadelphia location we also have vehicle accessory experts to help you with everything from window tinting with a lifetime warranty, to ladder racks, tool boxes and grille guards. In addition, our automotive repair services are guaranteed by an industry-leading 3 year/36,000 mile warranty!</p>
                        <p>Give us a call today, or <a href="schedule">easily schedule your appointment online</a>, and let our friendly team members take care of you and your vehicle! From oil changes and Pennsylvania state inspections, to 24” chrome wheels, headlight restoration and custom dual exhaust, Rapco Automotive is the name to trust!</p>
