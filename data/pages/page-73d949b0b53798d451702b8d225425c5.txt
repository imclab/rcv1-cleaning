    <p>Automotive  engineering organizations must deliver both conventional vehicles and &ldquo;green&rdquo;  or alternative-fuel vehicles that meet current and future environmental requirements  of their customers. And they must do this with smaller teams and less time  between vehicle programs.</p>
    <p>To meet the  challenge, leading engineering organizations worldwide have moved from a  paper-and-prototype design methodology to <a href="/model-based-design/">Model-Based Design</a>. </p>
    <p>Using MATLAB<sup>&reg;</sup> and  Simulink<sup>&reg;</sup>, these organizations speed up embedded controller development and  calibration, and deliver vehicles that meet market requirements for safety,  comfort, functionality, fuel economy, and vehicle performance.
    <p>
