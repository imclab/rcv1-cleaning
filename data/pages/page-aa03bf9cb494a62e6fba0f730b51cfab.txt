	<h1 class="h0 margin-bottom-quarter">2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe & Cabriolet Preview: Detroit Auto Show</h1>

	<div class="entry-meta">

			|
January 14, 2013	</div>

	<div class="auto-height">

	<div id="js_content_container" data-article-id="115011" style="display:none;">
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="1">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class coupe.jpg" class="rsImg">Why the 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet Matters:</a>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">Why the 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet Matters:</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><p>The E-Class is the worldwide butter in between the Mercedes-Benz bread. While the sedan gets the most action at the dealership, it&rsquo;s the coupe and cabriolet that add flair to the stately E-Class branch of the Benz family tree. Now, for the 2014 model year, the stylish coupe and drop-top cabriolet have been given a nice nip-tuck makeover.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>

			</div>
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="2">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class coupe rear.jpg" class="rsImg">What’s New for the 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet:</a>
			</div>
			<div class="js-page-content" data-page-num="2">
				
				<p class="pager-xofy" id="pageNumberDetail">Page 2 of 7</p>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">What’s New for the 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet:</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><ul>
<li>The exterior design has been reworked.</li>
<li>New safety systems have been added to the Mercedes-Benz intelligent drive technology.</li>
<li>The interior has received a refresh, with new switches, vents, and trim.</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>

			</div>
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="3">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class cabriolet.jpg" class="rsImg">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: Styling and Design</a>
			</div>
			<div class="js-page-content" data-page-num="3">
				
				<p class="pager-xofy" id="pageNumberDetail">Page 3 of 7</p>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: Styling and Design</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><p>Sleek and aggressive are two words that immediately come to mind when staring at the refreshed 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet. The face seems tighter and more drawn towards to rear of the car, which gives it a &ldquo;fast-standing-still&rdquo; vibe. Up front, the headlights are given partial LED lights are part of the standard equipment, but a full LED setup is available.</p>
<p>Around the side, the shoulder line runs in concert with the flat roof before terminating into the muscular rear section of the E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet. Overall, the treatment works to add a bit of extra muscle into what we consider an otherwise stately machine.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>

			</div>
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="4">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class cabriolet interior.jpg" class="rsImg">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: What’s Under the Hood</a>
			</div>
			<div class="js-page-content" data-page-num="4">
				
				<p class="pager-xofy" id="pageNumberDetail">Page 4 of 7</p>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: What’s Under the Hood</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><p>Efficient power flows forth from beneath the long hood of the 2014 E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet. The base engine found on the E350 is a 3.5-liter V6, which produces 302 horsepower and 273 pound-feet of torque. For more go, step up to the E550 and its twin-turbocharged 4.6-liter V8, and you&rsquo;ll be rewarded with 402 horsepower nad 443 pound-feet of torque.</p>
<p>This power is turned efficient thanks to the standard ECO Start/Stop system. Additionally, it&rsquo;s sent to the rear wheels by way of a seven-speed automatic gearbox. If you desire more traction, the E350 Coupe is offered with the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>

			</div>
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="5">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class coupe top.jpg" class="rsImg">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: Features and Technology</a>
			</div>
			<div class="js-page-content" data-page-num="5">
				
				<p class="pager-xofy" id="pageNumberDetail">Page 5 of 7</p>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: Features and Technology</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><p>It&rsquo;s not all style with the new E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet. Mercedes-Benz have provided a long list of new safety features and technology. Collision Prevention Assist, ECO Start/Stop, and Attention Assist 2.0 are now standard features. Items such as Distronic Plus with Steering Assist, Pre-Safe Brake with Pedestrian Detection, Active Lane Keeping, and a host of other bits of safety tech are also offered as optional extras.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>

			</div>
			<div class="js-page-slide" data-page-num="6">
                <a data-rsw="800" data-rsh="600" href="http://img.autobytel.com/car-reviews/autobytel/115011-2014-mercedes-benz-e-class-coupe-cabriolet-preview-detroit-auto-show/2014 mercedes-benz e-class cabriolet top down.jpg" class="rsImg">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: What Autobytel Thinks</a>
			</div>
			<div class="js-page-content" data-page-num="6">
				
				<p class="pager-xofy" id="pageNumberDetail">Page 6 of 7</p>

			<h3 class="h1 bold" id="pageTitle">New 2014 Mercedes-Benz E-Class Coupe &amp; Cabriolet: What Autobytel Thinks</h3>


				<div id="pageText"><p>There&rsquo;s plenty of nifty safety features added to the new E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet, and that&rsquo;s never a bad thing. On top of that, the refreshed style breathes a bit of new life into a design which, while not yet too old, is starting to become average. Average doesn&rsquo;t work for Mercedes-Benz, and the 2014 E-Class Coupe and Cabriolet is proof of that.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p></div>
