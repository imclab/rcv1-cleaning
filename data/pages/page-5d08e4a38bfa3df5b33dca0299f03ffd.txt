<p align="center">
	<strong><font color="#0000ff" size="5">AUTO CROSS</font></strong></p>
<p align="center">
	&nbsp;</p>
<p align="center">
	<strong><font size="4">Friday, August 24, 2012 at 7:00 p.m.</font></strong></p>
<p align="center">
	<font size="3"><strong>$10.00 13 &amp; up, $5.00 ages 5-12, under 5 Free</strong></font></p>
<p>
	&nbsp;</p>
<p align="center">
	<strong><font size="3"><font color="#ff0000" size="5"><em>Ready, set, race!</em></font> </font></strong></p>
<p>
	&nbsp;</p>
<p>
	<strong><font size="3">Autocross is an annual favorite at the Emmet-Charlevoix County Fair, where spectators can cheer on their favorite racers as they navigate an oval course for speed and precision. Autocross is a timed competition where drivers head out one at a time through the course, vying for both the best speed and driving skills. </font></strong></p>
<p>
	<strong><font size="3">Four- and six-cylinder vehicles will race in various heats throughout the event, with winners competing in the final feature race to determine the overall winner of each class. </font></strong></p>
<p>
	<strong><font size="3">A &ldquo;Powder Puff&rdquo; class for ladies is also a crowd favorite, with women racers competing against each other for overall champion honors. </font></strong></p>
<p align="center">
	&nbsp;</p>
<p align="center">
	<strong><font size="3"><em>There will be plenty of exciting racing for fans of all ages!</em> </font></strong></p>
