<p>The Lake Chelan Arts Council is a a private, nonprofit organization, <em><strong><span style="color: #446c80;">&#8220;to help promote visual and performing arts, to educate, encourage and enhance a cultural climate in the Chelan Valley and to provide support for such events&#8221;<span style="color: #000000;">.</span></span></strong></em></p>
<p><strong>Activities Sponsored and/or Supported by the Arts Council</strong><br />
Lake Chelan Fine Arts Festival<br />
Lake Chelan Outdoor Gallery<br />
Art By The Foot &#8211; Silent Auction<br />
Children and adults art classes<br />
Quarterly newsletters, membership meetings, and monthly Arts Update on KOZI<br />
We support Local groups involved in the arts community including:<br />
Chelan Valley Players, Bach Fest, and the Lake Chelan Artist Alliance</p>
<p><span style="color: #c0c0c0;">____________________________________________________________________________________________</span><strong></strong></p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #274e61;"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-402" alt="home1" src="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/home1.jpg" width="205" height="205" /> Lake Chelan Outdoor Gallery</span></strong></h2>
<p><em>Enhancing the culture and lifestyle of Greater Chelan through the enjoyment of outdoor public art.</em></p>
<p><strong>Call For Artists!</strong> We have an ongoing jurying process for selection of Outdoor Gallery pieces.</p>
<p>If you are interested in loaning your sculpture to the Lake Chelan Arts Council for display in the Chelan Valley, please contact Mary Ann Warren at (509) 682-4530, Barb Koenig at (509) 682-9781 or email LakeChelanArts@yahoo.com</p>
<p><a title="Outdoor Gallery" href="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/outdoor-gallery/">Click here for more information &gt;&gt;</a></p>
<p><span style="color: #c0c0c0;">____________________________________________________________________________________________</span></p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #274e61;"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-403" alt="home2" src="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/home2.jpg" width="205" height="205" />2013 Lake Chelan Fine Arts Festival</span></strong></h2>
<p><strong>Save the dates! August 16th – 18th, 2013</strong><br />
Friday – Noon to 8:00pm<br />
Saturday – 10:00am to 8:00pm<br />
Sunday – 10:00am to 4:00pm</p>
<p>This year on August 16, 17, &amp; 18 Riverwalk Park in Chelan will showcase artists and their original works of art.  The venue will be surrounded by entertainment, a children’s area and special events .  The Lake Chelan Arts Council invites you to join us for the 4th Annual Fine Arts Festival.</p>
<p><a title="Fine Arts Festival" href="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/events/lake-chelan-fine-arts-festival/">Click here for more information &gt;&gt;</a></p>
<p><span style="color: #c0c0c0;">____________________________________________________________________________________________</span></p>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #274e61;"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-403" alt="home2" src="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/home3.jpg" width="205" height="205" />Become A Member</span></strong></h2>
<p>The Lake Chelan Arts Council is a private, nonprofit organization created to promote and support the visual and performing arts, to educate, and to encourage and enhance a cultural climate in the Chelan Valley.</p>
<p>Become an Arts Council member today! You can also volunteer, or be added to a mailing list.</p>
<p><a title="About Us" href="http://lakechelanartscouncil.com/about-us/">Click here for more information &gt;&gt;</a></p>
