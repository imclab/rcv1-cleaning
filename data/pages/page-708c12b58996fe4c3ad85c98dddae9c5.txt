	</header><!-- .entry-header -->

		<div class="entry-content">
		<div align="center">
<p><a href="http://www.open-casting-calls.info/2013/05/open-casting-call-in-austin-tx-for.html"><img alt="Todd Rohal Sweet Cheeks Open Casting Call" src="http://i92.photobucket.com/albums/l37/theBULLDOGfan/LA%20Acting%20Auditions%20Examiner/ToddRohl300px_zps2ed2136c.jpg" /></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size:xx-small;"><a class="zem_slink" title="Todd Rohal" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Todd_Rohal" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Todd Rohal</a> will direct &#8220;Sweet Cheeks&#8221; from his own true story screenplay.</span><br />
<span style="font-size:xx-small;">Creative Commons</span></p>
</div>
<p><a class="zem_slink" title="Casting (performing arts)" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Casting_%28performing_arts%29" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Casting directors</a> for the upcoming <a class="zem_slink" title="Feature film" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feature_film" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">feature film</a> &#8220;Sweet Cheeks&#8221; are holding an open casting call seeking children available to work on the film in the Austin, Texas area. They will be casting two boys with great imaginations and natural wit for the lead roles in the movie. The boys should be extroverted kids who are full of ideas, comfortable around new people and environments, and who like hearing, telling, or creating stories. Kids with interesting faces and timeless, non-modern looks similar to those in &#8220;The <a class="zem_slink" title="Our Gang" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Our_Gang" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Little Rascals</a>&#8221; are especially encouraged to attend the open call or submit by email. No acting experience is necessary, and these are fully paid positions. Shooting will take place in August, 2013.</p>
<div align="center">
<p>Details Here:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.open-casting-calls.info/2013/05/open-casting-call-in-austin-tx-for.html">&#8220;Sweet Cheeks&#8221; Austin, TX Open Casting Call</a></p>
</div>

		<div class="entry-content">
		<div align="center"><a href="http://www.open-casting-calls.info/2013/02/open-casting-call-in-boston-for-major.html"><img alt="Bradley Cooper David O. Russell Project" src="http://i92.photobucket.com/albums/l37/theBULLDOGfan/LA%20Acting%20Auditions%20Examiner/BradleyCooper300px_zps50e14233.jpg" /></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size:xx-small;"><a class="zem_slink" title="Bradley Cooper" href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/celebrity/bradley_cooper" target="_blank" rel="rottentomatoes">Bradley Cooper</a> is playing <a class="zem_slink" title="Federal Bureau of Investigation" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=38.894465,-77.024503&amp;spn=0.01,0.01&amp;q=38.894465,-77.024503 (Federal%20Bureau%20of%20Investigation)&amp;t=h" target="_blank" rel="geolocation">FBI Agent</a> <a class="zem_slink" title="Jimmy Boyle (artist)" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jimmy_Boyle_%28artist%29" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">Jimmy Boyle</a>.</span><br />
<span style="font-size:xx-small;">Creative Commons</span></div>
<p>Atlas Entertainment and Annapurna Pictures are getting ready to begin shooting a major <a class="zem_slink" title="Feature film" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feature_film" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">feature film</a> in <a class="zem_slink" title="Boston" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=42.3577777778,-71.0616666667&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=42.3577777778,-71.0616666667 (Boston)&amp;t=h" target="_blank" rel="geolocation">Boston, MA</a> and casting directors are holding an open casting call for extras to portray 1970s era types. They are also seeking people with 60s &#8211; 70s vehicles to use as props in the film. The project is currently being called &#8220;Untitled <a class="zem_slink" title="David O. Russell" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_O._Russell" target="_blank" rel="wikipedia">David O. Russell</a> Project&#8221;. There is no experience required, and these are fully paid positions. The open casting call is completely free to attend, and all ages and ethnicities are encouraged to attend.</p>
<div align="center">Details Here:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.open-casting-calls.info/2013/02/open-casting-call-in-boston-for-major.html">Open Casting Call for &#8220;Untitled David O. Russell Project&#8221;</a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.examiner.com/acting-auditions-in-los-angeles/20th-century-fox-comedy-film-call-me-artie-casting-children-and-adults"><img src="http://i92.photobucket.com/albums/l37/theBULLDOGfan/MarisaTomei300px.jpg" alt="Marisa Tomei Alice Call Me Artie Us and Them" /></a></p>

<p>Chernin Entertainment and Twentieth Century Fox Film Corporation will begin shooting the family comedy feature film &#8220;Call Me Artie&#8221; on October 10th, 2011 in the Atlanta, GA area. The casting directors are casting supporting roles and smaller speaking roles in Atlanta, and the extras casting director is holding an open casting call for kids and adults to work on the film. These are fully paid positions.</p>

<p><span style="font-size:xx-small;"><a class="zem_slink" title="Heather Graham" href="http://www.rottentomatoes.com/celebrity/heather_graham" rel="rottentomatoes">Heather Graham</a> plays Dennis Quaids mistress Meredith Crown in &#8220;Heartland&#8221;.</span><br />
<span style="font-size:xx-small;">Creative Commons</span></p>
</div>
<p><a class="zem_slink" title="Casting (performing arts)" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Casting_%28performing_arts%29" rel="wikipedia">Casting directors</a> for the <a class="zem_slink" title="Independent film" href="http://www.indiewire.com" rel="homepage">independent feature film</a> &#8220;Heartland&#8221; are holding an open casting call in <a class="zem_slink" title="DeKalb, Illinois" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=41.9313888889,-88.7502777778&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=41.9313888889,-88.7502777778%20%28DeKalb%2C%20Illinois%29&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation">DeKalb, Illinois</a> on Sunday September 18, 2011. They are seeking local talent for small speaking roles, extras, and <a class="zem_slink" title="Stand-in" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stand-in" rel="wikipedia">stand-ins</a> for the film&#8217;s stars. All ages and types are needed, no experience is necessary. Shooting is taking place in the DeKalb area which is approximately 60 miles east of <a class="zem_slink" title="Chicago" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=41.8819444444,-87.6277777778&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=41.8819444444,-87.6277777778%20%28Chicago%29&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation">Chicago</a>.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.examiner.com/acting-auditions-in-los-angeles/feature-film-sleeping-around-casting-hundreds-of-extras-charlotte-nc"><img src="http://i92.photobucket.com/albums/l37/theBULLDOGfan/TamminSursok300px.jpg" alt="Beautiful Australian Tammin Sursok Sleeping Around" /></a></p>
<p><span style="font-size:xx-small;">Australian actress <a class="zem_slink" title="Tammin Sursok" href="http://tammin.com/" rel="homepage">Tammin Sursok</a> plays the lead role in &#8220;Sleeping Around&#8221;.</span><br />
<span style="font-size:xx-small;"><a class="zem_slink" title="Creative Commons" href="http://creativecommons.org" rel="homepage">Creative Commons</a></span></p>
</div>
<p>The independent feature film &#8220;Sleeping Around&#8221; in currently in production in the <a class="zem_slink" title="Charlotte, North Carolina" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=35.2269444444,-80.8433333333&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=35.2269444444,-80.8433333333%20%28Charlotte%2C%20North%20Carolina%29&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation">Charlotte, North Carolina</a> area and the extras casting director is currently seeking several hundred people to work as background artists on the film. This is for a big party scene to be filmed Thursday August 25, 2011 at <a class="zem_slink" title="Lake Norman" href="http://maps.google.com/maps?ll=35.4833333333,-80.9333333333&amp;spn=0.1,0.1&amp;q=35.4833333333,-80.9333333333%20%28Lake%20Norman%29&amp;t=h" rel="geolocation">Lake Norman</a>, which is just north of Charlotte. There could be a few additional days of filming also.</p>
<div align="center">
<p>Details Here:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.examiner.com/acting-auditions-in-los-angeles/feature-film-sleeping-around-casting-hundreds-of-extras-charlotte-nc">Indie Film &#8220;Sleeping Around&#8221; Charlotte Casting Call</a></p>
