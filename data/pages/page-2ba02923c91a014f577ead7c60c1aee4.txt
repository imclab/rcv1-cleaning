							</a>
							</h2>
						
						
							<p class="section_intro_full">YA fantasy authors have usually written a number of books, often related to a particular theme. Researching these authors can give you an idea of their oeuvres. 							</a>
							</h2>
						
						
							<p class="section_intro_full">If you&rsquo;re having trouble deciding what you or your child should read next, YA book awards and book lists offer ample material from which to choose.
