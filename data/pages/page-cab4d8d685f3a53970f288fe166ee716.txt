<br />
The new 335i is powered by N55 inline-six engine with a single twin-scroll turbocharger instead of the N54 inline-six engine of its predecessor.<br />
<br />
This new power-train outputs 300 horsepower and 300 pound-feet of torque. Inside the cabin, you will get a multifunction steering wheel with leather envelope.<br />

<br />
<span id="more-3125"></span><br />

<br />
