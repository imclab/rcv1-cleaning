<h1>Arts: Overview</h1>
<a name="comp00004b840767000000001d172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b840767000000001d172c"><span /></a>
<hr />
<?xml version='1.0' encoding='utf-8'?>
<div>

<div class="pic-left" style="width: 123px;">
	<img alt="Shakespeare performance" src="/main/images/topics/arts/ArtsInAcademics.jpg"></img>
</div>
<h3>
	Arts in Academics
</h3>

<p>
	The 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/"><b>Lewis Center for the Arts</b></a>
	leads a <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/presidents_initiative/">University Initiative</a> to make the arts a central feature 
        of Princeton's academic mission. 



	The Lewis Center is an academic unit that is home to the  
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/arts_at_princeton/creative_writing/">creative writing</a>, 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/arts_at_princeton/dance/">dance</a>, 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/arts_at_princeton/theater/">theater</a>
	and 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/arts_at_princeton/visual_arts/">visual arts</a> programs, 
each of which offers courses and an undergraduate certificate. <span id="p1" class="expander">The center's <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/arts_at_princeton/princeton_atelier/">interdisciplinary Atelier</a> program allows students to collaborate with professional artists. The Lewis Center also presents professional 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/events/calendar/">arts events</a>
	for the community.</span> <a href="#" class="morelink" style="display:none;">more</a><a href="#" class="lesslink" style="display:none;">close</a> 
</p>





<p>
	The 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.music.princeton.edu/"><b>Department of Music</b></a>
	offers academic courses for undergraduate and graduate students and supports performance ensembles. <span id="p2" class="expander">Performance, musicology and composition are fully represented in music courses, and students may 
        work toward an undergraduate major in 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/music/undergraduate-program/">music</a>,
        an undergraduate certificate program in 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/music/certificate-of-music/">musical performance</a>
	or 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/music/graduate-program/">graduate degree programs</a>. 
        The department sponsors 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/music/performance/">
		performances
	</a>
	by a number of student ensembles and professional groups, and manages a program for 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/music/private-lessons/">
		private music and vocal lessons</a>.
</span> <a href="#" class="morelink" style="display:none;">more</a><a href="#" class="lesslink" style="display:none;">close</a>
</p>


<p>
	Many 
	<a target="_top" href="/main/academics/departments/"><b>other academic disciplines</b></a>
	promote the study of the arts through criticism and the creation of new work.

<span id="p3" class="expander">
	The 
	<a target="_top" href="http://etc.princeton.edu/films/">Committee for Film Studies</a>
	encourages the interdisciplinary study of films and video. Students may apply to fulfill 
        the senior thesis requirement with creative work. The 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/%7Esoa/">architecture</a>
	school and 
	<a target="_top" href="http://www.princeton.edu/artandarchaeology/">art and archaeology</a>
	department, among others, provide historical and theoretical perspectives. Additionally, 
        many area and language studies incorporate art in their discussions of culture.
</span> <a href="#" class="morelink" style="display:none;">more</a><a href="#" class="lesslink" style="display:none;">close</a>
</p>


</div><a name="comp00004b840767000000001e172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b840767000000001e172c"><span /></a>
<hr />
<a name="comp00004b840767000000001f172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b840767000000001f172c"><span /></a><div class="pic-left" style="width:123px;"><img alt="Monet painting of bridge" src="/main/images/topics/arts/museum.jpg" /></div>
<h3>Art Museum and Libraries</h3>

<p>The <a href="http://artmuseum.princeton.edu/" target="_self">Princeton University Art Museum</a> is one of the nation’s leading art museums, with collections of more than 80,000 works of art spanning the globe. In addition, the museum organizes a dozen temporary exhibitions each year, as well as numerous educational programs and social offerings. The <a href="http://library.princeton.edu" target="_self">University library system</a> offers research materials and <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/~rbsc/exhibitions/" target="_self">exhibitions</a> — Firestone and Mudd libraries feature University-owned and borrowed works, while the Marquand Library of <a href="http://marquand.princeton.edu" target="_self">Art and Archaeology</a>, Mendel <a href="http://libweb.princeton.edu/libraries/music/index.php" target="_self">Music Library</a> and <a href="http://rbsc.princeton.edu/department/graphicarts/" target="_self">Graphic Arts Collection</a> also illuminate study of the arts.</p>
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000023172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000023172c"><span /></a>
<hr />
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000020172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000020172c"><span /></a><div class="pic-left" style="width:123px;"><img alt="Piano performance" src="/main/images/topics/arts/extracurricular.jpg" /></div>
<h3>Extracurricular Arts</h3>

<p>The full list of <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/odus/activities/organizations/directory/" target="_self">student-run organizations</a> reveals the breadth of students' engagement with the arts. Performing arts groups in <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/arts/performing/" target="_self">dance, music and theater</a> allow students to refine and present their skills. Also, <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/media/publications/" target="_self">student literary publications</a> and <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/news/multimedia/student/" target="_self">student videos</a> offer creative outlets for students to express and share their work.</p>
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000024172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000024172c"><span /></a>
<hr />
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000021172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000021172c"><span /></a><div class="pic-left" style="width:123px;"><img alt="Dancers" src="/main/images/topics/arts/venues.jpg" /></div>
<h3>Campus Art Events &amp; Venues</h3>

<p>Several <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/news/events/calendars/" target="_self">University calendars</a> list arts events around campus. The University and broader community may attend <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/arts/" target="_self">art exhibitions</a> around campus, <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/media/films/" target="_self">film screenings</a>, <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/arts/lectures/" target="_self">lectures and readings</a>, and student performances in <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/campuslife/arts/performing/" target="_self">dance, music and theater</a>. Tickets may be purchased through <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/utickets" target="_self">University Ticketing</a>, and students also receive tickets through the <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/odus/activities/initiatives/passport_to_the_arts/" target="_self">Passport to the Arts</a> program.</p>


<p>Among the <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/visiting/venues/" target="_self">campus venues</a> for arts events are the <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/arts/" target="_self">Lewis Center for the Arts</a>, the award-winning <a href="http://www.mccarter.org/" target="_self">McCarter Theatre Center</a> for the performing arts, <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/richaud" target="_self">Richardson Auditorium</a> and the Princeton University <a href="http://artmuseum.princeton.edu/" target="_self">Art Museum</a>.</p>
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000025172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000025172c"><span /></a>
<hr />
<a name="comp00004b8407670000000022172c"><span /></a><a name="comp00004b8407670000000022172c"><span /></a><div class="pic-left" style="width:123px;"><img alt="Chinese lion" src="/main/images/topics/arts/culture.jpg" /></div>
<h3>Local Arts &amp; Culture</h3>

<p>Princeton and its surrounding communities feature numerous resources in the <a href="http://www.princeton.edu/main/visiting/region/arts/" target="_self">fine arts, film, music and theater</a>.</p>
