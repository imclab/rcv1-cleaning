<h1>Learn Auto Repair Online</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>An online learning resource which will help you learn more about auto repair and maintenance topics.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We have been online since 1999 educating our online visitors on how not to get <a href="http://aarp.thehartford.com/Auto-Insurance/affordable-car-insurance.shtml" target="_blank">affordable car insurance</a>&nbsp;and not get ripped off by their mechanic. &nbsp;We also have helped car owners learn how to perform their own auto repair procedures.&nbsp;</p>
<p>If you have ever wondered if your mechanic is pulling a scam, or want to do it yourself (DIY) you need AutoEducation.com.&nbsp; Take a few minutes and browse the online learning sections to your left.&nbsp;</p>
</div>

<p>We are in the middle of redesigning the site, and have added a few new pages like <a href="car_insurance/index.htm">Car Insurance Tips.</a> We will keep you update on the new look, and new pages of quality automotive content.</p>
