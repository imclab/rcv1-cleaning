        <h1 style="padding-bottom: 8px;">
        How to Write an Agreement Letter for Selling a Car      </h1>
      	<div class="postHead" style="min-height:309px;">
        
		                <div style="margin-left: 15px; float:right;"><img width="346" height="260" src="http://www.stepbystep.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/Agreement-Letter-for-Selling-a-Car1-400x300.jpg" class="attachment-360x260 wp-post-image" alt="Agreement Letter for Selling a Car" title="How to Write an Agreement Letter for Selling a Car" /></div>
              
              
                   
       <p>
        <p style="text-align: justify">When you make a decision to sell a car, you can earn a reasonable profit through a very simple process. You simply have to write a formal document that contains all the basic information regarding the transfer of ownership of a car from a seller to a buyer. An agreement letter for selling a car can be typed or written on a paper and must be signed by both parties to make it legal. The agreement should lay out the terms and conditions of the sale including all the information regarding the type of vehicle involved and the mode of payment acceptable to the seller.</p><br />  <p style="text-align: justify">You can write an agreement letter for selling your car by following the simple steps given below:</p>           
        </p>

Add your address below your name. For example, “of (your / seller’s Address).</p>
