	<h2 class="generalLinkHeading">Performing Arts</h2><div class="linkDescription"><p>The university is widely recognized for the high quality of its music and performing arts.  The School of Music is home to the world-class Four Seasons Chamber Music Festival and the Billy Taylor Jazz Festival. More than 150 public musical performances, including sacred, classical, opera, jazz, and new music, are offered by the School of Music each year in campus theaters and recital halls.</p>
<p>The S. Rudolph Alexander Performing Arts Series has brought more than 350 outstanding performances to ECU since it started in 1962, including Yo-Yo Ma and the London Philharmonic Orchestra.</p>
