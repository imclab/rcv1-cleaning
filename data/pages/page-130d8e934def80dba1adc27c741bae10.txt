This unique biography looks at his interaction with each, starting with his family (his mother, Maria Anna and beloved and <a href="http://www.amazon.co.uk/Mozarts-Women-Family-Friends-ebook/dp/B00CEAAH7A/?tag=dailycheaprea-21" target="_blank"><img style="border: none; float: right; margin-right: 10px; margin-bottom: 10px; width: 50%;" alt="" src="http://ecx.images-amazon.com/images/I/51VPNmabHpL._BO2,204,203,35,-76_AA278_PIkin4,BottomRight,-63,22_AA300_SH20_OU02_.jpg" /></a>talented sister, Nannerl), and his marriage (which brought his &#8216;other family&#8217;, the Weber sisters).<br />

This is their story as well as his &#8212; and shows once again that a great part of the composer’s genius was in his understanding and musical expression of human nature. Evocative and beautifully written, <em><strong>Mozart’s Women </strong></em>illuminates the music, the man, and above all the women who inspired him.<br />
