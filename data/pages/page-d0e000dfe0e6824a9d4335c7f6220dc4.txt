<a href='http://automobileandamericanlife.blogspot.com/2013/08/why-choose-diesel-engine-over-ice-when.html'>Why choose a Diesel Engine over a ICE when Buying Your Next Car -- a Contribution from Ivy Delfin</a>

<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><br /></span></b></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-MIElpAlOVcg/Uhf7k0g3bZI/AAAAAAAAGf8/hSagwKjtIow/s1600/diesel+engine.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-MIElpAlOVcg/Uhf7k0g3bZI/AAAAAAAAGf8/hSagwKjtIow/s1600/diesel+engine.jpg" /></a></div>

<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><br /></span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><br /></span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><br /></span></b></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zIGbrwKpv9U/Uhf8NPMnA4I/AAAAAAAAGgE/kEavd7M0tCQ/s1600/diesel.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="224" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-zIGbrwKpv9U/Uhf8NPMnA4I/AAAAAAAAGgE/kEavd7M0tCQ/s320/diesel.jpg" width="320" /></a></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">

<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><br /></span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<b><span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;">The benefits of choosing a diesel engine<o:p></o:p></span></b></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<br /></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;">When deciding on your new car, one of the things
at the very top of your list is probably going to be the energy efficiency of
the vehicle. Earlier, this may just have meant buying a reliable car that
doesn&#8217;t cost hundreds to keep on the road, but our idea of energy efficient has
also come to include how the fuel used in the engine impacts on the
environment. Petrol engines have been the most popular kind of engines since
the invention of the common car, but it&#8217;s a wonder why diesel has typically
only been the standard for industrial vehicles up until this point when there
are so many advantages to having a diesel engine.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;"><a href="http://www.businessinsider.com.au/10-reasons-why-you-should-buy-a-diesel-car-2013-8"><span style="color: black; text-decoration: none; text-underline: none;">Diesel
engines</span></a> are an internal combustion engine, just like regular
gasoline engines, converting chemical energy in fuel into mechanical energy.
The mechanics involve moving pistons up and down inside cylinders, these
pistons are connected to the crankshaft where the motion of the pistons moving
up and down create the motion that turns the wheels. Unlike a petrol engine, a
diesel engine has no spark plug, it takes in air and compresses it before
injecting the fuel, the heat created from the compressed air is what lights the
fuel that gets the engine moving.<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;">Diesel engines get amazing mileage, generally
they are up to 30 percent more fuel efficient than regular petrol engines
meaning you don&#8217;t have to fill up as often. It contains more usable energy than
petrol, thus making it more fuel efficient; it works by turning heat into
energy rather than expelling heat like a petrol engine. However it does not
offer the same performance as a petrol engine due to the fact that the engine
has to work harder to use the energy. <o:p></o:p></span></div>
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;">Diesel engines are also built to withstand higher
demands on it&#8217;s use making them last a lot longer than their petrol
counterparts; they can withstand higher compression so they generally can go
much longer without needing a tune-up, unlike petrol engines, adding to it&#8217;s
efficiency. On that note, a diesel engine&#8217;s impact on the environment is
lessened by the fact that diesel engines don&#8217;t release fumes like petrol does,
it is also harder to burn so it is safer and won&#8217;t explode as easily as petrol.
Another environmental benefit is the ease of running a <a href="http://automobileandamericanlife.blogspot.com.au/2009/09/clean-green-diesels-will-they-find.html"><span style="color: windowtext; text-decoration: none; text-underline: none;"><span style="color: black;">diesel engine
on alternative fuel</span>.</span></a> Regular gasoline engines can only accept
petrol, otherwise they must be heavily modified to accept a more eco-friendly
fuel. Diesel engines love biofuels, so start collecting that vegetable oil and
feed your car!<o:p></o:p></span></div>
<br />
<div class="MsoNormal" style="line-height: 120%; margin-bottom: .0001pt; margin-bottom: 0in; mso-layout-grid-align: none; mso-pagination: none; tab-stops: 28.3pt 56.65pt 85.0pt 113.35pt 141.7pt 170.05pt 198.4pt 226.75pt 255.1pt 283.45pt 311.8pt 340.15pt; text-autospace: none; text-indent: 36.1pt;">
<span lang="EN-PH" style="font-family: &quot;Georgia&quot;,&quot;serif&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; line-height: 120%; mso-bidi-font-family: Georgia;">If you don&#8217;t already have a diesel engine, there
are plenty of companies, like <a href="http://www.industrialdiesels.com/"><span style="color: black; text-decoration: none; text-underline: none;">Industrial
Diesels,</span></a> that supply diesel engines to convert your current vehicle,
otherwise look for a diesel engine or energy efficient vehicle for your next
car, it could end up saving you hundreds, even thousands, in the long run.</span></div>

<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">RATS IN WINONA</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">While driving to business meetings in Minnesota last week, this writer spent a wonderful day and evening in the Mississippi River town of Winona, Minnesota, where he happened upon an evening cruise-in at Lakeview Drive-in, an eatery that dates to 1938. &nbsp;But the centerpiece of this particular cruise-in seemed to be a preponderance of &nbsp;what are termed as "rat rods."</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The "rat rod" &nbsp;is a style of hot rod or custom car that, in most cases, imitates (or exaggerates) the early hot rods of the 1940s and 1950s. The style is not to be confused with the somewhat closely related "traditional" hot rod, which is an accurate re-creation or period-correct restoration of a hot rod from the same era. Most rat rods appear "unfinished", regardless of their status, as only the vehicle's bare essentials are driven.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Rat rods were originally a counter-reaction to the high-priced "customs" and typical hot rods, many of which were seldom driven and served only a decorative purpose. The rat rod's inception signified a throwback to the hot rods of the earlier days of hot-rod culture-built according to the owner's abilities and with the intention of being driven. Rat rods are meant to loosely imitate, in both form and function, the "traditional" hot rods of the era.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The typical rat rod is a late-1920s through to late-1950s coupe or roadster, but sometimes a truck or sedan. Many early pre-World War II vehicles were not built with fenders, hoods, running boards, and bumpers. The bodies are frequently channeled over the frame and sectioned, or the roofs were chopped, for a lower profile. Later-era post-war vehicles were rarely constructed without fenders and were often customized in the fashion of lead sleds or low riders. Maltese crosses, skulls, and other accessories were often added. &nbsp;Note that the "rat" seen in Winona, Minnesota on cruise-in night has "fake" rats clamoring in the truck bed and one appearing to emerge from the vehicle's gas tank.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Recently, the term "rat rod" has been used to describe almost any vehicle that appears unfinished or is built simply to be driven. &nbsp;Chopped tops, shaved trim, grills, tail lights, and other miscellaneous body parts continue to be swapped between makes and models.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Frames from older cars or light trucks are sometimes preferred for rat rod conversions due to the chassis that is used for these types of vehicles-the chassis type provides a sturdy base for subsequent alterations. Older cars in poor condition are often advertised as candidates for rat rod conversions and, in some cases, the owner will purchase a custom frame, or design and build it himself/herself. In other cases, a rat rodder may use a small pick-up chassis, such as a Chevy S-10, to insert into an older car body, in order to create a vehicle that features the look of a classic rat rod, while also maintaining the reliability of a modern vehicle.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Rat rods often appear unfinished and, at most, primer-only paint jobs are applied; satin, or matte, black and other flat colors are also common. "Natural patina" (the original paint job, with rust, blemishes, and sometimes bullet holes, left intact); a patchwork of original paint and primer; or bare metal, in rusty or oiled varieties, with no finish at all are some of the other finishes that may be used-such finishes honor the anti-restoration slogan that "it's only original once". &nbsp;Contrary to the aesthetic of many car builders, rust is often acceptable and appreciated by rat rod owners.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Interiors of rat rods can range from fully finished, through to a spartan form. Mexican blankets and bomber seats form the basis of many rat rod interiors, and most are designed to be functional without many comforts; although, this will vary in accordance with the owner's taste.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Though a variety of engines may be used, the most common engine type that is used in rat rods are: Flathead V-8 Ford, early Chrysler Hemi engines, or more modern small block V8 engines from any manufacturer (Chevrolet is a common choice of small block engine). Straight 8's, straight 6's and straight 4's are also fairly commonly used in the construction of rat rods-these engines may exhibit varying displacements and modifications. While diesel engines are occasionally used, these engines are rarely fitted with emission controls, as such a feature was not part of the original construction, or the feature was not required under special license.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Most rat rods are rear wheel drive, with an open driveline. The rear endss and the transmissions are typically passenger vehicle pieces.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">My interest in "rat rods" was renewed this past spring while attending the Auburn Spring car auction and swap meet where I met the son and daughter-in-law of the late "Big Daddy" Roth, one of the major customizers and artists associated with rat car culture.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">"Rat Fink" is one of the severa hot rod characters created by Roth, one of the originators of Kustom Kulture of automobile enthusiasts. Roth conceived Rat Fink as an anti-hero answer to Mickey Mouse. &nbsp;Rat Fink is green, depraved-looking with bulging, bloodshot eyes, an oversize mouth with yellowed, narrow teeth, and a red T-shirt with yellow "R.F." on it. &nbsp;Yes, I bought an R. F. tee-shirt from Roth's son and plan to wear it to swap meets and car enthusiastic events (see attached photo).</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Roth began airbrushing and selling "Weirdo" t-shirts at Car Shows and in the pages of Hot Rod publications such as Car Craft in the late 1950s. By the August 1959 issue of Car Craft, "Weirdo shirts" had become a full blown craze with Ed Roth at the forefront of the movement.</span><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" /><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Nonetheless, "Rat Fink" and "Rat Cars" have been closely associated since the late 1950s. &nbsp;But who would have thought that the nice clean town of Winona, Minnesota, would be a hot-bed of rat rod culture? &nbsp;Rats!!!!!</span>

<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>

<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Note the dates -- proposals due October 31,2013; Conference April 10-12.</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;"><br /></span>
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">AUTOMOTIVE HISTORY CONFERENCE</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">CALL FOR PAPERS</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The Society of Automotive Historians is seeking proposals for papers to</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">be presented at its Tenth Biennial Automotive History Conference to be</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">held in Palo Alto, California, USA, from&nbsp;</span><span class="aBn" data-term="goog_147130837" style="background-color: white; border-bottom-color: rgb(204, 204, 204); border-bottom-style: dashed; border-bottom-width: 1px; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px; position: relative; top: -2px; z-index: 0;" tabindex="0"><span class="aQJ" style="position: relative; top: 2px; z-index: -1;">April 10 through 12</span></span><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">, 2014.The</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">conference will be co-sponsored by the Revs Automotive Research Program</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">at Stanford University and will take place at the Vail Automotive</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Innovation Facility on the Stanford campus.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The conference theme is *&#8220;The Evolution of Automotive Technology&#8221; *and</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">will focus on the continuing development of the motor vehicle and its</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">process of manufacture over a 120-year history.Although considered by</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">many to have reached a plateau a half-century ago, the industry and its</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">products seem in recent years to have entered a new phase of creative</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">development addressing again issues once thought resolved and responding</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">to new economic, social, and environmental conditions through</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">fundamental research.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Papers may address the search for an effective and portable source of</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">energy, one of the first issues confronted by automotive pioneer</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">designers and one that is being studied again from the point of view of</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">contemporary requirements. Papers may address the development of</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">standardized vehicle control systems, the arrangement of power</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">components or safety devices and the interface between the vehicle and</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">the driver, all of them under reconsideration in ways that have the</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">potential to fundamentally alter the relation between the vehicle, the</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">driver and the road.At the same time the industry, until recently</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">believed to be entering a phase of business consolidation, has expanded</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">to embrace many new producers, a process that could only be made</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">possible through innovations in production technology and international</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">trade agreements. Proposals for papers on automotive subjects unrelated</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">to the conference theme will also be considered.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The keynote speaker will be Dr. Rudi Volti, Emeritus Professor of</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Sociology at Pitzer College, Claremont, California.Professor Volti&#8217;s</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">fields of expertise cover technology and society, social bases of</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">economic structure and behavior, automobility, and the sociology of work</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">and occupations.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Following the conference, a certain number of the conference papers will</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">be selected for publication in /Automotive History Review/, the journal</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">of the Society.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">The Society of Automotive Historians is a unique interdisciplinary</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">organization devoted to all aspects of automotive history &#8211; engineering,</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">design, economics, the lives of automotive pioneers and innovators, the</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">history of motorsport competition, the integration of motor vehicles</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">into modern life and the culture of cars.In its publications and</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">conferences, historians of technology, business historians and social</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">historians interact to share their knowledge and perspectives.</span><br />
<br style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;" />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Proposals should include the title of the submission, names and</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">affiliations of presenters, chairs, participants, etc., together with</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">addresses, phone/fax numbers, email addresses of contact personnel,</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">proposed format (paper, panel, workshop, etc.) and a one-page abstract</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">describing the content of the presentation.Proposals must be received by</span><br />
<span class="aBn" data-term="goog_147130838" style="background-color: white; border-bottom-color: rgb(204, 204, 204); border-bottom-style: dashed; border-bottom-width: 1px; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px; position: relative; top: -2px; z-index: 0;" tabindex="0"><span class="aQJ" style="position: relative; top: 2px; z-index: -1;">October 31, 2013</span></span><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">; notification of preliminary acceptance is anticipated</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">by&nbsp;</span><span class="aBn" data-term="goog_147130839" style="background-color: white; border-bottom-color: rgb(204, 204, 204); border-bottom-style: dashed; border-bottom-width: 1px; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px; position: relative; top: -2px; z-index: 0;" tabindex="0"><span class="aQJ" style="position: relative; top: 2px; z-index: -1;">November 30</span></span><span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">.Proposals should be submitted by email to Arthur W.</span><br />
<span style="background-color: white; color: #222222; font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 13.333333969116211px;">Jones, Conference Chair, _nomecos@verizon.net._</span>
