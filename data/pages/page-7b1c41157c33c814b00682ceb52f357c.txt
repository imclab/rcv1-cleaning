<h1>15th Annual St. Charles Fine Art Show</h1>   <p>The award-winning, highly regarded show features the work of 100 juried artists in various mediums including watercolor, oil, sculpture, photography, jewelry, ceramics, fiber, wood, and glass, which are exhibited and available for sale during the event. Located in the heart of beautiful Downtown St. Charles, along the Fox River, approximately 40 miles west of Chicago, there are many fine restaurants and shops, as well as numerous parks, theaters and activities.</p>
<p> </p>
<p class="date"><strong>Date of Event:</strong> May 25 &amp; May 26, 2013<img style="float: right;" src="/files/3313/5093/7958/McLean_000552_548219_737953_1688.jpg" alt="McLean_000552_548219_737953_1688.jpg" width="325" height="325" /><strong><br />Time of Event:</strong> Sat. 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.<br />and Sun. 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (Rain or Shine) <br /><strong>Location:</strong> Riverside Avenue (formerly 1st Avenue) between Route 64/Main Street &amp; Illinois Avenue in St. Charles, IL <br /><strong>Admission:</strong> Free, with ample free parking in multiple city lots.</p>
