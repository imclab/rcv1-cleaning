More than 150 jury selected artists from throughout the United States and abroad display thousands of original pieces of art. You (and thousands of your friends) will find small to life-sized bronzes, paintings, pottery, photography, scratchboard, wood, clay, metal sculptures, glass sculptures and more.


<p><a href="http://phoenix.about.com/od/arizonapicturesandphotos/ig/Carefree-Art-Festival/Carefree-Art-Festival-01.htm">Enjoy these pictures from the Carefree Art and Wine Festival.</a>
</p>

<h3>When is the Carefree Art & Wine Festival?</h3>
Friday through Sunday, January 18 - 20, 2013

<p>
Times are from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. each day.
</p>

<h3>Where is the Carefree Fine Art & Wine Festival?</h3>
Downtown Carefree, Arizona. <a href="http://phoenix.about.com/library/blmapcarefree.htm">Here is a map and directions to downtown Carefree.</a> Parking is free.

<h3>How do I get tickets and how much are they?</h3>
You pay $5 as you enter the event. Residents of Carefree receive complimentary admission with proof of residency. Parking is free.

<h3>Are discounts available?</h3>
Not that I know of.

<h3>What else should I know?</h3>
The wine tasting part of the event includes a souvenir glass and six wine tasting tickets for $10. Between one and four tickets are exchanged for each tasting, depending on the wine you select. Additional tickets are available for purchase. 21+ only.
<p>
You'll also enjoy live musical entertainment at this event.
</p>

<p>
On the next page you can read my tips for attending this festival.
</p>


<h3> What if I have more questions about the Carefree Fine Art & Wine Festival?</h3>
For more information, contact Thunderbird Artists at 480-837-5637 or visit <a href="http://www.thunderbirdartists.com/calendar/" zT="-o1/XJ" target="_blank">Carefree Fine Art & Wine Festival online</a>.
