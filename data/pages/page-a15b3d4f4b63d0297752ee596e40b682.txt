<p><strong>The Daytona Coupe Le Mans Edition by Exotic Auto Restoration - the spirit of  Le Mans in your  driveway</strong></p>
<p >In 1964, Shelby  took the motorsports world by storm with the Daytona Coupe. With a  revolutionary design by Peter Brock, the car was capable of reaching an  incredible 187 mph on the Mulsanne Straight at Le Mans. In 1965, the Daytona Coupe became the  first and only American car to win the FIA GT World Championship, cementing its  legacy in motorsports history. Now Exotic Auto Restoration of Costa Mesa, CA is  allowing car enthusiasts to bring the legend of Le Mans to their own driveway. Based on the Shelby  Cobra Daytona Coupe built by Superformance and also designed by Peter Brock,  the Le Mans Edition has been upgraded in the spirit of the original racecar. ��</p>
<p >To enhance both the looks and performance of the Daytona  Coupe, Exotic Auto Restoration has designed new body and interior components that  blend seamlessly with the rest of the car. �I wanted to give the car a wider,  more aggressive stance as well as the capability of turning better lap times,� explains  George Cunha, owner of Exotic Auto Restoration. Named for the Le Mans 24 Hour race  that the Daytona Coupe won in 1965, this package includes the following:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Group 5 fender flares front and rear</li>
  <li>Wider 18-inch wheels and tires</li>
  <li>Heavy duty Bilstein shocks and H&R springs</li>
  <li>Functional billet aluminum top and side hood louvers</li>
  <li>Reclinable Alcantera suede seats with �Le Mans Edition�  embroidery </li>
  <li>Brushed billet aluminum dashboard with aircraft-style  toggle switches</li>
  <li>�Le Mans Edition� gauges with 200 mph speedometer and  10k rpm tachometer</li>
  <li>�Suede Momo  racing steering wheel</li>
</ol>
<p >Customers can choose from a variety of motors based on the  Ford 351 Windsor block. Powering this particular Daytona Coupe Le Mans is a  Roush 402R V8 producing 530 horsepower and 515 lb-ft torque mated to a Tremec  T56 6-speed transmission. </p>
<p >In addition, a track-oriented version is also available.  Only six will be built (two have been pre-sold, leaving four available) in this  configuration to commemorate the six original racecars, each featuring an  authentic Shelby-built V8. Adding to the standard Le Mans Edition components,  this package includes the following:</p>
<ol>
  <li>6-point roll cage</li>
  <li>Lightweight wheels with 2.5-inch locking nut</li>
  <li>14-inch, 6-piston brakes front, 13-inch, 6-piston  brakes rear</li>
  <li>Carbon fiber front splitter and rear diffuser</li>
  <li>A/C delete</li>
  <li>Carpet delete</li>
  <li>Full race seats with 6-point harnesses</li>
  <li>Quick-release steering wheel</li>
  <li>Dash-mounted programmable ignition module</li>
  <li>Dash-mounted brake bias controls</li>
  <li>Shelby  aluminum block 351 V8 bored and stroked to 427ci</li>
  <li>560 horsepower and 530 lb-ft torque</li>
  <li>Tremec TKO-600 transmission</li>
  <li>Full dry sump lubrication system</li>
</ol>
<p >The Daytona Coupe Le Mans Edition can be seen in  person at the Pasadena Art Center Car Classic on Sunday, July 19. For  additional information on the Daytona Coupe Le Mans Edition, contact Exotic  Auto Restorations at 714-979-7100</p>
