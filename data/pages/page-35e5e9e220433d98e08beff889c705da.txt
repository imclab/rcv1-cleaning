						<img alt="---" src="http://images.wne.edu/Arts-&amp;-Entertainment-Major-Head.jpg" title="---" />
<h1>Arts and Entertainment Management Major</h1> 						
						</div>
						
						<hr />
						
						
						<div id="body_elements">						
						<p>The arts and entertainment field is a global, multi-billion dollar industry with a need for individuals with knowledge and skills focused on the business side of the creative enterprise.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">To be successful, arts and entertainment organizations must have employees who both understand the nature of an artistic venture and have the business expertise and savvy to make them thrive. Our Arts and Entertainment Management major focuses on the business side of this creative industry. You will be well prepared to work in a wide variety of exciting organizations within the arts and entertainment industry.</p>
<p><strong>Quick Facts</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Read what inspired one student to choose the Arts and Entertainment Management major in <a href="http://businesswest.com/blog/setting-the-stage-4/" title="BusWest Arts &amp; Ent article"><em>BusinessWest</em> magazine</a></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will gain hands-on experience by managing student music, theater, and arts production</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li value="0">
<p>You will have an opportunity to work with professional artists and entertainers through our speakers series and internship program</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Arts and Entertainment Management Curriculum</strong></p>
<p>The arts and entertainment industry needs highly trained professionals with knowledge of all aspects of business operations. While some universities house arts and entertainment management majors within theater or communications programs, Western New England University emphasizes the business side of the field. In our cutting-edge major, you will take a common core of business classes including:</p>
<ul></ul>
<ul>
<li>accounting</li>
<li>business information systems</li>
<li>management</li>
<li>marketing</li>
</ul>
<ul></ul>
<p>To expand your knowledge of the field, you will take exciting arts and entertainment-specific courses such as:</p>
<ul></ul>
<ul>
<li>Managing Arts &amp; Entertainment Organizations</li>
<li>Arts &amp; Entertainment Venue Operations</li>
<li>Business Law for Arts &amp; Entertainment</li>
<li>Economics of Arts &amp; Entertainment</li>
</ul>
<ul></ul>
<p>Learning beyond the classroom includes tours of arts and entertainment facilities to see how things are managed behind the scenes. Students may travel to:</p>
<ul></ul>
<ul>
<li>Radio City Music Hall</li>
<li>Lincoln Center for Performing Arts</li>
<li>Tanglewood Music Center</li>
<li>Eric Carle Museum of Picture Book Art</li>
</ul>
<ul></ul>
<p>For details about courses and requirements, <a href="index.cfm?selection=doc.10354" title="course catalogue">please consult the catalogue</a>.</p>
<p><br /><a href="http://www1.wne.edu/business/index.cfm?selection=doc.1261" title="Business Faculty"><strong>Faculty</strong></a><br />You'll be taught in small classes by experienced professors, not graduate students, who take a personal interest in your progress. Their prime focus is teaching and mentoring you, and our students find their teachers easy to approach and available for one-to-one consultation. A diverse group of professionals, our management faculty members hold advanced degrees in accredited business majors from some of the finest colleges and universities. All are involved in research, provide consulting to regional and national firms, publish in national journals, and present their work at regional and national meetings.</p>
<p><strong>Business students are active in:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li value="0"><a href="http://www1.wne.edu/business/index.cfm?selection=doc.192" target="_blank">Honors Program</a></li>
<li value="0"><a href="http://www1.wne.edu/business/index.cfm?selection=doc.202" target="_blank">Study Abroad Program</a></li>
<li value="0"><a href="http://assets.wne.edu/cfm/popupcomplete.cfm?id=23392&amp;type=clubs-orgs">Beta Gamma Sigma</a> (Honor society)</li>
<li value="0"><a href="javascript:%20popup(411,'clubs-orgs',400,475)">Alpha Lambda Delta</a> (First-year honor society)</li>
<li value="0"><a href="javascript:%20popup(524,'clubs-orgs',400,475)">Alpha Kappa Psi</a> (Professional business fraternity)</li>
<li value="0"><a href="javascript:%20popup(538,'clubs-orgs',400,475)">Management Association</a></li>
</ul>
<p>as well as varsity and intramural sports, special interest groups, and student government.</p>
