        <h2 class="title">UPDATE!  THE NATIONAL ACADEMY OF TELEVISION ARTS &#038; SCIENCES ANNOUNCES WINNERS FOR THE 40TH ANNUAL DAYTIME ENTERTAINMENT CREATIVE ARTS EMMY® AWARDS</h2>
        
        <div class="postmeta-primary">
    
            <span class="meta_date">June 15, 2013</span>
           &nbsp; <span class="meta_categories"><a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/category/tv/" title="View all posts in TV" rel="category tag">TV</a></span>
    
                 &nbsp; <span class="meta_comments"><a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/2013/06/update-the-national-academy-of-television-arts-sciences-announces-winners-for-the-40th-annual-daytime-entertainment-creative-arts-emmy-awards/#respond" title="Comment on UPDATE!  THE NATIONAL ACADEMY OF TELEVISION ARTS &amp; SCIENCES ANNOUNCES WINNERS FOR THE 40TH ANNUAL DAYTIME ENTERTAINMENT CREATIVE ARTS EMMY® AWARDS">No comments</a></span> 
        </div>
        
        <div class="entry clearfix">
            
            <img width="300" height="200" src="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/1Overview-1-300x200.jpg" class="alignleft featured_image wp-post-image" alt="1Overview (1)" />

<div id="AOLMsgPart_1_47acef29-789c-4578-a663-d8ce701a600c">
<div>
<div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">Friday night&#8217;s gala was held at the Westin Bonaventure in Los Angeles.   The National Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences (NATAS) tonight announced the winners of the 40th Annual Daytime Entertainment Creative Arts Emmy® Awards in a sold-out, grand gala for over 1,100 attendees at the Westin Bonaventure, in downtown Los Angeles. <span id="more-41588"></span></span><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> &#8221;The Daytime community is well and prospering as witnessed by this overwhelming turnout honoring the best in Daytime television in more than fifty five categories,&#8221; said Malachy Wienges, Chairman, NATAS.  &#8221;With so many stars here to salute those that work so hard behind the scenes, it&#8217;s a wonderful night for Daytime Television.&#8221;</span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">  </span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> <a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/2MalachyGWienges.jpg"><img class="alignright size-large wp-image-41592" alt="2MalachyGWienges" src="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/2MalachyGWienges-1024x682.jpg" width="1024" height="682" /></a></span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> Friday night&#8217;s show was hosted by Teresa Ganzel, Actress, Voice Actress and Comedian of The Tonight Show with Johnny Carson&#8221; fame and Voice-over Actor, Bob Begen, the voice of &#8220;Porky Pig&#8221; and others.  They were joined by an illustrious group of presenters such as Susan Flannery and John McCook of &#8220;The Bold and the Beautiful,&#8221; Melissa Claire Egan and Ignaccio Serricchio of &#8220;The Young and the Restless,&#8221; Arianne Zucker, James Reynolds and James Scott of &#8220;Days of Our Lives,&#8221; Teresa Castillo and Marc Anthony Samuel of &#8220;General Hospital,&#8221; Robert Scott Wilson, &#8220;All My Children,&#8221; Denyse Tontz, &#8220;All My Children,&#8221; Corbin Bleu, &#8220;One Life to Live,&#8221; David Rudman, Sesame Street Workshop, Yvette Gonzalez-Nacer, Tara Perry and Thomas Hobson, &#8220;Fresh Beat Band,&#8221; and Christina Ferrare and Mark Steines, &#8220;Home and Family.&#8221;  In addition, there were several special presenters such as Geri Jewell, &#8220;Facts of Life,&#8221; Mary McDonough, &#8220;The Waltons,&#8221; Leslie Miller, &#8220;More: One Life to Live,&#8221; and &#8220;More: All My Children,&#8221; and Tippi Hedren, the iconic actress from Alfred Hitchcock&#8217;s &#8220;The Birds.&#8221;  </span></div>
<div><a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/3DavidMichaels.jpg"><img class="alignright size-large wp-image-41594" alt="3DavidMichaels" src="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/3DavidMichaels-1024x682.jpg" width="1024" height="682" /></a></div>
<div></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> </span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The Daytime Entertainment Creative Arts Emmy® Awards recognize outstanding achievement in all fields of daytime television production and are presented to individuals and programs broadcast from 2:00 a.m.-6:00 p.m. during the 2012 calendar year.<br />
</span></div>
<div><a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/4BBTG.jpg"><img class="alignright size-large wp-image-41595" alt="4BBTG" src="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/4BBTG-1024x682.jpg" width="1024" height="682" /></a></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> </span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The 40th Annual Daytime Entertainment Creative Arts Emmy® Awards is a presentation of the National Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences in cooperation with the Academy of Television Arts and Sciences.  A breakdown of the awards by network, as compiled by the independent accounting firm of Lutz &amp; Carr, LLP, follows.<br />
</span></div>
<div></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">The Westin Bonaventure is the official hotel of the Daytime Entertainment Creative Arts Emmy® Awards.   </span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;"> </span></div>
<div><a href="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/5MMDRLM.jpg"><img class="alignright size-large wp-image-41596" alt="5MMDRLM" src="http://highlighthollywood.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/5MMDRLM-1024x682.jpg" width="1024" height="682" /></a></div>
<div><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">A full list of all nominees in all categories can be downloaded from our website at </span><a href="http://www.emmyonline.tv/" target="_blank"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">www.emmyonline.tv</span></a><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, serif;">.  A breakdown of the Creative Arts &amp; Entertainment Emmy® Awards by broadcast, cable and other entities follows:<br />

<div>About The National Academy of Television Arts &amp; SciencesThe National Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences (NATAS) is a professional service organization dedicated to the advancement of the arts and sciences of television and the promotion of creative leadership for artistic, educational and technical achievements within the television industry. It recognizes excellence in television with the coveted Emmy® Award for News &amp; Documentary, Sports, Daytime Entertainment, Daytime Creative Arts &amp; Entertainment, Public &amp; Community Service, and Technology &amp; Engineering.  Regional Emmy® Awards are given in 19 regions across the United States. Emmy® Awards given out for primetime programming are presented by our sister organization, the Academy of Television Arts &amp; Sciences (ATAS).  Beyond awards, NATAS has extensive educational programs including Regional Student Television and its Student Award for Excellence for outstanding journalistic work by high school students, as well as scholarships, publications, and major activities for both industry professionals and the viewing public.  For more information, please visit the website at <a href="http://www.emmyonline.tv/" target="_blank">www.emmyonline.tv</a></div>
