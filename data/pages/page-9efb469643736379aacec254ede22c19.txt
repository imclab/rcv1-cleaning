		<h1>Buying and Selling a Car</h1>
<h2>Details on the vehicle registration documents and paperwork  needed to buy or sell a car in Germany...</h2>

<p>New cars and used cars can be bought in Germany. Cars are sold through  dealerships, or privately, with a transfer from one owner to another.</p>

<p>This section features information on <a href="http://belgium.angloinfo.com/countries/belgium/carbuy.asp#buying"><strong></strong></a><strong>buying a new car</strong> in Germany, as well as details on how to go about the private sale or purchase of a <strong>second-hand used car</strong>.</p>

<h5>Purchase Assistance</h5>

<p><a class="external" target="_blank" href="http://berlin.angloinfo.com/redirect/url/www.adac.de/"><strong>ADAC</strong></a><strong> </strong>(<em>Allgemeiner Deutscher Automobil-Club</em> <em>e. V.</em>) is the national German car club, which offers many vehicle services:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Pre-purchase vehicle inspections, price guides, purchase contracts and     preparation of a bill of sale</li>
	<li>Advice when buying a used car motorbike or camper van </li>
	<li>Information on selecting a car which uses an alternative fuel</li>
	<li>Advice on everyday issues such as using snow chains correctly or installing child seats </li>
	<li>Provides a breakdown and recovery service</li>
	<li><strong><a class="external" target="_blank" href="http://berlin.angloinfo.com/redirect/url/www.adac.de/">ADAC</a> </strong>(<em>Allgemeiner Deutscher Automobil-Club</em> <em>e. V.</em>) <strong><br />
 Tel</strong>: 0180 5 10 11 12<a href="http://germany.angloinfo.com/r.asp?http://www.adac.de/"><strong></strong></a></li>
</ul>

<p><a class="external" target="_blank" href="http://berlin.angloinfo.com/redirect/url/www.vcd.org/"><strong>VCD</strong></a> (<em>Verkehrsclub Deutschland</em>) is the German transport association and, while more environmentally aware than ADAC, it offers similar services.</p></article><div class="nextandlast"><div style="float:left;"><a title="Vehicle Ownership" href="/information/transport/vehicle-ownership/">&laquo; Vehicle Ownership</a></div><div style="float:right;"><a title="Buying a New Car" href="/information/transport/vehicle-ownership/buying-a-new-car/">Buying a New Car &raquo;</a></div><div class="clear"></div>
