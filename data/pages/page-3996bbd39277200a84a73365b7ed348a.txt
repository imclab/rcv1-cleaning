			<p>At the Los Angeles Auto Show Chrysler has introduced the all-new 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible, a completely redesigned car with a retractable electric roof that is built on the new D-segment platform.</p>
		</div>
	</div>
	</div>





<div id="article-box">
	<h2> Design &#8211; The Video</h2>
<p>In the below video, designer <strong>Joe Dehner </strong>speaks about the exterior and interior styling of the 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible and demonstrates its retractable hardtop roof.</p>

<p><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/4/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible-3.jpg" alt="2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible" width="355" height="226" border="0" class="image-right" /></a></p>
<p> The Chrysler  Sebring Convertible has long held the honor of America&#8217;s favorite convertible: it   has earned the title of best-selling convertible in the United States for seven  of the past 11 years.</p>
<p> The 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible will be available in U.S. dealerships in the second quarter of  2007 and in global volume markets in the second half of 2007. </p>
<h2>Design</h2>
<p>
    Chrysler designers looked at the future brand showroom when seeking inspiration  for the 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible.</p>
<p><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/3/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible-2.jpg" alt="2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible" width="355" height="226" border="0" class="image-right" /></a></p>
<p> From the original sketches to the  production vehicle, the all-new convertible was designed to provide a familial  tie to its new sedan sibling, and the resemblance is unmistakable.</p>
<p> Beyond that,  the convertible stretches the look to create usable space in the trunk when the  convertible top is stowed.</p>
<p> <em>&ldquo;Top up or  down, the all-new Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s design captures the essence of the  Chrysler brand,&rdquo;</em> said <strong>Trevor Creed</strong>, Senior Vice President &ndash; Design, Chrysler  Group. <em>&ldquo;The all-new 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s design is a showcase  for the expressive, refined, athletic and passionate.&rdquo;</em></p>
<p class="image-right"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/2004-Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible.jpg" width="355" height="221" />The previous Chrysler Sebring Convertible (2004) </p>
<p>From the  front view, the new Sebring Convertible shares many traits with its sedan  sibling, including the distinctive face of Chrysler, the brand&rsquo;s signature  eggcrate grille and a uniquely sculptured hood that features the Chrysler  brand&rsquo;s elegant lines. </p>
<p>The Chrysler winged medallion is centered at the top of  the grille just below the leading edge of the hood.</p>
<p> Large quad headlamps wrap  around to the side of the vehicle, where the all-new Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s  profile takes on a shape all its own. </p>

<p> The all-new  2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s wheelbase is 2.9 inches longer, and the car  is 3.5 inches taller and more than 2 inches wider than the vehicle it replaces.  Its overall length also is 3 inches longer than the sedan, which lengthens the  side profile and provides enough cargo room in the trunk to hold two golf bags  when the roof is retracted. </p>
<p class="image-right"><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/2/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/2007-Chrysler-Sebring-Sedan.jpg" alt="Chrysler Sebring Sedan" width="355" height="221" border="0" /></a>The Convertible is based on the 2007 Chrysler Sebring Sedan </p>
<p>Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s profile is defined by a long  hood, a long roofline and a high chrome beltline, all of which give the vehicle  a nicely proportioned, elegant greenhouse with the top up. </p>
<p>Designers also  minimized the width of the C-pillar, improving the convertible&rsquo;s sleek side  profile and increasing rearward visibility when the convertible top is up.  </p>
<p>Sculpted character lines run across the body, underneath the door handles and  across the lower bodyside, producing the Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s athletic stance. </p>
<p> From the  rear, wide tail lamps are mounted on the corners and deck lid for visibility  and visual impact. Rear fog lamps mounted in the backup lamps help maintain a  uniform appearance among models sold around the world. Chrome-tipped dual  exhaust outputs hint at the Limited model&rsquo;s performance capability when  equipped with the 3.5-liter V-6 engine and six-speed automatic transaxle.</p>
<h2>    Interior</h2>
<p><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/7/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible-interior-1.jpg" alt="2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible - Interior" width="355" height="267" border="0" class="image-right" /></a> </p>
<p>The all-new 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s interior features a   clean design with  focus on quality, craftsmanship and  execution. Soft surfaces on interior touch points, including the instrument  panel cover and door arm rests, complement the convertible&rsquo;s luxurious  interior. </p>
<p> Inspired by  the Chrysler winged badge, the convertible&rsquo;s center stack features a Chrysler  signature analog clock, which is surrounded by dual heating and air  conditioning vents. Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s instrument panel also includes a  locking glove box that can secure important documents and small items. </p>
<p>The  center floor console features dual storage bins and a sliding armrest that  moves 3 inches to provide a comfortable position for all drivers. </p>
<p><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/8/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible-interior-2.jpg" alt="2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible - Interior" width="355" height="267" border="0" class="image-right" /></a></p>
<p> As many as  four occupants can experience the new Sebring Convertible&rsquo;s comfortable, roomy  interior. Occupants sit 2.5 inches higher than they did in the previous Sebring  Convertible model, giving them a command-of-the-road seating position. Rear  seat passengers also have plenty of leg and head room, making this a functional  convertible that can be enjoyed year round in all seasons. </p>
<p> All 2008  Chrysler Sebring Convertible models feature a <strong>two-tone interior theme</strong> with dark  and lighter shades of Khaki or Gray on the instrument panel and door trim. The  Chrysler Sebring Convertible Limited model also features an exclusive Cream and  Pebble Beige interior with leather seats. Interior accents include Alloy Silver  and chrome on the Sebring Convertible model, Satin Silver and chrome on the  Touring model and Satin Silver, chrome and Tortoise Shell on the Limited model.</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<p>    The 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible is available in the United States in  three models: Chrysler Sebring Convertible, Chrysler Sebring Convertible  Touring and Chrysler Sebring Convertible Limited. </p>
<p> Three engine  options power the 2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible sold in the United States:  the standard 2.4-liter four-cylinder World Engine, an available Flexible Fuel  Vehicle (FFV) 2.7-liter V-6 engine and an available 3.5-liter V-6 engine  coupled with a new six-speed automatic transaxle with Auto Stick. </p>
<p>Compared with the model it replaces, the new  Sebring Convertible&#8217;s body structure is substantially stiffer than its  predecessor in bending and torsion &mdash; especially bending &mdash; due to very large  sills. In fact, its new front-wheel-drive architecture and the three-box  vehicle design contribute to a body that is 2.5 times stiffer in twist and 1.5  times stiffer in bending than the previous generation convertible</p>
<p><a href="/gallery/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/9/" target="_blank"><img src="/archive/2006/12/13-chrysler-sebring-convertible/Chrysler-Sebring-Convertible-interior-3.jpg" alt="2008 Chrysler Sebring Convertible - Interior" width="355" height="267" border="0" class="image-right" /></a></p>
<p>The 2008  Chrysler Sebring Convertible features MyGIG&trade;, a cutting-edge information,  entertainment and safety navigation audio system that   features a 6.5-inch Thin Film Transistor (TFT) Display with a touch-screen  panel that can support 65,000 colors, providing a three-dimensional appearance  to graphics and animation, as well as multiple font sizes and styles. </p>
<p>The MyGIG  system follows voice-activated commands and includes many new features for  music, sound, movies and personalized picture displays, including a 20 gigabyte hard disc drive, Universal Serial Bus (USB)       for both MP3 connectivity and downloading of WMA, MP3 and JPEG files and SIRIUS&reg; Satellite Radio,       which provides customers with more than 125 channels of the best music, </p>
<p align="right">(Source: Chrysler)</p>
