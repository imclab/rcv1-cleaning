<h1 style="text-align: center;">Events</h1>
<h3 style="text-align: center;">Current
Events<br>
</h3>
<p><font style="font-weight: bold;">FALC Exhibits:</font>
FALC has exhibits of the members' artwork showing almost continuously
at 12 locations including: the Cary Ballet Conservatory, the Glenaire
Retirement
Center, the International Ballet Academy. 2 Fonville Morrisey offices
and
in the hallways leading to
the FALC Office.&nbsp; See
the <a href="#FALC_Events_Calendar">Events Calendar</a>
below for information about the current exhibits.&nbsp;
Additionally, see the <a href="#Schedule_of_FALC_Sponsored_Individual_Artists_Exhibits">Individual
Artist's Exhibits</a> schedule below which gives dates for
individual FALC member artist's exhibits at several local businesses.
&nbsp;<a href="#Exhibit_Facilities_&amp;_Addresses">The
Exhibit Facilities &amp; Addresses Table</a> below gives
address information for all these locations and a link to a map showing
them. <br>
</p>

<h3>Upcoming Events<br>
</h3>
<p><a href="2013_Annual_Show_Main.htm"><span style="font-weight: bold;"></span></a><span style="font-weight: bold;">FALC 11th Annual
Senior Show:</span>
&nbsp;Will be held at the&nbsp;<a href="http://www.townofcary.org/Departments/Parks__Recreation___Cultural_Resources/Facilities/Senior_Center.htm" target="_blank">Cary Senior Center</a>, 120
Maury
O'Dell Place, Cary, NC 27513 from August 26 through
September&nbsp;19,&nbsp;2013.
&nbsp;This
show will feature&nbsp;two-dimensional
artworks&nbsp;by local residents over the age
of 55. Maximum size for works is 18 x 24 inches including frame.
Monetary awards will be presented at the Reception and
Awards Ceremony&nbsp;on Friday August&nbsp;30.&nbsp;The
show will be
judged by Marcelll Hooks. Entry Forms are available <a href="Senior%20Art%20Show%20-%202013.pdf" target="_blank">here
as a PDF download</a> or&nbsp;at the Senior Center
(919-469-4081).&nbsp;See the&nbsp;<a href="events.htm#FALC_Events_Calendar">Events Calendar</a>
for &nbsp;receiving &nbsp;hanging, and pick up dates
&amp; times. The show chair is Betty Charlton
&nbsp;919-467-5325.<span style="font-weight: bold;"></span>
</p>

<h3 align="center">Recent Events</h3>
<div style="text-align: left;">
<div style="text-align: center;">
<p><a style="font-weight: bold;" href="2013_Teen_Show.htm">The FALC 2013 Teen Art Show</a>
was on display from May 12
through June 2 at the Frankie G. Weems Art Gallery on the Meredith
College campus in Raleigh, NC. The reception and awards presentation
was on Sunday afternoon May 12. Students from ten high schools entered
40 artworks.
FALC cash awards were presented to the 3 top winners (with matching
awards to their schools) and Askew-Taylor
and Jerry's Artarama each awarded two&nbsp; Honorable Mention gift
certificates.&nbsp;The
show's chairperson&nbsp;was Anne Calvignac.<a href="http://www.fineartsleagueofcary.org/2013_annual_show_report.html"><span style="font-weight: bold;"></span></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.fineartsleagueofcary.org/2013_annual_show_report.html"><span style="font-weight: bold;">The FALC 2013 Annual Juried Art
Show</span></a>
This
show,&nbsp;FALC's&nbsp;major event of the year, was on
display from March 28 until April 21, 2013&nbsp;at the&nbsp;
Page-Walker Arts and History Center in&nbsp;Cary, NC.&nbsp;The
reception
and awards
ceremony was on Sunday afternoon April 7th. Cash awards
totaling&nbsp;$2800 plus
several&nbsp;non-cash awards were presented.&nbsp; One-hundred
and fifty-seven artists
entered 382 pieces. The juror: <a href="http://www.linkedin.com/pub/elaine-gustafson/17/170/307" target="_blank">Elaine D. Guftafson</a> selected 74
works by 74 artists.&nbsp;View the&nbsp;full <a href="http://www.fineartsleagueofcary.org/2013_annual_show_report.html">Show
Report</a> pages.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">ART55 -
Exhibition&nbsp;of Art by Local Artists over 55:</span>
Co-sponsored by The Friends of The Cary Senior Center, Inc. and the
Fine Arts League of
Cary.&nbsp; The show was on exhibit until March 22,
2013&nbsp;at the center. This
show&nbsp;was limited to
the first 55 two dimensional&nbsp;works
submitted. The Artists&#8217; Reception and Awards Ceremony was on
February&nbsp;22th. The awards were:&nbsp;First Place - Wade
Carmichael
for&nbsp;<span style="font-style: italic;">Bronze Koi;</span>
Second Place - Rocky Alexander for&nbsp;<span style="font-style: italic;">Afternoon Pick-Me-Up;</span>
Third Place - Niranjan Vora for&nbsp;<span style="font-style: italic;">Portrait of Granddaughter in
Traditional Indian Dressing</span>.
Honorable Mentions went to Gail Torbeck&nbsp; for <span style="font-style: italic;">Cigar</span>
and Judy Dunson&nbsp;for&nbsp;<span style="font-style: italic;">Landed Egret. </span>The
show judge was Dan Nelson, well known local artist.</p>
<p><a href="2012_Members_Show.htm"><span style="font-weight: bold;"> Members &nbsp;Show:</span></a>
&nbsp;This show was on display
from November 28 through&nbsp;Jan 2nd, 2013, at
the <a href="http://www.fineartsleagueofcary.org/events.htm#---">Page-Walker
Art
&amp; History Center</a>.&nbsp;
Eighty-two&nbsp;FALC
members each entered one
piece of artwork.&nbsp; The judge was &nbsp;Jennifer
Dasal, the
associate curator of contemporary art at the North Carolina Museum of
Art. The
show reception and presentation of awards was
on Sunday, December 2&nbsp;at the Page-Walker.</p>
<p><span style="font-weight: bold;">FALC 10th Annual
Senior Show:</span>
&nbsp;Was&nbsp;on exhibit at the Cary Senior Center 120
Maury
O'Dell Place, Cary, NC 27513 through September 21,&nbsp;2012.
&nbsp;This
show featured
71 two-dimensional
artworks&nbsp;by local residents over the age
of 55.&nbsp; Awards totaling
$405 were presented at the Reception and
Awards Ceremony&nbsp;on Friday August&nbsp;31. Roger J. Leber
was the
First Place winner for Arizona Sunrise, Second Place went to Paulette
Wright for Rodney and&nbsp;Kim Werfel's Fire and Ice
was&nbsp;the Third
Place winner. Honorable Mentions were presented to: George Carmichael,
Lou Cicardo, Barbara Dowd, Meera Goyal, Bettina Lewicki, Mark
Luczak,&nbsp; Joyce McKee, Lynn Patton, Sarah Sheffield, Sally
Sikes,
John Sweemer and&nbsp; Tracey Williams.The show was judged by Luana
Luconi Winner and the show
chairman was Don Hamilton (919-535-8942).</p>
