<p>This online auto repair videos section is a quick and easy way to 
learn more about DIY car repairs. On this page is a video that shows how
 to dig through information quickly in a web based service manual. Also 
how to find things that at first seem to be missing. More often then not
 what you need is in there but finding it is hard.

</p><p>More importantly this page will lead you to more clips about a 
wide variety of car repair subjects. Some of these videos I made myself 
and are available in other places on the you fix cars website. Also 
available is a new on demand training series that lets you stream 
technician training sessions about an hour long for around 3 bucks from 
Amazon.
</p>

</p><p>Some people learn faster by watching videos as opposed to reading

people want I built this page to connect you to some of the most 
educational automotive repair videos I could find. 

</p><p>Below you will find a brief description of each one followed by a text link that leads to the individual video page

most out of it.
