                <h2><a href="http://sandrarose.com/2013/08/lamar-odom-got-what-he-wanted-clippers-wont-re-sign-him/" rel="bookmark">Lamar Odom Got What He Wanted: Clippers Won&#8217;t Re-Sign Him</a></h2>
            <div class="postmeta20">Tuesday, August 27, 2013</div>

				<p><img src="http://sandrarose.com/images18/Lamar-Odom-khloe-kardashian-550x586.jpg?42e305" width="550" height="586" border="0" alt="Lamar Odom and Khloe Kardashian"/></p>
<p>The LA Clippers have announced they will not re-sign free agent Lamar Odom to a new contract, amid rumors of drug addictions and a cheating scandal. That&#8217;s probably exactly what Odom wanted.</p>
<p>According to <strong><a href="http://www.lakersnation.com/lakers-rumors-lamar-odom-still-wants-to-be-a-laker/2013/08/25/" target="_blank">Lakers Nation blog</a></strong>, Odom, 33, is still holding on to the hope that his former team, the Los Angeles Lakers, will sign him to a new contract.</p>
<p>Odom&#8217;s life has been an emotional roller coaster ever since he was dropped by the Lakers. Odom also played briefly for the Dallas Mavericks who cut him loose after a few games in 2012. The 6&#8242;-10&#8243; NBA forward has been living in a hotel as rumors swirl that he cheated on Khloe Kardashian with at least 2 groupies.</p>
