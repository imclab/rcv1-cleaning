<h2><span>What</span></h2>
<span itemprop="description">Get lost in the music and have fun at one of the fabulous events during the Pawleys Island Festival of Music and Art. You can purchase your tickets online or by calling the Festival’s office. For further information please visit the event's page. </span><br/><br/>

Chalk Under the Oaks: 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Saturday, September 28 & Sunday, September 29<br/>

Marily McCoo & Billy Davis, Jr.: 7 p.m. Saturday, October 5<br/>
Teach My People Collaborative Fundraiser Featuring Elise Testone: 6 p.m. Sunday, October 6<br/>
Emile Pandolfi: 7 p.m. Wednesday, October 9<br/>
Vivace: 7 p.m. Thursday, October 10<br/>
Mac Arnold & Plate Full O'Blues: 7 p.m. Friday, October 11<br/>
