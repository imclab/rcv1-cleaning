<p>2011 marks the 10th Annual Sonoran Arts League Festival of Fine Art in Cave Creek. This Cave Creek Art Show showcases 150 local and nationally-acclaimed painters,  sculptors, jewelers, photographers, as well as wood, glass and mixed  media artists. Come and enjoy 3 fun days of art, food and live  entertainment! Sponsored by the Sonoran Arts League. </p>
<p><strong>LOCATION</strong>: StageCoach Village</p>
<p><strong>DATES AND TIMES</strong>: February 18th - 20th from 10 - 5 pm</p>
<p><strong>COST</strong>: FREE Admission</p>
<p><strong>PHONE</strong>: 480-575-6624</p>
<p><strong>WEBSITE</strong>: <a href="http://SonoranArtsLeague.org" target="_blank" title="Sonoran Arts League | Cave Creek Art Shows">SonoranArtsLeague.org</a></p>
