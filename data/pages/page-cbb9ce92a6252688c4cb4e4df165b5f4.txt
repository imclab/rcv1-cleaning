<div class="entry-content"><p style="text-align: left;">Parker Colorado is alive with the arts and entertainment for all ages. The links below provide more information about the options available to residents of the Town of Parker and surrounding areas of Douglas County.</p>

<h2>P.A.C.E. Center Parker Arts &amp; Cultural Event Center</h2>
<p><strong><em>The Parker Performing Arts Center</em></strong></p>
<p>October 29th, 2011 marked the opening of South Denver&#8217;s newest arts center the P.A.C.E. Center in Parker.</p>
<div id="attachment_244" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 310px"><a href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/arts-entertainment/harmony-park-concert/" rel="attachment wp-att-244"><img class="size-medium wp-image-244" title="harmony parker pace center parker co" alt="harmony parker pace center parker co" src="http://www2.parkercolorado.net/wp-content/parkcouploads/harmony-park-concert-300x188.jpg" width="300" height="188" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Harmony Park Open House PACE Center</p></div>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong><a title="PACE Parker Arts Culture Events Center" href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/pace-parker-arts-culture-eventscenter/" target="_blank">Parker Arts and Cultural Center</a>. You&#8217;ll find the latest <a title="PACE Parker Arts Cultural events center events parker co" href="http://www.ourparkercolorado.com/pace-center/" target="_blank">PACE Center</a> Upcoming events here.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong>Four Cultural Partners were chosen for the PACE Center and they include the:</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong><a href="http://www.apexdance.org/index.php">Apex Contemporary Dance Theatre</a></strong> is a professional dance company dedicated to producing innovative dance concerts that entertain, engage and inspire. Co-founded by David Reuille and Heidi Nichter in 2008 and headquartered in Parker, the company also offers ongoing classes and educational outreach productions for schools in Colorado.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>The <a href="http://www.coloradowindensemble.org/">Colorado Wind Ensemble</a>’s</strong> mission is to provide opportunities for musicians to create, and audiences to experience, exceptional live performances of wind ensemble music. As the only wind ensemble in the Denver Metro area outside of academia, the Colorado Wind Ensemble performs music from all eras. The group is in its 29th season and is led by Conductor and Musical Director Matthew J. Roeder, DMA.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>The <a href="http://www.parkerchorale.org/">Parker Chorale</a> </strong>was formed in 2010 as a community group dedicated to fostering the arts and supporting music education in the Town of Parker and Douglas County. Lead by John Polinski, Artistic Director and Conductor, this adult chorus is devoted to sharing the gift of song through music performance.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;"><strong>The <a href="http://www.sscorchestra.org/concertinfo.php">Parker Symphony Orchestra</a></strong>, also known as the South Suburban Community Orchestra, was founded in 1994 and is led by Director René Knetch. The Orchestra provides opportunities for musical expression to many who would otherwise not have that chance.</p>
<p><a title="lone tree arts center" href="https://www.lonetreeartscenter.org" target="_blank"><strong>Lone Tree Arts Center</strong> </a>- This Arts Venue was the first in Douglas County and has recently gone into a partnership with the PACE Center to form the<a title="South Metro Childrens Theater a collaboration of Lone Tree and PACE Center" href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/2012/05/south-metro-childrens-theater-a-collaboration-of-lone-tree-and-pace-center/"> South Metro Childrens Theater Group</a>. The Arts Center will present critically-acclaimed national performing artists and will launch new relationships with an impressive group of Colorado’s professional arts companies by inviting them to perform at the Lone Tree Arts Center, the first publicly financed professional arts facility in the South Metro Denver Area.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong><a title="Parker Events Calendar" href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/calendar" target="_blank">See Local Events Calendar Here!</a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong><a href="http://parkerartistsguild.com./">Parker Artists Guild</a><br />
</strong>This group sponsors the Spring Art Show the end of March and Art in the Park the last weekend in August</p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center"><strong><a title="wildlife experience parker lone tree colorado kids" href="http://www.thewildlifeexperience.org/" target="_blank">The Wildlife Experience Parker Colorado</a> - </strong>Family friendly events and educational experiences throughout the year.</p>
<h3>Parker Chamber Events</h3>
<p>The Parker Chamber of Commerce is very active in the community and sponsors several events each year for the community of Parker</p>
<ul>
<li><a title="Parker Days Summer Festival Photos Video Parker Colorado" href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/festival/" target="_blank">Parker Days &#8211; Always the 2nd weekend of June.</a></li>
<li>Wine Walks on Mainstreet &#8211; May through October</li>
<li><a title="Free Movies in O’Brien Park Summer 2013" href="http://www.parkercolorado.net/free-movies-parker-colorado/" target="_blank">Parker Chamber Picture Show in the Park &#8211; Free family movies at O&#8217;Brien Park July &#8211; September</a></li>
</ul>
<p>Bringing the oldies back to the days of the drive-in theater, and allowing us to share those fun times with our kids who have never heard of one, the Parker Chamber has created fun, free, outdoor movie in the park events that are a blast for everyone.</p>
<p>Chamber of Commerce picture show in Parker Colorado each summer in O&#8217;Brien Park. Show up early to get the best seats, bring your own food, blankets, chairs and snacks or support the various fundraisers each week by purchasing your popcorn, pop and candy for a freakishly inexpensive rate of a buck a piece. Yes, just $1.00 buys you any of the snacks and answer trivia questions and get chances to win T-shirts, fun giveaways by the sponsors and just bragging rights among your neighbors or siblings. Watch the calendar for dates and movie&#8217;s each year.</p>
<p><strong>The best things in life really are free in Parker Colorado.</strong></p>
