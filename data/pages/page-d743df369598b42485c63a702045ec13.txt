<h2 align="left" class="h2_magenta">Capable of fixing any car!</h2>

<p align="left">Our highly trained staff, technical expertise, and state-of-the-art equipment allow us to service foreign and domestic cars, including BMW, Lexus, Toyota, Mercedes, Ford, Mazda, VW, Audi, Porsche, Volvo, Jaguar, Honda, Acura, GM, Chrysler, & Jeep. We do all factory approved maintenance services.</p>

<p align="left">Conveniently located in the heart of Silicon Valley, in Sunnyvale, Advanced Auto Repair Center performs all of the services a car may need, excluding body and upholstery work. (These may be done through our facility as a sublet repair.)</p>

<p align="left">We provide free estimates by e-mail, fax, or in person. Service recommendations are based on the technical needs of the car, time constraints, and budget considerations of each individual customer.</p>

<p align="left">Cars are handled with the level of care and cleanliness expected in this highly technical environment.</p>

<p align="left">For the convenience of our customers we provide a no cost ride to work or home, and for major repairs make reservation for low cost car rental.</p>

<p align="left">For those who prefer to do the work themselves; we can assist in purchasing quality new or used parts for lower than the average market price.</p>

<p align="left">We support recycling programs for oil, anti-freeze, and freon.</p>
