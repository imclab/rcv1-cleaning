<p style="text-align : center"><span style="color : #0000ff"><em><strong><span style="font-size : xx-large">Let's Ride!</span></strong></em></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span style="color : #000000">How cool is it going to be when you are riding around town and you know the driver of another passing convertible? How about when you park in a lot and you say...</span><span style="text-decoration : underline"><span style="color : #ff0000"><em><strong>Oh look "So and so" is here<span style="color : #ff0000">!</span></strong></em></span></span><span style="color : #000000">? That's my goal, for all of us to share that sense of community wherever we are...all over town.</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000"> </span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000">Care to share the dream? Then put your clothes on and your tops down... let's go cruise and have some fun!  I believe there is something</span> <strong><span style="color : #ff0000 ; font-size : large"><em>unique</em></span></strong> <span style="color : #000000">about people that love convertible cars, and I bet you will agree with me on that!</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000">The events will be what we want to create, from good beer to fine wine and of course from greasy burgers to the BLT. The ideas are limitless!</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000">Please take a moment to share your favorite drive ..and why...on the message board, since we will try to join you!</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000">Let's go take some drives, have some wine and get some good eats!</span></p>
<p><span style="color : #000000"> </span></p>
