                        <p style="width: 698px; margin-left: 40px"><span>The Spring Festival of <strong>Lx's</strong> has its roots in Southern California as an enthusiast gathering for the hottest car platform to hit the American car market in years. Starting in 2004 (for 2005 model years), <strong>LX</strong> first referred to the new generation vehicle platform for the Chrysler 300 and Dodge Magnum.&#160; For the 2006 model year, the Dodge Charger was added.&#160; We now use the <strong>Lx</strong> designation to generically refer to first generation <strong>LX</strong> as well as now encompassing the next generation platforms for <strong>LX</strong> (Chrysler 300), <strong>LC</strong> (Dodge Challenger) and <strong>LD</strong> (Dodge Charger).&#160; </span></p>
                        <p style="width: 698px; margin-left: 40px"><span>As a group of SoCal car enthusiasts, our first meet in 2004 consisted of only 4 vehicles.&#160; Our next few meets in 2005 quickly grew to approach 100 vehicles. Starting in 2006, we kicked it up a notch and surpassed 300 vehicles with participants from all over the US and Canada. This event has grown every year since and we now expect over 1000 vehicles for the 2012 Spring Festival. As a community and enthusiast event, we have the tremendous support of Chrysler, Dodge, Mopar and SRT.&#160; We have had many executives in attendance from both the local Irvine office as well as Auburn Hills.&#160; The Spring Festival is becoming one of their favorite events as well!</span></p>
                        <p style="width: 698px; margin-left: 40px"><span>As participants at this event, you share the passion we have for these cars. Through the various online forums, we post photos of our cars, our mods and ask questions. The Spring Festival is the place where you will see it all first hand and talk directly to other members that you have become friends with online. You may be a senior member or a newbie, have a full blown modified car or a totally stock car, it makes no difference. If it will be your first event you will not believe how great the people are. This is an experience of a lifetime and those who return year after year will tell you so. That's why they post and encourage everyone to attend. We hope to see you there. <br />
