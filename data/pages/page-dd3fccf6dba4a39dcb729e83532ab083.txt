    <div id="centertext">
      <p>Maynard embraces a vibrant arts community both in town and with our neigbors. Downtown features a number of galleries and stores, a neighborhood movie theatre (one of the few left of its kind), and a thriving arts center a short walk from the center of town.</p>
      <h2>Fine Arts / Crafts</h2>
      <p><a href="http://www.artspacemaynard.com/home.htm" target="_blank"><strong>ArtSpace Maynard</strong></a> is a non-profit arts center that features 43 studios for 70 artists, a large gallery for mid-size group exhibits, and a public printmaking studio. ArtSpace also hosts the Acme Theatre (see below) and a variety of community and special programs for the arts.</p>
      <p><a href="http://www.paintnpourma.com" target="_blank"><strong>Paint N' Pour</strong></a> on Main Street mixes art and fun by offering painting classes in a group setting.</p>
      <p>Downtown you will find stores such as <strong><a href="/biz/artandsoul/">Art &amp; Soul</a></strong>, <a href="http://earthchangespottery.com" target="_blank"><strong>Earth Changes Pottery</strong></a>, <strong><a href="http://gallerysevenmaynard.com" target="_blank">Gallery Seven</a></strong>, and <a href="http://www.marqueephotoworks.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Marquee Photoworks</strong></a> with a wide variety of fine art, photography, pottery, and crafts.</p>
      <p>The Gallery of the <a href="http://www.concordart.org/" target="_blank">Concord Art Association</a> is a regional center for the arts located just down the road a bit in Concord.</p>
      <h2>Theatre</h2>
      <p>Community theatre is strong both in Maynard and in surrounding towns. Maynard's own <a href="http://www.acmetheater.com/" target="_blank"><strong>ACME Theatre</strong></a> is run by volunteers who love producing quality community theatre. An award-winning company, they stage several shows a year.</p>
      <p>Our neigbors in  Acton and Sudbury also have active community theatre programs.</p>
      <h2>Movies</h2>
      <p>The <a href="http://www.famaynard.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Fine Arts Theatre</strong></a> on Summer Street combines the charm of yesterday's movie house with today's technology. You can see the latest blockbuster movies in Dolby Surround Sound - without the mall traffic!</p>
      <h2>Museums</h2>
      <p>While Maynard has no museums, we are fortunate to have two top-ranking institutions nearby. <a href="http://www.discoverymuseums.org/" target="_blank">The Discovery Museums</a> in South Acton provides education and fun for children of all ages. The <a href="http://www.decordova.org/" target="_blank">DeCordova Museum and Sculpture Park</a>, 10 miles east in Lincoln, focuses on modern and contemporary American art - with an emphasis on New England artists.</p>
      <h2>Music</h2>
      <p>The sounds of the <a href="http://maynardcommunityband.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Maynard Community Band</strong></a> fills Memorial Park during their free summer concerts. If you like performing or listening to great band music, you should try to catch a performance. Bring the kids!</p>
      <p>You'll often find bands playing at <a href="http://www.moreystavern.com/" target="_blank"><strong>Morey's Tavern</strong></a> or the <a href="http://www.myspace.com/pleasantcafeopenmic" target="_blank"><strong>Pleasant Cafe</strong></a> on Main Street.</p>
      <p>A few miles north on Route 2A the <a href="http://www.actonjazzcafe.com/" target="_blank">Acton Jazz Cafe</a> features live jazz, blues, and folk music every Thursday through Sunday night. In nearby Concord center <a href="http://concordscolonialinn.com/restaurants/events-a-entertainment.html" target="_blank">The Colonial Inn</a> features local artists in their Village Forge Tavern nightly.</p>
      <p>Like to sing? Consider joining the <a href="http://www.threeriverschorus.org/" target="_blank"><strong>Three Rivers Chorus</strong></a>.</p>
