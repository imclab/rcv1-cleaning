											<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://artscomments.wordpress.com/2013/04/27/little-one-movie-review/" rel="bookmark">Little One (Movie&nbsp;Review)</a></h2>					
					<div class="entry-meta">
													<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://artscomments.wordpress.com/2013/04/27/little-one-movie-review/" title="11:13" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">April 27, 2013</span></a>							<span class="by-author"><span class="sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://artscomments.wordpress.com/author/moirads/" title="View all posts by moirads" rel="author">moirads</a></span> </span>											</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
						<p>Title:  Little One<br />
Directed:  Darrell Roodt (written by Darrell Roodt)<br />
Starring:  Vuyelwa Msimang as Little One and Lindiwe Ndlovu as Pauline and Mutodi Neshehe as Detective Morena<br />
Date: 2012<br />
Genre:  Drama<br />
Rating:  5/10</p>
<p><a href="http://artscomments.files.wordpress.com/2013/04/little-one.jpg"><img src="http://artscomments.files.wordpress.com/2013/04/little-one.jpg?w=640&#038;h=648" alt="Little One" width="640" height="648" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2355" /></a></p>
<p><em>Little One</em> is a 2012 South African drama film directed by Darrell Roodt. The film was selected as the South African entry for the Best Foreign Language Oscar at the 85th Academy Awards, but it did not make the final shortlist.</p>
<p>There were lots of reasons for it not making the final shortlist.</p>
<p>Firstly it is not well written.  The story line has huge gaping holes in it in every direction.  Talking about direction, Darrell Roodt just fails to make any effort, never mind be meticulous about things.  And then it is emotionally manipulative rather than dramatically powerful.</p>
<p>It is sad, really, for this movie was an opportunity to make a powerful and relevant social point about child rape and abuse and it nearly completely fails because of poor artistic merit. </p>
<p>A lot of the value assigned to it in the rating is because of the power of the basic story line and some nice acting.  </p>
