	<h3>Arts, Music &amp; Entertainment in Omaha</h3>
		<p><img src="http://www.cityof.com/images/arts-entertainment-icon.png" alt="Arts, Music &amp; Entertainment in Omaha" class="floatLeft" />From the orchestra to opera to modern performing arts centers and historic theater venues, discover the best entertainment that Omaha has to offer. Check out our list of venues and theater companies to experience the arts, music and entertainment scene in this city.</p>

       <div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/opera-omaha.jpg" alt="Opera Omaha" width="170" height="37" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Reserve your box seats at <strong>Opera Omaha</strong>, to see the only professional opera company in the state of Nebraska. Enjoy an internationally renowned company as they perform at the 2,600 seat Orpheum Theater.  Electric operatic productions  are played out in unison with the Opera Omaha Chorus and the Omaha Symphony. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 1625 Farnam Street, # 100, Omaha, NE 68102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1625+farnam+street+number+100+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 346-7372 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.operaomaha.org/" target="_blank">www.operaomaha.org</a></p>
    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/omaha-symphony.jpg" alt="Omaha Symphony" width="170" height="55" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Don't miss the sounds of the <strong>Omaha Symphony</strong>. Delight at hearing the full orchestra perform over 200 concerts a year and engage in heralding entertainment and events like the &quot;Symphony Pops Series&quot; featuring renowned singers like LeAnn Rimes. Powerful music is promised from the Omaha symphony. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 1605 Howard Street, Omaha, NE 68102-2797 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1605+howard+street+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 342-3836 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.omahasymphony.org/" target="_blank">www.omahasymphony.org</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>

<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="omaha-performing-arts"></a><img src="images/omaha-performing-arts.jpg" alt="Omaha Performing Arts" width="170" height="77" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Huey Lewis and the News, Rock of Ages, and the Blue Man Group know that <strong>Omaha Performing Arts</strong> is the entertainment stage to be seen on. Be sure to get your ticket to see world-class concerts, Broadway shows and Opera performances. Get your ticket to the hottest entertainment in the city. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 1200 Douglas Street, Omaha, NE 68102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1200+douglas+street+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 345-0606 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.omahaperformingarts.org/" target="_blank">www.omahaperformingarts.org</a></p>
    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="quest-center"></a><img src="images/quest-center.jpg" alt="Qwest Center" width="170" height="42" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Get in line for the hottest sports events in Omaha at the <strong>Qwest Center</strong>. Omahans know the convention center and arena as home court to the Creighton Bluejays college basketball team and host to basketball, hockey games, professional wrestling events and big-name concerts like Katy Perry and Lil Wayne. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 455 North 10th Street, Omaha, NE 68102  - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=455+north+10th+street+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 341-1500 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.qwestcenteromaha.com/" target="_blank">www.qwestcenteromaha.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>

<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="joslyn-art-museum"></a><img src="images/joslyn-art-museum.jpg" alt="Joslyn Art Museum" width="170" height="59" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Art connoisseurs flock to the<strong> Joslyn Art Museum</strong> in Omaha to see fine art collections and installations, and discover paintings surrounded by an equally stunning museum. Art lovers also enjoy the grounds around the museum that host a Sculpture Garden and Discovery Garden, blooming with ornamental grasses, flowers, shrubs and trees.  
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 2200 Dodge Street, Omaha, NE 68102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=2200+dodge+street+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 342-3300 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.joslyn.org/" target="_blank">www.joslyn.org</a></p>
    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/omaha-community-playhouse.jpg" alt="Omaha Community Playhouse" width="170" height="46" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Marlon Brando appeared on stage at the <strong>Omaha Community Playhouse</strong>. Today, you can see this nationally recognized community theater group provide live theater entertainment, like productions of Broadway hits including Chicago and Hairspray.  Performances take place on the stage of one of the playhouse's two theaters, the Howard and Rhonda Hawks Mainstage Theater or the Howard Drew Theater. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 6915 Cass Street, Omaha, NE 68132 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=6915+cass+street+omaha+ne" target="_blank">MAP</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (402) 553-0800 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.omahaplayhouse.com/" target="_blank">www.omahaplayhouse.com</a></p>
	    </div>
