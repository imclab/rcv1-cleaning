<h1 class="contentHeader">Henderson Fine Arts Center</h1><h2><img alt="" style="width: 508px; height: 424px; vertical-align: top;" src="/~/media/Henderson/About/HFAC/FAC20Years.ashx" /></h2>
<p><img style="width: 292px; float: right; height: 520px;" alt="HAAA HFAC 20 Seasons Banner" src="/~/media/Henderson/About/HFAC/20 Seasons Banner.ashx" />Welcome to the Henderson Fine Arts Center, located on the campus of Henderson Community College. In this unique lake-side setting, Henderson Fine Arts Center&rsquo;s concerts and shows embrace a wide variety of the performing arts such as music, dance, drama, and comedy. We are&nbsp;the <strong>center for it all</strong>!&nbsp;<br />
<br />
The <a href="http://www.haaa.org/">Henderson Area Arts Alliance</a>, <strong><em>celebrating 20 seasons</em></strong>,&nbsp;presents a full season of <a href="http://www.henderson.kctcs.edu/About_Us/Henderson_Fine_Arts_Center/Upcoming_Events_and_Tickets.aspx">mainstage and family shows </a>at Henderson Fine Arts Center. <br />
<br />
The Center's fully-equipped&nbsp;venue includes the 981-seat McCormick Hall, Stagg Meeting Room, Scott Foyer and Logan Gallery. The&nbsp;galleries regularly display work of national recognition such as <a href="http://www.ohiovalleyart.org/">OVAL</a>&rsquo;s Kentucky National Wildlife Exhibit and the Art of Recycling Exhibit. <br />
<br />
In addition to being the focal point for the cultural arts in the community, Henderson Fine Arts Center can be utilized for seminars, conferences, meetings, receptions, and dinners. Our professional staff will cater to your needs with gracious service and attention to detail. <br />
<br />
Henderson Fine Arts Center Guild is the volunteer organization for the building. Numerous volunteer opportunities are available such as ushering, helping backstage, working the concessions, and assisting with special events. We invite you to be a part of Henderson Fine Arts Center Guild.&nbsp; Please call 270.831.9800 or&nbsp;<a href="mailto:rachael.baar@kctcs.edu">email</a> for more information.&nbsp;</p>
