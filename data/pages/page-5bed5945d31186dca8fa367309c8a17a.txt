	<h3>Arts, Music &amp; Entertainment in Oklahoma City</h3>
		<p><img src="http://www.cityof.com/images/arts-entertainment-icon.png" alt="Arts, Music &amp; Entertainment in Oklahoma City" class="floatLeft" />It's show time in Oklahoma City!  The city's line up of arts, music and entertainment makes for a standing ovation.  Browse the latest art exhibitions and museum exhibits.  Step out day or night and catch a concert featuring music rich in blues, rock, and of course country; or get your tickets to brilliant theatrical shows and exciting sporting events.  There's so much to experience, you'll wonder how you will be able to do it all.</p>

       <div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="oklahoma-city-museum"></a><img src="images/ok-museum-of-art.jpg" alt="Oklahoma City Museum" width="170" height="54" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Immerse yourself in the world of art at the <strong>Oklahoma City Museum</strong>, located in the downtown Oklahoma City Arts District. Stroll through the three-story museum to see art collections spanning 500 years. See the downtown skyline on the Roof Terrace; enjoy lunch at the Museum Caf&eacute; and find keepsake souvenirs at the OKCMOA Museum Store. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 415 Couch Dr Oklahoma City, OK 73102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=415+couch+dr+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 236-3100 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.okcmoa.com/" target="_blank">www.okcmoa.com</a></p>
    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
		
<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/jrb-art.jpg" alt="JRB" width="170" height="66" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Art connoisseurs, flock to the award winning <strong>JRB</strong> Art gallery located in Oklahoma City's historic Paseo Arts District. You will see works by emerging, established, and international artists showcased in the 4,500 space.  Whether you are a first time art buyer or art snob, make your next important art acquisition here.  Paintings, drawings, sculptures and photographs on display ensure something for everyone. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 2810 North Walker Oklahoma City, Oklahoma 73103 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=2810+north+walker+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 528-6336 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.jrbartgallery.com/" target="_blank">www.jrbartgallery.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
	
	<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="lyric-theatre"></a><img src="images/lyric-theatre.jpg" alt="Lyric Theatre" width="170" height="113" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">USA Today said the <strong>Lyric Theatre</strong> of Oklahoma is &quot;one of the ten great places to see the lights way off Broadway&quot;!  Located downtown in the Oklahoma City Civic Center, you can watch performances of Broadway plays like <em>Hairspray</em>, <em>Ragtime</em> and <em>A Chorus Line</em>. Sing, laugh and enjoy great live theater entertainment. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 1727 NW 16th St. Oklahoma City, OK 73106 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1727+nw+16th+st+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 524-9310 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.lyrictheatreokc.com/" target="_blank">www.lyrictheatreokc.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
	
	<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/ok-city-arena.jpg" alt="Oklahoma City Arena" width="170" height="58" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Big entertainment stops at the <strong>Chesapeake Energy Arena</strong>, the city's top venue for concerts, sporting events, and live entertainment.  The arena is home to the NBA Oklahoma City Thunder and the stage to world class talent from WWE Monday Night Raw to Taylor Swift.  The name might have changed but its top notch entertainment has not.
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 100 W Reno Ave., Oklahoma City, OK 73102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=100+w+reno+ave+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 602-8700 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.chesapeakearena.com/" target="_blank">www.chesapeakearena.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
	
	<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/civic-center-music-hall.jpg" alt="Civic Center Music Hall" width="170" height="128" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">See world class performing arts at the <strong>Civic Center Music Hall</strong> in Oklahoma City.  The entertainment venue is home to the Oklahoma City Philharmonic, Ballet Oklahoma, Canterbury Choral Society, and Lyric Theatre performances.  Enjoy watching nationally and internationally acclaimed performing arts in the hall's intimate atmosphere with superior acoustic sound. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 201 N Walker Ave Oklahoma City, OK 73102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=201+n+walker+ave+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 297-2584 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.okcciviccenter.com/" target="_blank">www.okcciviccenter.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
	
	<div class="cat-list-img"><a name="cox-convention-center"></a><img src="images/cox-convention-center.jpg" alt="Cox Convention Center" width="170" height="85" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">The Rolling Stones, Kiss and Nickleback have entertained at the <strong>Cox Convention Center</strong>, host to major concerts, sporting events and business conferences.  Conveniently located in OKC's Bricktown historical district, you are a quick walk to canal-side restaurants, clubs, music venues and attractions you can enjoy after the event. 
    <p><strong>Address</strong>: 1 Myriad Gardens Oklahoma City, OK 73102 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=1+myriad+gardens+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 602-8500 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.coxconventioncenter.com/" target="_blank">www.coxconventioncenter.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
	
	<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/ok-city-ballet.jpg" alt="Oklahoma City Ballet" width="170" height="59" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Pick up tickets to The <strong>Oklahoma City Ballet</strong>. From opening night to closing night, you are poised for a lovely time at the ballet.  The mesmerizing resident dancers bring to life the magical stories of ballet classics like <em>The Nutcracker</em> and <em>The Wizard of Oz</em>.  Come experience a night of entertainment filled with mixed emotions, story ballet, and exquisite ensemble dances. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 7421 N Classen Oklahoma City, OK 73116 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=7421+n+classen+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 843-9898 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.okcballet.com/" target="_blank">www.okcballet.com</a></p>
	    </div>
	<div class="line"></div>
		<div class="cat-list-img"><img src="images/carpenter-square-theatre.jpg" alt="Carpenter Square Theater" width="133" height="130" /></div>
	<div class="cat-list-txt">Catch award-wining modern works, hilarious comedies, and outlandish musicals at<strong> Carpenter Square Theater</strong>.  You will sing, laugh and enjoy great live theater in an entertainment venue that delights in offering rare plays that have not been presented in Oklahoma City. 
      <p><strong>Address</strong>: 900 West Main Street, Suite 100 Oklahoma City, OK 73106 - <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=900+west+main+st+oklahoma+city+ok" target="_blank" rel="nofollow">Map</a> <br />
        <strong>Phone</strong>: (405) 232-6500 <br />
        <strong>Web</strong>: <a href="http://www.carpentersquare.com/" target="_blank">www.carpentersquare.com</a></p>
	    </div>
