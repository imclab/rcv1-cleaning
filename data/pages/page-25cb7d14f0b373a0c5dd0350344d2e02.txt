<p>Upon hearing the news, Ben tweeted, &#8220;“Kacie B. is back for Sean’s season?! Ha, I love the producers on this show (you know who you are). I wonder how she is going to do with Sean? I will be watching. Good luck KCB.&#8221;</p>
<p>What does that mean, &#8220;you know who you are?&#8221; Ben is such a tween girl.</p>
