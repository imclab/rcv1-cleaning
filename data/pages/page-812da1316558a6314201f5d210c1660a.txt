<h2><a name="path" id="path"></a>
Career Information & Resources</h2>


<p><a name="profiles" id="profiles"></a><img  src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
<b>Career Profiles and Guides</b> </p>
<div class="indent">
<!--in this order per group-->

<p><a href="http://www.bls.gov/oco/oco1002.htm#art">Occupational Outlook Handbook</a> - Information about career, salary, training and employment projections for many different career fields, including arts and entertainment.</p>

<!--added per Dinorah-->
<p><a href="http://www.labormarketinfo.edd.ca.gov/occguides/">California Occupational Guides</a> - Includes job descriptions, outlooks and wages, education, training, and related occupations.</p>

<p><a href="/CareerApps/Callisto/CallistoLogin.aspx">Vault Guides</a> - Insider's information about careers in
this area. Features include "Day in the Life" career profiles, employee reviews of
careers and companies, and detailed information about key employers in the field. Available through <a href="/Callisto/Callisto.stm">Callisto</a>.</p>

<p><a href="/Pubs/ArtsEntertain.stm">Information Lab Publications</a> - Career guides, directories, and other publications
related to this career field.  Available in the Career Center.</p>

</div>




<p><b><a name="artseducation"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Arts Administration / Education / Therapy</b></p>
<div class="indent">
<p><a href="http://www.schoolsintheusa.com/careerprofiles_details.cfm?carid=999">Art Instructor</a> -  Provides information about work, skills and educational requirements from SchoolsInTheUSA.com.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.collegeboard.org/csearch/majors_careers/profiles/careers/106907.html">Arts Administrators</a>  - General information from collegeboard.org.
<p><a href="http://www.arttherapyblog.com/what-is-art-therapy/">What is Art Therapy?</a> - General information and an overview of the field.</p>
</div>



<p>  <b><a name="broadcasting"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Broadcasting / Film / Video</b></p>
<div class="indent">

<p><a href="http://www.careerpage.org/jobtypes.php">CareerPage/All About Broadcasting</a> - From the National Alliance of State Broadcasters Associations.  Provides information about types of jobs in broadcasting as well as job listings. </p>

<p><a href="http://www.entertainmentcareers.net/sbjobs/resources.asp">Entertainment Careers</a> - Includes information about types of studio jobs, as well as professional acting and becoming a film or TV "extra."</p>

<p><a href="http://www.soyouwanna.com/site/syws/tvnews/tvnews.html">SoYouWanna Get a Job in TV News?</a> - Get the scoop on internships and how to land a job in TV news whether its in front of the camera or behind. </p>

<p><a href="http://www.writersstore.com/breaking-into-television-writing/">Writers Store</a> - Article on breaking into television writing.</p>

</div>



<p>  <b><a name="fashion"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Fashion / Textile Art</b></p>
<div class="indent">

<p><a href="http://fashion.about.com/od/cooljobs/">About.com</a> - Offers links to job descriptions, hiring companies and related subjects including fashion history, costumes, shows and designers. </p>

<p><a href="http://www.princetonreview.com/Careers.aspx?cid=26">Buyer</a> - Read a Princeton Review Career Profile.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.fashion-schools.org/fashion-careers.htm">Fashion-Schools.org</a> - Has information on careers, salaries and schools. </p>

</div>





<p><b><a name="finearts"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Fine Arts / Visual Arts / Graphic Design</b></p>
<div class="indent">

<p><a href="http://www.aiga.org/salary-survey/">American Institute of Graphic Designers (AIGA) Survey of Design Salaries</a> -
Comprehensive annual survey of compensation data for the communication design profession in the U.S.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.iseek.org/careers/careerDetail?id=0&oc=100246&title=">Art Directors</a> - Provides information about work activities, skills, preparation, wages, employment and outlook from ISEEK, a partnership with the Minnesota Department of Education, Department of Employment and Economic Development, and other educational authorities.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.schoolsintheusa.com/careerprofiles_details.cfm?carid=1093">Illustrators</a> - Provides information on work, skills and educational requirements from SchoolsInTheUSA.com.</p>

<p><b>Princeton Review</b> -  Read about a Day in the Life of an <a href="http://www.princetonreview.com/Careers.aspx?page=1&cid=12">Art Dealer </a>, <a href="http://www.princetonreview.com/Careers.aspx?page=1&cid=74">Graphic Designer</a>, or <a href="http://www.princetonreview.com/Careers.aspx?page=1&cid=201">Digital Artist</a>, as well as past and future trends, paying your dues, and quality of life for these professions. </p>

</div>



<p> <b><a name="multiple"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Multiple Fields</b></p>
<div class="indent">

<p><a href="http://www.cac.ca.gov/">California Arts Council</a> - State agency     geared towards the development and support of arts in California. Offers grants     and fellowships to individual artists, news, a calendar of events, links to     California arts agencies and other organizations for the arts.  </p>

</div>



 <p> <b><a name="museums"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Museums / Libraries</b></p>
<div class="indent">

<p> <a href="http://www.conservation-us.org/index.cfm?fuseaction=Page.viewPage&pageId=478">American Institute for Conservation of Historic and Artistic Works</a> - Provides information on careers and academic training in the field of conservation.  </p>

<p> <a href="http://www.arlisna.org/">Art Libraries Society of North America</a> - Presents information on events, resources, and jobs relating to arts information careers. </p>

<p> <a href="http://www.sihr.si.edu/tour/amuseums.html">Careers in Museums</a> - Information from The Smithsonian Institution about a variety of museum careers.  </p>

<p> <a href="http://www.archivists.org/prof-education/arprof.asp">"So You Want to Be an Archivist: An Overview of the Archives Profession"</a> - Developed by the Society of American Archivists. </p>

</div>



 <p> <b><a name="theater"></a><img src="/Graphics/Icons/ArrowBlueSkinny.gif" width="19" height="10" alt="" />
Performing Arts / Music</b></p>
<div class="indent">
<p><a href="http://www.berklee.edu/careers/default.html">Careers in Music from Berklee College of Music</a> - Provides descriptions of hundreds of jobs in the music industry.</p>

<p> <a href="http://www2.stetson.edu/csata/thr_guid.html">McCoy's Guide to Theatre and Performance Studies</a> - Offers useful tools, information, and resource lists on topics such as acting, stagecraft and technical theatre, arts management, and more. The guide also presents good tips on research materials and ways of connecting to other theatre enthusiasts through a select list of listservs and newsgroups.  </p>

<p><a href="http://www.princetonreview.com/Careers.aspx?cid=108">Performing Arts Administrator</a> - Read a Princeton Review career profile. </p>

</div>


<h2><a name="student" id="student"></a>
Student & Professional Organizations </h2>
<p>Participating in these groups can be a valuable avenue for learning
  about an occupation or field. They are an excellent resource for
<a href="/Info/Networking.stm">networking</a>,
  and their websites often have information about careers.</p>

<div class="indent">
  <p>  <b>Cal Student Organizations</b></p>
  <div class="indent">
  <p>  Student organizations related to this field include:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="http://www.bampfa.berkeley.edu/student/students">Cal Students at Berkeley Art Museum/Pacific    Film Archive </a>    </li>
    <li><a href="http://bcec.berkeley.edu/">Business Careers in Entertainment Club</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://kalx.berkeley.edu/">KALX</a>    </li>
  </ul>

<p><a href="http://students.berkeley.edu/osl/studentgroups/public/index.asp">Other Student Organizations</a> - List of UC Berkeley
student organizations registered with Campus Life and Leadership.</p>
</div>

 <p>  <b>Professional Organizations</b></p>
 <div class="indent">
 <p>  A few of the major professional organizations include:</p>
 <div class="indent">
 <p><b>Arts Administration / Education / Therapy</b></p>
