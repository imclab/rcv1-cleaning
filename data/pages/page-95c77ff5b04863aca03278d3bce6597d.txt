<p><em><a href="http://www.gutenberg.org/wiki/Main_Page">Project Gutenberg</a></em> now has its own mobile webapp at&nbsp;<a title="mobile site" href="http://m.gutenberg.org/">m.gutenberg.org</a>. &nbsp;The project, which is celebrating its 40th anniversary in 2011, creates free eBook editions of books that are out of copyright or were published under a license such as <a href="http://creativecommons.org/">Creative Commons</a> that allows them to be distributed freely. <p>Project Gutenberg's mobile view appears to be optimized for the iPhone and other touch smartphones but degrades nicely in lesser browsers&nbsp;and worked well when tested with the Myriad (formerly Openwave) browser of a basic feature phone.</p>
