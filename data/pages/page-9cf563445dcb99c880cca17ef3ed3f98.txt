				<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/arts-entertainment-and-literature/what-will-happen-to-camera-films" title="What Will Happen To Camera Films?" rel="bookmark">What Will Happen To Camera Films?</a></h2> 
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2013-07-28T09:22:01+00:00">July 28, 2013</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/author/admin" class="fn n" title="admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>  </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2312" alt="" src="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/film.jpg" width="300" height="200" /></a>Did you know that the human eye is actually a form of camera? The eyes have lenses that act like the lens in the camera and the retina acts like the film. Our eyes actually take pictures of everything we see around. The only difference is that such pictures cannot be printed out like those photos in your camera.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">In most parts of the world, from schools, homes, to businesses, everything is becoming digital. With the emergence and popularity of digital cameras, the question is, “will camera film go extinct?”</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">According to surveys, it will in the near future. The demand for film is continuously going low. In fact, still film supply in the UK has dropped by 90 percent from the year 2000 to 2010. If the 10 percent film users, which comprises of universities and colleges, go digital, then the use of film will be history.</p>

				<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/arts-entertainment-and-literature/how-mixed-media-art-came-to-existence" title="How Mixed Media Art Came To Existence" rel="bookmark">How Mixed Media Art Came To Existence</a></h2> 
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2013-07-09T07:02:27+00:00">July 9, 2013</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/author/admin" class="fn n" title="admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>  </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com"><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-2186" title="Click Here" alt="" src="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/chipboard-arch-arty-oz-16-224x300.jpg" width="224" height="300" /></a>Mixed media art is any form of art which combines 2 or more mediums in a single work. The use of the term actually began in 1912 with Cubist collages as well as the art of Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque; however, these men were not the first to produce mixed media art.</p>
<p>These forms of art are said to begin during 330 to 453 AD where artists from Byzantine Empire used gilded gold leaf on their mosaic, manuscripts, frescoes and paintings. The arts stagnated during the dark ages, however, flourished with the rise of the Renaissance. Apart from working with tempera, a paint medium which dates back in ancient Egypt was oil painting. After that, plenty of artists applied gold leaf to painted wood panels in order to achieve vibrant skies.</p>
<p>It was only in the 20<sup>th</sup> century when three-dimensional images were accomplished. And in 1912, Pablo Picasso created his first true mixed media piece, “Still Life with chair Caning”. After that, he even created several arts applying various mixed media techniques.</p>
<p>These days, mixed media can now be accessed by both professional and amateur artists.</p>

				<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/arts-entertainment-and-literature/basic-info-on-photo-books" title="Basic Info On Photo-Books" rel="bookmark">Basic Info On Photo-Books</a></h2> 
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2013-07-04T11:16:09+00:00">July 4, 2013</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/author/admin" class="fn n" title="admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>  </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2156" alt="Photos" src="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/Photos.jpg" width="300" height="297" /></a>A lot of people are fascinated with photo-books, whether it has their personal photos, class photos, photo collections etc. Often times, people fill their photo-books with the memorable photographs that they cherish most.</p>
<p>But not everyone knows his or her photo-book well enough. Read on to learn a few facts that you may have not even encountered before.</p>
<p>The earliest of photo-books were first noticed around 1843 to 1853. It was for a collection of marine specimens, which were impressed into a light sensitive paper.</p>
<p>The Japanese refer to a photo-book as “shashinshū”. A lot of Japanese photographers in the post war period have produced one.</p>
<p>Today, anyone can make his or her own personalized photo books. With the use of appropriate software and equipment, one can create a photo-book with ease these days.</p>
<p>Photo-books differ from photo albums in a way that photo albums allow you to insert the images on the pages of the book, whereas photos in photo books are printed onto the actual pages of the book.</p>

				<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/arts-entertainment-and-literature/a-peek-beyond-the-picture-frame" title="A Peek Beyond The Picture Frame" rel="bookmark">A Peek Beyond The Picture Frame</a></h2> 
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2013-06-24T15:29:04+00:00">June 24, 2013</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/author/admin" class="fn n" title="admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>  </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-2096" alt="Picture Frame" src="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/587584_white_frame.jpg" width="300" height="224" /></a>A photo frame or picture frame is a border for a photograph or a painting. These frames usually have decorations and are meant to also protect the pictures. Picture frames have become very popular through different generations but who knew where they came from?</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The earliest recorded frame dates back to Second Century A.D, but the earliest carved wooden frames appeared around twelfth century. The most common material for creating picture frames is wood, but there have been other materials used such as gold, silver, bronze, plastic and aluminum.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">To protect the photos or paintings, frames have been developed to have a glass cover so that the entire picture is covered and protected from dust, water and other elements that may cause pictures to get destroyed. Photo frames these days have become more and more artistic, with the advancement of technology, different materials are now being explored and used to build frames.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">But the classic wood frames would always be a favorite for several people because it looks simple but yet elegant in different ways. Photo frames have been part of different people’s lives because they have been preserving the old memories painted on every photo they protect.</p>

				<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/arts-entertainment-and-literature/how-photography-works" title="How Photography Works" rel="bookmark">How Photography Works</a></h2> 
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date published time" title="2013-06-23T01:27:27+00:00">June 23, 2013</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/author/admin" class="fn n" title="admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>  </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p><a href="http://www.photographers.co.uk/html/portrait-photographers.cfm"><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-2087" alt="Portrait Photographers" src="http://www.improveyourbrain.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/camera5-300x199.jpg" width="300" height="199" /></a></p>
<p>Did you know around 400BC to 300BC, the ancient philosophers of more scientifically advanced civilizations (like Greece and China) were several first peoples to test out the camera obscura design for producing images. The concept is basic enough-set up a ample dark room with just tiny bit of light entering through a pinhole opposite a flat plane. The light travels in straight lines), crosses in conjunction with the pinhole, and produces a graphic on the flat plane on the reverse side. The outcome is an upside-down form of the objects being beamed in from the exact opposite side of the pinhole-an remarkable miracle, plus an astounding scientific breakthrough for individuals who existed spanning a millennium just before the “middle ages.”</p>
<p>Photography today has the same principle but has quite technological advancement. Like digital picture applies an electric image sensor to record the graphic as a set of electronic data as opposed to as chemical changes on film. A significant distinction between digital and chemical photography is that chemical photography resists photo manipulation since the device requires film and photographic paper, while digital imaging is a highly manipulative carrier. This difference enables a degree of image post-processing which is comparatively difficult in film-based photography and facilitates different communicative potentials and applications.</p>
<p>Resource info from <a href="http://www.photographers.co.uk/">http://www.photographers.co.uk/</a></p>
