<div itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/BlogPosting"><h1>Arts and Entertainment on Sanibel Island and Captiva Island</h1>
<div id="attachment_3921" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 160px"><a href="http://www.sanibel-captiva.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Sanibel-ArtGallery-restaurant.JPG-e1372212706353.jpg"><img class="size-thumbnail wp-image-3921" title="Sanibel Island Art Gallery-Restaurant" src="http://www.sanibel-captiva.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/Sanibel-ArtGallery-restaurant.JPG-150x150.jpg" alt="Sanibel Island Art Gallery-Restaurant" width="150" height="150" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Sanibel Island Art</p></div>
<p><strong>Sanibel Island</strong> and Captiva Island have something for everyone in the family. Take a break from the beach and enjoy one of the many arts and entertainment options &#8211;  museums, theaters and attractions, the islands have to offer. Some of our restaurants feature local artists&#8217; works.</p>
<h3>Arts &amp; Entertainment &#8211; Museums, Live Theater, Music and Movies</h3>
<ul>
<li><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;"><a title="Sanibel Island Museums &amp; Attractions" href="../sanibel-attractions-in/arts-and-entertainment/sanibel-places-to-go-to/"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;">Museums &amp; Attractions</span></a></span></span></li>
<li><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;"><a title="Sanibel Island Cinema, Theater &amp; Music" href="../sanibel-attractions-in/arts-and-entertainment/sanibel-theaters-music-festivals/"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;">Live Theater &amp; Music</span></a></span></span></li>
<li><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #333333;"><a title="Sanibel Island Art Galleries and Antiques" href="../sanibel-attractions-in/arts-and-entertainment/sanibel-artwork-and-paintings/"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;">Art Galleries &amp; Antiques</span></a></span></span></li>
<li><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #333333;"><a title="Sanibel Island Movie Theaters" href="http://vacation.sanibel-captiva.org/list/category/movie-theaters"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;">Movie Theaters</span></a></span></span></li>
<li><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span style="color: #333333;"><a title="Musical Performances" href="../sanibel-attractions-in/musical-performances/"><span style="color: #333333; text-decoration: underline;">Musical Performances</span></a></span></span></li>
</ul>
<p>Whether you are looking for an educational experience or want to relax listening to some live music, <em>Sanibel</em> and <em>Captiva</em> have it all. Don&#8217;t forget to check out the local theaters and see what performances are playing during your stay!</p>
