										<h2>Burgeoning Art and Entertainment Scene Fulfills Jackson Hole Real Estate Buyers</h2>
										<!--<h1>Jackson Hole Arts and Entertainment</h1>
<h2>Burgeoning Art and Entertainment Scene Fulfills Jackson Hole Real Estate Buyers</h2>-->

<p><img src="/media/galleries/jackson+wy/jackson+hole+area+orientation/lifestyle+articles/arts+and+entertainment/jackson_hole_8jys1082.jpg" alt="Jackson Hole Art Fireworks" title="Jackson Hole Art Fireworks" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="400" height="300" align="top" /><br />It&rsquo;s beginning to seem that everyone in Jackson Hole has an artistic flair or at least an affinity for art. Coffee shops and bars showcase local talent in addition to more than 30 galleries with visual delights from western, to contemporary, to abstract. Open mike nights, live music, and literary events are well attended.<br /><br />If Jackson Hole is a wildlife corridor, the town of Jackson&#39;s new <a href="http://www.jhcenterforthearts.org/" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art and Entertainment - Center for the Arts">Center for the Arts</a> is an artist&rsquo;s refuge. Supporting 20 art organizations, the Center has a 525-seat theater, plus, dance, pottery and painting studios all snug in one state-of-the-art building.  The Center is home to Off Square Theatre Company, Dancers&rsquo; Workshop and the <a href="http://www.jacksonholefilminstitute.org/" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art and Entertainment - Jackson Hole Film Institute">Jackson Hole Film Institute</a>, all regional leaders in visual or performing arts. As the newest addition to the art scene, the Center has fast become a mecca for regional artists while also attracting writers, performers, and technicians from across the country. A joint education component adds an interactive approach to the perennial schedule of culturally rich Jackson Hole entertainment.<br /><br /><img src="/media/galleries/jackson+wy/jackson+hole+area+orientation/lifestyle+articles/arts+and+entertainment/_list/8jys1072.jpg" alt="Jackson Hole Art Music In The Park" title="Jackson Hole Art Music In The Park" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="224" height="149" align="left" />The musical beat begins at the <a href="http://www.mangymoose.net/" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art and Entertainment - Mangy Moose">Mangy Moose</a> in Teton Village, which lines up a year-round calendar of national-class acts from rock, to jazz, to funk. The <a href="http://www.milliondollarcowboybar.com/" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art and Entertainment - Million Dollar Cowboy Bar">Cowboy Bar</a> has live country music to add to Jackson Hole entertainment possibilities and local pubs and resorts host acoustic and dance rhythms many nights of the week.  </p><p>On a highly sophisticated note, <a href="http://www.gtmf.org/" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art &amp; Entertainment - Grand Teton Music Festival">Grand Teton Music Festival</a> is the crown jewel of symphony orchestra and provides locals with worldly Jackson Hole entertainment. Musicians and conductors come from across the nation to spend summers playing in Jackson Hole. Entertainment with a patriotic twist is provided by the Festival&rsquo;s well-attended Music in the Hole, a free local concert every July 4th at Alpine Field.<br /><br /><img src="/media/galleries/jackson+wy/jackson+hole+area+orientation/lifestyle+articles/arts+and+entertainment/_list/8was1002.jpg" alt="Jackson Hole Art Museum Wildlife Art" title="Jackson Hole Art Museum Wildlife Art" hspace="5" vspace="5" width="224" height="149" align="right" />For two decades now the <a href="http://www.wildlifeart.org/Frame_HomePage.cfm" target="_blank" title="Jackson Hole Art and Entertainment - National Museum of Wildlife Art">National Museum of Wildlife Art</a> features a renowned permanent collection. The distinct building etched into the hillside north of Jackson Hole overlooks the 25,0000 acre National Elk Refuge. Jackson Hole art galleries number more than 30 and the town presents one of the top art markets in the nation. Although Western Art is a distinguished part of the art scene, every medium and genre are represented in Jackson Hole with an established and fast-blossoming contemporary arts movement. <br /><br />The active Chamber of Commerce leaves no artistic stone unturned during the Jackson Hole Fall Arts Festival. Chefs, contemporary artists, galleries, collectors, and appreciators come together in a mosaic of local abundance. As always, Western tastes are appeased as well with cowboy poetry, music and ranch tours. In 2007 the Western Design Conference adds prestige in the furnishings and fashion realm. The Fall Arts Festival is a feast of culture, new and old, lassoed together for 10 days in September in the town of Jackson.  //<br /> </p>
