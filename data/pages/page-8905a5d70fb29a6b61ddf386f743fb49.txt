                                <div class="product_detail">                                    <h2 class="video_title" style="margin-top:-5px;">Alfa Romeo Convertible Top Installation</h2><b>Important:</b> Do not begin until these instructions have been read completely. You must make
notes on the order of disassembly and some pieces must be reused. For an exploded convertible top view you can go to our <a href="http://www.international-auto.com/index.cfm?fa=ad&aid=81"> Convertible Top Installation</a> tech page.<br><br><b>Materials Needed:</b> Solvent (Varsol is good), contact cement (Weldwood, Duco, etc.), <a href="http://www.international-auto.com/index.cfm?fa=p&pid=2444&posid=858455">wire
cable</a> for sides of top.<br><br><ol><li>Unlatch front of top. It may be possible to install top without disassembling the bows,
but we have found it easier to take everything apart. Make notes on disassembly
order. Remove screws or pop rivets holding rear retaining strip to body from inside.
Loosen top tension cables at rear. Cut or pull apart the attachment straps on the top
bows. Remove trim strips and weather stripping from front bow (careful, it must be
re-used). Pull or cut top away from this front bow. The old top should now lift off the
car. Clean old cement and fabric from bows.<br><br><li>Detach front bow from top frame. After it is cleaned, it must be attached to the new
top with contact cement. Unfold the new top on a clean cloth or paper with the inside
up. Center the front bow on the top and make a mark on the centers of the bow and
the top with chalk. Apply cement to both sides of the bow and both of the top flaps
that will attach to it. Allow the cement to set (usually 10 minutes). Align the marks
made previously and start attaching the bow to the flap, working from center to ends.
