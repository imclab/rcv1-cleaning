           <h1>Performing Arts</h1>
           <p>Catch a fabulous show in Orange County! Located between Los Angeles and San Diego, The OC is your center for culture and entertainment. Broadway musicals, international operas, new dramas featuring stars from stage and screen, and innovative dance productions are just some of the amazing productions offered throughout the county.</p><p>Art, music, and theater make up the central hub of <a href="http://www.visittheoc.com/maps-and-information/city-and-county-info/costa-mesa/">Costa Mesa</a>. Find a Broadway-caliber show every night of the week at the<a href="http://www.scfta.org/home/default.aspx" target="_blank"> Segerstrom Center for the Arts</a>, located directly across from <a href="http://www.visittheoc.com/shopping/south-coast-plaza/">South Coast Plaza</a>. There you'll find the<a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/renee-and-henry-segerstrom-concert-hall/"> </a><a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/renee-and-henry-segerstrom-concert-hall/" target="_blank">Ren&eacute;e and Henry Segerstrom Concert Hall</a> which is home to resident companies Pacific Symphony, the Philharmonic Society of Orange County and Pacific Chorale;<a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/south-coast-repertory/"> </a><a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/south-coast-repertory/" target="_blank">South Coast Repertory Theater</a>, which is widely regarded as one of America's foremost producers of new plays since 1964. SCR's extensive new play development program consists of commissions, residencies, readings, and workshops, from which up to five world premieres are produced each season; and the 500-seat<a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/samueli-theater/"> </a><a href="http://www.travelcostamesa.com/play/costa-mesa-arts-and-recreation/samueli-theater/" target="_blank">Samueli Theater</a> which opened in 2006 and is designed to accommodate traditional theater-style seating, theater-in-the-round, cabaret-style productions, indy music concerts, meeting banquets and much more.</p><p>Check out the <i>Now Playing</i> and <i>Coming Soon</i> <a href="http://www.scfta.org/home/Events/EventCalendar.aspx?NavID=81" target="_blank">performance calendars</a> to catch your next show at the Segerstrom Center for the Arts.</p><p>Laguna Beach is proud to celebrate the <a href="http://www.lagunabeachinfo.com/listings/index.cfm?action=display&amp;listingID=1033" target="_blank">Laguna Playhouse</a>'s 90th anniversary!
